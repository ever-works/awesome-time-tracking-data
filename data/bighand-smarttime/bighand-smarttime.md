## Overview

BigHand SmartTime is an AI-powered time tracking and capture system designed specifically for law firms and legal professionals. It automatically monitors activities across Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Zoom, and other commonly used tools to generate accurate timesheets, ensuring no billable time is lost and reducing the administrative burden of manual time entry.

## AI-Powered Time Capture

### Automatic Activity Monitoring
- Tracks emails, documents, calls, meetings
- Monitors application usage
- Captures calendar events
- Records Zoom/Teams meetings
- Integrates with practice management systems

### AI-Generated Timesheets
- Automatically creates time entries
- Suggests matter/client associations

### Time-Gap Analysis

- Highlights potential billable work
- Maximizes revenue capture
