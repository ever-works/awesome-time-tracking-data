## Overview

Serialio specializes in touchless RFID badge reading solutions that transform smartphones and tablets into time clock systems. Their idChamp Badge & Credential Reader, combined with the iScanBrowser app, enables organizations to deploy contactless time tracking using existing employee badges and mobile devices.

## Features

- **Touchless RFID Reading**: Contactless badge scanning for hygienic, fast clock-ins
- **Mobile Device Support**: Works with iPhone and iPad devices
- **UKG Kronos Integration**: Direct integration with UKG Kronos workforce management system
- **Multi-Format Badge Support**: Reads various RFID badge formats and frequencies
- **iScanBrowser App**: Specialized iOS app that connects badge readers to time tracking systems
- **Bluetooth Connectivity**: Wireless connection between badge reader and mobile device
- **Fast Authentication**: Near-instant badge reading for quick employee check-in/out
- **Portable or Fixed**: Can be used as handheld or mounted solutions
- **Battery Powered**: Wireless operation without constant power connection
- **Cloud Integration**: Real-time data sync to cloud-based time tracking platforms

## idChamp Badge & Credential Reader

The idChamp reader is a compact, Bluetooth-enabled RFID badge scanner that reads employee credentials and transmits the data to iOS devices running iScanBrowser. This solution eliminates the need for dedicated time clock hardware while maintaining touchless, contactless operation.

## iScanBrowser

iScanBrowser is a specialized iOS application that acts as a bridge between the badge reader hardware and web-based time tracking systems like UKG Kronos, enabling badge-based time entry through standard web interfaces.

## Use Cases

Ideal for organizations transitioning to touchless time tracking, businesses with existing RFID badge infrastructure, or companies looking to deploy flexible time clock locations using mobile devices instead of fixed terminals.

## Applications

Healthcare facilities, offices, warehouses, retail stores, and any workplace requiring contactless attendance tracking using existing employee badges.
