## Overview

Deltek Replicon achieved FedRAMP Moderate Authorization in 2026, representing a significant milestone for time tracking software in the government contracting space. This authorization demonstrates that Replicon meets rigorous federal security requirements for cloud-based systems.

## What is FedRAMP?

The Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program (FedRAMP) is a government-wide program that provides a standardized approach to security assessment, authorization, and continuous monitoring for cloud products and services used by U.S. federal agencies.

## Significance for Time Tracking

### Government Contractor Requirements
Many federal contracts require contractors to use FedRAMP-authorized systems for time tracking and project management. Replicon's authorization enables government contractors to maintain compliance while using modern cloud-based time tracking.

### Security Standards
FedRAMP Moderate authorization requires:
- Continuous security monitoring
- Regular third-party assessments
- Strict data protection and encryption standards
- Comprehensive access controls and audit trails

## Key Features for Government Use

**Compliance Tracking**: Built-in support for DCAA (Defense Contract Audit Agency) requirements and other federal time tracking regulations.

**Audit Trails**: Comprehensive logging and reporting capabilities that meet federal audit requirements.

**Data Sovereignty**: Ensures data handling meets U.S. government requirements for cloud services.

**Integration**: Works with other FedRAMP-authorized systems commonly used by federal contractors.

## Impact

This authorization makes Replicon one of the few enterprise time tracking solutions available to federal agencies and contractors, addressing a critical gap in the government software ecosystem.
