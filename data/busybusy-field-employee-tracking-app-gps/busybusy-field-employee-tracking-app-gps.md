### busybusy Field Employee Tracking App (GPS)

**Category:** Field Team Management  
**Brand:** busybusy

#### Overview
A GPS-enabled field employee time tracking app for mobile crews, combining location data with detailed time tracking to better understand labor costs, improve job costing, and support accurate billing.

#### Features
- **GPS-based time tracking** – Records where and when field employees are working.  
- **Mobile time clock** – Employees can clock in and out from the mobile app in the field.  
- **Project and subproject tracking** – Time can be tracked against specific projects and subprojects to see which parts of a job take more or less time than planned.  
- **Location-linked timesheets** – Timesheets include location context for accountability and verification of work performed in the field.  
- **Job costing and labor insights** – Uses detailed time data to understand labor costs and improve estimating and job costing.  
- **Historical reporting** – Past reports help anticipate delays and understand how long similar work has taken previously.  
- **Timesheet storage and analysis** – Stores timesheets and provides tools to analyze time data for operational and financial decisions.  
- **Photo uploads to projects** – Field employees can upload photos to projects from the app (e.g., work progress, site conditions).  
- **Safety and time sign-off** – Safety and time sign-off workflows within the app (as indicated by safety sign-off imagery).  
- **Timecard signatures** – Supports signatures on timecards for verification and approval.  
- **Designed for field services** – Built specifically for mobile and field service crews that do not work from an office.  
- **Cross-platform mobile apps** – Available on iOS and Android devices via the App Store and Google Play.
