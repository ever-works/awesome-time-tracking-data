## Overview

The Freelance Rate Calculator is a free online tool that helps freelancers and independent contractors calculate their minimum hourly rate needed to meet financial goals. It takes into account various factors including desired salary, expenses, billable hours, and overhead.

## How It Works

The calculator uses the formula: Desired annual income ÷ billable hours per year = minimum hourly rate

It factors in:
- Desired yearly salary
- Business expenses and overhead
- Non-billable hours (admin, marketing, etc.)
- Taxes and savings goals
- Vacation and sick days

## Features

- Simple interface for quick calculations
- Advanced mode with detailed expense breakdowns
- Considers both full-time and part-time freelancing
- Provides insights on pricing strategy
- Helps avoid underpricing services

## Use Cases

- Setting initial freelance rates
- Evaluating rate increases
- Comparing freelance income to traditional employment
- Project-based pricing calculations
- Budget planning for freelance businesses
