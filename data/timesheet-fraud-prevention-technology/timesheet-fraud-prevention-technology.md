## The Problem

Timesheet fraud costs businesses billions annually through:
- Buddy punching (clocking in for absent coworkers)
- Inflated hours
- Unauthorized overtime
- False location claims
- Retroactive time entry

## Prevention Technologies

### GPS Verification
- Captures exact location at clock-in/out
- Validates employee presence at job site
- Creates audit trail of location data
- Detects impossible location jumps
- Generates location-stamped timesheets

### Geofencing
- Defines virtual boundaries around worksites
- Allows clock-in only within boundary
- Sends alerts for out-of-bounds activity
- Automatic reminders when entering/leaving zones
- Prevents off-site time logging

### Facial Recognition
- Verifies identity at clock-in
- AI-powered face matching
- Prevents buddy punching
- Works with tablets/kiosks
- Real-time verification

### Biometric Systems
- Fingerprint scanning
- Iris recognition
- Voice authentication
- Palm scanning
- Multi-factor biometric verification

### Photo Verification
- Selfie required at clock-in
- AI compares to profile photo
- Human review for edge cases
- Time-stamped photo evidence
- Audit trail with images

## Implementation Considerations

### Privacy Balance
- Transparency about monitoring

### Accuracy Requirements
- False positive management
- Alternative authentication methods
- Exception handling
- Backup procedures
