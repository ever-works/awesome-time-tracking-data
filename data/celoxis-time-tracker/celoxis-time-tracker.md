## Overview

Celoxis combines enterprise project management with sophisticated time tracking, resource management, and financial tracking for organizations managing complex project portfolios.

## Time Tracking Features

- Timesheet submission and approval
- Project and task-based time allocation
- Flexible time entry (daily, weekly views)
- Mobile time tracking
- Offline time entry
- Bulk time entry
- Time approval workflows

## Project Management Integration

- Gantt charts and scheduling
- Resource allocation and leveling
- Project portfolio management
- Risk management
- Issue tracking
- Document management
- Collaboration tools

## Enterprise Capabilities

- Multi-project tracking
- Resource capacity planning
- Budget vs actual analysis
- Profitability tracking
- Custom workflows
- Advanced permissions
- API and integrations

## Reporting & Analytics

- Time utilization reports
- Project profitability analysis
- Resource utilization
- Custom dashboards
- Executive summaries
