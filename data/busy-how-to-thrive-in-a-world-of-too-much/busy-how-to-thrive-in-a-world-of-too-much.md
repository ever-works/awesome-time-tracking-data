## Overview

Busy by Tony Crabbe tackles one of modern life's biggest challenges: the pervasive feeling of being constantly overwhelmed with too much to do and no time to slow down. The author combines research-backed insights with a clear three-step framework.

## Three-Step Approach

- The book addresses busyness through a structured methodology that goes beyond typical time management tips
- Each step builds on the previous to create sustainable change rather than quick fixes

## Evidence-Based Content

- Extensive references to scientific studies supporting every recommendation
- Research spans psychology, neuroscience, and organizational behavior
- Practical advice is grounded in empirical evidence rather than anecdote

## Target Audience

- People who feel perpetually busy with no time to slow down or reflect
- Professionals overwhelmed by the demands of modern work
- Anyone seeking to transition from a state of chronic busyness to intentional productivity
