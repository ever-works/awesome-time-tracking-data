## Overview

Habitica turns habit tracking into a role-playing game where you create an avatar, then earn rewards for completing real-life habits and tasks. Your productivity directly affects your character's progress in this unique gamified productivity system.

## Features

- **RPG Mechanics**: Create an avatar that levels up, unlocks equipment, and gains powers as you complete real-life tasks
- **Three Task Types**:
  - **Habits**: Track positive and negative behaviors
  - **Dailies**: Set recurring tasks with customizable schedules
  - **To-Dos**: One-time tasks with optional deadlines
- **Social Features**: Join parties, participate in challenges, and engage with a supportive community
- **Pet & Mount Collection**: Collect and raise pets and mounts by maintaining streaks
- **Guild System**: Join topic-based guilds for accountability and support
- **Quest System**: Team up with friends to defeat bosses by completing tasks
- **Customization**: Unlock costumes, backgrounds, and special items for your avatar

## Benefits

- Makes productivity fun and engaging through game mechanics
- Social accountability through party play and challenges
- Free core features make it accessible to everyone
- Cross-platform availability (iOS, Android, web)
- Active community provides motivation and support
