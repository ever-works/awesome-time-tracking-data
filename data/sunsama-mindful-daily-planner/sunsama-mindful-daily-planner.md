## Overview

Sunsama helps knowledge workers plan their day with intention through guided rituals for planning, time-blocking, and reflection, promoting sustainable productivity and work-life balance.

## Guided Rituals

### Daily Planning
- Review yesterday
- Import tasks from tools
- Prioritize work
- Time-block on calendar
- Set realistic goals

### Evening Shutdown
- Review completions
- Celebrate progress
- Move unfinished items
- Disconnect mentally
