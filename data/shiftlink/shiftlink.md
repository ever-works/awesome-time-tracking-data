## Overview

ShiftLink simplifies shift scheduling for healthcare and non-profit social services organizations, offering automated shift management that respects union agreements and ensures fairness in scheduling practices.

## Key Features

- **Automated Shift Callouts**: Quickly notify qualified staff of shift opportunities, post multi-vacancy shifts in seconds, and award them in minutes
- **Fairness and Transparency**: Time-stamped records and reporting ensure fairness in hospital shift scheduling with automated processes that minimize disputes
- **Union-Friendly**: Respects seniority rules and honors union agreements while automating processes
- **Cost Savings**: Significantly cuts overtime and agency staffing costs by optimizing internal staff utilization
- **AI-Powered Scheduling**: Smart scheduling tailored to department workflows with fairness logic
- **Mobile Apps**: Available on both Android and iOS for on-the-go scheduling
