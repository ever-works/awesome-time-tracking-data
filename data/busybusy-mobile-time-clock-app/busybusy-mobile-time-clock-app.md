## busybusy Mobile Time Clock App

### Overview
busybusy Mobile Time Clock App is a mobile time tracking solution that turns employees’ smartphones into time clocks for crews working in the field. It is geared toward construction and other field-based workforces that need to record time away from a traditional office or jobsite time clock.

### Features
- **Mobile time clock on smartphones** – Uses employees’ smartphones as time clocks instead of fixed hardware.
- **Clock in / clock out** – Employees can punch in and out directly from their mobile devices.
- **Task switching** – Workers can switch between tasks during the day so time is tracked per activity.
- **Field time submission** – Time can be recorded and submitted from anywhere in the field, not just from a central location.
- **Crew-focused workflows** – Designed to support crews working on jobs outside the office, such as construction sites.
