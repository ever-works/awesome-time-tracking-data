## Overview

Timeero is a comprehensive GPS time tracking and mileage tracking application designed for businesses with mobile workforces. The app tracks employee hours, GPS locations, and mileage traveled during work time, providing employers with real-time visibility into field operations while maintaining employee privacy during off-hours.

## Key Features

### GPS Time Tracking
- Track employee hours with GPS location verification
- Know where everyone is or has been while on company time
- Real-time location updates during work hours
- Historical location data and route playback
- Breadcrumb trail showing employee movements

### Mileage Tracking
- Automatic mileage capture while clocked in
- IRS-compliant mileage logs
- Separate business and personal mileage
- Route mapping and distance calculation
- Mileage reimbursement reports

### Geofencing
- Create virtual boundaries around job sites
- Automatic clock-in/out when entering/leaving geofences
- Alerts for unauthorized location access
- Job site verification

### Time Clock Features
- Mobile punch-in/out with location stamp
- Photo capture at clock-in for verification
- Break tracking
- Overtime calculations
- Job and task code assignment

## Privacy & Tracking

**On-Clock Only Tracking**
Timeero tracks GPS locations and mileage ONLY when employees are on the clock. No tracking occurs during:
- Off-hours
- Breaks (if configured)
- Personal time
- Weekends (unless scheduled)

This ensures employee privacy while providing necessary business oversight.
