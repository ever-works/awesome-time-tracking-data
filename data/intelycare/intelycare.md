## Overview

IntelyCare is a technology-enabled healthcare staffing platform that helps facilities manage nurse staffing and clinicians find shifts, especially in post-acute care settings such as skilled nursing facilities, home health, and long-term care.

## For Nursing Professionals

- **Flexible Scheduling**: Browse hundreds of available nursing shifts and choose when to work
- **Competitive Rates**: Access the best per diem rates or contract assignments
- **Work on Your Terms**: Work where you want, when you want, at the rates you want
- **W2 Employment**: Benefits available with W2 employment status
- **Mobile App**: On-demand mobile nursing job app for iOS and Android
- **Nationwide Network**: Profiles from all 50 states with vast array of specialties

## For Healthcare Facilities

- **Free Scheduling Platform**: Staff can view schedules, pick up shifts, and arrange swaps all in one place
- **SMS Shift Alerts**: Text nurses immediately to fill shifts internally before hiring agencies
- **AI Matching**: Ensures the right fit for each shift
- **Multiple Staffing Options**: Per diem, contract, full-time, and part-time services
- **Automatic Optimization**: Platform automatically optimizes and adds staff as needed

## Recent Developments

In January 2026, IntelyCare acquired CareRev, combining two major platforms for finding and filling shifts, merging post-acute and acute care staffing capabilities.

## Pricing

Free scheduling software for facilities. Staffing rates vary based on position and location.
