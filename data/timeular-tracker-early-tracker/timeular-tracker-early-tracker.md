## Overview

Timeular Tracker (now called EARLY Tracker) is an 8-sided physical time tracking device that revolutionizes how users engage with time tracking through a tangible, tactile experience.

## Features

- **Physical Tracker**: 8-sided device that users flip to switch between different tracked activities
- **Bluetooth Connectivity**: Wirelessly syncs with desktop and mobile apps
- **Activity Assignment**: Each side of the tracker can be assigned to a separate activity or project
- **Pause Functionality**: Place the tracker in its resting dock to pause time tracking
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Works with macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android
- **30-Day Trial**: Includes a 30-day trial period, with an additional 30 days free after connecting the tracker

## Pricing

The hardware tracker costs approximately $69, with subscription plans required for software access starting at $5-9/user per month.
