## Overview

TickTick is already one of the best task managers available, and it includes a brilliant Pomodoro timer built right in. You can attach a focus session to any task on your list, making it incredibly easy to switch from planning your day to actually doing the work.

## Features

- **Integrated Pomodoro Timer**: Built-in focus timer directly attached to tasks
- **Task-Based Focus Sessions**: Start Pomodoro sessions for specific tasks with one click
- **Seamless Planning-to-Execution**: Move from task planning to focused work without switching apps
- **Customizable Work Intervals**: Adjust focus session duration to your preferences
- **Break Reminders**: Automatic reminders to take breaks between focus sessions
- **Productivity Statistics**: Track completed Pomodoros and time spent on tasks
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Access tasks and timers across all devices
- **Task Management Features**: Full-featured to-do list with projects, tags, priorities, and due dates

## Why It Stands Out

Unlike standalone Pomodoro apps, TickTick's integration means you can plan your day, estimate how many Pomodoros each task requires, then execute without context switching.
