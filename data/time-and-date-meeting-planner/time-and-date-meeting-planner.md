## Overview

Time and Date Meeting Planner is a comprehensive tool for scheduling international meetings and calls. It helps users find suitable meeting times by displaying multiple time zones simultaneously and highlighting the best windows for collaboration.

## Key Features

- Support for unlimited locations
- Visual color-coding for business hours, early morning, evening, and night
- Automatic daylight saving time adjustments
- Multiple date and time format options
- Shareable meeting time links
- Email-ready time zone schedules

## How It Works

1. Add locations of all participants
2. Select a date range to check
3. View color-coded time comparison chart
4. Identify overlapping business hours
5. Choose the most convenient meeting time
6. Share the schedule with participants

## Color Coding

- Green: Normal working hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- Yellow: Early morning or evening (6 AM - 9 AM, 5 PM - 8 PM)
- Red: Night hours or very early morning
- Gray: Deep night hours

## Use Cases

- International business meetings
- Remote team collaboration scheduling
- Conference call planning
- Webinar timing
- Customer support coverage planning
- Project coordination across continents
