## Overview

Ultraworking provides structured work cycles, virtual coworking sessions, and accountability systems to help knowledge workers achieve consistent deep work and overcome procrastination.

## Core System

- **Work Cycles**: Structured 30-50 minute focus periods
- **Accountability Pods**: Small groups for mutual support
- **Virtual Coworking**: Live sessions with other focused workers
- **Clear Outcomes**: Define and track specific work outputs
- **Reflection Protocols**: Regular review and adjustment

## Features

- Live coworking sessions
- Work cycle methodology
- Accountability partnerships
- Progress tracking
- Community support
- Productivity training
- Anti-procrastination tools

## Target Users

Entrepreneurs, freelancers, knowledge workers, anyone struggling with self-directed work, people seeking accountability and structure
