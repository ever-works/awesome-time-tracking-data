## Overview

Humanity Time (from the TIMECO platform) is designed for businesses with 200 or fewer employees, simplifying time tracking, compliance, and budget management. TIMECO automates timesheets, scheduling, expense tracking, reporting, analytics, and all other labor-related processes.

- Real-time attendance tracking
