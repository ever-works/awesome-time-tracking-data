## Overview

Factor is a specialized project management platform built exclusively for architecture and engineering firms. The software addresses the unique challenges A/E firms face with time tracking, including phase-based billing, project budgeting, and the need for time tracking that doesn't disrupt creative workflows.

## Features

- **Phase-Based Time Tracking**: Track time by project phase (SD, DD, CD, CA) to understand phase profitability
- **Intuitive Time Entry**: Updated time tracking designed for how A/E teams actually work
- **Budget Management**: Monitor project budgets against time spent and fees
- **Resource Planning**: Allocate staff across projects and track utilization
- **Project Dashboards**: Real-time visibility into project health and profitability
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked time and project terms
- **Team Collaboration**: Share project information and coordinate work across teams
- **Consultant Management**: Track subconsultant time and fees
- **Mobile Time Entry**: Log time from anywhere via mobile app
- **Reporting**: Generate reports on project performance, staff utilization, and profitability

## A/E-Specific Time Tracking

Factor understands that time tracking is one of the most-used features in A/E firms because it affects nearly every part of the business. The platform allows:
- Tracking time by project phase to understand phase completion
- Charging time appropriately across different phases and tasks
- Seeing how long phases actually take vs. estimates
- Ensuring accurate client billing

## Why A/E Firms Choose Factor

Traditional project management tools don't understand the specific needs of architecture and engineering workflows. Factor was built from the ground up for A/E firms, with:
- Phase and task structures that match how architects work
- Time tracking that doesn't interrupt creative flow
- Budget tracking that accounts for percentage completion by phase
- Integration with A/E-specific billing practices
