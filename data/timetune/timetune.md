## Overview

TimeTune is a schedule planner and time blocking application designed to help you organize your day by creating and following structured routines. Available on Android, the app enables users to allocate specific time periods for tasks and activities, transforming scattered to-do lists into actionable timelines.

## Features

- **Visual Time Blocking**: Create color-coded time blocks for different activities throughout your day
- **Routine Templates**: Save and reuse daily, weekly, or custom routine templates
- **Task Scheduling**: Assign specific time slots to tasks and activities
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor adherence to your scheduled routines
- **Flexibility**: Adjust schedules on the fly while maintaining structure
- **Productivity Analytics**: See how well you're following your planned schedule

## Benefits

- Increases productivity by providing structure to your day
- Helps establish and maintain healthy routines
- Reduces decision fatigue by pre-scheduling activities
- Improves work-life balance through intentional time allocation
- Enhances focus by dedicating specific time blocks to important tasks

## Use Cases

- Students managing study schedules and class time
- Professionals balancing work projects and personal time
- Anyone looking to build better daily habits
- People who thrive on structure and routine
- Individuals seeking to maximize their productive hours
