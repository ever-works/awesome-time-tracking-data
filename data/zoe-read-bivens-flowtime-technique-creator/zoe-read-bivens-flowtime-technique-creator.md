## About Zoe Read-Bivens

Zoe Read-Bivens is an educator and productivity expert who created the Flowtime Technique (also known as Flowmodoro) in 2016. She developed this method after finding that the Pomodoro Technique hampered rather than improved her productivity.

## Innovation

Read-Bivens recognized that rigid 25-minute intervals often interrupt creative flow states. Her solution was to work until focus naturally fades, then take proportional breaks (approximately 20% of work time).

## Philosophy

The Flowtime Technique respects natural work rhythms rather than imposing artificial constraints. It's optimized for complex, creative work that benefits from long, uninterrupted periods of deep focus.

## Impact

The method has gained popularity among knowledge workers, programmers, writers, and creative professionals who need extended focus periods without interruption.

## Key Contribution

Read-Bivens showed that productivity methods should adapt to the work and individual, not force the individual to adapt to rigid systems.
