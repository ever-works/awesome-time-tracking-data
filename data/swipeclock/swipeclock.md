## Overview

SwipeClock provides comprehensive software solutions for managing time and attendance, making it particularly suitable for small to mid-sized businesses across various industries including retail, healthcare, and manufacturing.

## Features

- Time and attendance tracking
- Employee scheduling
- Payroll processing integration
- Multiple clock-in methods (web, mobile, biometric)
- Geofencing and GPS tracking
- Overtime management
- Leave and absence management
- Labor law compliance
- Real-time reporting and analytics
- Manager approvals workflow
- Integration with major payroll providers
- TimeWorksPlus platform
- Touch2 biometric time clocks

## Hardware Options

SwipeClock offers physical time clock hardware including:
- Touch2 Timeclock with touchscreen interface
- Biometric time clocks
- Badge and proximity card readers
- Wall-mounted and portable options

## Acquisition

SwipeClock was acquired by Iris Software on May 15, 2024, continuing its commitment to workforce management solutions.

## Target Industries

- Retail and restaurants
- Healthcare facilities
- Manufacturing plants
- Construction companies
- Professional services
- Any business with hourly employees

## Compliance Features

Helps ensure compliance with FLSA, state labor laws, and industry-specific regulations through automated rule enforcement and detailed audit trails.
