## Overview

Daily Grind is a minimalist time tracking and task management application designed specifically for freelancers who want a simple, privacy-focused tool that lives in the macOS menu bar without requiring accounts or cloud services.

## Features

- **Menu Bar Tracking**: Quick access to time tracking from the menu bar
- **Earnings Tracking**: Track hourly rates and calculate daily earnings in real-time
- **Task Management**: Simple task list with time tracking integration
- **No Account Required**: Works completely offline with no sign-ups
- **Local Data Only**: All data stored locally on your Mac for maximum privacy
- **Project Organization**: Organize time entries by client and project
- **Daily Goals**: Set daily earning goals and track progress
- **Simple Reports**: View daily, weekly, and monthly earnings summaries
- **Export**: Export time data for invoicing clients
- **Lightweight**: Minimal resource usage, runs silently in background
