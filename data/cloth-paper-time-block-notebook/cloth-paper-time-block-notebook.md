## Overview

Cloth & Paper offers a Time Block Notebook designed for individuals who prefer physical, analog productivity systems for managing their time.

## Features

- Undated weekly layouts for flexible start dates
- Customizable time blocks for structured planning  
- Two years' worth of weekly schedules with 104 pages
- Large format: 9.25" x 10.5" for ample writing space
- Designed for time blocking methodology popularized by Cal Newport

## Use Cases

- Daily schedule planning with dedicated time blocks
- Project and task time allocation
- Work-life balance visualization
- Deep work scheduling
