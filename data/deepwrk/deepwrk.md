## Overview

Deepwrk is a body doubling platform for people with ADHD to focus and get more done when working from home. The platform integrates coworking, gamification, and community to help neurodivergent adults escape distractions and complete tasks.

## What is Body Doubling?

Body doubling is a productivity technique where people complete tasks in the presence of others. It works on the principle of social facilitation - the idea that the presence of others can improve our performance on tasks.

## How It Works

Deepwrk hosts virtual ADHD coworking sessions where participants:
- Share their goals at the beginning of the session
- Work on individual tasks in silence
- Check in at the end to discuss progress

## Power Hour Sessions

One-hour focus sessions designed to help you focus better, work smarter, and get more done together in small groups.

## Benefits for ADHD

- **For Inattentive ADHD**: Virtual body doubling provides a light dopamine stimulus to initiate work
- **For Hyperactive ADHD**: The presence of others serves as an anchor to maintain focus and stay on task
- **Accountability**: Regular check-ins can increase the likelihood of achieving goals from 25% to 95%

## Features

- Small group sessions for focused work
- Gamified experience to help members start, stay on track, and complete tasks
- Community support from other ADHD adults
- Silent work sprints with structured check-ins

## Target Users

Designed specifically for ADHD and neurodivergent adults working remotely or from home.
