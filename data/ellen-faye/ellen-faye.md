## Overview

Ellen Faye is a certified productivity leadership coach who supports her clients in developing and integrating processes to lead effectively and efficiently.

## Areas of Expertise

- **Productive Strategic Planning** - Helping leaders develop systematic approaches to strategic planning that maximize efficiency
- **Board and Leadership Training** - Teaching leaders and boards how to increase productivity
- **Time Management** - Specialized coaching for time management improvement
- **Leadership Coaching** - Executive coaching for organizational and individual productivity

## Approach

Faye integrates process development with leadership coaching, helping client organizations build sustainable productivity systems that can be maintained long-term by their team members.
