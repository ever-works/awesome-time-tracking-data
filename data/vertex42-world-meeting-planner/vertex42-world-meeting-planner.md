## Overview

Vertex42's World Meeting Planner is a free Excel template designed to help schedule international meetings across different time zones. Unlike web-based tools, this downloadable template offers offline functionality and full customization.

## Key Features

- Excel-based for offline use
- Support for multiple time zones
- Automatic daylight saving time adjustments
- Color-coded hour displays
- Customizable locations and labels
- Printable meeting schedules
- No internet connection required after download

## Template Components

- Time zone selector with 400+ cities
- Visual time comparison grid
- Color-coding for business vs. off hours
- Meeting time suggestions
- Notes and agenda section
- Customizable formatting

## How to Use

1. Download the Excel template
2. Select your home time zone
3. Add participant locations
4. View the time comparison chart
5. Identify optimal meeting windows
6. Print or share the schedule

## Benefits

- Works offline without internet
- Full customization control
- Can be saved and reused
- Integrates with existing Excel workflows
- Privacy-friendly (data stays local)
