## Overview

Allied Time USA specializes in biometric time and attendance systems, offering a comprehensive range of fingerprint, facial recognition, and hand geometry time clocks to suit different business environments and requirements.

## Features

- Multiple biometric technologies (fingerprint, face, hand geometry)
- Standalone and networked models
- Cloud-based and on-premise options
- Touchscreen interfaces
- Badge and PIN backup options
- Real-time data transfer
- Integration with major payroll providers
- Employee self-service features
- Weatherproof models for outdoor use
- Large employee capacity
- Multiple shift support
- Detailed reporting capabilities
- Mobile app options
