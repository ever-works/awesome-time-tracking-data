## Overview

Fieldy is an all-in-one field service management platform that streamlines operations for service businesses with mobile teams. It connects job management, dispatching, time tracking, reporting, and invoicing in one comprehensive solution.

## Features

- GPS-enabled time tracking for field workers
- Real-time job dispatching and scheduling
- Mobile app for technicians and field staff
- Customer management and service history
- Estimates and invoicing
- Inventory management
- Route optimization
- Automated notifications and reminders
- Timesheet approval workflows
- Integration with QuickBooks and other accounting software

## Industry Focus

Fieldy is particularly well-suited for:
- HVAC companies
- Plumbing services
- Electrical contractors
- Landscaping businesses
- Cleaning services
- Pest control companies

## Benefits

- Reduces administrative overhead by centralizing operations
- Improves technician productivity through better scheduling
- Enhances customer satisfaction with accurate ETAs
- Accelerates payment cycles with mobile invoicing
- Provides real-time visibility into field operations

## Pricing

Fieldy offers tiered pricing based on the number of users and features required. Pricing typically starts around $50-80 per month for small teams, with enterprise plans available for larger organizations.
