## Overview

Goblin Tools is a collection of small, simple, single-task AI-based tools specifically designed to help neurodivergent people with tasks they find overwhelming or difficult. The tools are free to use and focus on breaking down complex tasks, managing executive dysfunction, and simplifying daily challenges.

## Available Tools

### Magic ToDo

Breaks down complex tasks into smaller, manageable steps with adjustable levels of detail. Perfect for people who struggle with task initiation or feel overwhelmed by large projects.

### Formalizer

Rewrites text to adjust tone and formality level, helping with professional communication when you're not sure how formal to be.

### Judge

Interprets the tone of text to help understand whether a message is friendly, angry, or neutral—useful for people who struggle with reading social cues in written communication.

### Estimator

Provides time estimates for tasks, helping with planning and time blindness—a common ADHD challenge.

### Compiler

Takes brain dumps of thoughts and organizes them into structured lists or actionable items.

### Chef

Helps with meal planning by suggesting recipes based on available ingredients and dietary restrictions.

## Target Audience

Specifically designed for neurodivergent individuals including:

- People with ADHD
- Autistic individuals
- Those with executive dysfunction
- Anyone who struggles with task initiation, time estimation, or organization

## Key Features

- **Free to Use**: All tools available at no cost
- **Simple Interface**: Clean, distraction-free design
- **Single-Purpose Tools**: Each tool does one thing well
- **AI-Powered**: Uses artificial intelligence to provide helpful suggestions
- **No Account Required**: Use immediately without signup
- **Neurodivergent-Focused**: Built specifically for neurodivergent challenges

## Time Management Benefits

- Breaks overwhelming tasks into achievable steps
- Provides realistic time estimates for better planning
- Helps organize scattered thoughts into actionable plans
- Reduces anxiety around task complexity
- Supports executive function challenges

## Accessibility

Goblin Tools is designed with accessibility in mind, using clear language, simple interfaces, and focusing on reducing cognitive load rather than adding features.
