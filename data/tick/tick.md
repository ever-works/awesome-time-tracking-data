## Overview

TICK (Tickspot) is a project-based time tracking tool with a simple user interface designed to help teams manage project budgets. It focuses on task-level time tracking with budget constraints to ensure projects stay within scope and on budget.

## Features

- Task-listing with assigned budgets for each task
- Budget management to keep projects financially on track
- Task import from project management tools (Asana, Trello, Basecamp)
- Basic analytics focused on budgeting over deep reporting
- Project-based pricing model rather than per-user pricing

## Pricing

- Free plan: Supports 1 project
- $19/mo for 10 projects
- $49/mo for 30 projects
- $79/mo for 60 projects
- $149/mo for unlimited projects

## Best For

Development teams that work on a project-to-project basis and need straightforward time tracking tied to project budgets.
