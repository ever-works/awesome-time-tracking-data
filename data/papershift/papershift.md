- Manage shifts, absences, vacations and employee time tracking on any online device

### Time Tracking
- Accurate employee time tracking anywhere with Papershift app

### Workforce Management

- Service and shift planning
- 100% rota coverage guarantee
- Staff rota to payroll integration
