## Overview

Timeero is a specialized time and mileage tracking application designed for mobile workforces and field teams. It combines accurate time tracking with comprehensive location management features, serving industries from home healthcare to construction.

## Features

- **GPS Time Tracking** - Track employee locations when clocked in with breadcrumb trail
- **Mileage Tracking** - Automatic mileage calculation for reimbursement and job costing
- **Geofencing** - Create virtual boundaries around job sites for automated tracking
- **Route Verification** - Verify employee routes and travel patterns
- **Segmented Tracking** - Track mileage by job, client, or project
- **PTO Management** - Manage time-off requests and accruals
- **Timesheet Editing** - Admin controls for timesheet corrections and approvals
- **Real-Time Tracking** - Live view of employee locations during work hours
- **Offline Tracking** - Continue tracking even without cell signal
- **Integrations** - Connect with QuickBooks, ADP, Paychex, and other systems
