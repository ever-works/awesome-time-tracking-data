## Overview

Focus Timer is a physical adjustable hourglass designed to provide frictionless time tracking without the distractions of digital devices. Simply flip it over to start timing.

## Features

- **Physical Flip Mechanism**: Start timer by flipping the device
- **Automatic Reset**: Resets to same duration when flipped back
- **Gentle Light Indicators**: Lights slowly deplete as time elapses
- **Glanceable Design**: Check remaining time at a glance without disruption
- **No Digital Distractions**: Completely analog experience
- **Adjustable Duration**: Set custom time intervals
- **Minimalist Aesthetic**: Beautiful design object for desk
- **Repeatable Sessions**: Flip back and forth for consecutive work sessions

## Philosophy

The Focus Timer embraces analog simplicity, removing the friction and distraction of starting digital timers while maintaining visual time awareness through gentle, non-intrusive light indicators.

## Use Cases

- Pomodoro technique sessions
- Deep work periods without phone distractions
- Mindful time awareness
- Study and focus sessions
- Meeting time management
- Digital detox productivity

## Benefits Over Digital Timers

- No screen time required
- Tactile, satisfying interaction
- No notifications or apps to distract
- Beautiful desk object
- Battery-powered portability
