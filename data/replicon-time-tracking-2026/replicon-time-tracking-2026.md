## Overview

Replicon Time Tracking is Deltek's comprehensive time tracking platform that combines AI-powered automation with global labor compliance, designed for professional services firms and enterprises with complex workforce management needs.

## 2026 Key Features

### AI-Powered ZeroTime
- Automatically captures employees' work hours and activities across multiple applications
- No manual input required for time entry
- Smart detection of work patterns and project assignments
- Continuous learning from user behavior

### Global Labor Compliance
- Pre-configured rules for 145+ jurisdictions across 75+ countries
- Automatic compliance with overtime, breaks, pay, and leave regulations
- Built-in libraries for local labor laws
- Real-time compliance validation

### 2026 Roadmap Enhancements
- Strengthened project financials capabilities
- Enhanced user experience improvements
- Deeper labor compliance features
- Embedded AI functionality throughout platform
