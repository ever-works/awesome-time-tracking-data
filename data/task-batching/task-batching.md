## Overview

Task Batching is a time management technique that involves grouping similar tasks together and completing them during a dedicated time block, reducing the cognitive load and time lost from constant context switching.

## Core Principle

Every time you switch between different types of tasks, your brain needs time to adjust to the new context, creating "attention residue" that reduces productivity. Batching similar tasks minimizes these transitions and maintains focus.

## Common Task Batches

### Communication Batching

- Email: Check and respond to emails 2-3 times per day in dedicated blocks
- Phone calls: Schedule all calls consecutively in morning or afternoon
- Messaging: Set specific times for Slack/Teams responses
- Meetings: Group meetings on specific days or time blocks

### Content Batching

- Writing: Batch all writing tasks together
- Content creation: Record multiple videos or create multiple graphics in one session
- Social media: Schedule and create posts for the week in one sitting

### Administrative Batching

- Expense reports and paperwork
- Invoice processing and billing
- Calendar management and scheduling

### Creative Batching

- Design work
- Brainstorming sessions
- Strategy planning

## Implementation Steps

1. **Audit Your Tasks**: Track tasks for a week and identify similar activities
2. **Group Similar Tasks**: Create categories based on required mental mode
3. **Schedule Batches**: Assign specific time blocks for each batch type
4. **Set Boundaries**: Resist urge to break batches for non-urgent items
5. **Measure Results**: Track time saved and productivity improvements

## Benefits

- Reduces context switching costs (up to 40% productivity loss from task switching)
- Improves focus and flow state
- Decreases mental fatigue
- More accurate time estimation
- Better work quality through sustained focus
