## Overview

Microsoft Lens is a mobile scanning app that uses optical character recognition (OCR) to digitize documents captured via a phone's camera. It extracts text from scanned documents, making them searchable and editable.

## Features

- Scan documents, whiteboards, and business cards using a phone camera
- Optical character recognition (OCR) to digitize text
- Convert scanned documents into editable and searchable formats
- Mobile accessibility for scanning on the go

## Use Cases

- Digitizing paper documents for digital storage and search
- Extracting text from whiteboards after meetings
- Converting business cards into digital contact information
