## Overview

DSP Timesheet Mobile is a GPS-enabled time tracking app designed for mobile workforces. It uses geofencing technology to automatically verify employee locations and streamline time tracking for field teams.

## Features

- GPS location tracking
- Geofencing with automatic clock in/out
- Real-time employee location visibility
- Mobile time clock app
- Photo verification at punch
- Offline time tracking capability
- Job and task assignment
- Mileage tracking
- Breadcrumb GPS trails
- Alerts for late arrivals or early departures
- Timesheet approval workflows
- Payroll integration
- Detailed location and time reports
- Multi-site management

## Pricing

Subscription-based pricing per user. Different tiers based on features and company size. Free trial available.
