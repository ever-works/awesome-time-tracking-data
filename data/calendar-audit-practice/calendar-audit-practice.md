## Overview

Calendar Audit systematically analyzes how calendar time is allocated versus priorities.

## Process

1. Review past 2-4 weeks of calendar
2. Categorize all time blocks
3. Calculate percentage per category
4. Compare to ideal/intended allocation
5. Identify largest gaps
6. Make systematic changes

## Common Findings

- 60%+ time in meetings vs 30% intended
- Minimal deep work blocks
- No strategic thinking time
- Personal priorities absent

## Actions

- Decline/delegate meetings
- Block focus time
- Set meeting policies
- Protect boundaries
