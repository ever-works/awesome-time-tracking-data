## Overview

Tempello is a legal technology company transforming the way law firms track billable time. Built by attorneys, Tempello automatically captures billable email activity, syncs it with practice management software, and generates accurate, customizable time entries without disrupting workflow.

## Features

- **Automatic Email Tracking**: Scans inbox and identifies every email related to active matters
- **AI Categorization**: Uses machine learning and metadata to auto-categorize communications with 99% accuracy
- **Auto Time Entry Generation**: Writes time entry descriptions and logs entries automatically
- **Practice Management Integration**: Seamlessly integrates with Clio, MyCase, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and Outlook
- **Matter Matching**: Matches emails to the right client and case with near-perfect accuracy
- **Customizable Entries**: Generates accurate, customizable time entries
- **No Workflow Disruption**: Works silently in the background without interrupting attorney work
- **Billable Hour Recovery**: Law firms report capturing 20-30% more billable time
