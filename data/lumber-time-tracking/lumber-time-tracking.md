## Overview

Lumber Time Tracking is custom-built specifically for the construction industry, offering comprehensive features designed for managing field crews, job costing, and offline time tracking on job sites.

## Features

- **Offline Mode**: Continue tracking time without internet service, syncs when back online
- **Real-Time Job Costing**: Track labor costs against specific jobs and phases in real-time
- **Two-Way Crew Messaging**: Communicate with field workers directly through the app
- **Foreman Mode**: Special access level for crew leaders to manage team timesheets
- **GPS Location Tracking**: Verify worker presence at job sites
- **Equipment Time Tracking**: Track both labor and equipment hours
- **Photo Documentation**: Attach photos to time entries for verification
- **Daily Reports**: Automated daily job progress and time reports

## Construction-Specific Capabilities

- Multiple job site support
- Cost code tracking
- Prevailing wage compliance
- Certified payroll reports
- Union vs. non-union worker management
- Integration with construction accounting software
