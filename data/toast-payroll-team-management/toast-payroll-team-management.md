## Overview

Toast Payroll & Team Management is integrated directly into the Toast POS system, offering seamless time tracking, payroll processing, and HR features specifically designed for restaurants and hospitality businesses.

## Features

- **POS Integration**: Fully integrated with Toast point-of-sale system
- **Automatic Time Tracking**: Clock-ins sync automatically from POS
- **Tip Management**: Automated tip pooling and distribution
- **Payroll Processing**: Full-service payroll with tax filing
- **Scheduling**: Employee scheduling integrated with POS
- **Labor Cost Management**: Real-time labor cost visibility
- **Compliance**: Automated compliance with labor laws
- **Mobile Access**: Employees manage schedules via mobile app
- **HR Tools**: Onboarding, benefits administration, and PTO tracking
- **Shift Trading**: Employees can swap shifts with approval
- **Overtime Alerts**: Prevent unplanned overtime
- **Reporting**: Labor analytics and performance reports

## Benefits of POS Integration

- Eliminates double entry of time data
- Ensures accurate wage calculations including tips
- Provides real-time labor cost vs sales metrics
- Simplifies tip reporting and compliance
- Reduces payroll processing time significantly

## Target Market

Restaurants using Toast POS system, including quick service, fast casual, full service, and multi-location restaurant groups.

## Industry Specialization

Built specifically for restaurant industry with features like tip pooling, shift differentials, and break compliance unique to food service.
