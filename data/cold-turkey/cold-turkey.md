## Overview

Cold Turkey is a productivity app that helps you block websites and applications to fight procrastination and digital distractions. It's known for being virtually impossible to bypass when blocking is active.

## Features

- **Website Blocking**: Block distracting websites
- **Application Blocking**: Block apps and games
- **Scheduled Blocks**: Recurring blocking schedules
- **Frozen Turkey**: Lock computer during breaks
- **Custom Block Messages**: Motivational reminders
- **Break Reminders**: Enforce regular breaks
- **Statistics**: Track blocking and productivity
- **Exceptions**: Whitelist specific sites within blocked domains
- **Timer Mode**: Block for specific duration
- **Password Protection**: Prevent easy disabling
