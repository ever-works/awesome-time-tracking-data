## Overview

Clue is a comprehensive construction management platform that combines project management, time tracking, GPS location tracking, and team communication. It's designed specifically for construction companies to manage field crews and track job costs.

## Features

- GPS-enabled time tracking
- Geofencing for automatic clock-in/out
- Project and job tracking
- Real-time crew location monitoring
- Digital timesheets and approvals
- Photo documentation and reporting
- Equipment tracking
- Job costing and budget tracking
- Team messaging and communication
- Schedule and task management
- Integration with accounting software (QuickBooks, Sage)
- Mobile apps for field workers

## Pricing

Clue offers tiered pricing based on company size and features:
- Starter plans for small crews
- Professional plans for growing companies
- Enterprise plans for large contractors

Pricing typically ranges from $10-40 per user per month depending on features and team size.
