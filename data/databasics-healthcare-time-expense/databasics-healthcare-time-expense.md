## Overview

DATABASICS offers HIPAA-compliant time and expense tracking software specifically designed for healthcare organizations. It provides secure, accurate time tracking while protecting sensitive healthcare data.

## Features

- HIPAA-compliant security with dual data encryption
- Automated work hour validation
- Expense tracking with receipt capture
- Audit-ready compliance reporting
- Project and grant time tracking
- Department and cost center allocation
- Mobile time entry
- Approval workflow automation
- Integration with payroll and accounting systems
- Detailed analytics and reporting
- Secure data storage and transmission
- Role-based access controls
- Regulatory compliance tools
