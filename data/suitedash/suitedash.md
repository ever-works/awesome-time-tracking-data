## Overview

SuiteDash is an all-in-one business software solution that empowers collaboration with your team and automates interaction with your clients. The platform combines multiple business tools into a single, cohesive system.

## Key Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track time spent on projects or tasks and add that time as a billable item on invoices
- **Project Management**: Manage tasks with assignments, timelines, priority settings, and progress tracking
- **Client Portal**: Secure, white-label client portal for seamless client communication
- **Invoicing & Billing**: Create invoices with time tracking integration and accept online payments
- **CRM**: Manage client relationships, proposals, and contracts
- **White Labeling**: Fully customizable branding to match your business identity

## Time & Expense Tracking

- Track billable and non-billable hours
- Add time entries to projects or specific tasks
- Generate invoices directly from tracked time
- Track expenses alongside time for complete project costing
- Monitor project profitability in real-time

## Pricing Model

SuiteDash uses an unlimited pricing model, allowing you to add unlimited team members, clients, or projects without increasing subscription costs.
