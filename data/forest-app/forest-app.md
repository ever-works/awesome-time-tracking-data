## Overview

Forest is a unique productivity app that gamifies focus time by growing virtual trees when you stay focused, and helps plant real trees on Earth through partnerships with environmental organizations.

## How It Works

### Growing Virtual Trees
1. **Start focus session**: Choose duration (25-120 minutes)
2. **Select tree species**: Different trees for variety
3. **Stay focused**: Tree grows during session
4. **Avoid phone**: Leaving app kills your tree
5. **Complete session**: Add tree to your forest

### Building Your Forest
- Each successful session adds a tree
- Failed sessions result in dead trees (motivation to stay focused)
- Over time, build entire forests
- Visual representation of productivity
- Different tree species to collect

## Real-World Impact

### Planting Real Trees
- Earn coins through focused sessions
- Use coins to plant real trees
- Partnership with **Trees for the Future**
- Over 1.5 million real trees planted
- Track environmental contribution

## Features

### Focus Tools
- **Pomodoro timer**: Built-in technique support

### Gamification Elements
- **Tree collection**: Unlock different species
- **Achievements**: Unlock rewards
- **Leaderboards**: Compete with friends
- **Statistics**: Track focus history
- **Streaks**: Maintain daily consistency

### Social Features
- **Friend groups**: Compare forests with friends
- **Team rooms**: Focus together remotely
- **Shared goals**: Collective tree growing
