## Overview

Corcava is time tracking software tailored specifically for designers and creative professionals. It understands the unique challenges of tracking creative work, including design revisions, client feedback cycles, and the non-linear nature of the creative process.

## Features

- **Design Tool Integration**: Track time in Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch automatically
- **Revision Tracking**: Log time spent on each design revision separately
- **Client Project Management**: Organize time by client and project
- **Approval Workflows**: Track time spent waiting for and implementing client feedback
- **Portfolio Time Analysis**: Understand time investment in portfolio pieces
- **Creative vs. Admin Time**: Separate creative work from administrative tasks
- **Invoice Generation**: Create professional invoices from tracked design time
- **Project Templates**: Reusable templates for common design project types

## Designer-Specific Benefits

- Accurately bill for revision rounds
- Demonstrate time investment to clients
- Identify scope creep early
- Improve project estimates over time
- Track creative process time realistically
- Balance portfolio work with paid work
