## Overview

Tanda is a powerful all-in-one tool for managing hourly employees in Australia, with a specific focus on Fair Work requirements and Modern Award pay rates. The platform serves over 10,000 businesses across Australia.

## Key Compliance Features

- **Award Interpretation & Templates**: Tanda's compliance team researches pay and conditions for the most common Awards, and their award templates help automate calculations for base rates right up to the most complex employment conditions
- **Roster Validations**: Automatically flags compliance risks on the roster and allows customization of roster validations to warn managers about issues before they occur
- **Break Compliance**: Creates rosters that are automatically populated with the correct breaks based on the industry award
- **Award Interpretation Engine**: Automatically calculates complex pay rates, penalties, and allowances based on industry and enterprise agreements

## Workforce Management Features

- Create schedules that comply with modern awards and enterprise agreements
- Automated time and attendance tracking with mobile clock-in/out
- Leave management and approval workflows
- Payroll integration for streamlined processing

## Target Users

Designed specifically for Australian businesses managing hourly workers, particularly in retail, hospitality, healthcare, and service industries where award compliance is critical.
