## Overview

A-Systems specializes in time clock hardware and workforce management software, providing both traditional and modern timekeeping solutions for businesses requiring reliable time and attendance tracking.

## Time Clock Hardware

### Mechanical & Electronic Punch Clocks
- **Traditional Punch Clocks**: Mechanical time stamp machines
- **Electronic Punch Clocks**: Digital card stamping systems
- **Time Card Supplies**: Compatible time cards and ribbons
- **Document Stamps**: Time and date stamping for documents
- **Heavy-Duty Construction**: Built for long-term industrial use

### Biometric Time Clocks
- **Fingerprint Time Clocks**: Prevent buddy punching with fingerprint scanning
- **Facial Recognition Clocks**: Touchless biometric verification
- **RFID Proximity Clocks**: Contactless badge readers
- **Combination Systems**: Multi-modal authentication options

### Time Clock Features
- **Automatic DST Adjustment**: Updates for daylight saving time
- **Battery Backup**: Maintains time during power outages
- **Multi-Language Support**: Interface in multiple languages
- **LCD Displays**: Clear, easy-to-read screens
- **Audit Trail**: Complete record of all punches
