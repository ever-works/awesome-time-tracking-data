## Overview

RSIGuard is one of the most comprehensive RSI (Repetitive Strain Injury) prevention programs available, combining computer activity monitoring, enforced break reminders, stretch coaching, and ergonomic analysis. Used by individuals and organizations worldwide to prevent computer-related injuries and improve workplace health.

## Features

- **ForgetMeNots (Microbreaks)**: Short breaks every 10-30 minutes (15 minutes recommended)
- **AutoClick**: Automatic clicking to reduce mouse strain
- **Stretch Exercises**: Library of guided stretches with visual demonstrations
- **Workpace**: Monitors work intensity and suggests breaks accordingly
- **Keystroke Analysis**: Identifies typing patterns that increase injury risk
- **Mouse Tracking**: Monitors mouse usage for repetitive motion risks
- **Break Enforcement**: Can lock screen to ensure breaks are taken
- **Ergonomic Feedback**: Real-time feedback on posture and hand position
- **Data Logging**: Tracks usage patterns for personal or organizational review
- **Customizable Schedules**: Adjust break frequency and duration
- **Multi-Monitor Support**: Works across multiple displays
- **Portable Mode**: Can run from USB drive without installation

## ForgetMeNots (Microbreaks)

RSIGuard's signature feature:
- Recommended range: 10-30 minutes between microbreaks
- Optimal setting: Every 15 minutes for most users
- Duration: Typically 20-30 seconds
- Purpose: Allow muscles brief recovery before fatigue sets in

## Break Types

1. **Microbreaks (ForgetMeNots)**: Very short (20-30 sec) breaks every 10-30 min
2. **Rest Breaks**: Longer breaks (5-10 min) every 1-2 hours
3. **Stretch Breaks**: Guided exercise breaks with visual coaching

## AutoClick Feature

Reduces mouse-related strain by:
- Automatically clicking when cursor pauses
- Configurable delay before auto-click
- Different modes for different applications
- Particularly helpful for graphic designers, CAD users, and gamers
