## Overview

Caflou is a visual project management tool designed specifically for agencies, creative teams, and service businesses managing multiple client projects simultaneously. The platform combines kanban-style task management with integrated time tracking and client collaboration features.

## Key Features

- **Visual Kanban Boards** - Drag-and-drop task management across customizable workflow stages
- **Built-In Time Tracking** - Track time directly on tasks and projects
- **Client Portals** - Give clients visibility into project progress without full platform access
- **Resource Planning** - Allocate team members to projects and monitor capacity
- **Gantt Timeline View** - Visualize project schedules and dependencies
- **Custom Workflows** - Create workflows tailored to your process
- **Time Reports** - Generate detailed time reports by project, client, or team member
- **Budget Management** - Track project budgets against actual time and costs

## Agency-Focused Design

Caflou is purpose-built for agencies and service businesses with features like:
- Multi-client project management
- Billable hour tracking
- Client communication and approval workflows
- Project profitability analysis
- Team workload balancing

## Integration & Collaboration

- **Client Collaboration** - Clients can view progress, approve deliverables, and communicate within projects
- **File Management** - Attach files and designs to tasks
- **Comments & @Mentions** - Team communication tied to specific tasks
- **Email Integration** - Turn emails into tasks automatically
