## Overview

The Time-Block Planner is a physical daily planner created by Cal Newport specifically designed to support deep work practices through structured time blocking, metric tracking, and shutdown rituals.

## Features

### Daily Pages

- **Time-Block Grid**: Pre-formatted grid for planning every minute of your workday
- **Work Day Columns**: Space to plan work activities in hourly blocks
- **Deep Work Tracking**: Dedicated section to record hours of deep work completed
- **Shutdown Ritual**: Checkbox and space for end-of-day shutdown completion
- **Notes Section**: Area for capturing ideas and reflections

### Planning Methodology

- **Minute-by-Minute Planning**: Assign every minute of workday to specific activity
- **Flexibility**: Built-in space for replanning when schedule changes
- **Metric Tracking**: Track deep work hours to monitor productivity trends
- **Reflection**: Weekly review pages to analyze what worked and what didn't

### Design Features

- Quarterly format (13 weeks per planner)
- High-quality paper suitable for pen and pencil
- Lay-flat binding for easy writing
- Compact size for portability
