## Overview

Timr is a time tracking software designed for professional teams who need to track time against projects, manage resources, and generate accurate billing. It offers a balance between simplicity and functionality, making it suitable for small to medium-sized businesses.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking
- Multiple time entry methods (timer, manual entry, calendar)
- Project and task-based tracking
- Mobile time tracking apps
- Offline time capture
- Automatic time rounding

### Project Management
- Project creation and organization
- Task assignment and tracking
- Budget monitoring
- Resource allocation
- Project timelines

### Billing & Invoicing
- Billable vs. non-billable hours
- Customizable billing rates
- Invoice generation from tracked time
- Multi-currency support
- Payment tracking

### Reporting
- Time reports by project, employee, or period
- Budget vs. actual analysis
- Productivity analytics
- Utilization reports
- Custom report builder

## Integration Capabilities

- Project management tools
- Accounting software
- Calendar applications
- Communication platforms
- Export to Excel/CSV

## Benefits

- **Improved Accuracy**: Capture all billable time
- **Better Project Visibility**: Real-time insights into project progress
- **Simplified Billing**: Automated invoice generation
- **Resource Optimization**: Understand team capacity and utilization
- **Client Transparency**: Detailed time breakdowns for clients

## Target Users

Consultants, agencies, professional services firms, and project-based businesses needing straightforward time tracking with project management and billing capabilities.
