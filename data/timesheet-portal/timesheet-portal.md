## Overview

Timesheet Portal is a web-based timesheet management system that provides comprehensive solutions for timesheets, expenses, holidays, pay, and billing. The platform is specifically tailored for recruitment agencies and professional services organizations.

## Key Features

- **Project Timesheet Software**: Accurate cost tracking for projects with detailed time allocation
- **Expense Management**: Track and manage employee expenses alongside time entries
- **Holiday/Leave Management**: Monitor and approve time-off requests
- **Billing Integration**: Generate invoices based on tracked time and expenses
- **Recruitment Agency Features**: Specialized tools for temporary staffing and contractor management

## Industry Focus

**Recruitment Agencies**: Timesheet Portal offers specialized features for recruitment agencies managing contractors and temporary staff, including:
- Contractor timesheet submission and approval workflows
- Client billing based on contractor hours
- Compliance reporting for staffing regulations

## Benefits

- Centralized timesheet approval process
- Real-time visibility into project costs
- Streamlined expense reimbursement
- Accurate billing to clients
- Reduced administrative overhead

## Platform Type

Cloud-based SaaS solution accessible from any device with an internet connection.

## Best For

- Recruitment and staffing agencies
- Professional services firms
- Organizations with contractors or temporary workers
- Companies needing integrated time, expense, and billing management
