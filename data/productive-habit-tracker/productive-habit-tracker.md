## Overview

Productive is a habit tracker app designed to help users build positive life-changing habits through structured time blocking and smart tracking features.

## Time Blocking Features

- Schedule habits across 3 customizable time blocks: morning, afternoon, and evening
- Set custom time ranges for each block to match your schedule
- Habits aren't counted as missed until end of day, even if you miss the scheduled time

## Key Features

- **Smart Tracking**: Flexible completion windows reduce pressure while maintaining streak motivation
- **Programs**: Daily habit tips and challenges to compete with other users globally
- **Statistics**: Track habits and analyze progress through motivating streak chains and detailed analytics
- **Reminders**: Location-based and time-specific notifications to prompt habit completion
- **Notes**: Add notes to highlight main points during the day
- **Customizable**: Organize and customize habits according to your daily routine

## Availability

Available on the iOS App Store for iPhone and iPad.

## Pricing

Free version available with limited habits. Premium subscription required for unlimited habits and additional features.
