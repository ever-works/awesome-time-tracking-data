## Overview

QGenda is a leading healthcare workforce management solution that helps hospitals, health systems, and medical groups optimize physician and provider scheduling while tracking time, credentials, and compliance requirements.

## Time Tracking Features

- **Clinical Time Tracking**: Track physician and provider hours across multiple locations and service lines
- **Automated Time Capture**: Integration with scheduling to automatically log worked hours
- **Shift Documentation**: Record actual vs. scheduled hours for accurate billing
- **On-Call Tracking**: Monitor and compensate for on-call duties
- **RVU Tracking**: Relative Value Unit tracking for productivity measurement
- **Overtime Management**: Monitor and manage provider overtime and burnout risk
- **Mobile Time Entry**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go time logging
- **Multi-Site Support**: Track time across different hospitals and clinics

## Scheduling Features

- **Automated Scheduling**: AI-powered schedule generation based on rules and preferences
- **Call Scheduling**: Complex on-call rotation management
- **Shift Trading**: Allow providers to swap shifts with approval workflows
- **Fair Distribution**: Ensure equitable distribution of shifts, weekends, and holidays
- **Vacation Management**: Handle time-off requests and coverage
- **Coverage Optimization**: Ensure appropriate staffing levels at all times
- **Schedule Templates**: Save and reuse common scheduling patterns

## Credentialing & Compliance

- **Credential Tracking**: Monitor license expirations and certifications
- **Privileging**: Track and manage clinical privileges by location
- **Automated Alerts**: Notifications for expiring credentials
- **Compliance Reporting**: Demonstrate regulatory compliance
- **Work Hour Rules**: Enforce ACGME duty hour restrictions
- **State License Tracking**: Monitor multi-state licensing requirements

## Analytics & Reporting

- **Financial Analytics**: Track labor costs and provider productivity
- **Operational Dashboards**: Real-time visibility into staffing and coverage
- **Productivity Metrics**: RVUs, patient encounters, and other KPIs
- **Custom Reports**: Build reports specific to your needs
- **Budget Management**: Compare actual vs. budgeted hours
- **Trend Analysis**: Identify patterns in scheduling and time allocation
