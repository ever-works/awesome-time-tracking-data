## Overview

Desklog is an advanced employee productivity tracking software that removes manual time entry through automation and delivers actionable efficiency insights aligned to organizational objectives.

## Key Features

### Automated Time Tracking
- Removes manual time entry requirements
- Accurate insights into time allocation
- Eliminates timesheet inaccuracies
- Automatic activity capture

### Productivity Analytics
- Real-time productivity metrics
- Performance dashboards
- Efficiency insights aligned to OKRs
- Transforms working hours into productive time

### Activity Monitoring
- Application and website tracking
- Active vs. idle time detection
- Work pattern analysis
- Productivity trend identification

### Workforce Intelligence
- Advanced analytics platform
- Performance metrics by individual and team
- Actionable recommendations
- Data-driven decision support
