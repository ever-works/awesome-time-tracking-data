## Overview

Netchex provides comprehensive workforce management software specifically designed for restaurant and hospitality businesses. The platform combines time tracking, payroll processing, HR management, and compliance tools in a unified cloud-based solution.

## Features

- **Restaurant Employee Time Clock**: Purpose-built time tracking for restaurant environments
- **Geofencing**: Ensure employees are on-site when clocking in/out
- **Job Costing**: Track labor costs by position, location, and shift
- **Tip Tracking**: Automated tip reporting and distribution management
- **POS Integration**: Sync with point-of-sale systems for seamless data flow
- **Role Switching**: Handle employees working multiple positions in a single shift
- **Break Compliance**: Automated meal and rest break tracking per labor laws
- **Overtime Alerts**: Real-time notifications when employees approach overtime
- **Mobile Clock-In**: Allow employees to clock in via mobile app
- **Schedule Management**: Create and publish employee schedules
- **Labor Cost Analytics**: Real-time visibility into labor costs vs. revenue

## Restaurant-Specific Features

Netchex understands the unique challenges of restaurant operations, including:
- Multiple pay rates for different roles (server, bartender, host)
- Tip credit calculations and FLSA compliance
- High turnover requiring quick onboarding
- Variable scheduling with split shifts
- Integration with major restaurant POS systems

## Payroll Integration

Time tracking data flows directly into Netchex payroll processing, eliminating manual timesheet entry and reducing payroll errors. The system handles complex restaurant payroll scenarios including tip reporting, garnishments, and multi-state operations.

## Compliance

Netchex helps restaurants maintain compliance with:
- Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA)
- State and local labor laws
- Break and meal period requirements
- Overtime regulations
- Tip credit rules
