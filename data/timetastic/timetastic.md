## Overview

Timetastic is a web-based staff leave planner and absence tracking software designed to help businesses streamline the process for requesting and approving time off. The platform is trusted by over 170,000 users worldwide.

## Key Features

**Leave Management:**
- Simple time-off request and approval workflows
- Visual calendar showing team availability
- Automated leave accruals and balances
- Support for multiple leave types (vacation, sick leave, personal days)

**Team Visibility:**
- At-a-glance view of who's in and who's out
- Department and company-wide calendars
- Prevent scheduling conflicts with visibility into team absences

**Integrations:**
- Slack integration for seamless notifications
- Microsoft Teams integration
- Outlook calendar sync
- Google Calendar sync
- Export options for payroll systems

**Notifications & Reminders:**
- Automated approval notifications
- Upcoming leave reminders
- Balance updates

**Reporting:**
- Track leave patterns and usage
- Generate reports for HR and payroll
- Monitor team availability trends

## Use Cases

Timetastic is ideal for small to medium-sized businesses that need a straightforward solution for managing employee time off without the complexity of full HR management systems. The platform excels at providing visibility into team schedules and simplifying the approval process.

## Platform

As a web-based solution, Timetastic works across all devices with internet access, ensuring managers and employees can manage time off from anywhere.
