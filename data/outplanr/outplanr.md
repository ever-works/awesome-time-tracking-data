## Overview

Outplanr is a task management app that turns your to-do list into daily work plans. This simple time tracker visualizes team workloads, tracks progress in real-time, checks how much time was spent on each task against your estimate, generates timesheets, and sends you a morning email with your daily tasks. It helps balance team work, making sure everyone is busy but not overworked, minimizing downtime and extra hours.

## Features

- To-do list view for easy task prioritization
- Visualize team workloads
- Real-time progress tracking
- Automatic timesheet generation
- Track time spent on each task against estimates
- Morning email summaries with daily tasks
- Workload balancing to prevent overwork and minimize downtime
- Intuitive interface with no overwhelming feature bloat

## Pros

- Easy task prioritization with the to-do list view
