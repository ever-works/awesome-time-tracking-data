## Overview

Calculate Hours offers a free Time Card Calculator that helps employers, employees, and freelancers accurately calculate billable hours. The tool automatically adds up time worked and generates formatted timesheets.

## Key Features

- Automatic total hours calculation
- Support for AM/PM or 24-hour format
- Break time deduction
- Overtime calculation
- Multiple days per timesheet
- Print-friendly output
- Mobile responsive

## How to Use

1. Enter clock-in and clock-out times
2. Add break durations
3. Repeat for each day
4. View total hours worked
5. Export or print the timesheet

## Calculation Features

- Daily hours total
- Weekly hours summary
- Overtime hours (over 40 hours/week)
- Break time deductions
- Decimal and hour:minute formats

## Use Cases

- Employee payroll processing
- Freelancer billing
- Contractor time tracking
- Project hour tracking
- Timesheet verification
- Labor cost estimation
