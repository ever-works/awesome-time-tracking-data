## Overview

Deputy is a comprehensive workforce management platform that combines AI-powered employee scheduling with time and attendance tracking, task management, and labor law compliance. Built for shift-based businesses, Deputy helps managers create optimal schedules, employees clock in/out easily, and ensures organizations stay compliant with labor regulations.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Scheduling**: Machine learning algorithms analyze historical data to forecast staffing needs and create optimal schedules
- **Drag-and-Drop Scheduling**: Visual schedule builder with drag-and-drop shift assignment
- **Auto-Scheduling**: AI suggests schedules based on demand forecasts, availability, and costs
- **Time Clock**: Multiple clock-in options including mobile app, tablet kiosk, and biometric devices
- **GPS Tracking**: Verify employee location at clock-in with geofencing
- **Photo Verification**: Capture employee photos at clock events
- **Break Management**: Track breaks and ensure compliance with break requirements
- **Overtime Alerts**: Real-time notifications when approaching overtime thresholds
- **Shift Swapping**: Employees can request shift swaps via mobile app
- **Availability Management**: Employees set availability preferences
- **Labor Cost Forecasting**: Predict labor costs and adjust schedules to stay within budget
- **Task Management**: Assign and track completion of tasks and checklists
- **Communication**: Send announcements and updates to team via app
- **Leave Management**: Request, approve, and track time off
- **Compliance Tools**: Built-in features for FLSA, ACA, and state labor laws
- **Payroll Integration**: Export to 300+ payroll systems
- **Reporting**: Analytics on labor costs, productivity, and attendance

## AI Scheduling Features

Deputy's AI engine:
- Forecasts customer demand based on historical data
- Recommends optimal staffing levels for each shift
- Considers employee skills, availability, and preferences
- Optimizes schedule to minimize labor costs
- Accounts for labor laws and overtime rules
- Learns from your scheduling decisions over time
