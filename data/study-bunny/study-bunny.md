## Overview

Study Bunny is a mobile app designed to help students focus and maximize their study sessions using timers to break down workload into manageable chunks. With over 7.8 million downloads and a 4.62 out of 5-star rating based on 78,000 reviews, it's one of the most popular study timer apps.

## Key Features

### **Focus Timer**
- Pomodoro-style timer allowing you to set dedicated study sessions and breaks
- Hit pause when you can't stay focused and receive motivational advice
- Time your studying with visual feedback and progress tracking

### **Gamified Rewards System**
- Earn Bunny Coins and Carrots for each successfully completed session
- Use earned coins to buy items or music in the store
- Accessorize your bunny with outfits and items as visual rewards
- 50 minutes of studying earns 5 coins

### **Productivity Tools**
- **To-Do Lists**: Stay organized with built-in task management
- **Flashcards**: Test your knowledge with study flashcards
- **Study Tracker**: Customizable tracker to visualize your progress
- **Charts & Statistics**: See how many hours you study each day and session length

### **Music & Audio Options**
- Range of focus music including:
  - Lofi beats
  - Piano
  - White noise
- Helps users maintain concentration during study sessions

### **Customization**
- Customize focus intervals to match personal productivity rhythms
- Adapt timer settings to individual study patterns
- Personalize bunny appearance and study environment

## Platform Availability

- **iOS**: Available on iPhone and iPad
- **Android**: Available on Google Play
- **Desktop**: Can be used on PC/Mac via emulators

## Pricing Model

**Completely Free**: Study Bunny is 100% free with no premium or locked features. This sets it apart from many competitors that have paywalls.

**Ad-Supported**: The app includes advertisements that appear when you close and reopen the app or finish a learning session.

## User Reception

### Positive Feedback:
- Significantly improved focus and productivity for users
- Some users report going from 1 hour to 4 hours of focused studying per day
- Praised for being completely free with all features unlocked
- Cute bunny character and rewards system keeps users motivated

### Criticisms:
- Ads can be intrusive, appearing frequently
- Some users feel the prizes in the store are expensive relative to coins earned
- Coin earning rate may feel slow for some users
