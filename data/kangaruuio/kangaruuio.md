## Overview
Kangaruu.io is a time tracking chatBot for teams that integrates with project management software to simplify planning and tracking workload.

## Features
- Integrates with team's project management software
- Simplicity in planning and tracking a team's workload
- Free 14-day trial
- Pricing starts at $1/user per month (up to €8)
- Team collaboration and project management tools
- Reporting capabilities
- Integrations with MS Teams, Slack, and other software
