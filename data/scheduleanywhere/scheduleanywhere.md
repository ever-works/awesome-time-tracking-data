## Overview

ScheduleAnywhere is a web-based employee scheduling solution designed for shift-based industries including healthcare, retail, hospitality, and emergency services. It provides tools for creating schedules, managing staff availability, and tracking time off.

## Features

- Drag-and-drop schedule creation
- Employee self-service portal
- Shift trading and swapping
- Time-off request management
- Availability tracking
- Automated schedule notifications
- Mobile app for iOS and Android
- Overtime and compliance tracking
- Shift templates for recurring schedules
- Multi-location support
- Integration with payroll systems
- Real-time schedule updates
- Reporting and analytics

## Pricing

ScheduleAnywhere offers tiered pricing based on the number of employees:
- Starter plans for small teams
- Professional plans for growing organizations
- Enterprise plans for large, multi-location operations

Pricing typically starts around $3-5 per employee per month with volume discounts available.
