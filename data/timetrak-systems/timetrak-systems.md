## Overview

TimeTrak Systems provides biometric fingerprint time clock solutions designed to prevent buddy punching and accurately track employee attendance. Their systems combine hardware terminals with cloud-based software for comprehensive workforce management.

## Features

- Biometric fingerprint authentication
- Prevents buddy punching and time theft
- Cloud-based time and attendance software
- Real-time clock-in/out tracking
- Automated timesheet generation
- Department and job tracking
- Overtime alerts and management
- PTO and absence tracking
- Payroll integration (QuickBooks, ADP, Paychex)
- Mobile app for remote employees
- Detailed reporting and analytics
- Multi-location support

## Pricing

TimeTrak offers both hardware and software pricing. Fingerprint time clock terminals are available for purchase, and cloud software subscriptions are charged monthly per employee. Contact TimeTrak for detailed pricing information.
