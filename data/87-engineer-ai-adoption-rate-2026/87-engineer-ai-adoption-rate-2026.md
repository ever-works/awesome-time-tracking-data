## Key Finding

Engineers lead other professions in AI adoption with an 87% adoption rate in 2026, according to Hubstaff's Global Trends and Benchmark Report analyzing data from 140,000+ people across 17,000 teams.

## Usage Patterns

- Engineering teams spend 8% of their tracked time in AI tools
- This represents nearly double the usage from the previous year
- Hybrid teams show the highest AI integration, with usage rising from 5% to 11% of the workday

## Implications for Time Tracking

This high adoption rate among engineers demonstrates the importance of time tracking software that can accurately capture and categorize AI tool usage, providing insights into how AI impacts productivity and workflow patterns.

## Data Source

Hubstaff's 2026 Global Trends and Benchmark Report provides comprehensive insights into how modern work functions across distributed environments, with special focus on AI adoption patterns across different professional roles.
