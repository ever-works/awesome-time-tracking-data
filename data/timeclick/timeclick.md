## Overview

TimeClick is a straightforward time clock software solution designed for small to medium-sized businesses that need reliable employee time tracking without unnecessary complexity. The software emphasizes simplicity and affordability while providing all essential features for accurate payroll processing.

## Features

- Simple punch-in/punch-out interface for employees
- Automatic overtime calculations
- Department and job code tracking
- Customizable pay periods and rounding rules
- Paid time off (PTO) and vacation tracking
- Multiple report formats for payroll processing
- Network-ready for multi-computer environments
- Biometric clock compatibility
- Photo capture on punch
- Mobile app for iOS and Android

## Deployment Options

- On-premise installation with one-time purchase
- Cloud-based subscription option
- Hybrid deployment available

## Pricing

TimeClick offers a one-time purchase option starting around $99-$199 for basic features, with cloud subscriptions available. The pricing model is transparent with no hidden fees, making it an affordable choice for small businesses looking to avoid recurring subscription costs.
