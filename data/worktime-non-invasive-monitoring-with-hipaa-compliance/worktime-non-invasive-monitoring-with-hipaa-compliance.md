## The Non-Invasive Approach

WorkTime positions itself as "the only unique non-invasive, pure productivity monitoring software on the market," focusing exclusively on productivity metrics rather than surveillance.

## What WorkTime Does NOT Track

- **No Screenshots**: Never captures screen images
- **No Keystroke Logging**: Doesn't record what you type
- **No Email Monitoring**: Doesn't read email content
- **No Content Capture**: Only tracks which apps/websites, not what's in them
- **No Webcam Surveillance**: No camera activation

## What WorkTime DOES Track

### Productivity Metrics
- Active vs. idle time
- Login/logout times
- Attendance (arrival and departure)
- Software and website usage durations
- Time in online meetings
- Social network activity time

### Aggregate Reporting
- Team-level insights
- Department productivity trends
- Application usage patterns
- Bandwidth on productive vs. distracting activities

## Built-in Compliance Modes

WorkTime is unique in offering specific compliance modes for regulated industries:

### HIPAA Mode
- **Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act**
- Required for healthcare organizations
- Protects patient privacy
- Ensures employee monitoring doesn't access protected health information
- Audit-ready reporting

### GDPR Mode
- **General Data Protection Regulation**
- European privacy law compliance
- Employee consent and transparency
- Data minimization principles
- Right to access and deletion

### GLBA Mode
- **Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act**
- Financial services compliance
- Protects consumer financial information
- Ensures employee monitoring doesn't expose customer data
