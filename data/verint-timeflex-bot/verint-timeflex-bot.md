## Overview

Verint TimeFleX Bot is call center scheduling software that supercharges workforce management for contact centers by introducing flexible scheduling capabilities powered by AI automation.

## Key Benefits

### Reduced Agent Attrition
Organizations that introduced flexible scheduling with the Verint TimeFlex Bot saw agent attrition drop by approximately 25% on average. This significant reduction in turnover helps contact centers maintain experienced staff and reduce recruitment and training costs.

### AI-Powered Automation
The TimeFleX Bot uses artificial intelligence to:
- Automate schedule creation and optimization
- Balance business needs with agent preferences
- Adapt to real-time demand fluctuations
- Reduce manual scheduling workload for managers

## Core Features

### Flexible Scheduling
Unlike traditional rigid scheduling systems, TimeFleX Bot enables:
- Agent self-service schedule selection
- Preference-based scheduling
- Shift-swapping capabilities
- Dynamic schedule adjustments

### Workforce Optimization
The system helps contact centers:
- Match staffing levels to predicted call volumes
- Minimize overstaffing and understaffing
- Improve service level adherence
- Enhance overall operational efficiency

### Employee Engagement
By giving agents more control over their schedules, the system:
- Improves work-life balance
- Increases job satisfaction
- Reduces burnout
- Enhances employee retention
