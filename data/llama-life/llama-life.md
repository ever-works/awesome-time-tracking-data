## Overview

Llama Life is a daily task planner and organizer designed to help you focus on ONE task at a time, built specifically for individuals with ADHD who struggle with task completion and time management.

## Features

- **Timeboxing with Timers**: Set a countdown timer for every task to create positive constraints around the time available
- **Single-Task Focus**: Gently coaches you to work on one task at a time rather than jumping between multiple items
- **Total List Time Display**: Shows estimated finishing time so you can better plan your day and be aware of time passing
- **AI Task Breakdown**: Integrated AI assistant helps break down larger tasks into smaller, manageable steps
- **Quick Task Capture**: Quickly dump new ideas into the task list without losing focus on current work
- **Celebration Features**: Get confetti when you finish a task to maintain motivation
