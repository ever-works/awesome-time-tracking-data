## Overview

Workstatus is an all-in-one workforce management platform that combines time tracking, employee monitoring, and productivity analytics. The platform is designed for businesses managing remote teams, field workers, and hybrid workforces who need comprehensive visibility into work activities.

## Core Features

- **Automated Time Tracking** - Tracks work hours automatically with screenshots and activity levels
- **GPS & Geofencing** - Location tracking for field teams with geofenced clock-in zones
- **Productivity Monitoring** - Track app and website usage with productivity categorization
- **Screenshot Capture** - Optional periodic screenshots for accountability
- **Project Time Allocation** - Assign time to specific projects and tasks
- **Timesheet Automation** - Generate accurate timesheets automatically
- **Attendance Management** - Track attendance, leaves, and time-off requests
- **Budget Tracking** - Monitor project budgets and labor costs

## Advanced Capabilities

- **Real-Time Dashboard** - Live view of who's working and what they're doing
- **Productivity Reports** - Detailed analytics on work patterns and efficiency
- **Idle Time Detection** - Automatically pause tracking during inactive periods
- **Mobile Workforce Management** - Complete field team tracking with GPS routes
- **Invoice Generation** - Create client invoices from tracked time
- **Integration Support** - Connect with project management and payroll tools

## Use Cases

- Remote team management and monitoring
- Field service workforce tracking
- Freelancer and contractor time verification
- Agency billing and client reporting
- Distributed team productivity optimization

## Privacy Considerations

Workstatus provides transparency controls, allowing managers to configure monitoring levels based on company policy and employee consent. Users are aware when monitoring is active.
