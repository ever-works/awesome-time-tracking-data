## Overview

Paymo is an all-in-one project management and time tracking solution designed specifically for freelancers, small businesses, and agencies handling multiple clients. It combines built-in project management, flexible time tracking, and seamless invoicing to streamline the entire workflow from task creation to getting paid.

## Time Tracking Features

- **Multiple tracking methods** - Clock in via stopwatch, fill in timesheets at day's end, or use Paymo Track for automatic time tracking
- **Paymo Track** - Desktop app for automatic activity monitoring (similar to RescueTime)
- **Manual timesheets** - Batch-enter time for multiple tasks at once
- **Timer widget** - Quick access timer for desktop and mobile
- **Billable vs. non-billable** - Distinguish between billable client work and internal tasks
- **Time reports** - Detailed analysis of time spent by project, client, or team member

## Project Management

- **Multiple views** - List, Table, Board (Kanban), and Calendar views
- **Task management** - Create tasks with descriptions, assignees, due dates, and priorities
- **File sharing** - Attach files and collaborate on documents
- **Team collaboration** - Comments, discussions, and notifications
- **Project templates** - Reuse project structures for recurring work
- **Resource scheduling** - Plan team capacity and workload

## Invoicing & Billing

- **One-click invoicing** - Turn timesheet data into professional invoices instantly
- **Multiple currencies** - Support for 85+ currencies
- **Online payments** - Clients pay via PayPal, Stripe, or Authorize.net
- **Recurring invoices** - Automate billing for retainer clients
- **Expense tracking** - Include expenses in client invoices
- **Invoice templates** - Customize invoice design and branding

## Additional Features

- **Budget tracking** - Set project budgets and monitor spending
- **Client portal** - Give clients access to view progress and approve work
- **Mobile apps** - iOS and Android apps for time tracking on the go
- **Integrations** - Connect with accounting software and productivity tools

## Pricing

- **Free**: $0/user/month - 1 user, 2 clients, basic features
- **Starter**: $3.9/user/month - Unlimited clients and projects
- **Small Office**: $6.9/user/month - Team collaboration and resource scheduling
- **Business**: $10.9/user/month - Advanced features and priority support

All plans billed annually. Free plan available with limitations.
