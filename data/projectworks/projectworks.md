## Overview

Projectworks is enterprise-grade project and portfolio management software designed specifically for consulting firms and professional services organizations. It combines robust project management capabilities with detailed time tracking to help firms deliver projects profitably.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Time Tracking**: Track time against projects, tasks, and phases
- **Project Portfolio Management**: Manage multiple projects and programs across the firm
- **Resource Management**: Allocate and track consultant utilization across engagements
- **Financial Management**: Track project budgets, costs, and profitability
- **Timesheet Management**: Automated timesheet workflows with approvals
- **Billable Hours Tracking**: Accurate tracking of billable vs non-billable hours
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Extensive reporting on project health, time, and finances
- **Client Portals**: Provide clients with visibility into project progress
- **Integration**: Connects with accounting, CRM, and other business systems

## Use Cases

- Management consulting firms managing complex engagements
- Engineering and technical consulting organizations
- Professional services firms with multiple practice areas
- Program management offices overseeing portfolios
- Large consulting practices requiring enterprise features

## Benefits for Consulting Firms

- Improves time tracking accuracy and compliance
- Enhances project profitability visibility
- Streamlines resource allocation across engagements
- Reduces administrative overhead
- Provides executive-level portfolio insights
