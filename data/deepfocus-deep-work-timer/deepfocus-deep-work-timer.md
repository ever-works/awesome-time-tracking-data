## Overview

DeepFocus is specifically designed to maximize productivity and help create the perfect environment for concentration through structured deep work sessions.

## Features

- **Deep Work Sessions**: Structured focus sessions optimized for flow states
- **Detailed Statistics**: Track daily, weekly, and monthly completed sessions
- **Total Deep Focus Time**: Monitor cumulative deep work hours
- **Streak Tracking**: Build consecutive day streaks to form lasting habits
- **Productivity Analysis**: Analyze your productivity patterns over time
- **Heatmap Visualization**: Visual heatmap showing your focus patterns
- **Session History**: Review past sessions and identify peak productivity times
- **Project-Based Tracking**: Sessions are saved by project to track progress

## Use Cases

Ideal for knowledge workers, developers, writers, and anyone needing sustained focus for complex cognitive tasks.
