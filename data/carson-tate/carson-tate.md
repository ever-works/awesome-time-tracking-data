## Overview

Carson Tate is the founder of Working Simply and the Carson Tate company. She is a speaker, coach, and best-selling author who has a mission to change how and why we work.

## Notable Works

- **Working Simply** - A framework for understanding individual working styles and optimizing productivity based on personal cognitive preferences

## Working Simply Methodology

Tate's approach centers on the idea that productivity is not one-size-fits-all. She emphasizes understanding individual working styles to develop personalized productivity strategies.

## Key Principles

- Understanding your unique working style
- Leveraging personal strengths rather than fighting against them
- Creating sustainable productivity habits
- Reducing complexity in workflows
