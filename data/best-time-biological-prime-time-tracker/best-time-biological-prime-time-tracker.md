## Overview

Best Time - Biological Prime Time Tracker is a free iOS application designed to help users identify their most productive and least productive times throughout the day by systematically tracking energy levels.

## How It Works

### Simple Input Method

Users simply input how they're feeling at every hour throughout the day. The app makes it easy to log energy, motivation, and focus levels with quick hourly check-ins.

### Visual Results

The app presents tracking results in simple and elegant formats:

- **Line Chart**: Visualizes energy patterns across hours and days
- **Table View**: Provides detailed numerical data for analysis
- **Trend Analysis**: Identifies consistent patterns over time

## Key Features

- **Hourly Tracking**: Quick energy level input throughout the day
- **Simple Interface**: Clean, intuitive design for fast logging
- **Visual Analytics**: Charts and graphs show productivity patterns
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies your biological prime time windows
- **Historical Data**: Track changes in energy patterns over weeks
- **Reminder Support**: Optional hourly reminders to log energy levels

## Benefits

- **Discover Peak Hours**: Identify when you're naturally most focused and energetic
- **Optimize Scheduling**: Schedule demanding tasks during peak energy times
- **Improve Planning**: Align work with natural productivity rhythms
- **Self-Awareness**: Gain insights into personal energy patterns
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: Make scheduling choices based on actual data, not assumptions

## Use Cases

- **Knowledge Workers**: Schedule deep work during biological prime time
- **Students**: Plan study sessions when naturally most alert
- **Remote Workers**: Structure workday around energy peaks and valleys
- **Freelancers**: Optimize client work and creative tasks
- **Anyone**: Improve work-life balance by understanding energy cycles

## Methodology

Based on the biological prime time concept coined by Sam Carpenter, which recognizes that everyone has specific times of day when they have the most energy and greatest potential for productivity.

### Recommended Tracking Period

For accurate results, experts recommend tracking for at least 2-3 weeks to identify consistent patterns and account for day-to-day variations.
