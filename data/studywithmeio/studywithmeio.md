## Overview

StudyWithMe.io is an aesthetic Pomodoro timer created by Gridfiti that keeps you focused and motivated through beautiful visual themes and ambient soundscapes. It combines the proven Pomodoro Technique with carefully designed aesthetics to make studying more enjoyable.

## Key Features

- Customizable Pomodoro timer
- Variety of aesthetic themes
- Embedded Lofi music playlists
- Beautiful background scenes
- Adjustable session and break lengths
- Ambient sound integration
- Simple, clean interface

## How to Use

1. Visit StudyWithMe.io
2. Select your preferred aesthetic theme
3. Choose a Lofi playlist or ambient sounds
4. Adjust session and break lengths
5. Start your focus session
6. Enjoy the beautiful environment while working
