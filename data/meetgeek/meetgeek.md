## Overview

MeetGeek automatically joins your calendar meetings to generate recordings, transcripts, and meeting notes. It provides automatic recording, transcription, and summaries for Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet.

## Key Features

### Recording & Transcription

- Automatically records Zoom, MS Teams, & Google Meet calls without limits
- Provides accurate and free AI transcription in a few minutes
- Supports 50+ languages and dialects including French, Spanish, Portuguese, German, Arabic, Chinese and more

### AI-Powered Summaries

- AI automatically detects call type (sales, team meeting, onboarding, interview, etc.)
- Applies the perfect summary template based on call type
- Provides context-aware notes and actionable insights: action items, objections, feature requests

### Mobile Applications (2026)

- Comprehensive AI notetaker for professionals and teams
- Mobile apps for Android and iOS enable seamless recording and transcription
- Works for both online and in-person meetings
- Offline capability for face-to-face conversations
