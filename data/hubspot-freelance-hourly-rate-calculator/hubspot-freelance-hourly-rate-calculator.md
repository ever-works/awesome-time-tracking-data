## Overview

HubSpot's Freelance Hourly Rate Calculator is a free, easy-to-use tool specifically designed for freelance marketers, consultants, and creative professionals. It takes the guessing out of setting rates with simple inputs about expenses and income goals.

## Simple Inputs Required

- Overhead expenses
- Desired lifestyle costs
- Preferred annual salary
- Typical working hours per year

## Calculation Method

Starting with an estimate of yearly income, divide by typical work hours in a year (approximately 2,000 hours for a full-time professional working 40 hours/week for 50 weeks).
