## Overview

Expensify can capture and categorize receipts, card transactions, mileage, and more for tax purposes. Named a TrustRadius Buyer's Choice 2026 award winner, Expensify streamlines expense management and reimbursement processes for businesses and individuals.

## Key Features

### Receipt Management
- **SmartScan Technology**: Automatically extracts data from receipt photos
- **Real-Time Processing**: Instant categorization and coding
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Handle international expenses
- **Receipt Matching**: Auto-match receipts to card transactions
- **Cloud Storage**: Secure digital receipt archive

### Mileage Tracking
- **GPS-Based Tracking**: Automatic mileage capture
- **IRS Compliance**: 2026 standard mileage rate (72.5 cents per mile) built-in
- **Route Mapping**: Visual representation of business travel
- **Trip Classification**: Categorize business vs. personal miles
- **Automated Reports**: Generate mileage reports for reimbursement

### Card Integration
- Direct connection to corporate and personal credit cards
- Automatic expense import
- Real-time transaction categorization
- Duplicate detection
- Spend analytics

## Time-Related Features

While primarily an expense tool, Expensify tracks:
- **Time Spent on Expense Reports**: Minimal time required due to automation
- **Submission Timing**: Track when expenses are submitted vs. incurred
- **Approval Workflows**: Time-stamped approval chains
- **Reimbursement Cycles**: Monitor time-to-reimbursement
