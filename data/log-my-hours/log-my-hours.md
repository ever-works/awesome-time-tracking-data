## Overview

Log My Hours is a comprehensive time tracking solution that combines a free time card calculator with professional time tracking capabilities. It's designed for freelancers, contractors, and legal professionals who need accurate billable hours tracking for client invoicing.

## Features

- **Time Card Calculator**: Calculate billable hours in decimal format
- **Flexible Time Formats**: Support for 12-hour and military time formats
- **Break Tracking**: Automatic calculation of breaks and actual work hours
- **Overtime Calculation**: Tracks and calculates overtime hours
- **Pay Calculation**: Computes total pay based on hourly rates
- **Billable Hours Tracking**: Specifically designed for tracking client billable time
- **Decimal Time Format**: Simplifies billing and invoicing with decimal hour calculations
- **Export Options**: Generate reports for payroll or client billing
- **Free to Use**: Core calculator functionality available at no cost

## Legal Professional Features

For legal professionals, Log My Hours simplifies tracking billable hours for accurate invoicing and client trust by providing:
- 6-minute increment billing (common in legal billing)
- Detailed time entry descriptions
- Client and matter code tracking
- Professional timesheet reports
