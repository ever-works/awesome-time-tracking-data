## Overview

YPT (Yeolpumta) is a study time tracking application that creates a social studying experience by allowing students to track their study time, join virtual study rooms with others, and compete on leaderboards while building productive study habits.

## Features

- **Time Tracking by Subject**: Track study time for each subject separately with detailed statistics
- **Virtual Study Rooms**: Study together with other students in real-time virtual rooms
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Built-in focus timer with customizable work and break intervals
- **Study Streaks**: Build and maintain study streaks to stay motivated
- **Leaderboards**: Compete with friends and global community on study time rankings
- **Study Planner**: Plan daily study goals and track completion
- **Statistics**: Detailed analytics on study time patterns, most productive hours, and subject distribution
- **App Blocking**: Block distracting apps during study sessions (Android)
- **Study Certificates**: Earn certificates for study achievements
- **Community**: Chat and connect with other students for motivation and accountability

## Pricing

Free with optional in-app purchases for premium features like unlimited virtual study rooms, custom themes, and advanced statistics.
