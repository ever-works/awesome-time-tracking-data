## Overview

Morgen is an all-in-one calendar, scheduler, and task manager that brings together all your calendars and tasks in one beautiful interface. It includes built-in time tracking, scheduling links, and works across Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, Android, and web.

## Features

- **Unified Calendar**: View all calendars (Google, Outlook, Apple) in one place
- **Task Integration**: Connect with Todoist, Microsoft To Do, and other task managers
- **Time Tracking**: Built-in Pomodoro timer and time tracking
- **Scheduling Links**: Share your availability like Calendly
- **Time Zone Support**: Automatic time zone conversion for global teams
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Extensive keyboard navigation
- **Calendar Overlay**: See multiple calendars overlaid
- **Templates**: Create event templates for recurring meeting types
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on all major platforms
- **Privacy-Focused**: End-to-end encryption option
