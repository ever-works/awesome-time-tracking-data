## Overview

Research on professional services firms shows that automated time capture methods consistently recover 10-20% more billable time compared to manual time tracking approaches. This represents significant revenue recovery for consulting, legal, and agency teams.

## Key Findings

- Automatic tracking detects work that manual methods miss
- 10-20% more billable time captured on average
- Particularly effective for multitasking knowledge workers
- Reduces reliance on memory for time reconstruction
- Captures brief tasks and context switches often forgotten
- Provides suggested entries that users confirm
- Direct impact on professional services revenue

## Impact

For a consultant billing $150/hour working 40 hours weekly, recovering just 15% more billable time equals approximately $28,000 in additional annual revenue. At the firm level, this compounds significantly.

## Implementation

Successful automated capture requires intelligent detection systems, user-friendly confirmation workflows, integration with work tools, and training on reviewing and approving suggested time entries.
