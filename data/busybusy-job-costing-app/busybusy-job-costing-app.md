# busybusy Job Costing App

## Overview
busybusy Job Costing App is a construction-focused job costing and time tracking solution. It uses tracked labor and equipment time to attribute costs to specific projects and cost codes, giving contractors real-time visibility into job performance and profitability.

## Features
- **Job Costing & Profitability**
  - Attribute labor and equipment time to specific projects and cost codes.
  - Analyze job costing data from past and current projects.
  - Compare project progress against allocated budgets and labor hours.
  - Use actual job costs to refine and verify estimates for future projects.

- **Time Tracking for Construction Crews**
  - Precise labor time tracking, including for mobile and distributed crews.
  - GPS-based reminders for employees to clock in and out when moving between locations.
  - Track time spent on different projects and tasks throughout the day.

- **GPS & Location-Based Data**
  - GPS-enabled clock in/out, breaks, and project switching, with time and location stamps.
  - Color-coordinated, location-based data views for at-a-glance understanding of where crews are working.
  - Employee detail views for more granular, per-employee insight.

- **Flexible Clock-In Options**
  - Employees can individually clock themselves in and out.
  - Supervisors can be given control to clock in and out for an entire group or crew.

- **Payroll & Administrative Efficiency**
  - Replaces paper time cards with digital time tracking.
  - Reduces manual data collection and transcription for payroll.
