## Overview

'Who's Got the Monkey?' is a classic management time framework originally published in Harvard Business Review. The metaphor of 'monkeys' represents tasks and responsibilities that need to be handled.

## Types of Tasks

- **Boss-imposed** - Tasks and responsibilities assigned by your manager
- **System-imposed** - Tasks required by organizational processes and policies
- **Self-imposed** - Tasks you take on voluntarily or accept from others

## Core Principle

Many people unknowingly take on other people's 'monkeys' — tasks that should be handled by someone else. The technique teaches you to:

- Identify which tasks truly belong to you
- Find ways to delegate tasks back to their rightful owners
- Avoid collecting monkeys that aren't yours
- Manage boss-imposed and system-imposed tasks efficiently
