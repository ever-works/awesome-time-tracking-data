## Overview

Timely is an AI-powered automatic time tracking software that passively tracks how time is spent across apps, documents, and meetings. The platform builds a private timeline of activities and uses AI to help users create accurate timesheets with minimal manual effort, positioning itself as "time tracking without timers."

## Core Technology

### Memory AI
- Automatic capture of all computer activity
- App and website usage tracking
- Document and file tracking
- Meeting attendance from calendar
- Email and communication time
- Creates comprehensive timeline of workday

### Privacy-First Design
- All data stored locally and encrypted
- No screenshots or keystroke logging
- Activity data visible only to the user
- User controls what gets shared
- GDPR compliant

## How It Works

### 1. Automatic Tracking
- Desktop app runs in background
- Captures activities across all applications
- Logs time spent in each app/document
- Records meeting times from calendar
- No manual timers needed

### 2. Memory Timeline
- Visual timeline of your day
- Shows what you worked on and when
- Detailed breakdown of time allocation
- Private view only you can see

### 3. AI Suggestions
- AI analyzes your timeline
- Suggests time entries based on activities
- Learns from your patterns
- Reduces manual categorization

### 4. Timesheet Creation
- Drag activities to projects/tasks
- Bulk categorization options
- One-click approval
- Export to clients or payroll
