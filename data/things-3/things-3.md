## Overview

Things 3 is an award-winning task manager exclusively for Apple devices, praised for its elegant design and intuitive GTD-compatible workflow.

## Features

- **Beautiful Design**: Award-winning interface with attention to detail
- **Today View**: Focus on what matters today
- **Upcoming**: See your schedule ahead
- **Areas of Responsibility**: Organize by life areas
- **Projects**: Break down complex goals
- **Checklists**: Add subtasks within tasks
- **Tags**: Flexible categorization system
- **Quick Entry**: Capture ideas instantly with keyboard shortcut
- **Natural Language Input**: Type "Meeting tomorrow at 2pm" and it parses automatically
- **Calendar Integration**: See events alongside tasks

## Platform Support

- macOS
- iOS  
- iPadOS
- watchOS

## Philosophy

Balances power and simplicity - sophisticated enough for complex workflows but simple enough for daily use.

## Awards

Multiple Apple Design Awards and App of the Year honors.

## Pricing

One-time purchase per platform (Mac, iPhone/iPad sold separately).

## Best For

Apple users who value beautiful design and want GTD capabilities without overwhelming complexity.
