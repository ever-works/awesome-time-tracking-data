## Overview

SaneBox is an AI email management service designed to save 3-4 hours per week on email by using sophisticated algorithms and machine learning to automatically sort incoming emails into different folders based on their importance. The service works in the background with existing email clients rather than requiring users to learn a new platform.

## Key Features

### Intelligent Email Sorting
SaneBox uses AI to automatically categorize incoming emails:
- Separates important messages from less urgent ones
- Creates customized folders based on email priority
- Learns from user behavior and preferences
- Achieves 98.5% accuracy after 1-2 weeks of use
- Shows good results within 2-3 days

### Privacy-First Approach
Unlike many email tools, SaneBox prioritizes user privacy:
- Only analyzes email headers (sender, subject, timestamp)
- Never reads the actual content of messages
- Does not store email content on SaneBox servers
- Maintains security while providing intelligent organization

This privacy-conscious approach aligns with the 2026 Email Intelligence Report finding that 63% of people would switch email clients for stronger privacy and security.

## How It Works

### Machine Learning Algorithm
1. SaneBox analyzes email headers when messages arrive
2. Determines importance based on:
   - Sender reputation and history
   - Subject line patterns
   - Time and frequency of emails
   - User's past interactions with similar messages
3. Automatically files emails into appropriate folders
4. Continuously learns and improves from user actions
