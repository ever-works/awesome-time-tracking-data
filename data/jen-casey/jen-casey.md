## Overview

Jen Casey is known as the Brain-Based Business Coach (CEO Psyche). She is a top industry speaker and the creator of the CEO Psyche brand and podcast.

## Programs that Profit

Her signature program, Programs that Profit, helps coaches build, sell, and run high-level group programs. The approach combines neuroscience-informed strategies with business development.

## Brain-Based Approach

Casey integrates brain science and psychology into her coaching methodology, helping clients understand how their cognitive processes affect productivity, decision-making, and business outcomes.
