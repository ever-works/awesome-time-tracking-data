## Overview

Digital Wellbeing is Google's built-in Android feature offering free, powerful screen time tracking and blocking features to help users develop healthier relationships with technology.

## Features

- **Screen Time Dashboard**: See how much time you spend on your phone and in apps
- **App Timers**: Set daily limits for specific apps
- **Focus Mode**: Pause distracting apps when you need to concentrate
- **Bedtime Mode**: Automatically enable grayscale and Do Not Disturb at bedtime
- **Notification Management**: Track and reduce notification interruptions
- **Wind Down**: Gradual transition to bedtime mode
- **Family Link Integration**: Monitor children's device usage
- **Usage Insights**: Weekly reports on digital habits

## Time Management Benefits

By understanding and controlling screen time, users can reclaim hours previously lost to mindless scrolling and make more intentional choices about digital time allocation.

## Availability

Built into Android 9 and higher, available on all compatible devices.

## Pricing

Completely free, included with Android.

## Similar Tools

Apple's Screen Time offers similar functionality for iOS devices.
