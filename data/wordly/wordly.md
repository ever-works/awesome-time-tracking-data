## Overview

Wordly is a productivity app designed for writers that combines time tracking with word count monitoring to help writers track their productivity and improve their writing habits.

## Features

- **Timer Functionality**: Start a timer when you begin writing, stop when you finish, and enter the number of words written during the session
- **Words Per Hour Tracking**: Automatically calculates your writing speed and tracks words per hour for each writing session
- **Comprehensive Statistics**: View detailed statistics including words written today, this week, and this month, plus hours logged per time period
- **Visual Progress**: See visual graphs showing writing trends over time in a simple and attractive design
- **Data Export**: Export all data as a CSV file for viewing in Excel or other spreadsheet applications
- **Session Tracking**: Track multiple writing sessions throughout the day with individual time and word count records

## Platform

Available as an iOS app on the Apple App Store.

## Who It's For

Perfect for novelists, bloggers, content writers, journalists, and anyone who wants to track their writing productivity and establish consistent writing habits.
