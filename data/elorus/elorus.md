## Overview

Elorus is a comprehensive time tracking and invoicing platform that helps businesses track billable hours, manage projects, and create professional invoices. The software is accessible via web and browser extensions for seamless time tracking across different platforms.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track time with browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox that integrate with popular work tools
- **Invoicing**: Create and send professional invoices based on tracked time
- **Project Management**: Organize work by projects and clients
- **Expense Tracking**: Record and manage business expenses
- **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports on time usage and billable hours
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Work with clients globally using multiple currencies
- **Team Collaboration**: Manage team members and their time entries
- **Client Portal**: Allow clients to view invoices and project progress

## Browser Extensions

Elorus offers browser extensions for Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox that enable time tracking directly from the browser, with synchronized time entries across all devices.

## Pricing

Elorus offers tiered pricing plans based on the number of users and features required. A free trial is available to test the platform before committing to a paid plan.
