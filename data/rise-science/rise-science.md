## Overview

RISE is an energy and sleep tracker app created by sleep experts at Rise Science that helps unlock better sleep and enjoy more energy, with over 10 million users worldwide.

## Core Features

- **Sleep Debt Tracking**: The only sleep tracker that measures your sleep need and sleep debt, comparing how much sleep you need against how much you've been getting
- **Circadian Rhythm Predictions**: Predicts when you'll have the most and least energy each day based on your personal circadian rhythm
- **Energy Optimization**: Sends notifications for the best time to do demanding tasks, take a nap, have your last coffee, and when to start winding down for bed
- **Historical Data Analysis**: Pulls historical data from your phone on day one to calculate your sleep need, sleep debt, and optimal bedtime

## Science-Based Approach

Studies show the most impactful things you can do to improve energy levels and boost productivity are:
- Paying back your sleep debt
- Taking advantage of circadian rhythm peaks and dips in energy throughout the day

## Integrations

Automatic sleep tracking by integrating with:
