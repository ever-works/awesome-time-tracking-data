## Overview

Polaris PSA for Government Contracting is Deltek's specialized Professional Services Automation solution launched in February 2024, designed specifically to meet the unique requirements of government contractors.

## Government Compliance Features

### Regulatory Support
- Built-in FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulation) compliance
- DFARS (Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement) compliance support
- CAS (Cost Accounting Standards) accounting and timekeeping compliance
- Streamlines DCAA (Defense Contract Audit Agency) audit preparation
- CMMC Level 2/3 compliance capabilities

### Security Certification
- FedRAMP Moderate authorized platform
- DFARS 7012 compliant data security
- Meets federal government security standards for cloud services
- Secure handling of Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)

## Time Tracking Capabilities

### Automated Compliance
- Pre-configured rules for federal labor regulations
- Automatic enforcement of contractor-specific compliance requirements
- Built-in validation for time entry against contract requirements
- Audit trail documentation for all time entries

### Project Management
- Government contract-specific project structures
- Budget tracking against federal contracts
- Resource allocation with compliance constraints
- Milestone and deliverable tracking

## Integration

### ERP Connectivity
- Seamless integration with Deltek Costpoint
- SAP integration capabilities
- Support for government-focused accounting systems
- Automated data synchronization for billing and payroll
