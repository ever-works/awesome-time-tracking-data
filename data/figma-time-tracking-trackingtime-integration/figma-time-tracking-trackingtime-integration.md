## Figma Time Tracking (TrackingTime Integration)

### Overview
A browser extension–based integration that lets you track time on Figma work directly from the Figma interface using TrackingTime. It creates automatic timesheets and connects your design activity to TrackingTime’s analytics and project management features.

---

### Features
- **In-app time tracking for Figma**  
  - Start and stop timers from within Figma via the TrackingTime Button extension.  
  - Track time on design tasks without leaving your design environment.

- **Browser extension support**  
  - Works via the TrackingTime Button extension for:  
    - Chrome  
    - Firefox  
    - Safari  
    - Microsoft Edge

- **Automatic timesheets**  
  - Logged time is captured automatically into TrackingTime, generating timesheets for review, reporting, and billing.

- **Analytics and reporting**  
  - Connects Figma time entries to TrackingTime’s analytics to review:  
    - Time spent on design tasks and projects  
    - Overall workload and time allocation across tasks (e.g., wireframing, UI, revisions)

- **Time allocation visibility**  
  - See how time is divided across different design tasks or projects.  
  - Use tracked data to identify bottlenecks and adjust workflows.

- **Project planning support**  
  - Use historical tracked time to create more accurate estimates for future design work and project timelines.

- **Client and stakeholder communication support**  
  - Use detailed time records and reports from TrackingTime to communicate effort and progress to clients or stakeholders.

- **Team collaboration support**  
  - Shared time data helps teams understand how design work is distributed and coordinate more effectively.

---

### How It Works / Setup
1. **Sign up for TrackingTime**  
   Create a TrackingTime account to manage time, projects, and reports.
2. **Install the TrackingTime Button**  
   Add the browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
3. **Track time in Figma**  
   Log into Figma, open your design work, and start tracking time directly from Figma via the extension.
