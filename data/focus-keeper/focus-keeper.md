## Overview

Focus Keeper is the ultimate focus timer and Pomodoro timer designed to help you boost productivity and fight against procrastination. The app uses the classic Pomodoro method to help users manage time by breaking work into 25-minute intervals, aiding focus and reducing procrastination.

## How It Works

The app follows the classic Pomodoro method:
1. Choose a task and set the clock for 25 minutes
2. Work until the timer rings
3. Take a 5-minute break after each session to refresh your mind
4. After completing 4 focus sessions, take a longer break of 20-30 minutes to recharge
