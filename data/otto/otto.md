## Overview

Otto is a modern Pomodoro timer that goes beyond simple time tracking by actively helping users eliminate distractions. By combining the Pomodoro Technique with website blocking and detailed productivity analytics, Otto creates an environment conducive to deep focus and sustained attention.

## Features

### Pomodoro Timer
- **Customizable Intervals**: Adjust work and break durations to match your focus capacity
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Different timer settings for different types of work
- **Auto-Start Options**: Seamlessly transition between work and break periods
- **Visual and Audio Cues**: Clear notifications when sessions begin and end
- **Session History**: Track completed Pomodoros over time

### Website Blocking
- **Distraction Blocking**: Block distracting websites during work sessions
- **Custom Block Lists**: Create personalized lists of sites to block
- **Scheduled Blocking**: Different blocking rules for different times of day
- **Allowlist Support**: Permit specific sites needed for work
- **Browser Integration**: Works with major web browsers
- **App Blocking**: Block distracting applications (premium feature)

### Productivity Tracking
- **Detailed Analytics**: Visualize focus time and productivity patterns
- **Daily/Weekly Reports**: Track progress over time
- **Goal Setting**: Set and monitor focus time goals
- **Streak Tracking**: Maintain motivation with consecutive day achievements
- **Time Distribution**: See how time is allocated across tasks
- **Distraction Logging**: Identify when and why focus breaks occur

### ADHD-Friendly Features

Otto is particularly well-suited for neurodivergent users:
- **External Structure**: Timer and blocking provide external accountability
- **Clear Boundaries**: Defined work and break periods reduce decision fatigue
- **Visual Feedback**: Clear indication of current session status
- **Flexible Duration**: Adjust session lengths to match attention capacity
- **Break Enforcement**: Mandatory breaks prevent hyperfocus exhaustion
- **Progress Visualization**: Tangible evidence of work accomplished

## How Otto Works

1. **Set Your Focus Session**: Choose duration and task to work on
2. **Block Distractions**: Otto blocks predetermined distracting websites
3. **Work with Focus**: Concentrate on your task without digital interruptions
4. **Take Scheduled Breaks**: Otto enforces break times for rest and recovery
5. **Review Progress**: Check analytics to understand productivity patterns
6. **Adjust as Needed**: Refine blocking lists and session durations based on results

## Customization Options

- Work session duration (5-120 minutes)
- Short break duration (1-30 minutes)
- Long break duration (5-60 minutes)
- Number of Pomodoros before long break
- Website block lists
- Notification preferences
- Theme and appearance
- Strictness level (how hard it is to bypass blocks)
