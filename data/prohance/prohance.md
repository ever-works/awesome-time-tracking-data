## Overview

ProHance is a comprehensive workplace analytics and operations enablement platform used by over 400,000 professionals across 25 countries. The platform focuses on providing real-time insights into workforce productivity, enabling process optimization, and driving operational excellence across organizations.

## Features

### Workforce Intelligence
- **Automated Time Tracking**: Captures work hours and application usage automatically
- **Productivity Analytics**: Real-time dashboards showing individual and team productivity
- **Activity Classification**: Categorizes work into productive, neutral, and unproductive time
- **Application & Website Tracking**: Monitors which tools and sites employees use
- **Idle Time Detection**: Identifies periods of inactivity

### Process Optimization
- **Workflow Analysis**: Identifies bottlenecks and inefficiencies in business processes
- **Task Mining**: Discovers how work actually flows through the organization
- **Best Practice Identification**: Highlights high-performing processes and behaviors
- **Process Standardization**: Helps establish consistent workflows

### Operational Dashboards
- Real-time visibility into team performance
- Customizable KPI tracking
- Attendance and availability monitoring
- Project-level productivity insights
- Historical trend analysis

### Employee Engagement
- Self-service productivity insights for employees
- Goal-setting and tracking capabilities
- Performance feedback mechanisms
- Recognition and rewards integration

### Reporting & Analytics
- Comprehensive productivity reports
- Utilization analysis
- Cost-per-task calculations
- Custom report builder
- Scheduled automated reporting
- Data export for external analysis

## Privacy Considerations

ProHance provides detailed activity tracking at the individual level, which can be more intrusive than privacy-first alternatives like Worklytics. Organizations implementing ProHance should:

- Communicate clearly with employees about monitoring policies
- Ensure compliance with local labor laws and privacy regulations
- Use data ethically to support employees rather than for punitive measures
- Provide employees with access to their own productivity data
