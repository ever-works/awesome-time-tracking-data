## Overview

TaxDome is an all-in-one practice management platform built specifically for accounting and tax firms, combining time tracking, document management, client communication, and billing in a single solution.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track billable and non-billable time with timers or manual entry
- **Client Portal**: Secure portal for clients to upload documents and communicate
- **Workflow Automation**: Automate repetitive tasks and approval processes
- **Document Management**: Organize client documents with version control and e-signatures
- **CRM**: Manage client relationships, track leads, and monitor firm pipeline
- **Billing & Invoicing**: Generate invoices from tracked time with customizable templates
- **Team Collaboration**: Internal chat, task assignment, and team coordination
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for time tracking and client communication on the go
- **Integration**: Connects with QuickBooks, Xero, and other accounting software
- **Compliance**: Secure infrastructure with encryption and compliance certifications

## Pricing

Starts at $50 per month for up to 20 clients. Professional plan at $109 per month for up to 100 clients. Enterprise plans available for larger firms with custom pricing.
