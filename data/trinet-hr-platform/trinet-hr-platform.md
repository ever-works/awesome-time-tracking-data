## Overview

TriNet HR Platform (formerly Zenefits) is a comprehensive HR solution that helps manage workforce data, time tracking, attendance, time off, performance management, and more. As a PEO (Professional Employer Organization), TriNet provides full-service HR, payroll, benefits, and risk management for small to mid-sized businesses.

## Time & Attendance Features

### Time Tracking
- Web and mobile time entry
- Configurable time clock
- Geolocation verification
- Automated timesheet generation
- Manager approval workflows

### Attendance Management
- Real-time attendance monitoring
- Absence tracking
- Late arrival and early departure alerts
- Attendance policy enforcement
- Schedule adherence reporting

### Time Off Management
- PTO request and approval
- Accrual tracking
- Multiple time-off types (vacation, sick, personal)
- Balance visibility for employees
- Integration with payroll

## Comprehensive HR Platform

### Payroll Services
- Full-service payroll processing
- Tax filing and compliance
- Direct deposit
- Multi-state payroll
- Year-end tax forms

### Benefits Administration
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision)

- FSA/HSA administration
- Life and disability insurance

### Performance Management
- Goal setting and tracking
- Performance reviews
- Feedback and coaching tools

### Compliance & Risk
- HR compliance guidance
- Labor law updates
- Workers' compensation
- Unemployment claims management
- Employment practices liability insurance

## PEO Advantages

As a PEO, TriNet provides:
- Access to enterprise-level benefits at small business prices
- Shared liability for certain HR functions
- Dedicated HR support
- Compliance expertise
- Risk management services
