## Overview

Beebole is a cloud-based project time tracking and timesheet software that helps teams and companies efficiently measure time spent on projects and clients along with associated costs, billing, and margins. The platform has been serving thousands of companies worldwide since 2008.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking
- Track time spent on clients, projects, subprojects, and tasks
- Record both billable and non-billable hours
- Manage time off and leave requests
- Multiple entry methods for different work styles

### Budgeting and Cost Management
- Set costs and billing rates on people, clients, projects, subprojects, and tasks
- Track budgets in real-time with automated alerts
- Monitor budget status and prevent overruns
- Automated reminders for timesheet submissions

### Reporting and Analytics
- Transform project time tracking data into actionable insights
- KPI dashboards for real-time business metrics
- Track billable vs. non-billable hours
- Monitor time spent on projects and clients
- Analyze overtime patterns
- Calculate profit margins
- Report on budget status and costs

### Integrations
- Excel export capabilities
- Google Sheets integration
- Google Workspace compatibility
- Open API for custom integrations
