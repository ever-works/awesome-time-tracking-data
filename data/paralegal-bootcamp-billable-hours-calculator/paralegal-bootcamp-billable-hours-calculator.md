## Overview

A free billable hours calculator designed specifically for paralegals and lawyers to calculate time and revenue using standard legal billing practices.

## Features

- **Legal Billing Increments**: Supports standard 6-minute (0.1 hour) increments
- **Time Conversion**: Convert minutes to decimal hours for billing
- **Revenue Calculation**: Calculate total billable revenue based on hourly rates
- **Multiple Entries**: Calculate multiple time entries at once
- **Professional Format**: Designed for legal billing standards
- **Free Access**: No registration or payment required
- **Mobile Friendly**: Works on all devices

## Legal Billing Context

Most law firms bill in 6-minute increments (1/10th of an hour), making it essential for paralegals and lawyers to accurately convert minutes worked into billable decimals.

## Use Cases

- Paralegals tracking billable time
- Attorneys calculating daily billings
- Law students learning billing practices
- Legal departments managing time tracking

## Platform

Web-based calculator accessible from any browser.
