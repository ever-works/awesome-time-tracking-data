## Overview

Timesheet Reminder Automation eliminates manual chasing of timesheet submissions by systematically notifying employees when action is needed, dramatically improving compliance rates.

## Reminder Types

### Daily Prompts
- End-of-day: "Did you log your time today?"
- Morning: "You didn't log time yesterday"
- Lunchtime: "Remember to track this morning"

### Weekly Reminders
- Thursday: "Timesheet due tomorrow"
- Friday AM: "Submit by 5 PM today"
- Monday: "Last week's timesheet still pending"

### Approval Reminders
- Manager: "5 timesheets awaiting your approval"
- Escalation: CC to skip-level if not approved
- Employee: "Your timesheet was returned for correction"

## Delivery Channels

**Email**:
- Detailed information
- Links to timesheet
- Summary of missing entries

**Slack/Teams**:
- Real-time notifications
- Quick access buttons
- Channel or DM delivery

**SMS**:
- Critical deadlines
- High-priority items
- Weekend/after-hours

**In-App**:
- Push notifications
- Dashboard alerts
- Login prompts

## Configuration Options

### Timing
- Specific times (e.g., 4 PM daily)
- Relative (2 hours before deadline)
- Recurring schedules
- Time zone aware

### Escalation
- First reminder: Employee only
- Second reminder: CC manager
- Final reminder: Include HR/payroll

### Personalization
- Use employee name
- Specific missing dates
- Project/client details
- Actual hours needed

## Impact Metrics

Organizations report:
- 30-50% reduction in late timesheets
- 80%+ decrease in manager follow-up time
- 90%+ same-day completion rates
- Fewer payroll errors
