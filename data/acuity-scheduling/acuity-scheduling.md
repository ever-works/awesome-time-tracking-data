## Overview

Acuity Scheduling is a robust online appointment booking platform that simplifies scheduling and managing appointments for service-based businesses and professionals. Clients can view real-time availability and book appointments 24/7 without staff intervention.

## Time Management Benefits

### Eliminates Scheduling Back-and-Forth
- Clients see available times instantly
- Self-service booking reduces phone/email tag
- Automated confirmations eliminate confusion
- Saves hours per week on scheduling coordination

### Prevents Double-Booking
- Two-way calendar synchronization with Google Calendar and Outlook
- Real-time availability updates
- Blocks personal calendar events from business bookings
- Integrates with multiple team member calendars

### Optimizes Daily Schedule
- Customizable availability rules
- Buffer time between appointments
- Block personal time automatically
- Reduce scheduling gaps
- Set minimum advance booking times
