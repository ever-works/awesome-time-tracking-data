## Overview

VersionOne (now part of Planview AgilePlace) is an enterprise-grade agile planning and portfolio management platform designed to help large organizations scale agile methodologies across multiple teams with integrated time tracking and capacity planning.

## Features

- **Portfolio Management**: Manage multiple projects and programs with strategic portfolio planning
- **Sprint Planning**: Comprehensive sprint and release planning with capacity management
- **Time Tracking**: Track actual time spent on stories and tasks against estimates
- **Burndown & Velocity**: Monitor team velocity and sprint progress with burndown charts
- **Backlog Management**: Prioritize and groom product backlogs across teams
- **Dependency Management**: Visualize and manage dependencies across teams and projects
- **Custom Workflows**: Configure workflows to match organizational processes
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Executive dashboards, velocity reports, and predictive analytics
- **Integration**: Connects with development tools including Jira, GitHub, Jenkins, and more
- **Scaled Agile**: Supports SAFe, LeSS, and other scaling frameworks
