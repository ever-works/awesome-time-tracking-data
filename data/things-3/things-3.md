## Overview

Things 3 is a premium task management application exclusively for Apple devices, renowned for its elegant design, intuitive interface, and powerful organizational capabilities. It implements Getting Things Done (GTD) principles while maintaining simplicity and ease of use, making it popular among productivity enthusiasts and professionals in the Apple ecosystem.

## Core Features

### Task Organization
- Inbox for quick capture
- Today view for daily focus
- Upcoming view for planning ahead
- Anytime for unscheduled tasks
- Someday list for future possibilities

### Projects & Areas
- Projects with tasks and headings
- Areas of responsibility
- Project templates
- Hierarchical organization
- Checklists within tasks

### Scheduling
- Calendar integration
- Start dates
- Deadlines
- Repeating tasks
- Evening planning routine

## Pricing

One-time purchase per platform:
- Mac app
- iPhone/iPad app (universal)
- Apple Watch (included with iPhone)

No subscription, lifetime ownership.

## Limitations

- **Apple Only**: No Windows, Android, or web
- **No Collaboration**: Single-user only
- **Limited Sharing**: Can't assign tasks to others
- **No Calendar**: Just integration, not built-in calendar

## Ideal For

Apple ecosystem users, individuals preferring beautiful design, GTD practitioners, people wanting one-time purchase, professionals managing personal productivity, and users valuing privacy and offline capability.
