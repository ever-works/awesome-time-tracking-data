## Overview

Hours Tracker by Cribasoft is a comprehensive time tracking and earnings app for iPhone and Android that helps workers track their hours, breaks, pay, and location-based time entries.

## Key Features

### Quick and Easy Time Entry
- Record time using timers with support for breaks and pauses, including automatic breaks
- Pick any time to start, stop, break or pause the timer (7 minutes ago, 10 minutes from now)
- Manually enter time entries with minimal effort thanks to smart, adaptive defaults

### Pay Tracking
- Track your pay, including Tips, Mileage, and flexible ± time and earnings adjustments
- Automatic daily and weekly overtime earnings calculations
- Multiple pay rates and job support

### Location-Based Features
- Set job locations to get clock in and out reminders when you arrive or leave
- Fully automate your time tracking with geofencing

### Apple Watch Integration
- Control timers, dictate comments, and apply tags using your Apple Watch
- Interactive Live Activities - track time right from your Lock Screen or Dynamic Island (iOS 17+)

### Reporting and Data Management
- Built-in reports by Day, Week, and Month
- Support for most common pay period schedules
- Robust tagging and filtering
- Export data in JSON format using Apple Shortcuts app
- Export to spreadsheets for further analysis

### Comments and Notes
- Enter comments of any length with your time entries
- Optionally include comments in your exports

## Platform

iOS and Android
