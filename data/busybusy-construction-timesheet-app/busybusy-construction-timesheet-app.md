## busybusy Construction Timesheet App

### Description
Construction-focused timesheet software and mobile app that replaces paper timesheets, consolidates daily employee hours, job and cost code allocations, and approvals to streamline payroll and job costing.

### Features
- Digital timesheets to replace paper-based time cards
- Daily and weekly employee hours tracking
- Job and cost code allocations on time entries
- Mobile time tracking app for field crews
- iOS app (via Apple App Store)
- Android app (via Google Play Store)
- Employee scheduling with cost codes
- Time cards with digital signatures
- Upload photos to projects/jobs to document work
- Centralized time data to support payroll processing and labor cost analysis
