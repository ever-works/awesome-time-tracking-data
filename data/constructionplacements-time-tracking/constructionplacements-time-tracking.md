## Overview

ConstructionPlacements provides time tracking and workforce management solutions tailored for the construction industry. Their platform focuses on GPS-verified time tracking, job costing, and crew management to help contractors improve profitability.

## Features

- GPS geofencing for job sites
- Automated clock-in/out at project locations
- Mobile time tracking for field crews
- Job costing and labor allocation
- Equipment time tracking
- Crew scheduling and dispatch
- Digital timesheets and approvals
- Overtime tracking and alerts
- Integration with construction accounting software
- Photo and document capture
- Real-time project reporting
- Payroll export capabilities
