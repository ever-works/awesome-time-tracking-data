## Overview

Motion is an AI-powered task manager and calendar that automatically plans your day by intelligently scheduling tasks, managing priorities, and adapting to changes in real-time.

## Features

- **AI Task Scheduling** - Automatically schedules tasks on your calendar based on priority and deadlines
- **Dynamic Rescheduling** - Automatically adjusts your schedule when things change
- **Project Management** - Manage projects with automatic task prioritization
- **Calendar Integration** - Works with Google Calendar and Outlook
- **Meeting Booking** - Intelligent meeting scheduling that considers your tasks
- **Team Coordination** - Coordinate schedules across team members
- **Mobile Apps** - Full iOS and Android apps
- **Time Savings** - Claims to save users 30+ days per year through automation

## Use Cases

Ideal for executives, entrepreneurs, project managers, and busy professionals who struggle with constant task reprioritization and schedule changes.
