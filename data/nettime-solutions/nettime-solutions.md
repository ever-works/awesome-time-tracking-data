## Overview

nettime solutions' stratustime is a cloud-based time and attendance platform designed to help companies increase efficiency and make informed workplace decisions. It simplifies time tracking for both employees and administrators while integrating with hundreds of payroll systems, HR platforms, and devices.

## Features

- Employee clock-in/out functionality
- Time-off request management
- Labor cost dashboards and wage requirement information
- Compensatory time tracking
- Geofencing capabilities
- Integration with hundreds of payroll systems and HR platforms
- Manager dashboards for workforce insights
- Scalable for small businesses
