## Overview

Trevor AI combines task management with intelligent calendar scheduling. Unlike traditional todo apps, Trevor automatically finds time for your tasks within your existing calendar, creating a realistic daily plan that adapts as your day evolves.

## Key Features

- AI-powered auto-scheduling of tasks
- Integration with Google Calendar, Outlook
- Daily planning interface
- Drag-and-drop task rescheduling
- Time blocking automation
- Priority-based scheduling
- Deadline tracking
- Recurring task management

## How It Works

1. Add tasks with estimates and deadlines
2. Trevor AI finds optimal time slots in your calendar
3. Tasks automatically scheduled around meetings
4. As day changes, Trevor reschedules incomplete tasks
5. Learn your patterns and preferences over time
