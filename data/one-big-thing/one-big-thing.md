## Overview

One Big Thing is a simple daily todo app that helps you focus on what matters most each day. The app encourages you to identify one primary task and optionally add a few smaller supporting tasks.

## Philosophy

The app is built on the principle that focusing on one significant task per day is more effective than managing overwhelming to-do lists. It implements a variation of the "1-3-5 Rule" - tackle one big thing and potentially a few little tasks.

## How It Works

- **Daily Focus** - Every day, type in one big thing you want to accomplish
- **Supporting Tasks** - Add a few little tasks you might also want to get done
- **Check Throughout Day** - Use the app to stay focused on your goals
- **Fresh Start** - Next day provides a blank slate to refocus
- **Minimal Complexity** - Dead simple interface with "just a light sprinkling of delight"

## Key Features

- Single primary task focus
- Optional secondary tasks
- Daily reset for fresh perspective
- Minimalist design
- No overwhelming lists
- Clear prioritization

## Benefits

- **Reduced Overwhelm** - Eliminates the stress of endless to-do lists
- **Clear Priority** - Always know what's most important
- **Daily Achievement** - Focus on completing one meaningful task
- **Simplicity** - No complex features or learning curve
- **Mental Clarity** - Helps cut through noise and distractions

## Target Users

Ideal for:
- People overwhelmed by traditional to-do lists
- Those practicing single-tasking
- Individuals with ADHD or focus challenges
- Minimalists seeking simple productivity tools
- Anyone wanting clearer daily priorities
