## Overview

Fathom AI is an AI-powered meeting assistant designed for productivity, trusted by 500,000+ users worldwide. The platform automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams calls.

## Key Features

- Unlimited recording time with 95% transcription accuracy
- Instant AI summaries delivered within 30 seconds after meetings
- Automatic tracking of billable time and client requirements
- Professional meeting record maintenance
- CRM integration for sales and customer success teams
- Supports multiple meeting platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams)

## Benefits

- Stay present in meetings while ensuring nothing gets missed
- Receive accurate, actionable meeting summaries and action items moments after meetings
- Save significant time through automatic transcription and structured summaries
- Track billable time accurately without manual note-taking
- Document client requirements professionally

## Pricing

Fathom offers four pricing tiers including:
- Free plan: Unlimited recordings but only 5 AI summaries per month
- Premium features: $19/month

The free tier makes it accessible for individuals and small teams to get started with AI-powered meeting assistance.
