## Overview

Clio Duo is an AI-powered time tracking assistant built into Clio Manage, one of the leading legal practice management platforms. It analyzes lawyer activities including calls, emails, notes, and calendar events to suggest time entries, ensuring no billable time is missed.

## Features

- AI-suggested time entries based on activity
- Automatic detection of unlogged work
- Email and calendar integration
- Phone call time tracking
- Document activity monitoring
- One-click time entry approval
- Matter and client association
- Mobile time tracking
- Integration with Clio's full practice management suite
- Billing and invoicing capabilities
- Trust accounting compliance
- Detailed time reports and analytics
