## Overview

TCP Humanity Schedule (formerly Humanity) is an enterprise workforce management platform with advanced AI-powered scheduling capabilities. It's particularly strong for restaurants, retail, and other businesses with fluctuating staffing needs and complex compliance requirements.

## Features

- AI-powered demand forecasting and scheduling
- Automated compliance monitoring (labor laws, overtime, breaks)
- Real-time labor cost tracking and controls
- Employee self-service mobile app
- Shift bidding and trading
- Time and attendance tracking
- GPS-enabled mobile clock-in
- Integration with POS and payroll systems
- Automated schedule generation
- Manager override capabilities
- Detailed analytics and reporting
- Multi-location support
