## Overview

Time tracking dashboards transform raw time data into visual insights through charts, graphs, and key metrics. They enable quick comprehension of productivity patterns, project status, and team utilization.

## Common Visualizations

- Time distribution by project/client
- Daily/weekly/monthly trend charts
- Team capacity and utilization
- Budget vs. actual comparisons
- Productivity heatmaps
- Billable vs. non-billable time ratios

## Benefits

- Quick insights without manual analysis
- Pattern identification across time periods
- Real-time project health monitoring
- Team performance visibility
- Data-driven decision making

## Use Cases

- Project managers monitoring progress
- Agency leaders tracking utilization
- Individuals analyzing personal productivity
- Teams identifying bottlenecks
- Executives reviewing resource allocation
