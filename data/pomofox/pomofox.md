## Overview

PomoFox is a customizable and free online Pomodoro timer designed to boost focus, improve productivity, and manage time efficiently using the proven Pomodoro Technique. Available on both desktop and mobile browsers, PomoFox combines functionality with a delightful pixel-art aesthetic.

## Features

### Core Functionality
- **Customizable Pomodoro Timer**: Set your preferred work and break intervals
- **Integrated Lofi Music Player**: Background music to enhance concentration
- **Cross-Platform**: Works on desktop and mobile browsers
- **Light/Dark Mode**: Choose the theme that suits your environment

### Unique Design Elements
- Charming pixel-art backgrounds that create an engaging work environment
- Cute fox mascot that serves as your productivity companion
- Playful mini-game where the fox dodges attacking tomatoes during breaks
- Design-forward UX that emphasizes aesthetics as motivation

### Gamification
- Fun, gamified approach to the Pomodoro Technique
- Interactive break activities to keep sessions entertaining
- Visual rewards through pixel-art progression

## Philosophy

PomoFox demonstrates that productivity tools don't have to be boring—combining the proven Pomodoro Technique with playful design elements to make time management more enjoyable while maintaining effectiveness.
