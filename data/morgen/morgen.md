## Overview

Morgen is a comprehensive calendar and productivity platform designed to unify calendars, tasks, and time-blocking in one streamlined interface. It serves as a central hub for managing time across multiple calendar services and task management tools.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Calendar Integration**: Connects Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar, and more
- **Task Management**: Integrates with Todoist, Asana, ClickUp, and other task apps
- **Time Blocking**: Visual time-blocking interface
- **Smart Scheduling**: Intelligent meeting scheduling with availability detection
- **Time Zone Management**: Seamless handling of multiple time zones
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Power-user keyboard navigation
- **Team Scheduling**: Coordinate meetings across teams
- **Customizable Views**: Day, week, month, and agenda views

## Unified Time Management

Morgen consolidates:
- Multiple calendar accounts in one view
- Tasks from various productivity apps
- Scheduling links and availability
- Time zones for global teams
- Meeting templates and preferences

## Time Blocking Capabilities

- Drag-and-drop task scheduling
- Visual time allocation
- Buffer time configuration
- Focus time protection
- Calendar color coding

## Scheduling Features

- **Scheduling Links**: Share availability for meeting booking
- **Team Availability**: See when team members are free
- **Smart Suggestions**: AI-powered meeting time recommendations
- **Conflict Detection**: Warns of scheduling conflicts
- **Timezone Intelligence**: Automatic timezone conversion

## Platform Availability

- **Web**: Full-featured web application
- **Desktop**: Native apps for Windows, macOS, Linux
- **Mobile**: iOS and Android applications
- **Browser Extension**: Quick access from Chrome, Firefox, Edge

## Integration Ecosystem

Supported integrations include:
- **Calendars**: Google, Microsoft 365, Apple, CalDAV
- **Tasks**: Todoist, Asana, ClickUp, Microsoft To Do, Google Tasks
- **Video**: Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams
- **Other**: Slack, Notion, Linear

## Target Audience

- Professionals managing multiple calendar accounts
- Remote and distributed teams
- Consultants scheduling across time zones
- Knowledge workers combining calendar and task management
- Anyone seeking a unified time management solution

## Pricing

Offers free tier with basic features, plus premium subscription for advanced capabilities including AI features, unlimited integrations, and team collaboration tools.

## Competitive Position (2026)

Morgen positions itself as an alternative to tools like Sunsama, Akiflow, and Motion by offering unified calendar management with time-blocking capabilities, without requiring full commitment to a specific productivity methodology.

## Unique Selling Points

- Platform agnostic: Works with any calendar service
- Cross-platform: Available on all major operating systems
- Privacy-focused: Data encryption and local storage options
- Open integration: Connects with most popular productivity tools
- No vendor lock-in: Works alongside existing tools
