## Overview

Gauzy is an open-source business management platform that combines time tracking, project management, HR, and invoicing in one integrated solution. It's built for agencies, studios, and teams who want full control over their data and infrastructure.

## Features

- Comprehensive time tracking with screenshots
- Project and task management
- Employee and contractor management
- Invoicing and payments
- Expense tracking
- Goal and OKR tracking
- Employee scheduling
- Timesheets and approvals
- Integration with productivity tools
- Self-hosted or cloud deployment
- Mobile apps for time tracking
- Extensive API for customization
- Open-source under AGPL license
