## Overview

Welltory is an all-in-one health and productivity app that uses heart rate variability (HRV) analysis to help users understand their stress levels, energy, and overall wellness, with integrated time tracking for activities and habits.

## Features

- **HRV Measurement**: Measure heart rate variability using your smartphone camera or connected wearables
- **Stress & Energy Tracking**: Daily tracking of stress levels and energy reserves
- **Productivity Analysis**: Correlate productivity with health metrics and sleep quality
- **Wearable Integration**: Syncs with Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, Oura Ring, and 1000+ other apps
- **Sleep Analysis**: Track sleep quality and duration from multiple sources
- **Activity Tracking**: Monitor physical activity, steps, and workouts
- **Blood Pressure**: Track and analyze blood pressure trends
- **Personalized Insights**: AI-powered recommendations based on your unique patterns
- **Habit Tracking**: Build healthy habits and track their impact on wellness
- **Data Aggregation**: Centralize health data from all your devices and apps
