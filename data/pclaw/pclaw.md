## Overview

PCLaw is a long-standing practice management, billing and accounting solution for small and midsize law firms. It includes lightweight practice management features (clients, cases, and calendars) coupled with robust time tracking, billing, and accounting capabilities designed specifically for legal practices.

## Time Tracking Features

### Legal-Specific Time Capture
- Activity code-based time entry
- Matter-based time allocation
- Billable vs. non-billable time tracking
- Timer functionality for active work
- Quick entry for past time
- Detailed time slip narratives

### Billing Integration
- Seamless time-to-bill conversion
- Customizable billing rates (hourly, flat fee, contingency)
- WIP (Work in Progress) reporting
- Pre-billing review and adjustments
- Client-specific billing preferences
- Electronic billing (e-billing) support

## Practice Management Components

### Client & Matter Management
- Client intake and conflict checking
- Matter tracking and organization
- Contact management
- Document management integration
- Case notes and communication logs

### Calendar & Deadlines
- Court date tracking
- Statute of limitations monitoring
- Task management
- Reminder system
- Staff scheduling

## Robust Accounting

PCLaw distinguishes itself with comprehensive legal accounting:
- **Trust Accounting**: IOLTA compliance and three-way reconciliation
- **Operating Accounts**: General ledger and financial reporting
- **Accounts Receivable**: Aging reports and payment tracking
- **Expense Tracking**: Billable expense management
- **Financial Reports**: Profit & loss, balance sheets, custom reports
