## Overview

BetterFlow is an innovative AI-powered time tracking platform that doesn't just log time automatically—it verifies time entries against actual work output. This unique approach eliminates timesheet fraud while respecting employee privacy and autonomy.

## Features

- AI verification of time entries against work output
- Fraud detection and prevention
- Automatic work activity analysis
- Productivity insights without surveillance
- Integration with project management tools
- Billable vs non-billable time classification
- Client and project reporting
- Team productivity analytics
- Privacy-respecting monitoring
- Work pattern recognition
- Automated time entry suggestions
- Compliance reporting
