## Overview

TimeWellScheduled offers free employee scheduling, time clock software, and payroll integration specifically designed for retail, hotels, and restaurants.

## Key Features

### Employee Scheduling

- Create and publish employee schedules
- Handle shift assignments
- Manage employee availability
- Schedule templates for recurring shifts
- Multi-location support

### Time Clock

- Digital time clock for employees
- Punch in/out tracking
- Break management
- Overtime monitoring
- Attendance reporting

### Payroll Integration

- Seamless connection to payroll systems
- Automated timesheet export
- Accurate hour calculations
- Reduced manual data entry
- Error-free payroll processing
