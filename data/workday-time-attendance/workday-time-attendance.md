## Overview

Workday Time & Attendance is part of Workday's comprehensive cloud-based Human Capital Management (HCM) suite, designed to streamline time tracking, attendance management, and workforce analytics for enterprise organizations operating globally.

## Company Background

Workday is a leading enterprise cloud provider centered on Human Capital Management (HCM) and financial applications. The platform emphasizes core HR workflows, talent management, and advanced people analytics, providing a unified system for managing the entire employee lifecycle.

## Key Features

- **Mobile Time Tracking**: Workers can check in and out from mobile devices as they enter and exit job sites
- **Automated Reminders**: Automatic notifications prompt employees to check in/out based on location
- **Geofencing**: Location-based tracking to verify employee presence at work sites
- **Manager Oversight**: Managers don't need to spend time manually monitoring mobile employees
- **Integrated Timesheets**: Time data flows directly into payroll and project costing
- **Global Compliance**: Built-in support for international labor laws and regulations
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Real-time workforce analytics and reporting

## Strengths

- **Unified HCM Platform**: Seamless integration with HR, payroll, talent, and finance modules
- **Advanced Analytics**: Superior people analytics with HR-finance convergence
- **Global Scale**: Designed for multi-national organizations with complex requirements
- **Employee Experience**: Modern, intuitive interface across all devices
- **Strategic Workforce Planning**: Advanced capabilities for workforce modeling and planning
