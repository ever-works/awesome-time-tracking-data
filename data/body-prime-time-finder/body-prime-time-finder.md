## Overview

Body Prime Time Finder is a web-based application that enables users to systematically track their energy, motivation, and focus levels throughout the day to discover their biological prime time for maximum productivity.

## Core Features

### Energy Tracking

The tool allows users to log three key metrics at regular intervals:

- **Energy Levels**: Physical vitality and alertness
- **Motivation**: Drive and willingness to work
- **Focus**: Ability to concentrate and maintain attention

### Visual Analytics

Results are presented through simple, easy-to-understand graphs that reveal patterns in:

- Daily energy fluctuations
- Peak productivity windows
- Low-energy periods requiring breaks or lighter tasks
- Trends over multiple days and weeks

## How to Use

1. **Regular Logging**: Input your current state at consistent intervals (typically hourly)
2. **Track Multiple Days**: Continue logging for at least 1-2 weeks for reliable patterns
3. **Review Graphs**: Analyze the visual data to identify your prime time windows
4. **Apply Insights**: Schedule demanding work during identified peak periods
5. **Adjust Schedule**: Align your calendar with natural energy rhythms

## Best Practices

### Tracking Duration

Experts recommend tracking for at least three weeks to:

- Establish consistent patterns
- Account for day-to-day variations
- Identify weekly trends
- Build reliable data for decision-making

### Optimal Usage

- Set hourly reminders to ensure consistent logging
- Track during your entire working window
- Be honest about your actual state, not ideal state
- Avoid making major changes to schedule during tracking period
- Review data weekly to spot emerging patterns

## Use Cases

- **Students**: Identify best times for studying complex material
- **Creatives**: Schedule creative work during peak inspiration periods

Based on research into ultradian rhythms and circadian cycles that affect cognitive performance, alertness, and productivity throughout the day. The tool helps make these natural cycles visible and actionable.
