## Overview

Flown is a platform focused on online body doubling with daily focus sessions featuring built-in accountability, expert facilitation and friendly community. It's a virtual coworking platform built to help you stay accountable, focused, and energized throughout your workday.

## Deep Work Sessions

Flown provides virtual coworking sessions and curates spaces ideal for deep work. They offer facilitated focus sessions, peer-to-peer virtual co-working, 24/7 Drop-ins and on-demand "work with me" videos.

## How It Works

The sessions follow a structured format:

- Users share work goals for the session for extra accountability
- Work silently alongside other people in distraction-free focus
- Share how they did and celebrate success at the end

When you see others focusing in virtual co-working sessions you'll find it easier to do the same through 'body doubling' - a technique to overcome procrastination, distractions, and attention deficits.

## Research & Results

A 2023 study of 101 Flown members found that:

- 95% reported above average improvement in focus
- 94% reported improvement in productivity
- 87% reported above average improvement in stress level

Flown's users have tripled every year since launching during the Covid-19 pandemic, with average focus sessions per user rising from 4.3 to 6 per week.
