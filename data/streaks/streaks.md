## Overview

Streaks is a sleek, user-friendly iOS app designed to help users build and maintain good habits by encouraging daily consistency. Track up to 24 tasks with visual streak indicators that motivate continued progress.

## Features

- **Up to 24 Habit Tracking**: Monitor multiple habits simultaneously with visual streak counters
- **Apple Health Integration**: Automatically log health-related habits like steps, workouts, mindfulness sessions, and sleep
- **Native iOS Design**: Beautifully designed interface that feels native to Apple devices
- **Widget Support**: Quick access to habit tracking from your home screen
- **Apple Watch Support**: Track and complete habits directly from your wrist
- **Customizable Themes**: Personalize the app appearance to match your style
- **Streak Statistics**: View detailed statistics and charts showing your progress over time

## Benefits

- One-time purchase model (no subscription required)
- Seamless integration with Apple ecosystem
- Automatic tracking reduces manual data entry
- Visual streak motivators encourage consistency
- Users report increased productivity and habit adherence
