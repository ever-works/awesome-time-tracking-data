## Overview

Timeular has been rebranded as EARLY in 2025, though you may still see the Timeular name online. The Tracker is an eight-sided die which lets you track all your activities by flipping it so your current activity is facing up.

## How It Works

### Setup
1. **Connect the Tracker** to your notebook or phone via Bluetooth LE
2. **Assign activities** to the different sides (can be changed anytime)
3. **Customize sides** using sticker sheets or dry-erase pencil
4. **Start tracking** by flipping to the side representing your current activity

### Tracking Process
- Place the Tracker with your current activity facing up
- Time tracking starts automatically
- Flip to another side to switch activities
- Place in resting dock to pause tracking

## Features

### Physical Device
- **Eight-sided die** for up to 8 different activities
- **Bluetooth connectivity** to desktop and mobile devices
- **Joker side**: Keep one side empty to use as a wildcard for additional tasks
- **Resting dock** for pausing time tracking
- **Durable design** for frequent flipping

### Digital Platform
- **EARLY app** syncs with the physical Tracker
- **Cross-platform**: Works on desktop and mobile
- **Activity management**: Easily reassign sides as needed
- **Reports and analytics**: View tracked time data
- **Integrations**: Connect with other productivity tools

## Subscription Requirement

To use the Tracker, you need an EARLY subscription. All new users get 2 months free subscription when connecting a new Tracker.

## Comparison to Similar Products

### vs. TIMEFLIP2
- **EARLY**: 8-sided dice
- **TIMEFLIP2**: 12-sided dice (4 more activities)

### vs. timeBuzzer
- **EARLY**: Physical dice with flipping
- **timeBuzzer**: Physical button with touch, rotate, press

### vs. Software Trackers
- **Physical interaction**: Tangible reminder to track time
- **No screen required**: Track without opening apps
- **Visual cue**: See current activity at a glance

## Best For

- People who prefer physical tools over digital
- Freelancers tracking multiple client projects
- Anyone who forgets to start/stop digital timers
- Users wanting a constant visual reminder
- Teams seeking tactile time tracking solution
