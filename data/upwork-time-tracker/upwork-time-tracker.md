## Overview

Upwork's Time Tracker is a desktop application that helps freelancers efficiently log hours and maintain transparency with clients on the Upwork platform, providing payment protection for hourly contracts.

## Features

- Easy start/stop time tracking
- Automatic screenshot capture (6 per hour)
- Activity level monitoring
- Detailed work memos for each time segment
- Project and contract labeling
- Editable time entries
- Weekly time limit management
- Offline time tracking with later sync
- Desktop app for Windows, Mac, and Linux
- Integration with Upwork payment protection

## How It Works

To use the Upwork time tracker, freelancers need to install the desktop app on their computer. The tracker captures screenshots and monitors activity levels to provide clients with transparency into work being performed.

## Payment Protection

When using Upwork's tracker for hourly contracts:
- Tracked hours with screenshots are protected by Upwork's payment guarantee
- Manual time entries without screenshots are not protected
- Clients can see screenshots and activity levels
- Disputes are resolved based on tracked evidence

## Limitations and Considerations

**Service Fees:**
Upwork charges freelancers up to 15% in service fees, which can be costly for growing teams or high-earning freelancers.

**Platform Lock-In:**
The tracker limits flexibility by tying time tracking exclusively to Upwork's marketplace. Freelancers cannot use it for non-Upwork clients.

**Privacy Considerations:**
Automatic screenshots may raise privacy concerns for some freelancers, though they can be reviewed and deleted before submission.

## Best Practices

1. Add detailed memos to explain work performed
2. Review screenshots before submission
3. Track time in real-time rather than adding manually
4. Use project labels to organize different contracts
5. Monitor weekly limits to avoid exceeding client budgets
6. Keep the app updated to latest version

## Alternatives

Many freelancers seek alternatives that offer:
- Lower service fees
- Cross-platform client management
- More flexible tracking options
- Better privacy controls

Popular alternatives include Hubstaff, Timely, and Toggl, though these don't provide Upwork's payment protection features.

## Pricing

The Upwork Time Tracker app itself is free to use. Upwork's service fees (10-20% depending on lifetime billings with a client) apply to all work performed on the platform.
