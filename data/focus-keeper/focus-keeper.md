## Overview

Focus Keeper is a Pomodoro timer app that combines time tracking with habit tracking to help users maintain productivity and understand their focus patterns.

## Features

- Classic Pomodoro timer (25-5-15 minute intervals)
- Customizable work and break durations
- Timer history tracking
- Productivity statistics
- Task integration
- Daily and weekly reports
- Habit tracking
- Goal setting
- Minimal, distraction-free interface

## Benefits

- Builds sustained focus
- Tracks productivity patterns
- Maintains work-life balance through breaks
- Visual progress tracking
- Helps build consistent work habits

## Pricing

Free version available with basic features. Premium version unlocks advanced tracking and customization.
