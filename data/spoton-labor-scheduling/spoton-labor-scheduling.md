## Overview

SpotOn Labor & Scheduling is a comprehensive workforce management solution designed specifically for restaurants and integrated seamlessly with the SpotOn point-of-sale system. It helps restaurants optimize labor costs while maintaining service quality.

## Features

### Scheduling
- **Drag-and-Drop Scheduler** - Intuitive schedule building interface
- **Demand-Based Scheduling** - Use sales data to predict staffing needs
- **POS Integration** - Access sales and labor data for informed scheduling
- **Shift Templates** - Save and reuse common shift patterns
- **Employee Availability** - Track when staff can and can't work
- **Shift Trading** - Allow employees to swap shifts with approval
- **Mobile Access** - Staff view schedules on their phones

### Labor Management
- **Labor Cost Tracking** - Real-time visibility into labor costs vs. sales
- **Sales-to-Labor Ratio** - Monitor and optimize labor percentage
- **Overtime Alerts** - Get warned before scheduling overtime
- **Budget Forecasting** - Project labor costs for future schedules
- **Multi-Location** - Manage multiple restaurant locations

### Time & Attendance
- **POS Clock-In** - Employees clock in directly from POS terminal
- **Break Tracking** - Monitor and enforce required breaks
- **Timesheet Approval** - Digital approval workflow
- **Payroll Export** - Export hours to payroll systems

### Reporting
- **Real-Time Reports** - Live labor cost and sales data
- **Historical Analysis** - Compare labor trends over time
- **Performance Metrics** - Track labor efficiency by shift, day, location
