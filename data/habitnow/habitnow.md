## Overview

HabitNow is a highly-rated habit tracking app exclusively for Android devices. Users overwhelmingly praise its clean and intuitive interface and comprehensive feature set, with extensive customization of habits and to-dos.

## Key Features

- **Flexible Tracking Options**: Track daily, weekly, and monthly habits, as well as add recurring tasks (which aren't tracked) and single tasks (one-off to-dos)
- **Comprehensive Statistics**: Offers all the stats you could want, including current streak, completion rate, and completions over the last week
- **Goal Setting**: Customize daily, weekly, and monthly goals for each habit
- **Categories and Lists**: Organize habits using categories and lists for better management
- **Clean Interface**: Users consistently praise the app's intuitive and easy-to-navigate design
- **Detailed Tracking**: Monitor progress with visual indicators and detailed completion data
- **Reminders**: Set custom reminders to help maintain consistency
- **Widgets**: Access your habits directly from your Android home screen

## Platform Availability

- **Android Only**: Designed specifically for Android devices

## Pricing Model

- **Free Version**: Track up to 7 habits with essential features
- **Premium Upgrade**: $11.99 one-time purchase (no subscription required)

Many users appreciate that the one-time premium upgrade unlocks all features without requiring an ongoing subscription, which is seen as affordable and fair compared to subscription-based alternatives.

## User Reception

HabitNow receives consistent praise for its balance of comprehensive features and user-friendly design, making it a top choice among Android users who want powerful habit tracking without complexity.
