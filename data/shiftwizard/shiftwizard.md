## Overview

ShiftWizard is a nurse scheduling platform designed specifically for healthcare facilities. Built by HealthStream, it focuses on employee engagement and mobile accessibility, catering to the complex scheduling needs of nursing staff with varying shift lengths, specialty requirements, and certification tracking.

## Features

- Mobile-first design for on-the-go access
- Self-scheduling capabilities for nurses
- Shift trading and swapping
- Open shift management
- Certification and credential tracking
- Staff availability management
- Automated scheduling based on rules
- Time-off request management
- Shift reminder notifications
- Integration with payroll systems
- Compliance tracking for labor laws
- Multi-location and multi-department support
