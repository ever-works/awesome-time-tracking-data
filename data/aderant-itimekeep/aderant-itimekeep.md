## Overview

Aderant iTimekeep is the #1 mobile time tracking software for attorneys that increases revenue by producing the most accurate records, minimizing time leakage, and creating shorter billing cycles. Used by over 200,000 legal professionals worldwide.

## Features

- **Mobile-First Design**: Capture time anytime, anywhere on iOS and Android
- **Voice-to-Text**: Record time entries using voice commands
- **Smart Templates**: Pre-built entry templates for common legal tasks
- **Real-Time Sync**: Automatically syncs with practice management systems
- **Timer Function**: Running timers for active work sessions
- **Quick Entry**: Fast entry of multiple time entries
- **Expense Tracking**: Capture billable expenses on the go
- **ABA Task Codes**: Built-in American Bar Association task codes
- **Client/Matter Lookup**: Easy search and selection of matters
- **Narrative Editing**: AI-assisted narrative generation and editing

## Pricing

Pricing available through Aderant sales. Typically licensed per user with annual or multi-year agreements. Integration with Aderant Expert or other practice management systems.
