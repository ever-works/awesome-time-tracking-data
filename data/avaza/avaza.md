## Overview

Avaza is an integrated platform that combines time tracking, project management, invoicing, and expense tracking. It's designed for freelancers, agencies, and professional services teams who need an all-in-one solution.

## Features

- Time tracking with timers and manual entry
- Project management with tasks and milestones
- Invoice generation from tracked time
- Expense tracking and receipt capture
- Quote and estimate creation
- Team collaboration tools
- Resource scheduling
- Reporting and analytics
- Client portal access
- Integration with accounting software
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Multiple currency support
- Customizable permissions

## Pricing

Free plan available for up to 5 users. Paid plans start at competitive rates with additional features and unlimited users.
