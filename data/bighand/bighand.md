## Overview

BigHand is a comprehensive legal productivity platform that includes SmartTime, an AI-powered time capture and entry solution. It helps law firms maximize billable hours by automatically capturing time spent on client work and generating accurate timesheets.

## Features

- SmartTime AI-powered automatic time capture
- Background activity tracking across applications
- Email and document time tracking
- Time-gap analysis to identify missing billable hours
- AI-generated timesheet suggestions
- Voice-to-text time entry
- Integration with leading legal billing systems (Elite, Aderant, etc.)
- Mobile time capture app
- Matter and client code lookup
- Narrative generation for time entries
- Approval workflows
- Analytics and reporting on time capture efficiency
