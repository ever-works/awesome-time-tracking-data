## Overview

Everhour is a time tracking solution that integrates directly into popular project management tools, allowing teams to track time without leaving their existing workflows. Instead of being a standalone application, Everhour adds time tracking functionality to the tools teams already use daily, reducing friction and improving adoption.

## Native Integrations

### Supported Platforms
- **Asana**: Time tracking in task view
- **Trello**: Timer on cards
- **Jira**: Time logging in issues
- **Basecamp**: Integrated time entry
- **Monday.com**: Native time tracking
- **ClickUp**: Seamless integration
- **GitHub**: Track time on issues
- **Notion**: Time tracking in databases

### How It Works
- Browser extension adds timer to each platform
- Time automatically associated with tasks/issues
- No need to switch applications
- Data syncs between platform and Everhour
- Unified reporting across all platforms

## Key Features

### Time Tracking
- One-click timer start in integrated tools
- Manual time entry
- Time estimates vs actuals
- Billable vs non-billable
- Approval workflows

### Project Management
- Project budgets (hours and money)
- Time budget alerts
- Resource planning
- Capacity management

### Reporting
- Time by project, person, client
- Billable hours summary
- Budget vs actual reports

### Invoicing

- Expense inclusion

- Payment tracking

## Pricing

Per-user per-month subscription with free tier for small teams. Significantly cheaper than enterprise project management platforms with built-in time tracking.

## Ideal For

Teams already using Asana, Trello, Jira, or similar tools, agencies needing time tracking across multiple PM platforms, consultants billing by the hour, and remote teams wanting seamless time capture.
