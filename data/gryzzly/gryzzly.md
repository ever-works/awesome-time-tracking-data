## Overview

Gryzzly is a time tracking chatbot that integrates seamlessly with Slack and Microsoft Teams, allowing teams to track time directly within their communication platforms. The bot eliminates context switching by bringing time tracking into the tools teams already use for daily collaboration.

## Features

- **Slack Integration**: Track time directly in Slack channels and DMs
- **Microsoft Teams Integration**: Full support for Teams workspaces
- **Conversational Interface**: Log time using natural language commands
- **Automated Reminders**: Bot pings team members to record time entries
- **Quick Time Entry**: Log time in seconds without switching apps
- **Project Assignment**: Assign time to specific projects and tasks
- **Reporting**: Generate time reports from logged entries
- **Team Management**: Manage team time tracking from admin dashboard
- **Export Capabilities**: Export timesheet data for billing and payroll
- **Multi-Platform**: Works across Slack and Teams simultaneously

## How It Works

Gryzzly works through simple chat commands:
1. The bot pings team members in Slack or Teams
2. Users respond with time entries using conversational commands
3. Time is logged and categorized automatically
4. Managers can view reports and timesheets in real-time

## Benefits

- **No Context Switching**: Track time where teams already communicate
- **Increased Compliance**: Automated reminders improve timesheet completion rates
- **Reduced Friction**: Conversational interface is faster than traditional time tracking
- **Better Adoption**: Teams are more likely to use tools they're already in
- **Real-Time Data**: Immediate visibility into team time allocation

## Use Cases

- Remote teams using Slack or Teams for daily communication
- Agencies tracking billable hours across multiple clients
- Consultants needing quick, frictionless time entry
- Project-based teams requiring time tracking without disrupting workflow
