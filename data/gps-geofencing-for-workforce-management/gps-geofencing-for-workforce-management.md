## Overview

GPS geofencing creates virtual boundaries using GPS technology. Time tracking automatically starts/stops when employees cross these boundaries.

## Platforms
- Workyard: GPS updates every 5 minutes
- Instawork: Geofencing jobsites for temp workers
- Timeero: GPS verification time clock
- ClockShark: Construction geofencing

## Benefits
- Prevent time theft
- Eliminate buddy punching
- Automatic tracking
- Real-time visibility

## Pricing
$3-10 per user per month as part of workforce management platforms.
