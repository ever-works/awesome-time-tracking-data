## Overview

Relax Eyes-Pro is an iOS application specifically designed to prevent eye strain and fatigue through the scientifically-backed 20-20-20 rule. The app helps users maintain healthy vision habits during extended periods of screen use.

## The 20-20-20 Rule

The app is based on the 20-20-20 principle developed by eye care professionals:
- Every **20 minutes** of screen time
- Look at something **20 feet** away
- For at least **20 seconds**

This simple practice gives eye muscles a chance to relax and refocus, reducing the cumulative strain from sustained near-focus work.

## Health Benefits

**Eye Strain Reduction:**
Regular breaks prevent eye muscle fatigue, dryness, and the blurred vision that comes from extended screen focus.

**Headache Prevention:**
Eye strain is a common cause of tension headaches. Regular eye breaks can significantly reduce headache frequency.

**Long-term Vision Health:**
Consistent use of the 20-20-20 rule may help protect vision health over time, particularly for those who spend many hours daily on screens.
