## Overview

Timing is automatic time tracking software for Mac that runs quietly in the background, recording application and web usage automatically without manual timers. The app prioritizes privacy while providing comprehensive activity tracking.

## Automatic Tracking

Timing automatically tracks:
- Applications used
- Window titles
- Documents worked on
- Websites visited
- Time spent on each activity

All tracking happens in the background with no manual timer starting/stopping required.

## Privacy Features

### Local Storage
All tracked data stored locally on your Mac and never leaves unless you opt into sync. This local-first approach is a core privacy feature.

### Privacy Controls
- Private or incognito browser tabs automatically have information discarded
- Add any activities to exclusion list to prevent tracking
- Full control over what gets monitored

### Team Privacy
For team usage, admins only see aggregate times associated with team projects - no personal times or private details shared.

## Platform

### Mac-Only
Timing supports automatic time tracking on macOS exclusively:
- Background tracking of apps, websites, and documents
- Idle time detection
- Ability to assign time to projects

### Not Available on App Store
Timing isn't available on the Mac App Store since Apple doesn't allow apps with such comprehensive monitoring due to strict privacy policies.
