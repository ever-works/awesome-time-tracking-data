## Overview

Habits are Reclaim.ai's innovative approach to creating routines on your calendar for work and personal priorities. Unlike traditional recurring calendar events, Habits flex around your schedule and automatically adapt as things come up, giving you the time you need without being overly rigid.

## How Habits Work

Habits use smart AI scheduling to make intelligent decisions based on:
- Priority levels you assign
- Scheduling rules and preferences
- Availability around meetings and commitments
- Upcoming deadlines and time constraints

The system automatically finds optimal time slots for your habits and adjusts them dynamically as your calendar changes.

## Habits vs Tasks vs Focus Time

### Habits
Use Habits for recurring routines and activities you want to do regularly (daily, weekly, or monthly). Examples:
- Morning exercise or meditation
- Weekly planning sessions
- Regular learning or skill development
- Lunch breaks
- End-of-day reviews

### Tasks
Use Tasks when you have specific work to complete with a due date. Tasks auto-schedule time to complete work before deadlines, around existing priorities.

### Focus Time
Focus Time lets you set a weekly goal for heads-down work and uses AI to defend the time needed, automatically adjusting as plans change.

## Key Benefits

### Flexibility
Unlike rigid recurring events, Habits automatically reschedule when conflicts arise, ensuring you still get time for important routines even when meetings or urgent tasks appear.

### Consistency Without Rigidity
Maintain important habits without the stress of fixed time slots. The system ensures the habit happens while adapting to your real schedule.

### Priority-Based Scheduling
Habits respect priority hierarchies. Higher-priority Habits get better time slots, while lower-priority ones adapt more flexibly.

### Automatic Rescheduling
When meetings or tasks conflict with a Habit, Reclaim.ai automatically finds an alternative time slot rather than leaving a gap in your routine.

## Best Practices

### Start Small
Begin with 2-3 critical habits rather than trying to schedule everything at once.

### Set Realistic Priorities
Not everything can be high priority. Use priority levels strategically to reflect actual importance.

### Allow Flexibility
Give the AI room to work by providing acceptable time windows rather than demanding specific slots.

### Review Regularly
Periodically assess which habits are consistently scheduling and which are getting bumped, adjusting priorities as needed.

### Combine with Tasks
Use Habits for recurring activities and Tasks for one-time deliverables for optimal calendar management.

## Key Advantage

The Habits feature solves a fundamental calendar problem: how to maintain important routines without rigid recurring events that create conflicts and scheduling chaos. By making routine time blocks intelligent and adaptive, Habits help you maintain consistency in what matters most while staying flexible for the unexpected.
