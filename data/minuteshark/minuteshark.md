## Overview

MinuteShark is a time tracking application designed specifically for freelancers, solo professionals, and consultants who need accurate billing and straightforward time management without the complexity of traditional project management tools.

## Key Features

**Time Tracking & Management:**
- Start/stop timers with smart notifications for long-running timers
- Manual time entry for retroactive logging
- Lock tasks when work is complete to prevent accidental time additions
- Live syncing across all devices (mobile and desktop)

**Project Organization:**
- Flexible project hierarchy that adapts to your workflow
- Rich text editor for detailed project descriptions
- Break down complex work into tasks and subtasks
- Define and organize clients, projects, and tasks systematically

**Billing Features:**
- Configure billable rates per project or task
- Time rounding logic to ensure accurate billing
- Track every minute precisely for revenue optimization

**Reporting:**
- Clear, easy-to-read reports showing time distribution across projects and teams
- Dashboard with productivity widgets
- Track daily progress and manage to-do lists

**Mobile Access:**
- iOS and Android apps for tracking on the go
- Perfect for field work and client meetings
- Full dashboard management from mobile devices

**Team Collaboration:**
- Invite team members for collaborative tracking
- Monitor team productivity and project status

## Target Audience

MinuteShark is built for professionals who bill by the hour and need a clean, focused time tracking solution that emphasizes accuracy and simplicity over complex project management features.
