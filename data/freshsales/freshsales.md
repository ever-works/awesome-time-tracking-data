## Overview

Freshsales is a CRM platform from Freshworks that combines customer relationship management with time and activity tracking capabilities. It helps sales teams manage their time effectively while tracking customer interactions and deal progress.

## Time Tracking Features

### Activity Management
- Create activities with specific deadlines
- Choose activity types (follow-ups, call reminders, meetings)
- Set priorities and assign owners
- Monitor tasks from linked contact or deal pages

### Activities Dashboard
- Centralized view of all activities and their status
- Time-based filtering and sorting
- Activity completion tracking
- Integration with contact and deal records

### Mobile Time Tracking
- Time tracking for support tickets via mobile app
- Field service task tracking on the go
- Mobile check-in/check-out capabilities
- Real-time sync across devices

## Integration with CRM Workflow

Freshsales embeds time tracking directly into the CRM workflow, allowing sales professionals to:
- Track time spent on specific deals and accounts
- Log customer interactions with timestamps
- Monitor team productivity and activity levels
- Generate reports on time allocation across the sales pipeline

## 2026 Enhancements

According to a 2026 in-depth review, Freshsales continues to refine its time and activity management features, though it doesn't have a dedicated standalone tab for activity management, instead integrating these features throughout the platform.

## Benefits

- **Sales Efficiency**: Understand where sales time is being spent
- **Pipeline Management**: Track time investment per deal stage
- **Team Productivity**: Monitor activity levels across the sales team
- **Customer Engagement**: Log and analyze customer interaction patterns
- **Forecasting**: Use historical time data to predict deal timelines

## Target Users

Sales teams, account managers, and customer-facing professionals who need to combine CRM functionality with time and activity tracking to optimize their sales processes.
