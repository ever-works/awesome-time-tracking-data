## The 100ms Rule

Vimcal's 100ms Rule is a core design principle where the team obsessively tests every interaction to ensure response times stay under 100 milliseconds. This creates what they call "the silkiest, fastest mobile experience" for calendar management.

## Why It Matters

For professionals managing dozens of meetings per week:
- Every second saved compounds across hundreds of calendar interactions
- Fast response times reduce cognitive friction
- Instant feedback improves user confidence and satisfaction
- Speed enables keyboard-first workflows

## Implementation

Vimcal achieves sub-100ms response times through:
- Optimized code and performance testing
- Minimal UI render times
- Instant local updates with background sync
- Careful attention to animation timing
- Native platform implementations rather than web wrappers

## User Experience Impact

The focus on speed manifests in:
- Instant calendar switching
- Immediate availability checking
- Fast meeting creation workflows
- Responsive keyboard shortcuts
- Smooth scrolling and navigation

## Philosophy

The 100ms Rule reflects Vimcal's positioning as the "fastest calendar" and its target audience of executives and founders who value time above all else. Every millisecond saved is seen as a productivity gain.

## Comparison to Competitors

While many calendar apps focus on features or visual design, Vimcal differentiates primarily on speed and efficiency, making the 100ms Rule a key competitive advantage for power users.
