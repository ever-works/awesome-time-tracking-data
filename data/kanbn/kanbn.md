## Overview

Kan.bn is a free and open-source project management tool that provides Trello-like kanban boards with integrated time tracking capabilities, designed for teams who want to self-host their project management infrastructure.

## Features

- **Kanban Boards**: Drag-and-drop kanban boards for visual project management
- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracking on cards and tasks
- **Self-Hosted**: Deploy on your own server for complete data ownership
- **Multiple Boards**: Create unlimited boards for different projects
- **Card Management**: Checklists, labels, due dates, and attachments on cards
- **Collaboration**: Team members, comments, and activity feeds
- **Markdown Support**: Rich text formatting with Markdown in descriptions
- **Search & Filter**: Advanced filtering and search across boards
- **Export**: Export boards and data in various formats
- **API**: REST API for integrations and automation
- **No Vendor Lock-in**: Open-source license allows full customization

## Pricing

Completely free and open-source under MIT license. Self-hosting requires your own server infrastructure. Cloud-hosted version may be available through third-party providers.
