## Overview

Polaris PSA is Replicon's AI-driven self-driving professional services automation platform designed for IT and consulting firms. It harvests 100% accurate business data in real-time, performs heavy-lifting analysis, and provides proactive recommendations for better decision-making.

## Features

- **Self-Driving PSA**: AI analyzes data and suggests resourcing, forecasting, and pricing decisions
- **MissionControl**: Real-time visibility into resourcing, financials, and project information in one place
- **SmartBudget**: Create rate cards for profitable bidding scenarios factoring in utilization, costs, and margins
- **Time Tracking**: Comprehensive time and expense tracking
- **Resource Management**: Optimize resource allocation across projects
- **Project Accounting**: Track project profitability and financial performance
- **Forecasting**: AI-powered forecasting based on time data, project updates, and historical patterns
- **Business Intelligence**: Real-time BI dashboards and analytics
- **Practice Management**: Tools specifically for consulting and professional services
- **Integration Ecosystem**: Connects with accounting, CRM, and other business systems
- **Mobile Access**: Track time and manage projects from mobile devices
- **Government Contracting**: Specialized features for government contractors

## AI Capabilities

Polaris leverages time data, project updates, and historical patterns to suggest better resourcing, forecasting, and pricing decisions, reducing manual analysis and improving accuracy.

## Target Market

IT consulting firms, professional services organizations, government contractors, and mid-to-large consulting practices.

## Competitive Positioning

Positioned as a more intelligent, automated alternative to traditional PSA tools like Mavenlink/Kantata, emphasizing AI-driven insights and real-time decision support.
