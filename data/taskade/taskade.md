## Overview

Taskade is an AI-powered productivity platform that combines task management, notes, mind maps, video chat, and time tracking in one unified workspace. It's designed for remote teams that need real-time collaboration.

## Features

- **AI Assistant**: AI-powered task generation and organization
- **Multiple Views**: List, board, calendar, mind map, and org chart views
- **Real-Time Collaboration**: Live editing and cursor tracking
- **Time Tracking**: Built-in timers for tasks and projects
- **Video Chat**: Integrated video conferencing
- **Templates**: Pre-built templates for workflows
- **Automation**: Workflow automation with triggers
- **Mobile Apps**: Full-featured iOS and Android apps
- **Offline Mode**: Work offline and sync when connected
- **Integrations**: Connect with calendars, Slack, and other tools

## AI Features

- Generate tasks from project descriptions
- Outline creation assistance
- Meeting notes summarization
- Workflow suggestions

## Best For

- Remote teams
- Startup teams
- Creative collaborations
- Agile project management
