## Overview

CoAmplifi is a comprehensive workforce management platform that acts as a company's virtual headquarters (vHQ), enabling businesses to effectively manage teams regardless of their work location - remote, hybrid, or on-site.

## Time Tracking Features

- Automated time tracking for remote, hybrid, and on-site employees
- Real-time task assignment and tracking
- Timecard management and analytics
- Integration with payroll systems
- Wage-hour compliance monitoring
- Deliverables tracking and time spent analysis

## Collaboration Tools

- Virtual Campus for team connectivity
- Instant messaging and real-time commenting on tasks
- File sharing capabilities
- Employee social networks
- Message boards for shared meeting notes and agendas
- Built-in discussion forums
- Manager feedback tools

## Productivity Management

- Resource Productivity Index (RPI) for quantifying employee productivity
- Automated rest break and overtime notifications
- Burnout detection and prevention
- Business analytics and reporting
- Forecasting and scheduling automation
- Project management tools

## Benefits for Distributed Teams

CoAmplifi provides organizations with tools to help distributed teams work seamlessly together, offering an all-in-one solution that encompasses multiple company operations including time tracking, payroll, project management, and compliance.
