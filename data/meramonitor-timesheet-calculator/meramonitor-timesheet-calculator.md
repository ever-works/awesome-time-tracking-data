## Overview

MeraMonitor Timesheet Calculator is a free online tool designed to calculate work hours, breaks, overtime, and pay accurately. The calculator stands out for its support of overnight shifts and decimal hour formatting.

## Key Features

**Overnight Shift Support:**
- Calculate shifts that cross midnight
- Handle night shift workers
- Accurate time calculations across date boundaries
- Graveyard shift compatibility

**Decimal Hours:**
- Convert time to decimal format
- Payroll-ready output
- Easy integration with accounting systems
- Standard or decimal display options

**Comprehensive Calculations:**
- Total work hours
- Break time deductions
- Overtime calculations
- Pay amount computations

**Free Access:**
- No registration required
- No hidden fees
- Unlimited calculations
- Instant results

## Payroll Integration

The calculator outputs results in formats ready for payroll processing, including decimal hours which many payroll systems prefer over HH:MM format.

## Use Cases

- Night shift workers tracking overnight hours
- Employers calculating shift worker pay
- Payroll departments needing decimal conversions
- Anyone needing accurate break and overtime calculations

## Platform

Web-based tool accessible from any device without installation.
