## Overview

factoHR is a comprehensive HR management system that stands out for its exceptional mobile experience, making it particularly suitable for organizations with large deskless or frontline workforces. It combines traditional HRMS features with modern engagement tools.

## Time & Attendance

### Mobile Attendance
- Face recognition attendance via mobile app
- GPS and geofencing for location verification
- QR code-based attendance for shared devices
- Offline attendance capture with sync
- Selfie attendance with timestamps
- Bulk attendance upload for supervisors

### Shift & Roster Management
- Flexible shift scheduling
- Auto-rostering based on demand
- Employee shift preferences
- Swap shift requests and approvals
- Multiple location and department support

### Leave & Regularization
- Mobile leave applications
- Manager approval on mobile
- Leave balance visibility
- Regularization workflows
- Half-day and permission tracking

## Employee Engagement

- Team collaboration features

## Performance Management

- Goal setting and OKRs

## Mobile-First Philosophy

Unlike competitors who adapted desktop software for mobile, factoHR was designed mobile-first:

- Full functionality in mobile apps
- Intuitive touch-friendly interface
- Works on low-bandwidth connections
- Minimal data usage
- Offline capabilities
- Push notifications for important actions
