## Overview

greytHR (styled with lowercase 'greyt') is one of India's leading cloud-based HR and payroll platforms, serving over 20,000 organizations and 2 million employees. It's built specifically for Indian businesses with comprehensive compliance features and local integrations.

## Time & Attendance Module

### Attendance Capture
- Biometric device integration (150+ devices supported)
- Mobile attendance with GPS verification
- Web-based punch in/out
- RFID and facial recognition support
- Attendance regularization workflows
- Shift management and rosters

### Leave Management
- Configurable leave policies
- Automated accrual calculations
- Leave approval workflows with delegation
- Calendar views and team dashboards
- Encashment and carryforward rules
- Negative balance handling

### Overtime & Shift Management
- Multiple shift patterns
- Automatic overtime calculations
- Shift allowance configurations
- Week-off management
- Compensatory off tracking
