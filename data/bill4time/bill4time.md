## Overview

Bill4Time is the most trusted name in legal time and billing software, designed specifically for attorneys and law firms. It helps legal professionals record time, track expenses, and invoice clients accurately with industry-specific features.

## Features

### Time Tracking
- Timer-based time entry
- Manual time entry options
- Mobile time tracking
- Activity-based time capture

### Billing & Invoicing
- Automated invoice generation
- Customizable invoice templates
- Multiple billing rates
- Expense tracking and billing
- Trust accounting capabilities

### Legal-Specific Features
- ABA task code support
- Matter management
- Client management
- Retainer tracking
- LEDES billing format support

### Reporting
- Detailed time and billing reports
- Profitability analysis
- Productivity metrics
- Custom report generation
