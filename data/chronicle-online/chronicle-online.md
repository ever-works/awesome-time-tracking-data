## Overview

Chronicle Online is a cloud-based workforce management software designed to help businesses in various industries, such as agriculture, construction, healthcare, and hospitality, record employee work hours and compute payroll.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Overtime Calculation**: Overtime is automatically calculated making payroll a smooth process
- **Biometric Time Capture**: Supervisors can capture employees' clocking data through biometrics, proximity terminals or mobile devices
- **Compliance Management**: Built-in features such as overtime tracking, break and meal period management, and automated alerts for compliance violations
- **Real-Time Data**: Track current working or leave status in real-time
- **Payroll Integration**: Integration with many payroll software products to automate the process of calculating employee wages and benefits based on recorded hours
- **Document Management**: Centralized document storage and management
- **Job Costing**: Track time against specific jobs and projects

## Benefits

- Minimal IT infrastructure costs with cloud-based deployment
- Rapid implementation and automatic data updates and backups
- Compliance with labour laws and industry standards
- Streamlined payroll processing
