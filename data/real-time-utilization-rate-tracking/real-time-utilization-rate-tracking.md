## Overview

Real-Time Utilization Rate Tracking provides live visibility into what percentage of available work time is being used for billable activities, enabling immediate resource optimization and preventing both underutilization waste and overwork burnout.

## Key Metrics

### Billable Utilization Rate
- Formula: (Billable Hours / Total Available Hours) × 100
- Target: Typically 70-85% for sustainable performance
- Real-time calculation as time is logged
- Individual and team views

### Capacity Tracking
- Total available hours per person
- Current commitments
- Remaining capacity
- Future availability

### Trend Analysis
- Week-over-week patterns
- Seasonal variations
- Individual performance trends
- Team benchmarks

## Benefits

### Resource Optimization
- Identify underutilized team members
- Redistribute work load
- Fill capacity gaps proactively
- Maximize revenue potential

### Burnout Prevention
- Detect overutilization early
- Prevent unsustainable workloads
- Balance team capacity
- Protect employee wellbeing

### Financial Planning
- Forecast revenue accurately
- Project profitability
- Identify hiring needs
- Optimize team size

## Thresholds

### Healthy Range
- 70-85%: Optimal sustainable utilization
- Allows for growth, learning, admin time
- Prevents burnout
- Maximizes profitability

### Warning Signs
- Below 60%: Underutilization, revenue loss
- Above 90%: Burnout risk, quality concerns
- Extreme variance: Capacity planning issues

## Tools and Platforms

## Best Practices

- Monitor weekly, not daily
- Account for non-billable value work
- Set realistic targets per role
- Use trends, not snapshots
- Combine with qualitative feedback
- Address issues proactively
