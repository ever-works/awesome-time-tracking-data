## Overview

Memtime's billable hours calculator is a free online tool that allows you to enter hours per day (billable & non-billable) with totals that update automatically, especially useful for attorneys, consultants, and accountants.

## Features

- **Billable vs Non-Billable**: Separate tracking for billable and non-billable time
- **6-Minute Increments**: Display time in standard billing increments
- **Automatic Totals**: Real-time calculation as you enter hours
- **Three Calculation Modes**: Different modes for various tracking needs
- **Daily Breakdown**: Track hours on a per-day basis
- **Weekly Totals**: See weekly billable hour totals
- **Professional Format**: Designed for client billing and invoicing
- **Export Options**: Export calculations for billing purposes

## Use Cases

Perfect for:
- Attorneys billing in 6-minute increments
- Consultants tracking project hours
- Accountants managing client time
- Freelancers calculating revenue
- Any professional billing by the hour

Web-based, works on all devices.
