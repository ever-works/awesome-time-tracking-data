## Overview

Efficient App unifies calendar management, task tracking, note-taking, and time management in a single, streamlined interface designed for comprehensive personal productivity.

## Features

- Unified calendar view
- Task management with priorities
- Note-taking and organization
- Time blocking and scheduling
- Cross-device sync
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Multiple calendar integration

## Target Users

Professionals seeking unified productivity tools, knowledge workers, busy individuals managing multiple commitments
