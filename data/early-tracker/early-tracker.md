## Overview

The EARLY Tracker (previously called Timeular Tracker or ZEI°) is an eight-sided cube that makes time tracking interactive and engaging. Timeular changed its name to EARLY on March 18, 2025.

## Features

- 8-sided physical cube for tracking up to 8 different tasks
- Bluetooth connectivity to desktop and mobile devices
- Automatic time tracking when you flip the cube
- Companion app for iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows
- Visual indicator shows which task is currently being tracked
- Integrates with popular project management tools
- Subscription-based software (can be used with or without physical tracker)

## How It Works

Assign a task to each side of the 8-sided cube. When you want to start tracking time for a task, simply flip the dice to the matching side. The app automatically reads which side is facing up and starts tracking time for the corresponding task.

## Technical Details

- Size: As big as an orange
- Connectivity: Bluetooth to iPhone, iPad, or computer
- Battery: Rechargeable

## Pricing

Requires an EARLY subscription to use the software. The physical Tracker is sold separately and works with the subscription-based time tracking app.
