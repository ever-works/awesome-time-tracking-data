## Overview

Kimai is a completely free, open-source time tracking software that can be self-hosted, making it an excellent choice for developers, agencies, and privacy-conscious organizations who want complete control over their data.

## Core Philosophy

### Complete Freedom
- **100% Free**: No subscription fees ever
- **Open Source**: Full access to source code
- **Self-Hostable**: Deploy on your own servers
- **No Vendor Lock-In**: Own and control all your data
- **Community-Driven**: Active development community

### Privacy-First
- Data stays on your infrastructure
- No third-party data sharing
- Full GDPR compliance control
- Complete audit trail
- No cloud dependency
