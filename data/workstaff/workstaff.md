## Overview

WorkStaff is a comprehensive staff scheduling and management platform built specifically for healthcare organizations. It helps hospitals and medical facilities manage complex nursing schedules, track certifications, and ensure adequate staff coverage.

## Features

- Intelligent nurse scheduling with AI optimization
- Certification and credential tracking
- Shift bidding and self-scheduling
- Time-off and absence management
- Shift trading and swapping
- Mobile app for staff access
- Real-time schedule updates
- Compliance tracking for labor laws
- Overtime and fatigue management
- Multi-department and multi-location support
- Integration with payroll and HR systems
- Detailed staffing reports and analytics
