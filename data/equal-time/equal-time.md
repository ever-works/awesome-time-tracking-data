## Overview

Equal Time is an AI meeting assistant that goes beyond transcription to track speaking time, monitor inclusion metrics, and detect conversation imbalances. It supports Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams with 102 languages.

## Speaking Time Equity Features

### Real-Time Speaking Time Analysis

- Displays sidebar during meetings showing speaking time for every participant as percentage
- See at a glance who is dominating the conversation
- Identify who may need more space to contribute
- Promotes balanced participation

### Inclusion Analytics

The tool tracks:
- Participation rate
- Monologues (extended speaking periods)
- Interruptions
- Mansplaining detection (identifies condescending explanations)
- Conversation imbalances

### Gender-Based Analysis

- Detailed breakdown of speaking time by gender after each meeting
- Categorized into man, woman, and nonbinary participants
- Email summary with gender-based metrics
- Track equity trends over time
