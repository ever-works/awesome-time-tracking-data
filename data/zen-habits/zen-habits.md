## Overview

Zen Habits is a long-running blog created by Leo Babauta that explores the intersection of mindfulness, simplicity, and productivity. It offers practical advice on building good habits, eliminating clutter from life, and managing time through intentional living.

## Topics Covered

- Minimalism and simplicity in daily life
- Habit formation and breaking bad habits
- Mindfulness practices for productivity
- Morning routines and rituals
- Focus and deep concentration techniques
- Time management through intentional prioritization
