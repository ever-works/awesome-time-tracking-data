## Overview

Tab Snooze is a productivity-enhancing Chrome extension that helps manage browser tabs like effective to-dos. Instead of keeping dozens of tabs open or losing important pages in bookmarks, Tab Snooze lets you schedule tabs to reappear exactly when you need them.

## Key Features

### Tab Scheduling
- **Preset Times**: Quickly snooze tabs for later today, tomorrow, next week, or custom dates
- **Custom Date and Time**: Schedule tabs to reopen at specific dates and times
- **Smart Notifications**: Gentle notifications when snoozed tabs wake up
- **Exact Position**: Tabs return to where you left off

### Organization
- **Todos Tab**: Central location to view all snoozed tabs
- **Cloud Syncing**: Sync snoozed tabs across devices (in some versions)
- **Tab Management**: Keep your browser clean by temporarily closing tabs you don't need now

### Productivity Benefits
- Cut down on distraction and clutter
- Ensure important pages resurface at the right moment
- Reduce memory usage by closing unnecessary tabs
- Maintain focus on current work
- Never lose track of pages you want to revisit

## How It Works

1. Right-click a tab you want to snooze
2. Select when you want it to reappear
3. The tab closes, freeing up screen space and mental bandwidth
4. At the scheduled time, the tab automatically reopens with a notification

## Alternative Versions

Several variations exist:
- **Tab Snooze (Manifest V3)**: Updated version compatible with latest Chrome standards
- **Snooze Tabs**: Alternative with preset times and custom scheduling
- **PauseTab**: Privacy-focused version that is 100% offline and collects no data

## Availability

- Platform: Chrome Web Store
- Extensions for: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and other Chromium-based browsers
- Pricing: Free (most versions)

## Privacy

Some versions like PauseTab emphasize privacy by being completely offline and collecting zero user data.

## Use Cases

- Managing research tabs for later review
- Scheduling reminders to check websites
- Reducing tab overload during focus sessions
- Planning content to review at specific times
- Managing work-life separation by scheduling work tabs
