## Overview

Deepworktimer.io is a revolutionary social deep work timer inspired by Cal Newport's deep work principles. It transforms long focus sessions (typically 60 to 180 minutes) into engaging challenges that give users a natural dopamine boost while maintaining focus on cognitively demanding tasks.

## Features

- **Long Focus Sessions**: Designed for extended work periods (60-180 minutes) rather than short Pomodoro intervals
- **Social Features**: Community elements that transform individual focus into engaging social challenges
- **Gamification**: Provides natural dopamine rewards for completing deep work sessions
- **Cal Newport Inspired**: Based on proven deep work principles for cognitively demanding tasks
- **Collaborative Features**: Includes heatmaps and habit tracking to visualize progress

## Ideal Use Cases

- Coding
- Writing
- Research
- Strategic planning
- Any cognitively demanding deep work

## Philosophy

Unlike traditional Pomodoro timers with 25-minute intervals, Deep Work Timer focuses on extended sessions that allow for true deep work and flow states. The social and gamification elements help maintain motivation during these longer, more challenging focus periods.
