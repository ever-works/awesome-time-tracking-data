## Overview

Atlassian Team Central is a collaboration platform that serves as a unified hub for teams using Atlassian products. While not a dedicated time tracking tool, it aggregates time-related data from Jira, Confluence, and other Atlassian tools to provide visibility into team capacity, work allocation, and project progress.

## Time-Related Capabilities

**Work Visibility** - Team Central surfaces:
- Time logged in Jira issues across projects
- Sprint burndown and time remaining
- Team member workload and capacity
- Project timelines and deadlines
- Work distribution across the team

**Integration with Jira Time Tracking** - Displays:
- Original estimates from Jira issues
- Time spent logged on tickets
- Remaining time to completion
- Sprint time metrics
- Velocity and capacity trends

**Team Health Metrics** - Monitors:
- Overallocation and bandwidth issues
- Work-life balance indicators
- Meeting load and collaboration time
- Focus time vs meeting time ratios

## Key Features for Time Management

**Team Calendar** - Unified view of:
- Sprint schedules and milestones
- Team member availability
- Project deadlines
- Holidays and time off
- Company events

**Goal Tracking** - Connect time investment to:
- Team OKRs and goals
- Project objectives
- Individual contributions
- Progress toward targets

**Dependency Management** - Identify:
- Blocked work affecting timelines
- Cross-team dependencies
- Resource conflicts
- Scheduling bottlenecks

**Team Spaces** - Centralized collaboration:
- Project workspaces with time context
- Shared documents and decisions
- Status updates and progress reports
- Communication threaded by project

## Integration Ecosystem

Team Central connects with:
- **Jira** - Work tracking and time logging
- **Confluence** - Documentation and knowledge base
- **Trello** - Simple project boards
- **Bitbucket** - Code repositories and commits
- **Statuspage** - Service status communication
- **Opsgenie** - Incident management and on-call

## Workflow Examples

**Sprint Planning**:
1. Review team capacity in Team Central
2. See time allocation across projects
3. Check individual workload
4. Plan sprint commitments accordingly
5. Monitor burndown through sprint

**Project Oversight**:
1. View all project timelines in one place
2. Check time logged vs estimated across projects
3. Identify at-risk projects
4. Reallocate resources as needed

**Team Health Monitoring**:
1. Review team member workload
2. Identify over-allocated individuals
3. See meeting load impact on work time
4. Take action to rebalance work

## Benefits for Distributed Teams

**Async-Friendly** - Supports distributed work:
- Time zone aware scheduling
- Asynchronous updates and progress tracking
- Work visibility without constant meetings
- Documentation-first culture

**Transparency** - Everyone sees:
- What teammates are working on
- Project status and timelines
- Team capacity and availability
- Goals and priorities

**Reduced Meeting Overhead** - Information available without meetings:
- Status updates via Team Central
- Progress visible in dashboards
- Questions answered in spaces
- Time saved for focused work

## Limitations

- Not a dedicated time tracking platform
- Requires Atlassian ecosystem adoption
- Time data depends on team logging in Jira
- Advanced analytics require additional tools
- No automatic time capture or timers

## Complementary Time Tracking Apps

Teams using Team Central often add:
- **Tempo Timesheets** - Advanced Jira time tracking
- **Harvest** - Invoicing and client billing
- **Everhour** - Enhanced time tracking and budgeting
- **Hubstaff** - Automatic tracking and monitoring

## Pricing & Availability

Team Central availability:
- Included with various Atlassian Cloud products
- Part of Atlassian's platform approach
- Specific features vary by subscription tier
- Some capabilities in free/starter tiers

## Best For

- Teams already using multiple Atlassian products
- Organizations wanting unified work visibility
- Distributed teams needing async collaboration
- Companies tracking work in Jira
- Teams seeking lightweight time awareness without heavy tracking

## When to Use Dedicated Time Tracking

Consider specialized tools when needing:
- Client billing and invoicing
- Automatic time capture
- Detailed productivity analytics
- Timesheet approvals and workflows
- Integration with non-Atlassian systems
- Payroll integration
