## Overview

The Time Now is a comprehensive world clock and time zone resource offering tools for international meeting planning, time zone conversion, and global time coordination. The platform helps individuals and businesses schedule meetings across multiple time zones with automatic handling of daylight saving time complexities.

## Features

- **International Meeting Planner**: Find optimal meeting times across multiple time zones
- **World Clock**: View current time in cities worldwide
- **Time Zone Converter**: Convert times between different time zones
- **Daylight Saving Time Handling**: Automatic DST adjustments for all time zones
- **Future Date Support**: Plan meetings for future dates with accurate DST calculations
- **Multiple Locations**: Compare times across numerous cities simultaneously
- **City Search**: Quickly find specific cities and their time zones
- **Current or Future Dates**: Accept both current date and chosen future dates
- **Visual Display**: Easy-to-read time comparisons
- **Mobile Friendly**: Accessible from smartphones and tablets

## Meeting Planner Tool

The meeting planner helps you:
- Select multiple cities where participants are located
- View a comparison of times across all selected zones
- Identify overlapping business hours
- Find suitable meeting times that work for all participants
- Account for daylight saving time changes
- Plan for future meetings with accurate future DST handling

## How It Works

1. **Select Locations**: Choose cities where meeting participants are located
2. **View Time Grid**: See a side-by-side comparison of times
3. **Find Overlap**: Identify times when all locations are in business hours
4. **Schedule Meeting**: Select the optimal time for all participants
5. **Future Planning**: For future meetings, the tool accounts for upcoming DST changes
