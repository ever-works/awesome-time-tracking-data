## Overview

UKG (Ultimate Kronos Group) provides enterprise-grade workforce management solutions through two primary platforms: UKG Dimensions for mid-to-large enterprises and UKG Ready for small to mid-sized businesses. The platform emphasizes advanced scheduling, time tracking, and compliance management with machine learning capabilities.

## Features

- **Time and Attendance**: Comprehensive time tracking with multiple clock-in methods including biometric, mobile, and web
- **Advanced Scheduling**: AI-powered scheduling that considers skills, availability, and labor laws
- **Compliance Management**: Automated compliance tracking for union agreements, collective bargaining, and labor regulations
- **Exception Management**: Identify and resolve timekeeping exceptions before payroll runs
- **Shift-Driven Scheduling**: Optimized for organizations with shift-based workforces
- **Labor Cost Forecasting**: Predict and control labor costs in real-time
- **Payroll Integration**: Seamless connection with payroll systems
- **Mobile Workforce**: Full mobile capabilities for field and remote workers
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Comprehensive workforce analytics and insights
- **Leave Management**: PTO, sick time, and absence tracking
- **Geofencing**: Location-based clock-in restrictions
- **Multi-Location Support**: Manage workforce across multiple sites

## Target Market

UKG targets operational problems tied to timekeeping, labor rules, union/collective bargaining compliance, exception management, and shift-driven scheduling.
