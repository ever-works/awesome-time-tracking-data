## Overview

Napkin is designed for effortless thought capture without the friction of traditional note-taking apps, using AI to organize and surface notes when relevant.

## Core Philosophy

### Capture First, Organize Later
- Save thoughts instantly
- No folder decisions
- No tag selection upfront
- Just write and save

### AI-Powered Organization
- Automatically connects related notes
- Surfaces relevant past notes
- Finds patterns in your thinking
- Contextual retrieval

## Key Features

### Quick Capture
- Minimal interface
- Fast input
- No organizational overhead
- Mobile and desktop

### Smart Connections
- AI links related concepts
- Discovers themes
- Shows note clusters
- Builds knowledge graph

### Contextual Surfacing
- Relevant notes appear when needed
- Right information at right time
- Serendipitous rediscovery
- Enhanced recall

## Best For

- Busy professionals capturing fleeting thoughts
- Researchers connecting ideas
- Writers gathering inspiration
- Anyone overwhelmed by note organization
- People wanting AI-assisted knowledge management
