## Overview

Brite is an all-in-one productivity app with deep customization that combines multiple productivity tools into a single platform, eliminating the need to switch between different apps for task management, calendar scheduling, and daily planning.

## Key Features

- **My Day View**: Customizable view showing tasks for the day, calendars from various sources, habits, and ongoing projects all in a single screen
- **Full-Featured Calendar**: Syncs with Google and Apple calendars for seamless scheduling
- **Task Management**: To-do lists, shopping lists, and task organizer with smart reminders
- **Habit Tracking**: Built-in habit list to develop and maintain positive routines
- **Mood Diary**: Track your emotional well-being alongside your productivity
- **Budget Tracking**: Simple budget management integrated into the same app
- **Notes**: Convenient note-taking feature for quick thoughts and ideas

## Platform Availability

Brite is available across multiple platforms:
- Web app
- Chrome extension
- iOS (iPhone and iPad)
- Android
- Mac
- Windows

## Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Available with core features
- **Brite Premium**: $3.29/month (billed annually) or $4.50/month (billed monthly) for advanced options

## Use Case

Ideal for users who want to consolidate their productivity stack into one customizable platform for daily planning, task management, and habit tracking.
