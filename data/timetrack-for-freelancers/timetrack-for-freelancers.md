## Overview

TimeTrack for Freelancers is a mobile time tracking application available on iOS that helps freelancers better understand their projects and get paid faster. It combines time tracking, expense management, and invoicing in a single app optimized for mobile use.

## Key Features

- **Project Time Tracker**: Track time spent on different client projects with easy start/stop functionality
- **Expense Tracker**: Record project-related expenses alongside time entries
- **Professional Invoicing**: Generate and send invoices directly from tracked time and expenses
- **Client Management**: Organize work by client and project
- **Mobile-Optimized**: Designed specifically for iPhone and iPad use
- **Hourly Rate Configuration**: Set different rates for different projects or clients
- **Reports**: View earnings and time breakdowns by project or client

## For Freelancers

The app is purpose-built for the freelance workflow:
- Track time on-the-go from your mobile device
- Capture expenses immediately when they occur
- Generate invoices quickly at the end of a billing period
- Get insights into project profitability
- Understand where time is actually being spent

## Use Cases

Ideal for:
- Consultants billing by the hour
- Creative professionals managing multiple clients
- Independent contractors needing mobile time tracking
- Anyone who wants to streamline the freelance billing process

The mobile-first approach recognizes that freelancers often work from various locations and need time tracking that travels with them.
