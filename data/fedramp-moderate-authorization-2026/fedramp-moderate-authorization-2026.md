## Overview

FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program) Moderate Authorization represents a significant cybersecurity compliance milestone for time tracking software serving government contractors and federal agencies. In 2026, this certification is increasingly critical for workforce management platforms.

## What is FedRAMP Moderate?

### Security Standards
FedRAMP Moderate authorization requires cloud service providers to implement robust security controls protecting federal information at a moderate impact level. This includes:
- Advanced encryption protocols
- Rigorous access controls
- Continuous monitoring and audit capabilities
- Incident response procedures
- Regular security assessments

### Government Contracting Requirements
For companies working with federal agencies, FedRAMP Moderate authorization is often mandatory for:
- Time and attendance tracking systems
- Project management platforms
- Workforce scheduling applications
- Payroll and billing software
