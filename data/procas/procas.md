## Overview

PROCAS is comprehensive accounting and timekeeping software designed specifically for government contractors to achieve and maintain DCAA compliance. For over 25 years, PROCAS has helped companies become DCAA compliant and successfully pass DCAA accounting system audits. The platform provides integrated project accounting, timekeeping, expense reporting, and management reporting solutions.

## Features

### DCAA-Compliant Timekeeping
- **Timesheet Entry**: Daily time recording with project/task allocation
- **Detailed Audit Trail**: Complete history of all timesheet entries, changes, and approvals
- **Change Justification**: System requires employees to document reasons for any alterations
- **Supervisor Approvals**: Multi-level approval workflows
- **Employee Certification**: Employees certify accuracy of their timesheets
- **Integration with Accounting**: Time flows directly to project accounting for accurate labor distribution

### Project Accounting
- **Job Costing**: Real-time labor cost allocation by project
- **Contract Management**: Track multiple contracts and projects
- **Budget Tracking**: Monitor project budgets vs. actual costs
- **Billing Support**: Automated invoice preparation with accurate labor data
- **Indirect Rate Calculations**: Proper allocation of indirect costs
- **Compliance Reporting**: Reports required for DCAA audits

### Expense Reporting
- **Integrated Expense Tracking**: Capture project-related expenses
- **Receipt Management**: Digital receipt storage and attachment
- **Approval Workflows**: Multi-level expense approval process
- **Direct Integration**: Expenses flow directly to accounting system
- **Reimbursement Processing**: Streamlined employee reimbursement

### Audit Trail Features
- **Complete History**: Every entry, change, and approval logged
- **Reason Documentation**: Required justification for all modifications
- **Time-Stamped Records**: Precise tracking of when changes occurred
- **User Attribution**: Clear identification of who made each change
- **Permanent Storage**: Secure retention meeting federal requirements

### Labor Distribution
- **Automated Allocation**: Time information flows directly from timekeeping to accounting
- **Project-Based Distribution**: Accurate allocation to appropriate projects
- **Direct vs. Indirect**: Proper separation of labor categories
- **Cost Pool Management**: Support for complex indirect cost structures
- **Fringe Benefit Allocation**: Automated benefit cost distribution

### Compliance Features
- **FAR/DFARS Alignment**: Meets Federal Acquisition Regulation requirements
- **CAM Compliance**: Aligns with DCAA's Contract Audit Manual
- **Pre-Configured Templates**: DCAA-ready forms and reports
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Automatic checks for regulatory adherence
- **Audit Support**: Reports and documentation for DCAA audits

## DCAA Compliance Requirements Addressed

### Timekeeping Standards
- Daily labor hour recording
- Detailed audit trail with documented change reasons
- Employee and supervisor certification
- Separation of direct and indirect time
- Project/task code structure

### Accounting System Requirements
- Adequate internal controls
- Proper segregation of costs
- Identification of costs by contract
- Timely recording of costs
- Exclusion of unallowable costs
