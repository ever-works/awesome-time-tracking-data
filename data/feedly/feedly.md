## Overview

Feedly is a content aggregation and RSS reader platform that collects updates from websites, blogs, YouTube channels, and news sources into a unified feed. It provides a streamlined way to stay informed across multiple content sources without visiting individual sites.

## Features

- Clean, intuitive user interface
- RSS feed aggregation from multiple sources
- Category-based organization of feeds
- AI-powered content filtering and prioritization (paid plans)
- Topic tracking and alerts
- Integration with various productivity and research tools
- Search across saved articles

## Pricing

Free plan with basic features. Pro plan starts at $6/month with AI-powered features and additional integrations.
