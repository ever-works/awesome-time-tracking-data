## Overview

UKG Dimensions (formerly Kronos Workforce Dimensions) is an enterprise workforce management solution serving organizations across healthcare, manufacturing, retail, hospitality, and public sector with expertise in complex, shift-based industries.

## Features

- **Time & Attendance**: Advanced time clock options including biometric
- **Scheduling**: AI-powered schedule optimization
- **Labor Forecasting**: Predictive analytics for staffing needs
- **Absence Management**: Comprehensive leave and PTO tracking
- **Task Management**: Operational task assignment and tracking
- **Compliance**: Multi-jurisdiction labor law compliance
- **Analytics**: Workforce analytics and reporting
- **Mobile Access**: Full mobile workforce management
- **Employee Self-Service**: Portal for schedules, time off, and more

## Industry Solutions

- Healthcare with complex shift patterns and union rules
- Manufacturing with shop floor time tracking
- Retail with multi-location management
- Hospitality with fluctuating demand
- Public sector with compliance requirements

## Benefits
