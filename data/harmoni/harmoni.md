## Overview

Harmoni is a modern shop floor management platform that transforms how manufacturing teams track labor hours, monitor production, and manage job costs through barcode scanning and real-time data capture.

## Features

- **Barcode Time Tracking**: Scan to clock in/out of jobs, operations, and work centers
- **Digital Timesheets**: Automatic timesheet generation from shop floor activity
- **Job Tracking**: Monitor job progress, labor hours, and material usage in real-time
- **Labor Routing**: Track time against specific routing operations and work centers
- **Work Instructions**: Display digital work instructions and quality requirements
- **Production Reporting**: Real-time dashboards showing shop floor status and metrics
- **Downtime Tracking**: Record and categorize machine and labor downtime
- **Scrap Tracking**: Log scrap quantities and reasons at the point of production
- **Quality Data**: Capture in-process measurements and inspection results
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Access from tablets, smartphones, or shop floor terminals
- **ERP Integration**: Sync with ERP systems for seamless data flow
- **Analytics**: Labor efficiency, job profitability, and OEE reporting

## Benefits

Eliminates manual time card errors, provides accurate job costing data, simplifies payroll approvals, and delivers real-time visibility into production status and labor allocation.

## Target Market

Discrete manufacturers, machine shops, and fabricators who need accurate labor tracking and job costing without complex enterprise systems.

## Pricing

Subscription-based pricing with different tiers based on features and number of shop floor users.
