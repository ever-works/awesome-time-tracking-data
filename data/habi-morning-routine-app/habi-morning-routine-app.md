## Getting the Most from Habi

### Best Practices

1. **Start small**: 2-3 habits maximum initially
2. **Be realistic**: Choose sustainable habits
3. **Use blocker**: Enable during vulnerable scrolling times
4. **Stack habits**: Link new habits to existing ones
5. **Review weekly**: Adjust habits that aren't working

- Setting unrealistic habit times
- Not using the screen blocker
- Skipping habit review and adjustment
- Trying to be perfect rather than consistent
