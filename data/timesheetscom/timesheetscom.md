### Timesheets.com

**Category:** Web-based time tracking

#### Overview
Timesheets.com is an online employee time tracking and timesheet system for payroll, billing, and attendance. It supports hourly time tracking, project and job-costing, expense capture, PTO and sick leave accruals, and mobile access with geo-authentication and security controls.

#### Features
- **Time & attendance**
  - Web-based employee timesheets for payroll and attendance
  - Hourly employee time tracking
  - Manual hours entry
  - Mobile phone access to timesheets
  - Geo-authentication and security features for time entries

- **Payroll, billing & job costing**
  - Timesheets for payroll processing
  - Timesheets for client billing and invoicing
  - Project timesheet entry
  - Job costing capabilities
  - Expense tracking (including mileage and other expenses)

- **Time off & leave management**
  - Time off and accrual tracking (PTO, vacation, sick leave)
  - PTO accrual ledgers and sick leave reporting
  - Supports PTO accrual via biweekly timesheets or hourly timesheets based on actual work hours
  - Enforces PTO accrual ledger and manual-hours-entry rules, including support for “use-it-or-lose-it” policies
  - Leave request submission directly from the timesheet

- **Compliance & templates**
  - Customizable timesheet templates
  - Alignment with FLSA timesheet guidelines and state labor timesheet standards

- **Approvals & workflows**
  - Supervisor sign-off on timesheets and leave
  - Approval paths and built-in timecard approval workflows
  - Notifications related to approvals and timesheet activity

- **Scheduling & calendars**
  - Employee scheduling tools
  - Custom calendars for planning work and time off

- **HR & administration**
  - HR-related tools (general HR tracking module mentioned)
