## Overview

Caveday offers facilitated virtual coworking sessions called "Caves" where participants engage in structured deep work sprints guided by trained facilitators, creating accountability through shared focus time.

## How It Works

- Join live virtual coworking sessions ("Caves")
- Facilitator guides the session structure
- Timed work sprints (typically 40-50 minutes)
- Brief social breaks between sprints
- Share intentions and outcomes
- Community accountability

## Session Structure

1. **Kickoff**: Set intentions and goals
2. **Sprint 1**: Focused work period
3. **Break**: Social connection with group
4. **Sprint 2**: Second deep work period
5. **Break**: Reflection and sharing
6. **Sprint 3**: Final focus session
7. **Closing**: Review accomplishments

## Benefits

- Structured accountability
- Reduced procrastination
- Social connection while working
- Facilitated focus sessions
- Community support
- Regular deep work practice
