## YouTrack

**Description**  
YouTrack is an issue tracker and project management tool by JetBrains. It includes built-in time tracking, work item logging, and reporting, helping teams track time spent on tasks and analyze productivity.

**Features**
- Issue tracking for tasks and bugs
- Project management capabilities
- Built-in time tracking for tasks and work items
- Work item logging to record time and activities
- Reporting tools to analyze time usage and team productivity
