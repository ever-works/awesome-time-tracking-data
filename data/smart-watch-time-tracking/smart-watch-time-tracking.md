## Overview

Smart Watch Time Tracking brings time capture to the most accessible device, enabling instant logging without interrupting workflow or requiring phone access.

## Supported Platforms

### Apple Watch
- Most developed ecosystem
- Complications support
- Siri integration
- Haptic feedback

### Wear OS (Android)
- Google Assistant
- Tile support
- Widget complications

### Garmin
- Physical buttons
- Long battery life
- Custom apps

## Key Features

### Quick Actions
- Tap to start/stop timer
- Swipe between projects
- Voice timer start
- Force touch options

### Watch Face Complications
- Current timer display
- Today's hours
- Active project
- Quick start button

### Notifications
- Reminder to clock in
- Time to clock out
- Approval requests
- Budget alerts

## Use Cases

**Healthcare Workers**:
- Hands often occupied
- Frequent patient switches
- Clean environment requirements

**Field Service**:
- Gloves or dirty hands
- Phone in vehicle
- Quick job changes

**Manufacturing**:
- Assembly line work
- Equipment operation
- Safety gear worn

**Retail**:
- Customer interaction
- Phone not accessible
- Quick task switching

## Benefits

**Convenience**:
- No phone required
- Always on wrist
- Instant access
- Discreet tracking

**Accuracy**:
- Capture in the moment
- Less forgotten time
- Real-time logging
- Better habits

## Limitations

- Small screen
- Limited functionality
- Battery dependent
- Requires phone nearby (usually)
- Simple tasks only
