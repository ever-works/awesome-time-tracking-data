## Overview

Chris Bailey is a productivity expert, speaker, and author who conducted a year-long experiment testing dozens of productivity techniques and time management strategies. His evidence-based approach combines research with personal experimentation.

## A Year of Productivity

After graduating college, Bailey spent an entire year testing productivity techniques full-time:
- Experimented with various time management methods
- Tracked results meticulously
- Shared findings through his blog and later books
- Identified what actually works versus productivity myths

## Key Concepts

**Biological Prime Time**: Identifying your personal peak energy hours for optimal scheduling

**The Rule of Three**: Focus on accomplishing just three things each day

**Attention Management**: Managing attention is more important than time management

**Hyperfocus and Scatterfocus**: Alternating between intense focus and creative wandering

## Published Works

- "The Productivity Project: Accomplishing More by Managing Your Time, Attention, and Energy" (2016)
- "Hyperfocus: How to Manage Your Attention in a World of Distraction" (2018)
- "How to Calm Your Mind: Finding Presence and Productivity in Anxious Times" (2022)

## Research Approach

Bailey's methodology involves:
- Personal experimentation with techniques
- Review of academic productivity research
- Interviews with productivity experts
- Practical application and iteration

## Impact

His work has influenced modern understanding of:
- Energy management in addition to time management
- The importance of attention in productivity
- Evidence-based productivity versus popular myths
