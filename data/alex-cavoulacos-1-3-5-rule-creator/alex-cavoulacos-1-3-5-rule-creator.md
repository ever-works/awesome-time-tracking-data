## About Alex Cavoulacos

Alex Cavoulacos is the founder and president of The Muse, described as "the most productive person we know" by her team. She created the 1-3-5 Rule as a practical framework for daily task management.

## The 1-3-5 Rule

You can reasonably get one big thing, three medium things, and five small things done each day. Keep your daily to-do list to just these nine items.

## Philosophy

Cavoulacos recognized that people have limited time and can only accomplish a finite number of tasks daily. The 1-3-5 system forces realistic prioritization by accepting capacity limits rather than creating endless to-do lists.

## Impact

The rule gained widespread adoption through The Muse platform, with downloadable templates and guides helping millions of professionals implement more realistic daily planning.

## Key Insight

By limiting daily tasks to nine carefully prioritized items, you ensure your most important work gets completed while avoiding the stress and overwhelm of impossible to-do lists.
