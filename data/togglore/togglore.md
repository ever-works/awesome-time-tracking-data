## Overview

**togglore** is a simple Python-based tool for comparing time tracked in Toggl with expected working hours over a specified period. It is designed to help with auditing time-tracking data and analyzing workload patterns.

## Features

- **Toggl time comparison**: Compares hours tracked in Toggl against expected working hours.
- **Configurable time period**: Runs analysis over a chosen date range or period.
- **Audit support**: Highlights differences between tracked and expected hours to support time-tracking audits.
- **Workload analysis**: Provides a basis for understanding workload distribution and identifying under- or over-worked periods.
- **Python-based tool**: Implemented in Python, suitable for automation and scripting workflows.
