## Overview

Scoro is an end-to-end work management software that brings together project management, time tracking, CRM, billing, and reporting in a single platform. It's designed specifically for professional services firms, consultancies, and agencies that need to manage their entire business workflow from one place.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track time spent on projects, tasks, and client work with timers or manual entry
- **Project Management**: Plan, execute, and monitor projects with task management and Gantt charts
- **CRM**: Manage client relationships, sales pipeline, and opportunities
- **Quoting & Invoicing**: Create quotes, generate invoices, and track payments
- **Resource Planning**: Allocate team members to projects and manage capacity
- **Financial Management**: Track budgets, profitability, and financial performance
- **Reporting & Dashboards**: Real-time visibility into business performance with customizable reports
- **Calendar Management**: Unified calendar showing meetings, tasks, and deadlines
- **Team Collaboration**: Share files, communicate, and collaborate within projects
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for time tracking and project management on the go

## Use Cases

- Consulting firms
- Marketing agencies
- IT services companies
- Creative studios
- Professional services organizations

## Pricing

Scoro offers tiered pricing plans starting from essential features for small teams to enterprise solutions with advanced capabilities. Pricing is per user per month and requires contacting Scoro for specific quotes.
