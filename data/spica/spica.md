## Overview

Spica is positioned as the number one productivity tool for visualizing tasks through Kanban boards. It provides flexible workflow management with drag-and-drop simplicity, helping teams and individuals organize and track their work visually.

## Kanban Features

### Board Customization
- Customizable columns
- Unlimited boards
- Swimlanes for categories
- Card templates
- Color coding

### Task Management
- Drag-and-drop cards
- Task details and descriptions
- Checklists and subtasks
- Attachments
- Labels and tags

### Workflow Visualization
- See all work at a glance
- Track progress visually
- Identify bottlenecks
- Monitor work distribution
- Balance workload

## Productivity Benefits

- Reduce multitasking
- Limit work in progress
- Improve task completion rates
- Enhance team transparency
- Better priority management

## Use Cases

- Project management
- Sprint planning
- Personal task organization
- Team collaboration
- Process improvement

## Target Users

Teams and individuals seeking visual task management, Agile practitioners using Kanban methodology, and anyone wanting to organize work through intuitive board-based interfaces.
