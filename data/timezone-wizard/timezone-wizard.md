## Overview

Timezone Wizard is a simple and efficient tool for finding optimal meeting times across multiple time zones. It's used by business professionals, gamers, and anyone who needs to coordinate schedules globally.

## Key Features

- Clean, intuitive interface
- Multiple timezone support
- Visual time comparison
- Quick timezone search
- Mobile-responsive design
- No account required

## How to Use

1. Add your location and participant locations
2. View the time comparison grid
3. Slide through different hours to find overlap
4. Identify mutually convenient meeting times
5. Copy and share the selected time

## Target Users

- Remote teams and distributed companies
- International business professionals
- Online gaming communities
- Event organizers with global audiences
- Freelancers with international clients

## Benefits

- Eliminates timezone confusion
- Saves time in scheduling
- Reduces back-and-forth communication
- Helps avoid scheduling mistakes
- Improves meeting attendance
