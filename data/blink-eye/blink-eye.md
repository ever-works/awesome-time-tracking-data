## Overview

Blink Eye is a minimalist eye care and break reminder application designed to prevent eye strain, repetitive strain injury (RSI), and Computer Vision Syndrome. Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux as an open-source solution.

## Features

- **20-20-20 Rule Support**: Built-in support for the 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds)
- **Customizable Timers**: Set custom break intervals and durations to match your workflow
- **Full-Screen Break Notifications**: Ensures you actually take breaks with unavoidable full-screen reminders
- **Daily Usage Analytics**: Track your computer usage patterns and break adherence
- **Built-in TODO Functionality**: Manage tasks directly within the app
- **Cross-Platform**: Works seamlessly across Windows, macOS, and Linux
- **Minimalist Design**: Clean, unobtrusive interface that stays out of your way
- **Open Source**: Free and open-source software available on GitHub

## Use Cases

- Preventing digital eye strain during long work sessions
- Reducing risk of repetitive strain injuries
- Maintaining healthier work habits for remote workers
- Managing Computer Vision Syndrome symptoms
