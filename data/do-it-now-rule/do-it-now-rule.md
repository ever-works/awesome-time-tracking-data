## Overview

The Do It Now Rule is a straightforward time management principle designed to prevent small tasks from accumulating and cluttering your to-do list.

## The Rule

If a task takes **less than 3 minutes**, do it now, without any thinking or planning.

## Rationale

The cognitive overhead of deciding to later, scheduling, recording, and remembering a 3-minute task far exceeds the actual time it takes to complete the task. By doing it immediately, you eliminate this overhead and keep your mental workspace clear.
