## Overview

Forest partners with Trees for the Future, a real-tree-planting organization, allowing users to convert virtual coins earned in the app into actual trees planted on Earth. Over 1.5 million real trees have been planted through this partnership.

## How It Works

1. Users focus using Forest app, growing virtual trees
2. Successful focus sessions earn virtual coins
3. Accumulate coins over time
4. Spend coins to plant real trees
5. Forest donates to Trees for the Future
6. Real trees planted in developing countries

## Business Model Integration

Forest monetizes through:
- App purchases (£3.99 iOS, paid Android version)
- In-app purchases
- Premium features

Portion of revenue funds tree planting donations, making the business model sustainable while creating environmental impact.
