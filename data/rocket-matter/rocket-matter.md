## Overview

Rocket Matter by ProfitSolv is a scalable practice management suite with time tracking included in all subscription plans. Time tracking is tightly integrated with billing and matter management features, providing a comprehensive solution designed specifically for law firms.

## Advanced Time Tracking Features

### Multi-Timer Capability
- Run multiple timers simultaneously for different matters
- Quick switching between active timers
- Pause and resume individual timers
- Automatic time capture while working
- Background timer operation

### Multi-Device Accessibility
- Desktop application for office work
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Web-based access from any browser
- Automatic synchronization across all devices
- Offline time capture with sync when reconnected

### Automatic Time Entries
- AI-suggested time entries based on activity
- Calendar integration for meeting time capture
- Email time tracking integration
- Document work time auto-logging
- Smart time entry suggestions
