## Overview

Minutes is a minimalist time tracking application that simplifies the time tracking process by focusing on tracking minutes worked without requiring complex project setups or category hierarchies.

## Features

- Simple timer interface
- Quick manual time entry
- Minimal project organization
- Clean, distraction-free design
- Daily, weekly, monthly views
- Basic reporting
- CSV export
- Cross-platform sync
- Mobile and desktop apps
- No account required for basic use

## Pricing

Minutes offers:
- Free version with basic features
- Premium version with sync and advanced features

Pricing is designed to be affordable for individual freelancers.
