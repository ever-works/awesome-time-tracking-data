## Overview

timeEdition is a time tracking application available on multiple platforms: macOS, iOS, and Android. With timeEdition you get a detailed documentation of your working hours and can track both your work time and your colleagues' work time.

## Key Features

**Cross-Platform Sync**
All timeEdition apps can be synced through Dropbox. You can sync with an unlimited number of devices, ensuring your time data is always accessible across all your devices.

**No Subscription Model**
You do not need a subscription for timeEdition. This is a one-time purchase software, making it cost-effective for long-term use.

**Export Options**
With timeEdition you can export to:
- Excel
- CSV
- XML
- iCal
- PDF

**Revenue Tracking**
Simply set up the hourly rate for your activities or use individual hourly rates for individual tasks. Now timeEdition will track your income and your working hours.

**Calendar Integration**
timeEdition will write all your recordings to your calendar, providing seamless integration with your existing scheduling tools.

**User-Friendly Interface**
The user first sees only the functions that are needed for daily time tracking:
- Start and Stop recording of work time
- Work time elapsed
- Customer
- Project
- Task

## Additional Capabilities

- Automatic time rounding based on a threshold you specify
- Custom Siri command support on iOS
- Track project work outlay
- Generate detailed client invoices
- Individual task and project management
