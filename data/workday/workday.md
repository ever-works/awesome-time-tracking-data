## Overview

Workday provides enterprise-grade workforce management within its comprehensive HCM platform, delivering scheduling, time tracking, and workforce analytics designed for large organizations with global operations and complex requirements. The cloud-native architecture combines workforce management with core HR, payroll, talent management, and analytics in a single unified platform.

## Features

- **Unified HCM Platform**: Workforce management integrated with HR, payroll, and talent in one system
- **Time Tracking**: Enterprise-grade time and attendance tracking
- **Workforce Scheduling**: Advanced scheduling with optimization algorithms
- **Global Workforce Support**: Multi-country, multi-currency, multi-language capabilities
- **Workforce Analytics**: Advanced analytics and reporting across all HR functions
- **Cloud-Native Architecture**: Built from the ground up for the cloud, not retrofitted
- **Consistent Data Model**: Single source of truth across all HR processes
- **Absence Management**: Comprehensive leave and absence tracking
- **Labor Cost Management**: Real-time visibility into labor costs
- **Compliance Tracking**: Automated compliance with global labor regulations
- **Mobile Access**: Full mobile capabilities for managers and employees
- **API Integration**: Open API for connecting with third-party systems

## Competitive Advantages

Workday's cloud-native architecture ensures consistent data across all HR functions, unlike legacy systems that bolt workforce management onto existing platforms. This unified approach helps organizations maintain data consistency and simplify reporting.

## Target Market

Large enterprises (typically 1,000+ employees) with global operations and complex HCM requirements. Popular in industries like technology, professional services, healthcare, and higher education.

## Strengths vs Competitors

While UKG excels at time & attendance and complex shift rules, Workday provides broader HCM workflows and analytics for enterprise HR teams managing the complete employee lifecycle.

## Pricing

Custom enterprise pricing based on employee count and modules. Typical implementations for large enterprises range from hundreds of thousands to millions annually.
