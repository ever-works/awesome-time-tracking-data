## Overview

minimalist phone is a minimalist launcher app that helps users employ their phones mindfully by transforming cluttered home screens into icon-free, text-based interfaces that eliminate visual triggers and reduce screen time.

## Key Features

- **Icon-Free Interface** - Text-based layout eliminates visual triggers and reduces sensory overload to prevent opening apps on autopilot
- **App Blocking** - Block distracting apps for specific durations ranging from 4 hours to 30 days
- **Notification Filter** - Selectively allow notifications from chosen apps while suppressing others
- **Grayscale Mode** - Reduces visual appeal of the phone to discourage excessive use
- **Smart Folders & Widgets** - Organize essential apps without visual clutter
- **Usage Analytics** - Track screen time and app usage patterns
- **Scheduled Blocking** - Set automatic blocking schedules for work hours, sleep time, etc.
- **Focus Mode** - Enhanced blocking during important activities

## Effectiveness

Users report significant improvements:
- 93% of users reported spending less screen time after three days
- Average screen time reduction from 6 hours/day to 3 hours/day within the first day
- Addresses the fact that Americans check their phones an average of 344 times per day

## Digital Wellbeing Benefits

- Prevents mindless scrolling and app autopilot behavior

- Improves mental wellbeing and stress levels
- Increases productivity by limiting distractions

Unlike traditional launcher apps, minimalist phone was specifically designed to:

The app addresses the digital minimalism movement's response to increasingly overwhelming digital lives by transforming phones from sources of distraction into intentional tools for productivity and wellbeing.
