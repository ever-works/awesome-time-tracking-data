## Overview

MyStudyLife is a free, cross-platform student planner designed to replace traditional paper planners and generic calendar apps. Built specifically for academic life, it understands the unique scheduling needs of students including rotating class schedules, assignment deadlines, and exam periods.

## Features

- **Academic Calendar**: Calendar designed for student schedules, not business meetings
- **Class Schedule Management**: Input class times, locations, and rotation schedules
- **Assignment Tracking**: Record assignments with due dates and priority levels
- **Exam Planning**: Schedule exams and create countdown timers
- **Task Management**: Smart to-do lists with intelligent reminders
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Access from web, iOS, Android, and Windows - all synced in real-time
- **Offline Access**: Works without internet connection
- **Rotation Schedules**: Handles complex class rotation patterns (A/B weeks, etc.)
- **Subject Organization**: Organize by course, subject, or class
- **Cloud Storage**: Securely stores schedule data in the cloud
- **Revision Planner**: Plan study sessions for exam preparation
- **Color Coding**: Visual organization with customizable colors

## Academic-Specific Features

Unlike generic calendars, MyStudyLife understands:
- Classes that meet on specific days (MWF, TTh, etc.)
- Semester and term structures
- Assignment vs. exam vs. class distinctions
- Rotation schedules common in schools
- Academic year calendars
