## Overview

In December 2025, Kantata (formerly Mavenlink) introduced integrated e-invoicing capabilities through a strategic partnership with Avalara, marking a significant advancement in automated, compliant invoicing for professional services firms.

## Key Features

- **Automated E-Invoicing**: Seamlessly generates compliant electronic invoices from tracked time and project data
- **Multi-Region Compliance**: Supports invoicing requirements across different jurisdictions and countries
- **Avalara Integration**: Leverages Avalara's tax compliance and e-invoicing platform for global operations
- **Professional Services Focus**: Designed specifically for consulting, agency, and project-based businesses

## Benefits

### Compliance
Ensures invoicing meets regulatory requirements across different regions, particularly important for international operations and government contracting.

### Efficiency
Automates the invoicing process from time tracking through payment, reducing manual data entry and administrative overhead.

### Accuracy
Minimizes errors by directly connecting tracked time, expenses, and project data to invoice generation.

## Use Cases

Ideal for professional services organizations operating across multiple jurisdictions that need to maintain compliance while streamlining their time-to-cash cycle.

## Impact

This integration represents the growing trend toward fully automated, compliant financial workflows in professional services automation platforms.
