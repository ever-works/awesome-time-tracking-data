## Overview

When2Meet is a minimalist web tool that helps you find a time slot that works for a group without requiring registration or paid subscriptions. The tool is entirely free and funded by ads and donations from users.

## How It Works

1. Create an event and choose potential dates and a time range
2. Share a link with participants
3. Each participant highlights the blocks when they're available on a visual grid
4. Overlapping green areas show the best times when most people are available

## Key Features

- **No Registration Required**: Users can create and participate in polls without creating an account
- **Visual Grid Interface**: Intuitive visual representation of availability overlaps
- **Completely Free**: 100% free tier with no premium options, supported by ads and donations
- **Simple Sharing**: Easy link sharing for participant access
- **No Integrations**: Standalone tool without calendar integration
- **Quick Setup**: Create scheduling polls in seconds

## Use Cases

Ideal for:
- One-off group meetings and events
- Student group projects
- Informal gatherings
- Club and organization meetings
- Any scenario where simple group scheduling is needed without complex features

## Limitations

- No calendar integration with Google Calendar, Outlook, or other platforms
- Limited to basic group polling functionality
- Ad-supported interface
- No advanced automation or professional features

## When to Choose When2Meet

Best when you need a free, ultra-simple way to find a time that works for a group without the overhead of professional scheduling tools or calendar integrations.
