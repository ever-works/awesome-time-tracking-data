## Overview

Workday Scheduling and Labor Optimization is an enterprise workforce management feature announced in January 2026, designed for retail and hospitality companies. It provides demand forecasting, AI-powered scheduling, and comprehensive labor optimization for frontline workers.

## Key Features (2026)

**Demand Forecasting**
Available late January 2026, predicts staffing needs based on historical data, events, weather, and trends.

**Workday Frontline Agent**
AI-powered assistant available Spring 2026 for deskless worker support.

**Paradox Integration**
Hiring tools now generally available in Workday for streamlined recruitment.
