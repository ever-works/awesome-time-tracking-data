## Overview

Sage HRMS Time and Attendance is a comprehensive, state-of-the-art on-premise workforce management solution that enables organizations to collect, analyze, and control employees' attendance and labor data in real-time.

## Key Features

### Attendance Data Collection
- Effortlessly collects attendance data from biometric clocks, mobile or web punches, and timesheets
- Supports multiple data collection methods including touchless facial recognition, RFID badge, fingerprint, and web-based solutions
- Real-time synchronization of attendance data across all collection points

### Labor Compliance
- Automatically controls overtime labor costs while remaining fully compliant with labor regulations
- Built-in alert functionality to automatically notify supervisors of employees approaching overtime, those who haven't shown up for their shift, or who have punched in late
- Ensures policies and procedures are followed with automated enforcement

### Management Tools
- Real-time alerts for managers on policy and compliance violations
- Relieves administrative burdens by automating manual processes
- Effective job allocation and scheduling capabilities
- Advanced time-off tracking features

### Cost Savings
- Companies save an average of 3-5% in expenses through improved job allocation, scheduling, and time-off tracking
- Reduces administrative overhead through automated workflows
- Eliminates buddy punching and time theft through biometric verification

## Integration

Seamlessly integrates with Sage payroll and HR management systems for complete workforce management functionality.

## Platform

On-premise solution designed for Windows environments with support for cloud connectivity where needed.
