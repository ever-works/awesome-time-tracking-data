## Overview

GoCo offers a modern, streamlined, and easy-to-use HR platform designed specifically for small and mid-sized businesses. The platform combines time off software, time tracking, employee management, and payroll integration in an all-in-one solution that eliminates manual data entry and streamlines HR processes.

## Time Off & Leave Tracking

### Customization Options
- **Flexible Policies**: Customize PTO types, carryover rules, accruals, and tenure-based policies
- **Multiple Time Off Types**: Create and track various leave types including PTO, Jury Duty, Bereavement, and Work From Home
- **Policy Templates**: Select from multiple built-in time off types or craft custom policies that fit your company culture

### Employee Self-Service
- **Request Management**: Staff members can submit time off requests, view PTO balances, and track request status in real-time
- **Mobile Access**: Employees can easily submit requests and check balances from GoCo's modern mobile app
- **Balance Visibility**: Employees can see their current PTO balances and how requests will impact future balances

### Manager Approvals
- **Contextual Information**: Managers see available balances, hours used year-to-date, and who else is out at the same time
- **Quick Approvals**: Streamlined approval workflow for faster decision-making
- **Overlap Visibility**: See team availability to prevent scheduling conflicts

## Integration Capabilities

### Seamless Data Flow
- **Payroll Integration**: Time off data automatically flows to payroll systems
- **Schedule Synchronization**: Time off requests populate schedules automatically
- **Timesheet Integration**: Time off and time tracking features work together, with requests automatically populating on timesheets

### Time Tracking Integration
Employees can instantly log time off requests on time sheets, and approved requests will automatically populate with the time already added, eliminating double entry.

## Reporting & Compliance

### Reporting Tools
- **Time Off Taken Reports**: Show how much time each employee has taken for specific periods
- **Time Off Balances Reports**: Display breakdown of each employee's accrued balances on particular dates
- **Custom Reporting**: Generate reports for various compliance and management needs

### Compliance Features
- **COVID-19 Leave Tracking**: Track time off requests related to COVID-19 paid-leave rules
- **Digital Documentation**: All requests, approvals, and supporting documents are collected and tracked digitally for compliance
- **Audit Trail**: Complete history of all time off transactions
