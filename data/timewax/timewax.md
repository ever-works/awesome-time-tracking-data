## Overview

Timewax offers a complete solution for efficient resource planning that optimizes capacity, deadlines, and processes with reliable resource planning software designed for fast-evolving service organizations.

## Features

- **Dual Planning Approach**: Supports both high-level capacity planning and detailed short-term scheduling in one system
- **Visual Planning**: Complex schedules presented in a single, visual overview that automatically keeps everyone up-to-date
- **Skills-Based Assignment**: Built-in skill matrix allows defining skills and proficiency levels, matching qualified employees to appropriate work
- **AI Planning Agent (Tim)**: AI assistant with voice, avatar, and conversational interface that handles complex multi-variable optimization
- **Capacity Forecasting**: Predictive insights into future capacity to accurately forecast staffing needs and identify growth opportunities
- **Service Delivery Scheduling**: Schedule project timelines, service intervals, and resources to meet customer expectations
- **Utilization Analysis**: Analyze productivity, forecast workload, and identify staffing issues
- **Integration Capabilities**: Seamlessly integrates with existing systems for synchronized data
