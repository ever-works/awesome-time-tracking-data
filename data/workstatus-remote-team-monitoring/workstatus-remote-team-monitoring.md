## Overview

Workstatus is an all-in-one platform for tracking time, managing projects, automating attendance & payroll, and optimizing workforce productivity with enterprise-grade precision, designed specifically for remote and hybrid teams.

## Time Tracking & Monitoring

### Activity Monitoring
- Track time spent on websites and apps
- Measure keyboard and mouse activity levels
- Capture optional screenshots of work
- Real-time productivity insights

### Online Timesheets
Workstatus timer and online timesheets help:
- Review time spent on every task
- Track task completion
- Leverage productivity intelligence
- Monitor focus and efficiency

## Location & Attendance Tracking

### GPS Tracking
Real-time insights into work locations:
- Track employee locations in real-time
- Verify work-from-home compliance
- Monitor field worker locations

### Selfie-Based Validation
Instantly verify WFH employees' attendance:
- Selfie capture on clock-in/out
- Secure attendance verification
- Accurate clock-in validation
- Prevents buddy punching
