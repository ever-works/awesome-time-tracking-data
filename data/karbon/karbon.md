## Overview

Karbon is a comprehensive practice management platform built specifically for accounting firms and professional services. In Karbon, teams can track time, estimate budgets, bill and invoice clients, and collect payment all in one single location.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track billable and non-billable time with integration to client work and projects
- **Budget Management**: Set and monitor project budgets with real-time tracking against actuals
- **Invoicing**: Generate professional invoices based on tracked time and fixed-fee arrangements
- **Work Management**: Manage client work, tasks, and deadlines in a unified workflow
- **Client Collaboration**: Communicate with clients and share documents securely
- **Team Capacity Planning**: View team workload and allocate resources effectively
- **Automated Workflows**: Create repeatable processes for common accounting tasks
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Comprehensive reports on firm performance, profitability, and utilization
