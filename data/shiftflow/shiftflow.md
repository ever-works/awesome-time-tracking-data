## Overview

ShiftFlow is a comprehensive restaurant scheduling platform that helps managers create optimized schedules, track time and attendance, control labor costs, and communicate effectively with their teams through intuitive mobile and web applications.

## Key Features

### Smart Scheduling
- **AI-Powered Recommendations**: Get scheduling suggestions based on sales forecasts and historical data
- **Drag-and-Drop Builder**: Intuitive interface for creating and editing schedules
- **Auto-Fill Shifts**: Automatically assign shifts based on availability and qualifications
- **Template Scheduling**: Save recurring schedules as templates
- **Availability Management**: Collect employee availability and preferences
- **Shift Marketplace**: Employees can pick up open shifts or request shift trades
- **Conflict Prevention**: Automatic detection of scheduling conflicts
- **Multi-Location Support**: Manage schedules across multiple restaurant locations

### Time & Attendance
- **Mobile Clock-In/Out**: Employees track time from their smartphones
- **QR Code Clock-In**: Scan QR codes at workstations for quick check-in
- **Break Tracking**: Monitor and enforce required meal and rest breaks
- **Overtime Alerts**: Real-time notifications when approaching overtime
- **Late/No-Show Tracking**: Monitor tardiness and attendance issues
- **Timesheet Approval**: Manager review and edit capabilities
- **PTO Tracking**: Manage paid time off and accruals

### Labor Cost Management
- **Real-Time Labor Tracking**: Monitor labor costs as they happen during shifts
- **Sales Integration**: Import sales data to calculate labor percentage
- **Budget Forecasting**: Set labor budgets and track performance
- **What-If Scenarios**: Test different scheduling options before publishing
- **Cost per Shift**: Calculate exact labor costs for each scheduled shift
- **Projected vs. Actual**: Compare planned labor to actual hours worked

### Team Communication
- **Team Chat**: Built-in messaging for shift coordination
- **Schedule Notifications**: Automatic alerts for new schedules and changes
- **Announcement Board**: Share updates, policies, and important information
- **Shift Reminders**: Automated reminders before scheduled shifts
- **Document Library**: Store training materials and handbooks

### Mobile Experience
- **Employee Mobile App**: View schedules, request time off, trade shifts
- **Manager Mobile Dashboard**: Approve requests and monitor labor on-the-go
- **Push Notifications**: Instant alerts for schedule changes and requests
- **Offline Access**: View schedules even without internet connection
