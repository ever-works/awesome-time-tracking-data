## Overview

Freedom is a distraction-blocking app that helps you stay focused by blocking distracting websites, apps, and even the entire internet across all your devices simultaneously.

## Features

- **Cross-Device Sync** - Block distractions on Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android simultaneously
- **Website Blocking** - Block specific websites or entire categories
- **App Blocking** - Block distracting desktop and mobile applications
- **Scheduled Sessions** - Schedule recurring focus sessions in advance
- **Locked Mode** - Prevent yourself from bypassing blocks
- **Block Lists** - Create custom lists of sites/apps to block
- **Ambient Sounds** - Built-in focus sounds and music
- **Session History** - Track your focus sessions over time
- **Recurring Schedules** - Set up daily or weekly blocking schedules

## Blocking Options

### Website Blocking
- Block specific URLs or entire domains
- Block all websites except a whitelist
- Schedule blocks for specific times

### App Blocking
- Block desktop applications
- Block mobile apps (iOS and Android)
- Sync blocks across devices

### Internet Blocking
- Block entire internet connection
- Perfect for offline deep work
- Scheduled internet-free time

## Use Cases

- Deep work sessions requiring no distractions
- Studying and exam preparation
- Writing and creative work
- Breaking social media addiction
- Creating work-life boundaries
- Improving sleep by blocking devices at night
