## Overview

The MiGoals Time Block Desk Pad keeps everything that matters to you right before your eyes, going beyond just mapping out daily appointments. The Time Block Desk Pad ensures you make every hour count by allowing users to block out time for important tasks.

## Key Sections

- **Daily Goals**: Set intentions for each day
- **Key Tasks**: List up to 10 most important tasks
- **Schedule**: Daily schedule from 4am to midnight with hourly time blocks
- **Habit Tracker**: Monitor daily habits and routines
- **Notes Section**: Additional space for thoughts and reminders
- **Gratitude Prompts**: End-of-day reflection

## Specifications

- **Size**: B5 format (180mm x 240mm)
- **Pages**: 50 sheets
- **Paper**: 100gsm FSC-certified paper
- **Backing**: Cardboard backing for stability
- **Design**: Made in Melbourne, Australia

## Philosophy

The core philosophy behind the Time Block Desk Pad invites users to plan their day with care to focus on making progress each day. It helps create consistent positive habits that maximise productivity and motivation while easily seeing key tasks at a glance.

## Use Case

With space to plan your schedule hour by hour and a to-do list, habit tracker, notes and more, it keeps priorities right in front of you throughout the workday.
