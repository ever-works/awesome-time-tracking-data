Deltek Replicon achieved FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program) Moderate Authorization, demonstrating compliance with federal security standards for cloud services.

- Approved for government agency use
- Validated third-party security assessment
- Continuous monitoring and compliance
- Suitable for sensitive but unclassified data
