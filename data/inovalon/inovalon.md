## Overview

Inovalon's workforce management software helps healthcare teams simplify scheduling, time tracking, credentialing, and agency use—reducing burnout, avoiding overtime, and giving staff the tools to own their shifts. The mobile-friendly solution is used by many of the top 25 U.S. health systems.

## Key Products and Features

### Schedule Management
- All-in-one workforce management solution for building smart schedules across teams, job levels, and locations
- Automates scheduling and simplifies shift coverage
- Reduces overtime and labor costs
- Mobile-friendly interface for staff and managers

### Workforce Intelligence
- Transforms complex staffing data into clear insights
- Prevents staff burnout through data-driven scheduling
- Controls costs with real-time analytics
- Improves patient care through optimal staffing levels

### Healthcare Agency Management
- 24/7 access to manage agency relationships
- Track costs associated with agency staffing
- Keep entire workforce aligned
- Reduce reliance on external staffing

### Credential Management
- Collects, organizes, and tracks staff licenses and certifications
- Monitors expiration dates automatically
- Maintains quality care standards
- Ensures position and scheduling requirements are met
