## Overview

Project profitability tracking uses time tracking data combined with rates and budgets to monitor project financial health in real-time. This enables proactive management of project costs and profitability.

## Key Components

### Time Cost Calculation
- Track hours by team member
- Apply individual or role-based rates
- Calculate total labor costs
- Include overhead and expenses

### Revenue Tracking
- Compare costs to project budget
- Track billable vs. non-billable time
- Monitor scope creep impact
- Calculate profit margins

### Alert Systems
- Warnings at budget thresholds (e.g., 75%)
- Real-time burn rate monitoring
- Projected overrun forecasts
- Automatic stakeholder notifications

## Benefits

- Early detection of cost overruns
- Data-driven project decisions
- Improved future estimating
- Better resource allocation
- Protected profit margins

## Use Cases

- Agencies managing client projects
- Consulting firms tracking engagements
- Software development teams
- Professional services organizations
- Any project-based business
