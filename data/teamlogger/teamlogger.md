## Overview

TeamLogger is an employee monitoring and time tracking solution designed for managing remote and distributed teams, offering optional screenshots, activity monitoring, and comprehensive productivity analytics.

## Features

- **Automatic Time Tracking**: Desktop app tracks work hours automatically
- **Optional Screenshots**: Configurable screenshot capture for transparency
- **Activity Monitoring**: Track keyboard and mouse activity levels
- **Application & Website Tracking**: Monitor which apps and sites are used
- **Productivity Categorization**: Classify activities as productive or distracting
- **Real-Time Dashboard**: See team activity and status in real-time
- **Detailed Reports**: Comprehensive analytics on time usage and productivity
- **Project & Task Tracking**: Assign time to specific projects
- **Idle Time Detection**: Automatically detect and handle idle periods
