## Overview

Shyft is a mobile scheduling app designed to give employees control over their work schedules through an easy-to-use shift swapping and marketplace system.

## Key Features

- **Shift Marketplace**: Post and pick up shifts through a centralized marketplace
- **Shift Swapping**: Request to swap shifts with coworkers directly from the mobile app
- **Team Messaging**: Communicate with team members about schedule changes and shift coverage
- **Schedule Management**: View upcoming shifts and manage availability
- **Push Notifications**: Receive alerts about shift opportunities, swap requests, and schedule changes
- **Time Off Requests**: Submit and track PTO and time off requests

## How Shift Swapping Works

1. Employee posts a shift they need to give away
2. Qualified coworkers receive notifications about available shifts
3. Coworkers can claim the shift or make a swap proposal
4. Manager approves or denies the swap request
5. Schedules automatically update for all parties

## Benefits

**For Employees:**
- Flexible scheduling and work-life balance
- Easy shift coverage when conflicts arise
- Direct communication with coworkers
- Opportunity to pick up extra shifts for additional income

**For Managers:**
- Reduced time spent manually managing shift changes
- Better shift coverage and fewer no-shows
- Employee empowerment leads to higher satisfaction
- Automated approval workflows
