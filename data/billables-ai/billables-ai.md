## Overview

Billables AI is an automated legal time-tracking solution that uses artificial intelligence to automatically capture billable time for attorneys. It eliminates the need for manual time entry by tracking work in the background and intelligently categorizing activities.

## Features

- **AI Time Capture**: Automatically tracks time across all applications and documents
- **Smart Categorization**: AI categorizes work by client and matter
- **Email Tracking**: Tracks time spent reading and writing emails
- **Document Time**: Captures time spent in Word, PDF, and other documents
- **Browser Tracking**: Tracks legal research and web-based work
- **One-Click Timesheets**: Generate timesheets with a single click
- **ABA Codes**: Supports American Bar Association task codes
- **Integration**: Exports to major practice management systems
- **Privacy-Focused**: All tracking happens locally on your device
- **Memory Gaps**: Fill in forgotten time with activity history

## Benefits

- Captures 100% of billable time without manual entry
- Reduces time leakage and revenue loss
- Eliminates end-of-day time reconstruction
- Increases billable hours recovery
- Reduces administrative burden
