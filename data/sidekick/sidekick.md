## Overview

Sidekick is an AI-powered scheduling assistant designed to streamline calendar coordination and meeting scheduling for teams and professionals. The platform uses artificial intelligence to automatically find optimal meeting times, manage calendar conflicts, and handle scheduling requests through natural language interactions.

## Key Features

- **AI Meeting Scheduling** - Automatically finds the best meeting times across multiple calendars and time zones
- **Natural Language Processing** - Schedule meetings using conversational language without manual calendar checking
- **Team Coordination** - Manages group scheduling and finds times that work for all participants
- **Calendar Integration** - Connects with Google Calendar, Outlook, and other major calendar platforms
- **Automated Workflows** - Handles booking requests, sends reminders, and manages rescheduling automatically
- **Free Plan Available** - Accessible entry point for individuals and small teams

## Use Cases

- Teams managing frequent cross-functional meetings
- Sales professionals coordinating client calls
- Recruiters scheduling candidate interviews
- Executives managing complex calendars
- Remote teams across multiple time zones

## 2026 Market Position

Sidekick is recognized as one of the leading AI scheduling assistants in 2026, competing with tools like Reclaim AI, Clockwise, and Calendly. The platform differentiates itself through its conversational interface and focus on automated scheduling workflows.

## Pricing

Sidekick offers a free plan for basic scheduling needs, with premium plans adding advanced features like team scheduling, analytics, and custom branding.
