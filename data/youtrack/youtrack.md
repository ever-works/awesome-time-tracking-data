## Overview

YouTrack is a comprehensive project management platform from JetBrains, designed for software development teams with integrated issue tracking, agile boards, and time management features that work seamlessly with development workflows.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track time estimates and spent time directly in issues
- **Work Items**: Track issues, tasks, bugs, and features with custom workflows
- **Agile Boards**: Scrum and Kanban boards with sprint planning
- **Burndown Charts**: Visualize sprint progress based on time or story points
- **Gantt Charts**: Timeline view of projects and dependencies
- **Custom Workflows**: Design workflows that match your processes
- **Knowledge Base**: Built-in documentation and article management
- **Reports**: Time reports, burndown, cumulative flow, and custom reports
- **VCS Integration**: Connect with GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket for commit tracking
- **IDE Integration**: Track time from JetBrains IDEs like IntelliJ IDEA
- **Command Window**: Quick actions using keyboard shortcuts and commands
- **Mobile Apps**: Manage issues and track time on iOS and Android
- **Notifications**: Smart notifications for issue updates and mentions

## Developer-Focused

Built by JetBrains specifically for development teams, with deep integration into their suite of IDEs and understanding of software development workflows.

## Benefits

Combines issue tracking, agile project management, and time tracking in one tool designed for developers, with seamless integration into development environments.

## Pricing

Free for up to 10 users. Paid plans for larger teams with cloud or on-premise deployment options.
