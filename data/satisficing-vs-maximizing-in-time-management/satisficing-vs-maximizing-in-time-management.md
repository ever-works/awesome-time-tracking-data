## Overview

Satisficing accepts adequate solutions rather than exhaustively seeking perfect ones.

## Concept

- Satisficing = Satisfy + Suffice
- Set minimum acceptable standard
- Accept first option meeting standard
- Move on without additional search

## Time Benefits

- Reduces decision time dramatically
- Prevents analysis paralysis
- Saves cognitive energy for important choices
- Enables progress over perfection

## When to Satisfice

- Low-stakes decisions
- Reversible choices
- Diminishing returns evident
- Time-constrained situations

## When to Maximize

- High-impact decisions
- Irreversible choices
- Core values involved
