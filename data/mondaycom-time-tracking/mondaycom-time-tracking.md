## Overview

Monday.com includes time tracking capabilities within its Work OS platform. The integration combines visual project management with time tracking, allowing teams to track hours directly on project boards and tasks without switching between tools.

## Time Tracking Features

- **In-Board Tracking**: Track time directly on monday.com boards
- **Time Tracking Column**: Add time tracking to any board
- **Manual Entry**: Log time for tasks and projects
- **Timers**: Start/stop timers for active work
- **Time Estimates**: Set estimated hours for tasks
- **Progress Tracking**: Compare actual vs estimated time
- **Timeline View**: Visualize time allocation
- **Automations**: Automate time tracking workflows

## Integration with Work OS

- **Unified Platform**: Time tracking + project management + collaboration
- **Visual Boards**: See time data on kanban, gantt, calendar views
- **Custom Workflows**: Build time tracking into your processes
- **Dashboards**: Visualize time metrics alongside other KPIs
- **Reporting**: Time reports integrated with project reports

## Project Management

- **Visual Workflows**: Kanban, gantt, timeline, calendar views
- **Automations**: Automate repetitive tasks
- **Integrations**: 200+ app integrations
- **Collaboration**: Comments, files, updates on items
