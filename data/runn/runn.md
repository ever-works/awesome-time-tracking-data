## Overview

Runn is real-time resource planning software with leadership-ready dashboards that align capacity to strategy. With over 53,000 projects staffed, 5.9 million days scheduled, and 38,000 people managed, Runn is a trusted partner in resource management.

## Features

### Resource Planning
- Full visualization of resource allocation
- Plan workloads by skills and availability
- Instantly see team availability and workload on projects
- Predict resource availability to meet upcoming demand
- Maximize billable time
- Prevent team burnout

### Capacity Planning
- Forecast resources using placeholders and tentative work
- Understand team capacity
- Plan for potential hires or reallocations
- Strategic alignment of capacity to needs
- Predict demand and assess resource needs

### Project Management
- Project forecasting
- Keep projects on track
- Resource allocation optimization
- Project staffing workflow

### Financial Reporting

- Budget tracking

- Revenue forecasting

### Analytics & Dashboards
- Leadership-ready dashboards
- Real-time reporting
- Workload visualization
- Utilization tracking
- Comprehensive project views
