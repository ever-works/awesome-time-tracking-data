## Overview

The Recalculate feature is FlowSavvy's signature capability that allows users to instantly reschedule their entire task list with a single action when plans change, tasks are incomplete, or priorities shift.

## How It Works

### The Problem It Solves

Traditional time blocking requires manually dragging and rearranging time blocks when:
- You don't complete a task as planned
- Urgent items arise and disrupt your schedule
- You fall behind on your daily plan
- Priorities change mid-day or mid-week

This manual rearrangement is tedious and time-consuming, often taking 10-15 minutes or more.

### The Recalculate Solution

**One-Click Rescheduling**: Hit the "Recalculate" button and FlowSavvy instantly:
1. Identifies all incomplete tasks
2. Considers current priorities and deadlines
3. Analyzes available calendar time
4. Reschedules everything into optimal time slots
5. Updates your calendar view immediately

## Key Features

### Intelligent Rescheduling

FlowSavvy's algorithm considers:
- **Task Priorities**: Higher priority items get earlier/better time slots
- **Deadlines**: Ensures deadline-critical work is scheduled with buffer
- **Task Duration**: Matches task length to available blocks
- **Scheduling Hours**: Respects your work hours and personal time preferences
- **Existing Events**: Works around fixed calendar events
- **Task Dependencies**: Can respect ordering requirements

### Automatic Updates

FlowSavvy automatically reschedules tasks whenever you make changes:
- Move a meeting: Tasks adjust around it
- Complete a task early: Remaining tasks shift forward
- Add a new urgent task: Everything reorganizes to fit it in

## Use Cases

**Daily Replanning**:
- Morning: Plan your day
- Afternoon: Hit recalculate to adjust for morning progress
- Evening: Reschedule incomplete items to tomorrow

**Weekly Recovery**:
- Monday: Recalculate to redistribute Friday's incomplete tasks
- Wednesday: Adjust for mid-week priority changes
- Friday: Reschedule to next week what won't finish

**Emergency Handling**:
- Urgent task arrives: Add it, hit recalculate, everything adjusts
- Meeting runs long: Recalculate redistributes afternoon tasks
- Sick day: Recalculate pushes everything forward

**Akiflow**: Has "Replan" feature with similar functionality
**Sunsama**: More manual dragging required
**Traditional Time Blocking**: Completely manual rearrangement

FlowSavvy differentiates by making rescheduling explicit, visible, and user-initiated rather than invisible background changes.

## User Experience

The Recalculate button typically appears:
- In the main toolbar
- When incomplete tasks are detected
- After calendar changes
- As a keyboard shortcut for power users

Visual feedback shows tasks being reassigned to new time slots in real-time.

## Limitations

- Works best when tasks have accurate duration estimates
- May create imperfect schedules if calendar is extremely constrained
- Doesn't account for energy levels or task difficulty
- May schedule less-preferred times if that's all that's available
