## Overview

Resource Guru is a resource management software trusted by 60,000+ people at companies including NASA, Accenture, and CNN, providing fast and simple scheduling for people, equipment, and other resources.

## Key Features

### Scheduling
- Simple drag-and-drop scheduling interface
- Clash management identifies conflicts
- Calendar view with real-time updates
- Waiting list for unresolved bookings
- Advanced filtering by skills, location, department

### Time Management
- Timesheets auto-fill from scheduled work
- One-click timesheet submission
- Built-in approval workflows
- Resource utilization tracking
- Billable vs non-billable time
- Overtime and time-off monitoring

### Leave Management
- Leave schedules integrated into planning
- Complete visibility of availability
- Prevents scheduling conflicts with time off
