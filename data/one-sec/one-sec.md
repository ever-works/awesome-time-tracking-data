## Overview

One Sec takes a fundamentally different approach to screen time management by delaying access to apps instead of blocking them completely. The app forces users to take a deep breath before opening addictive apps, creating intentional friction that reduces mindless scrolling.

## Features

- **Breathing Exercise**: Requires a one-second deep breath before accessing selected apps
- **Apple Shortcuts Integration**: Leverages iOS Shortcuts for powerful blocking capabilities
- **App Selection**: Choose which apps require the breathing pause
- **Usage Statistics**: Track how the intervention affects your screen time patterns
- **Focus Mode Support**: Integrates with iOS Focus Modes for contextual app management
- **Customizable Delays**: Adjust the breathing time and intervention strength

## How It Works

Instead of completely blocking distracting apps, one sec adds a small layer of friction by requiring you to take a conscious breath and moment of reflection before the app opens. This interrupts the automatic habit loop and gives you a chance to make an intentional choice.

- Users report cutting social media usage by 57% on average
- Less expensive than most app blockers

- Builds awareness and mindfulness around phone usage
