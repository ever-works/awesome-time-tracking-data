## Overview

Endorphin is an AI-powered focus music app that generates personalized, adaptive soundscapes designed to enhance concentration, creativity, or relaxation based on your current needs and productivity patterns.

## Features

- **AI-Generated Music** - Personalized soundscapes created by artificial intelligence
- **Adaptive Audio** - Music adjusts based on task type and time of day
- **Learning Algorithm** - Improves recommendations based on your patterns
- **Task-Specific Modes** - Different audio profiles for focus, creativity, relaxation
- **Pomodoro Integration** - Built-in Pomodoro timer with music transitions
- **Productivity Tracking** - Monitor focus time and effectiveness
- **No Lyrics** - Instrumental music designed not to distract
- **Offline Mode** - Download tracks for offline listening

## How It Works

1. Select your current goal (focus, creativity, or relaxation)
2. AI generates appropriate soundscape
3. Work while music adapts to maintain optimal state
4. App learns from your productivity patterns
5. Future sessions use improved personalization

## The Science

Endorphin uses neuroscience research on:
- How different frequencies affect brain states
- The relationship between music tempo and productivity
- Individual differences in audio preferences for focus

## Use Cases

Ideal for knowledge workers, creatives, and anyone who finds background music helpful for focus but struggles with traditional music's distracting elements.
