## Overview

ServiceM8's Automated Timesheet Sync add-on eliminates double data entry by automatically exporting staff Clock-On/Off times to MYOB, Xero, and QuickBooks Online as employee timesheets.

## Features

### Automated Sync
- **One-Click Export**: Send timesheets with a single click
- **Scheduled Sync**: Automatically sync at set intervals
- **Real-Time Updates**: Changes reflect in accounting software immediately
- **Batch Processing**: Export multiple timesheets at once

### Multi-Platform Support
- **Xero Integration**: Direct sync to Xero Payroll
- **QuickBooks Online**: Export to QuickBooks Online timesheets
- **MYOB**: Integration with MYOB accounting

### Time Tracking
- **Clock-On/Off**: Simple employee time clock in ServiceM8
- **Job Association**: Time linked to specific jobs and customers
- **Break Tracking**: Record break times
- **GPS Location**: Capture location data with clock events

### Data Mapping
- **Employee Mapping**: Match ServiceM8 staff to accounting software employees
- **Pay Item Mapping**: Map time to correct payroll categories
- **Job Costing**: Link time to jobs in accounting system
- **Custom Fields**: Transfer additional data as needed

## Benefits

- **Eliminates Double Entry**: No manual re-entering of timesheet data
- **Reduces Errors**: Automated sync prevents transcription mistakes
- **Saves Time**: Hours saved each pay period
- **Improves Accuracy**: Real field data flows directly to payroll
- **Better Job Costing**: Accurate labor costs by job

## How It Works

1. **Field workers clock on/off in ServiceM8** mobile app at job sites
2. **Time data is captured** with job association and GPS location
3. **Add-on syncs automatically** or on-demand to accounting software
4. **Timesheets appear** in Xero, QuickBooks, or MYOB ready for payroll
5. **Payroll processing** proceeds with accurate, verified hours

## Target Users

- Field service businesses using ServiceM8
- Companies processing payroll in Xero, QuickBooks, or MYOB
- Service contractors needing job costing
- Businesses wanting to eliminate timesheet double-entry

## Requirements

- Active ServiceM8 subscription
- Xero, QuickBooks Online, or MYOB account
- ServiceM8 Automated Timesheet Sync add-on
