## Overview

Office Timeline is a project timeline and Gantt chart maker that works directly within Microsoft PowerPoint, enabling project managers to create professional project visuals and timelines with ease.

## Key Features

- **PowerPoint Integration**: Creates timelines and Gantt charts directly in PowerPoint
- **Professional Templates**: Library of professional timeline templates
- **Import Capabilities**: Import data from Excel, Project, and other tools
- **Automatic Updates**: Timelines update automatically when data changes
- **Client-Ready Output**: Creates presentation-ready project visuals

## Recent Development

In July 2025, Office Timeline announced its acquisition of Timeneye, a time tracking platform, to expand operational capabilities for project managers. This integration combines Office Timeline's visualization capabilities with Timeneye's time tracking features to provide the complete story of projects from initial planning through final delivery.

## Platform

Works as an add-in for Microsoft PowerPoint on Windows and Mac, with web-based options also available.

## Target Users

Project managers, program managers, executives, and consultants who need to create professional project timelines for stakeholder presentations and client meetings.

## Pricing

Subscription-based pricing with different tiers for individuals and teams, including a free version with limited features.
