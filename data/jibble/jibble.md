## Overview

Jibble is a free time and attendance tracking app that offers unlimited users, facial recognition, GPS tracking, and comprehensive timesheets. It's designed for businesses that need accurate time tracking without subscription costs.

## Features

- **Facial Recognition**: Selfie-based clock-in to prevent buddy punching
- **GPS Tracking**: Location-verified time entries
- **Unlimited Users**: Truly free for unlimited team members
- **Timesheets**: Automatic timesheet generation
- **Attendance Tracking**: Track employee attendance and absences
- **Project Time Tracking**: Assign time to projects and activities
- **Kiosk Mode**: Shared tablet for team clock-in
- **Geofencing**: Restrict clock-ins to specific locations
- **PTO Management**: Track vacation and sick leave
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive time and attendance reports
- **Integrations**: Export to payroll systems

## Free Forever

Jibble's free plan includes unlimited users, unlimited time tracking, and all core features. Premium add-ons available for advanced features.

## Pricing

Free forever for unlimited users with core features. Premium add-ons start at $2.49/user/month for advanced scheduling and reporting.
