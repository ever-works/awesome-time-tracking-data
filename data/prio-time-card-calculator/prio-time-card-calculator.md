## Overview

Prio Time Card Calculator is a straightforward, free online tool for calculating work hours with built-in lunch break support. The calculator focuses on simplicity and ease of use for quick timesheet calculations.

## Key Features

**Lunch Break Support:**
- Built-in lunch break deductions
- Automatic break time calculations
- Customizable break lengths
- Accurate net work hours

**Time Card Functionality:**
- Clock in/out time entry
- Daily hours calculation
- Weekly totals
- Overtime tracking

**Simple Interface:**
- Clean, intuitive design
- Easy data entry
- No complicated settings
- Instant results

**Free Access:**
- No registration required
- No fees or subscriptions
- Unlimited use
- No advertisements

## Use Cases

- Employees verifying paychecks
- Small businesses without automated time tracking
- Freelancers logging hours
- Quick ad-hoc time calculations

## Platform

Web-based calculator accessible from desktop or mobile browsers.
