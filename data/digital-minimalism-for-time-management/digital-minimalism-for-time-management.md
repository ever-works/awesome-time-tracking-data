## Overview

Digital Minimalism applies minimalist philosophy to technology use for time reclamation.

## 30-Day Declutter Process

1. Define core values
2. Take 30-day break from optional technologies
3. Rediscover offline activities
4. Selectively reintroduce tools
5. Only keep what significantly serves values

## Time Benefits

- Reclaim 2-4 hours daily
- Eliminate fake urgency
- Reduce context switching
- Increase deep work capacity
