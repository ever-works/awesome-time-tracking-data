## Overview

Worklytics is a privacy-conscious workplace analytics platform that helps organizations understand how work happens across their teams. Unlike traditional employee monitoring software, Worklytics focuses on organizational patterns and collaboration dynamics through fully anonymized data, making it a privacy-first alternative to tools like ActivTrak, Prodoscore, and ProHance.

## Features

### Privacy-First Architecture
- **Full Anonymization**: Individual employee data is completely anonymized, not just pseudonymized
- **Aggregate-Only Reporting**: All insights are presented at team and organizational levels
- **No Individual Tracking**: Cannot identify specific employee activities or behaviors
- **Transparent Data Collection**: Clear documentation of what data is collected and how it's used
- **GDPR & CCPA Compliant**: Meets strict privacy regulations

### Collaboration Analytics
- **Meeting Culture Analysis**: Understand meeting frequency, duration, and participation patterns
- **Communication Patterns**: Analyze email and messaging trends across the organization
- **Cross-Team Collaboration**: Identify how different departments work together
- **Network Analysis**: Visualize organizational communication networks
- **Siloed Work Detection**: Identify teams or individuals working in isolation

### Workplace Insights
- Focus time availability and fragmentation
- After-hours work patterns
- Manager span and engagement
- Meeting load distribution
- Collaboration overload indicators
- Organizational responsiveness metrics

### Integration Capabilities
- **Microsoft 365**: Email, Calendar, Teams data
- **Google Workspace**: Gmail, Calendar, Meet analytics
- **Slack**: Channel and messaging patterns
- **Zoom**: Meeting and video collaboration data
- **Other Platforms**: Extensible integration framework

### Dashboard & Reporting
- Executive-level dashboards for organizational health
- Team-level insights for managers
- Trend analysis and historical comparisons
- Custom report builder
- Scheduled automated reports
- Data export capabilities

## Use Cases

Track overall collaboration patterns and identify potential burnout risks through after-hours work and meeting load analysis.

### Hybrid Work Optimization
Understand how distributed teams collaborate and ensure remote employees remain connected and engaged.

### Meeting Culture Reform
Quantify meeting effectiveness and identify opportunities to reclaim time for focused work.

### Merger & Acquisition Integration
Monitor how newly combined organizations are collaborating and integrating.

### Change Management
Measure the impact of organizational changes on collaboration patterns and team dynamics.

## Key Differentiators

### vs. ActivTrak
ActivTrak monitors individual computer activity; Worklytics analyzes organizational collaboration patterns with full anonymization.

### vs. Prodoscore
Prodoscore creates individual productivity scores; Worklytics provides aggregate organizational insights without individual metrics.

### vs. ProHance
ProHance focuses on operational efficiency with some individual tracking; Worklytics emphasizes privacy-first organizational analytics.
