## Overview

Flora is a free productivity app that helps you stay off your phone, clear to-do lists, and build positive, life-changing habits. Whenever you want to make progress towards your goals, grow plants in Flora! It's one of the most innovative distraction-free apps that makes blocking sites rewarding.

## Features

- **Plant Growing**: Grow virtual plants by staying focused and off your phone
- **Social Accountability**: Create rooms with friends to grow plants together
- **Group Sessions**: Share focus sessions with friends for mutual accountability
- **To-Do Lists**: Integrate task management with focus sessions
- **Habit Building**: Track daily habits and build streaks
- **Real Tree Planting**: Plant real trees through partnerships with tree-planting organizations
- **Statistics**: Detailed analytics on focus time and productivity
- **Customizable Timers**: Set focus durations that work for your schedule
- **App Blocking**: Block distracting apps during focus sessions
- **Plant Collection**: Unlock different plant species as you progress
- **Motivational Quotes**: Encouraging messages to maintain focus
- **Free Core Features**: No paywall for essential functionality

## Social Features

Flora's standout feature is social accountability:
- **Rooms**: Create or join rooms with friends
- **Shared Goals**: Work toward collective focus time
- **Peer Pressure**: If one person leaves, everyone's plant dies
- **Community**: Connect with other users pursuing similar goals

## Real-World Impact

Flora partners with tree-planting organizations to convert virtual achievements into real environmental impact. Users can use earned coins to sponsor the planting of actual trees around the world.

## Pricing

- **Free**: Core features available at no cost
- **Premium**: Optional paid features for enhanced customization
- One of the few truly free productivity apps with full functionality

## Best For

- Students who work better with friends
- People motivated by social accountability
- Teams wanting to build focus culture
- Users seeking a free alternative to Forest
- Anyone who wants their productivity to benefit the environment

## Platform Availability

- iOS (iPhone and iPad)
- Android
- Regular updates and active development

## Key Differentiator

While similar to Forest, Flora's emphasis on social features and group accountability makes it unique. The ability to grow plants with friends adds a collaborative dimension to focus sessions.
