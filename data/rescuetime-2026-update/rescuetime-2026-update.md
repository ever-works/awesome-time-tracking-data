## Overview

RescueTime is a pioneering automatic time tracking tool that has evolved significantly by 2026, now offering enhanced AI-powered insights, focus session management, and productivity coaching features.

## Core Features

- **Automatic Tracking**: Monitors all applications and websites used on computer and mobile devices
- **Productivity Scoring**: Assigns productivity scores to different activities
- **Category Classification**: Automatically categorizes activities (Very Productive, Productive, Neutral, Distracting, Very Distracting)
- **Detailed Reports**: Daily, weekly, and monthly productivity reports
- **Goals and Alerts**: Set productivity goals and receive alerts
- **FocusTime**: Blocks distracting websites during dedicated focus sessions
- **Offline Time**: Tracks time away from devices
- **Mobile Tracking**: iOS and Android apps for comprehensive tracking

## 2026 Limitations (Important)

- **No Manual Logging**: RescueTime does not allow manual time entry, which is critical for service-based businesses
- **Individual Focus**: Lacks workforce-level features like employee timesheets or team productivity comparisons
- **No Payroll Integration**: Not designed for business billing or payroll needs
- **Privacy Concerns**: Some users uncomfortable with comprehensive activity monitoring

## Best Use Cases

- Individual professionals wanting self-awareness about time usage
- Remote workers tracking personal productivity
- Anyone struggling with digital distractions
- People wanting to understand their work patterns

## Not Ideal For

- Businesses needing billable hours tracking
- Teams requiring shared time tracking
- Companies needing payroll integration
- Project-based time allocation

## Popular Alternatives (2026)

- **ActivityWatch**: Free, open-source alternative
- **Flowace**: Better for teams and enterprises
- **Timely**: AI-powered with team features
- **ManicTime**: Desktop-focused with better privacy

## Pricing

Free plan available with basic features. Premium plans start at $12 per month for advanced features including FocusTime.
