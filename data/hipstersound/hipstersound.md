## Overview

Hipstersound is an ambient noise generator designed to create a positive work environment by providing cafe, restaurant, and nature sounds that boost creativity and focus while blocking out distracting noises.

## Features

### Sound Library
- **Coffee Shop Sounds**: Recreate the ambient atmosphere of busy cafes
- **Restaurant Ambience**: Quiet restaurant sounds for subtle background noise
- **Nature Sounds**: Rain, ocean, forest, and other natural soundscapes
- **Library Sounds**: Quiet study environment atmosphere

### Customization
- Combine multiple ambient sounds to create unique working environments
- Adjust individual sound volumes for personalized mixes
- Save custom sound combinations for different tasks
- Premium tracks for enhanced variety

### Premium Features
- Instant access to extensive premium track library
- Supplemental ambient sounds for creating unique environments
- Vibes software: Windows ambient noise generator (originally $14.99) included with premium membership
- No ads or interruptions during focus sessions

## Use Cases

- **Work & Study**: Create focused environment for deep work
- **Relaxation**: Unwind with calming nature sounds
- **Sleep**: Fall asleep to gentle ambient noise
- **Distraction Blocking**: Mask disruptive environmental sounds

## Availability

Accessible via web browser at hipstersound.com with premium membership options for expanded features.

## Philosophy

Hipstersound provides a creative boost by simulating productive environments like coffee shops while offering the flexibility to blend in nature sounds for relaxation and stress reduction.

## Pricing

Free basic access with premium membership available for additional tracks and features including the Vibes Windows software.
