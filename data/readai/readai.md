## Overview

Read is your AI copilot—transforming meetings, emails, and messages into summaries, insights, and instant answers on every device, wherever you work. Read is the only AI solution that seamlessly works across Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, and in-person meetings.

## Real-Time Meeting Analytics

Read.ai analyzes virtual meetings with real-time engagement tracking, sentiment analysis, and actionable insights. The platform can measure sentiment, engagement, and talk time as the meeting happens.

## Key Features

### Engagement Tracking
- Monitors participant activity and engagement levels during meetings
- Provides insights into who is actively contributing and who is disengaged
- Tracks talk time and participation metrics

### Sentiment Analysis
- Analyzes participant reactions, tone, and feedback
- Gauges meeting effectiveness through emotional responses
- Identifies engagement trends across team meetings

### Search Copilot (New in 2026)
A first-of-its-kind, AI-powered search and discovery engine that retrieves insights from:
- Meetings
- Emails
- Chats
- Workflows
- Cloud storage
- CRMs
