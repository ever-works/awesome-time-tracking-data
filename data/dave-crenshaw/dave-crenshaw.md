## Overview

Dave Crenshaw is a public speaker, online trainer, and author focused on productivity and leadership. He is widely known for his work on the hidden costs of multitasking and developing systems for focused work.

## Notable Works

- **The Myth of Multitasking: How Doing It All Gets Nothing Done** - A book that exposes the productivity loss caused by task-switching
- **The Result: A Leadership Story** - A narrative-driven leadership book

## Key Philosophy

Crenshaw is known for distinguishing between "switchover cost" and multitasking. His research demonstrates that what people call multitasking is actually rapid task-switching, which incurs significant time and quality costs. His approach advocates for dedicated blocks of time for specific tasks rather than attempting to juggle multiple activities simultaneously.
