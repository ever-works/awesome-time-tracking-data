## Overview

WorkMax TIME combines facial recognition biometrics with GPS tracking for construction workforce management, preventing time theft and ensuring accurate job site attendance.

## Key Features

- Patented facial recognition technology confirms identity at clock-in
- GPS location stamps capture exact job site location
- Mobile app for field workers
- Real-time job costing and labor tracking
- Works offline with automatic sync
- Integration with accounting software

## Anti-Fraud Technology

- Facial biometrics prevent buddy punching
- GPS verification confirms physical presence
- Photo capture at each clock event
- Tamper-resistant time records
