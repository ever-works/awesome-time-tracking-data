## Overview

Supernormal uses state-of-the-art AI models including GPT-4 and GPT-4o to automatically capture meetings and deliver detailed notes with transcripts, summaries, and action items. The Chrome extension was updated on March 4, 2026.

- Delivers outputs that are spot on with full context

## Google Calendar Integration

### Automatic Capture

Supernormal will automatically capture transcriptions for any Google Meet, Teams and Zoom meeting on the calendar that you've linked to your Supernormal account. When you add your calendar to Supernormal at signup, it automatically:
- Captures your meetings
- Delivers detailed meeting notes when a meeting ends
- Syncs across your calendar events

### Sharing to Calendar

With Supernormal's Zapier integration:
- Add notes to meeting invites immediately following meetings
- Automatically add meeting notes to calendar events
- Centralized record-keeping in Google Calendar
- Seamless workflow integration

### Selective Capture

Manage which calendar events Supernormal joins:
- Choose specific meetings to capture
- Exclude certain event types
- Control automatic joining behavior
- Privacy-friendly selective recording
