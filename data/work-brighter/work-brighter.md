## Overview

Work Brighter is a community for productive professionals that focuses on energy management as the foundation of productivity. The approach emphasizes recognizing how energy levels fluctuate throughout the day and arranging productivity systems around those rhythms rather than forcing yourself to work against your natural patterns.

## Core Philosophy

Energy management needs to come before any other productivity system. The Work Brighter approach differs from traditional time management:

- **Energy-First Approach**: Energy management systems encourage you to change your schedule according to your energy, while other systems expect you to change your energy to fit your schedule
- **Capacity Assessment**: Personal energy management is about remembering to not just look at "what do I technically have room for in my schedule?" but also "what do I have the energetic, physical, and mental capacity to do right now?"
- **Sustainable Productivity**: Focus on working smarter by aligning tasks with energy levels rather than pushing through fatigue

## Key Concepts

### The Energy Effect

Work Brighter offers a program called "The Energy Effect" that helps professionals unlock their productivity potential through energy management principles.

### Four Dimensions of Energy

The methodology recognizes multiple aspects of personal energy:
- Physical energy
- Mental energy
- Emotional energy
- Capacity for deep work vs. administrative tasks
