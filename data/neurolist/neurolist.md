## Overview

neurolist is an AI-powered planner specifically designed to help neurodivergent individuals, particularly those with ADHD, overcome time-blindness through intelligent task organization and timing.

## Features

- Smart timer functionality for time-blindness
- Task playlist organization
- Dedicated time slots for each subtask
- AI-powered task management
- ADHD-specific design considerations
- Automatic time allocation

## How It Works

The app transforms tasks into a structured playlist where each task becomes part of a sequence with dedicated time slots for subtasks. This approach helps neurodivergent users better understand time allocation and maintain focus on current activities.

## Benefits

By addressing time-blindness—a common challenge for those with ADHD—neurolist helps users develop a more accurate sense of time passage and task duration. The playlist-based approach provides structure while maintaining flexibility for neurodivergent thinking patterns.
