## Overview

Timeneye's Timeline View introduces a visual approach to time tracking that displays work with specific start and end timestamps. This feature provides an intuitive, hour-by-hour representation of how time is allocated throughout the day.

## Key Features

- **Hourly Visualization**: See your entire day laid out in a visual timeline
- **Start/End Timestamps**: Track not just duration but exact work periods
- **Visual Time Blocks**: Different projects and tasks appear as distinct blocks
- **Gap Identification**: Easily spot untracked time or breaks in your day
- **Drag-and-Drop Editing**: Adjust time entries visually by dragging block boundaries
- **Overlap Detection**: System identifies when time blocks overlap
- **Color Coding**: Different projects or clients shown in different colors

## How It Works

The Timeline View presents your workday as a visual chart where:
- The horizontal axis represents time of day (e.g., 9 AM to 5 PM)
- Each task/project appears as a colored block
- Block length corresponds to time duration
- Blocks stack vertically when tasks overlap or run concurrently
- Gaps between blocks show untracked time

## Benefits Over Traditional Lists

**Visual Understanding**:
- Instantly grasp how your day was structured
- Identify patterns in when you work on different types of tasks
- Recognize time management issues at a glance

**Accurate Time Capture**:
- Start and end times provide more precise billing data
- Better for clients who require detailed time logs
- Improved accuracy for project estimates

**Better Planning**:
- See realistic time allocations visually
- Understand capacity and availability
- Plan future work based on historical timeline patterns

## Use Cases

- **Legal Professionals**: Document exact times for client billing requirements
- **Consultants**: Provide detailed breakdowns of how consulting hours were used
- **Project Managers**: Analyze team capacity and scheduling
- **Freelancers**: Demonstrate value to clients with detailed time logs

## Integration with Reporting

Timeline data enhances Timeneye's reporting capabilities:
- Generate visual timeline reports for clients
- Export timeline data for detailed invoicing
- Analyze productivity patterns by time of day
- Identify optimal working hours

The Timeline View represents a more sophisticated approach to time tracking, moving beyond simple duration tracking to provide rich, visual insights into work patterns and time allocation.
