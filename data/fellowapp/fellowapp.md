## Overview

Fellow is an AI meeting notetaker that records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Slack huddles, helping teams focus on discussions rather than manual note-taking.

## Collaborative Meeting Agendas

Fellow's collaborative approach transforms meetings into productive work sessions:
- Build collaborative agendas
- Record notes and action items in real-time
- Create shared agendas
- Add talking points
- Prep collaboratively with your team
