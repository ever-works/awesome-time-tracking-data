## Overview

Flat Tomato is a beautifully designed tool inspired by the Pomodoro Technique for people who want to avoid distractions and get more done. It's described as an underrated Pomodoro timer that keeps you sharp by splitting your study into manageable intervals with minimal effort, yet maximizes focus.

## Key Features

### **Calendar Integration**
- Integrated calendar to record your tasks and activities
- Estimate effort required for activities to make Pomodoro more effective
- Plan your day with time-boxed Pomodoro sessions
- Visual representation of scheduled focus time

### **Task Estimation**
- Estimate how many Pomodoros each task will require
- Better planning through effort estimation
- Track accuracy of estimates over time
- Improve time management skills through data

### **Pomodoro Timer**
- Classic Pomodoro Technique implementation
- Customizable work and break intervals
- Clean, minimal interface for distraction-free focus
- Audio and visual notifications for session changes

### **Customization Options**
- Extensive customization to create your own virtual productivity world
- Adjust timer lengths to match your workflow
- Personalize visual themes and appearance
- Configure notification preferences

### **Productivity Statistics**
- **Multiple Visualization Methods**:
  - Tree view: Organic growth visualization of productivity
  - Line graph: Track progress over time
  - Box view: Gridded representation of completed sessions
- Comprehensive analytics to understand productivity patterns
- Historical data to identify trends and improvements

### **Todoist Integration**
- Seamless integration with Todoist organization tool
- Sync tasks from Todoist
- Track Pomodoros against Todoist projects
- Unified task and time management

## Unique Selling Points

### Calendar-First Approach
Unlike basic Pomodoro timers, Flat Tomato integrates calendar functionality, allowing you to plan and visualize your entire day in Pomodoro intervals. This makes it ideal for structured planning.

### Effort Estimation
The ability to estimate how many Pomodoros a task will take helps users:
- Plan their day more realistically
- Understand how long tasks actually take
- Improve estimation skills over time
- Avoid overcommitting

### Multiple Statistics Views
Offering tree, line graph, and box visualizations caters to different learning and analytical styles, helping users understand their productivity in the way that resonates with them.
