## Overview

Timesheet Mobile is a time tracking solution designed specifically for mobile workforces, field service teams, and remote workers. It combines time tracking with GPS location tracking and job site documentation.

## Features

- **GPS Tracking**: Automatic location tracking for field workers
- **Geofencing**: Auto clock-in/out based on location
- **Offline Mode**: Track time without internet connection
- **Photo Attachments**: Add photos to time entries for job documentation
- **Mileage Tracking**: Automatic mileage calculation and logging
- **Job Costing**: Track time and expenses by job or project
- **Crew Management**: Manage multiple crews and team members
- **Scheduling**: Assign workers to jobs and shifts
- **Timesheets**: Digital timesheets with approval workflows
- **Reporting**: Detailed reports on hours, costs, and productivity
- **Integrations**: Syncs with QuickBooks, Xero, and payroll systems
- **Break Tracking**: Mandatory break enforcement
