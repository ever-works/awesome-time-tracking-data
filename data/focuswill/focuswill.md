## Overview

Focus@Will combines neuroscience and music to boost productivity and tune out distractions, founded in Los Angeles in 2011 by Will Henshall and Graham Lyus.

## The Neuroscience Approach

Developed in partnership with leading experts including Dr. Evian Gordon and UCLA Professor of Psychology Dr. Stephen Sideroff. The platform uses data from the largest brain database in the world to match audio patterns to human attention spans.

## How It Works

The audio engages your brain's limbic system to soothe your natural fight-or-flight response, keeping you calm and locked onto your task. Each piece of music is related to the previous piece in a way that keeps you from being distracted by changes, but different enough so you don't habituate to the music, balancing your mind between distraction and habituation.

## Features

- **Multiple music channels** tailored to different work types and preferences
- **Productivity tracker** to measure focus time and improvement
- **EEG-validated tracks** shown to tune brains to task-focused attention frequencies
- **Customizable session lengths** to match your work patterns
- **Cross-platform availability** (web, mobile, desktop)

## Reported Results

- Users saw a 200% to 400% increase in focus time
- Average productivity score for a one-hour session is about 75%
- EEG studies show tracks tune brains to frequencies associated with sustained, task-focused attention
