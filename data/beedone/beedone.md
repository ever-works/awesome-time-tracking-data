## Overview

BeeDone is an AI-enhanced task management app that combines gamification, habit tracking, and mindfulness techniques to help users become more productive while also promoting personal development.

## Key Gamification Features

### Earning Rewards

Users can earn XP points by completing tasks and unlocking new features. The app also generates fun and motivational images and phrases to encourage users to stay focused and engaged.

### Game Mechanics

BeeDone triggers dopamine responses by awarding XP, unlocking achievements, and celebrating streaks. BeeDone turns task management into a fun and engaging experience through gamification. Users can earn points, unlock achievements, and compete with friends, making productivity feel like a game.

## AI-Powered Features

BeeDone eliminates fragmentation by combining task management, habit tracking, focus tools, AI coaching, and gamification into a single ecosystem.

The app includes:

- **AI Coach Options**: Choose between Drill Sergeant, Zen Master, or Hype Man coaching personalities
- **AI-Generated Visuals**: Custom motivation and imagery for every task
- **AI Task Splitter**: Breaks down complex tasks into manageable steps
- **Smart Sort**: Automatic task organization based on priority

## Productivity Tools

BeeDone offers:

- AI Task Splitting
- Task Roulette for decision fatigue
- Three AI coaching personalities
- Habit tracking with Habit Tokens
- Streak Artist visual rewards
- Deep Work Timer
- Priority Master Eisenhower Matrix

## Pricing

- **Novice Plan (Free)**: Basic gamification, 3 active habits, and AI-powered task visuals
- **Pro Plan ($8/month)**: Unlocks the full gamification suite with unlimited habits, all 20+ features, personal AI coach, and advanced analytics

## Target Audience

Ideal for individuals who struggle with traditional task management and benefit from visual rewards, game-like progression, and AI-powered motivation.
