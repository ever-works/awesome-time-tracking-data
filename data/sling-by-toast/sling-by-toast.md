## Overview

Sling by Toast is an employee management and communication platform that offers scheduling, time tracking, and team messaging features with the option to integrate with Toast's POS system for restaurants and shift-based businesses.

## Features

- **Employee Scheduling**: Drag-and-drop shift scheduling
- **Time Clock**: Mobile and web-based time tracking
- **Team Communication**: Built-in messaging and announcements
- **Shift Trading**: Employees can swap shifts with approval
- **Labor Cost Tracking**: Monitor labor costs vs budgets in real-time
- **Availability Management**: Track employee availability preferences
- **PTO Management**: Manage time-off requests and approvals
- **Tasks & Checklists**: Assign and track daily tasks
- **Mobile Apps**: Full-featured iOS and Android apps
- **Toast POS Integration**: Optional integration with Toast systems
- **Notifications**: Automatic alerts for schedule changes
- **Reporting**: Labor and scheduling analytics

## Toast Integration Benefits

- Sync schedules with Toast POS
- Integrated labor cost reporting
- Unified employee management
- Streamlined payroll processing

## Target Market

Restaurants, retail stores, hospitality businesses, and any shift-based operation needing employee scheduling and communication.

## Free Plan

Offers a free tier with basic scheduling and time tracking features, with premium plans adding advanced features like labor cost forecasting and advanced integrations.

## Pricing

Free for basic features; Premium plans start at approximately $2-4 per user per month.
