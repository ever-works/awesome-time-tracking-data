## Overview

Smarter Time is an automated time-tracking application that uses your location, your input, and your phone's sensors to automatically guess your activities and offer relevant analytics and goals, all while keeping your data fully private.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Activity Detection**: Uses phone sensors and location data to automatically detect what you're doing
- **Multi-Platform**: Available on Android, Mac, and Windows
- **Location-Based Tracking**: Leverages GPS and location services to understand context
- **Privacy-Focused**: All data is kept fully private on your device
- **Activity Analytics**: Provides insights and analytics based on detected activities
- **Goal Setting**: Set goals based on your tracked time and activities
- **Sensor Integration**: Uses various device sensors to improve activity detection accuracy

## How It Works

Smarter Time combines multiple data sources—location, phone sensors, and user input—to automatically categorize your activities throughout the day. The app learns from your patterns to improve its accuracy over time.

## Privacy

A key differentiator for Smarter Time is its commitment to privacy. All tracking data remains on your device and is not sent to external servers.

## Use Cases

Ideal for individuals who want automatic time tracking without manual input, particularly those who move between different locations throughout their workday.
