## Overview

timeBro is an automatic PC time tracking app for Windows & Mac that records your time spent in programs on PC to help you remember your project hours faster.

## Key Features

### Automatic Tracking
- Works in the background tracking time spent in all applications
- No manual timers or start/stop buttons required
- Captures detailed activity data automatically
- Runs silently without interrupting workflow

### Privacy Protection
- All data stored exclusively on your device
- Cannot be used for monitoring purposes
- No cloud uploads or external data transmission
- Complete user control over tracked information

### Time Reconstruction
- Quickly review what you worked on throughout the day
- Assign tracked time to specific projects retroactively
- Fill out timesheets accurately based on actual computer usage
- Eliminate guesswork in time reporting

## Use Cases

- Freelancers tracking billable hours across multiple projects
- Remote workers documenting daily activities
- Consultants preparing accurate client invoices
- Anyone needing to reconstruct their work hours

## Platform Support

- Windows desktop application
- Mac desktop application

## Important Note

timeBro is designed as a personal productivity tool and cannot be used for employee monitoring purposes. It prioritizes user privacy with local-only data storage.
