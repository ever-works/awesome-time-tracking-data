## Overview

PracticePanther is a comprehensive law practice management tool that stands out for its in-depth time tracking capabilities, making it excellent for maximizing billable hours and profitability in legal practices.

## Key Features

**Time Tracking:**
- Detailed billable hour tracking
- Timer functionality for active time capture
- Manual time entry options
- Activity-based time logging

**Panther Payments:**
- Native billing and payment processing
- Manage invoicing within the platform
- Accept online payments
- Track payments and outstanding invoices

**Practice Management:**
- Contact and client management
- Document organization
- Calendar and scheduling
- Task and deadline tracking

**Automation:**
- Advanced automation features for mid-sized firms
- Workflow automation
- Document automation
- Communication automation

## Target Market

Best suited for mid-sized law firms (11-50 employees) that benefit from advanced automation and collaboration features while maintaining detailed time tracking for billing purposes.

## Integration

All time tracking, invoicing, and payment processing happen within the same environment, streamlining billing workflows and reducing the need for external tools.
