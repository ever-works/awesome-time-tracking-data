## Overview

Timery is a comprehensive time tracking application designed specifically for the Apple ecosystem, featuring deep integration with iOS, iPadOS, watchOS, and macOS.

## Features

- **Interactive Widgets**: Start and stop time entries, page through timers, show time-tracked details, and switch report time periods without opening the app
- **Apple Watch Support**: Comprehensive watchOS 10 support with widgets in the Smart Stack, updated design, and new complication and widget options
- **Seamless Integration**: Works across all Apple devices with deep system integration
- **Quick Actions**: Widgets and complications provide instant access to time tracking functionality
- **Report Views**: View and navigate through time tracking reports directly from widgets

## Platform

Available on iOS, iPadOS, watchOS, and macOS, providing a unified time tracking experience across all Apple devices.

## Apple Watch Features

On watchOS 10, Timery features:
- Widgets in the Smart Stack for quick access
- Updated complication designs
- New widget options for customization
- Quick timer start/stop from your wrist

## Use Cases

Ideal for professionals, freelancers, and teams who work primarily within the Apple ecosystem and want seamless time tracking across all their devices.
