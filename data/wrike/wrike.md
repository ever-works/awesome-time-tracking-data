## Overview

Wrike is a comprehensive project management and collaboration platform that includes time tracking, resource management, and workflow automation, designed to help teams streamline work and improve productivity.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracking on tasks with timesheets and reporting
- **Gantt Charts**: Interactive Gantt charts for project planning and dependency management
- **Kanban Boards**: Customizable kanban boards for agile workflow visualization
- **Workload View**: Visual workload management to balance team capacity
- **Custom Workflows**: Create custom workflows with automation rules
- **Proofing & Approvals**: Built-in file proofing with markup and approval workflows
- **Forms & Requests**: Intake forms for standardizing project requests
- **Dashboards**: Customizable dashboards with real-time project metrics
- **Integrations**: 400+ integrations including Slack, Microsoft Teams, Salesforce, Adobe
- **Mobile Apps**: Full-featured iOS and Android apps

## Pricing

Free plan for unlimited users with basic features. Professional at $9.80 per user per month includes Gantt charts and custom workflows. Business at $24.80 per user per month adds time tracking and advanced reporting.
