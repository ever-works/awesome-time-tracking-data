## Feature Release

In February 2026, Laurel introduced the Insights tab in the Work Activity panel, providing legal, accounting, and consulting professionals with instant visibility into how they spend their time.

## What It Shows

The Insights tab offers a clear, at-a-glance breakdown of time spent across specific work categories:

- **Meetings**: Time in virtual and in-person meetings
- **Document Work**: Time spent creating, editing, and reviewing documents
- **Email**: Time in email clients and correspondence
- **Research**: Time on web research and information gathering
- **Other Categories**: Custom categories based on firm needs

## Key Benefits

### For Individual Timekeepers
- Understand personal time usage patterns
- Identify productivity blockers
- Make data-driven decisions about time allocation
- Validate billed hours with activity evidence

### For Firm Management
- Benchmark timekeeper performance
- Identify training needs
- Optimize staffing based on actual work patterns
- Improve billable vs. non-billable ratios

## Integration with Laurel's Core Features

The Insights tab works alongside:
- Automatic activity capture from Zoom, Teams, Exchange
- Drag-and-drop timesheet entry (released January 2026)
- AI-powered time suggestions
- Real-time compliance monitoring

## Target Users

Designed for:
- Law firms tracking billable hours
- Accounting firms managing client work
- Consulting firms optimizing utilization
- Professional services requiring detailed time records

## Impact on Time Tracking

Laurel reports that users recover an average of 28 daily billable minutes per professional. The Insights tab helps identify where that time is being lost and recovered.
