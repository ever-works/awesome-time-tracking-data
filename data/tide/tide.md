## Overview

Tide is a productivity and mindfulness app that combines focus timers with calming ambient sounds and meditation features. The app helps users concentrate on work while maintaining mental wellbeing through integrated mindfulness practices.

## Features

- **Pomodoro Timer**: Customizable work and break intervals
- **Ambient Sounds**: Nature sounds, white noise, rain, ocean waves
- **Meditation**: Guided meditation sessions for stress relief
- **Breathing Exercises**: Timed breathing techniques for relaxation
- **Sleep Sounds**: Ambient audio to help with sleep
- **Focus Statistics**: Track daily and weekly focus time
- **Nap Timer**: Power nap feature with gentle wake-up
- **Minimalist Design**: Clean, distraction-free interface
- **Offline Mode**: Use sounds and timers without internet
- **Cross-Platform**: iOS, Android, and web versions

## Pricing

Free with optional in-app purchases for premium sound packs and features.
