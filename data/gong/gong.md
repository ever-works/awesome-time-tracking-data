## Overview

Gong is a Revenue AI Operating System serving more than 5,000 companies worldwide. The platform provides purpose-built AI for revenue teams that goes beyond transcription or surface-level analytics.

## Core Capabilities

### Conversation Intelligence

- Captures 99% of customer interactions automatically (calls, emails, meetings, and more)
- Surfaces deal risks and buying signals in real-time
- Deep conversation analytics and forecasting capabilities
- Actionable AI that turns every interaction into a competitive edge

### Revenue Intelligence

- Captures front-line conversations to better understand deals, teams and market
- Provides insights from 7.1 million sales opportunities across 3,613 companies
- Helps revenue leaders drive growth with AI-powered insights
- Real-time conversation guidance
