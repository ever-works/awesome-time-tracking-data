## Overview

Screenshot monitoring software captures images of employee computer screens at random or fixed intervals during work hours. These screenshots provide visual verification of work activity and help managers understand how time is being spent.

## How It Works

- Screenshots taken at configurable intervals (5, 10, 15, or 30 minutes)
- Images stored securely in cloud or local servers
- Activity levels tracked alongside screenshots
- Blur options available for privacy protection
- Employees can pause monitoring during breaks

## Key Features

- **Random Intervals**: Screenshots taken at unpredictable times
- **Blur Sensitive Data**: Option to blur screenshots for privacy
- **Activity Tracking**: Monitor keyboard/mouse activity levels
- **Application Tracking**: See which apps are being used
- **Time Correlation**: Link screenshots to specific time entries

## Benefits

- Visual proof of work performed
- Helps identify productivity patterns
- Useful for client billing verification
- Deters time theft
- Assists in training and quality assurance

## Privacy Considerations

- Employee notification required
- Private time modes available
- Blur options for sensitive information
- Compliance with local privacy laws
- Clear company policies needed

## Common Applications

- Remote team monitoring
- Freelancer verification
- Client project documentation
- Quality assurance review
- Training and development

## Legal and Ethical Considerations

- Must comply with GDPR, CCPA, and local laws
- Requires employee consent
- Should be disclosed in employment agreements
- Balance monitoring with trust
- Avoid excessive surveillance
