## Overview

Pomotodo is a Pomodoro-inspired time management app specifically designed for creative professionals who need structured focus sessions combined with task management capabilities.

## Key Features

- **Pomodoro Timer**: Implements the Pomodoro Technique with customizable work and break intervals
- **Task Management**: Integrated todo list that works seamlessly with your Pomodoro sessions
- **Cross-Platform**: Works with both iOS and Android devices for flexibility
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your productivity through completed Pomodoro sessions
- **Statistics**: View detailed analytics about your work patterns and focus time
- **Creative Focus**: Specifically optimized for creative workflows and project-based work

## Platform Availability

- **iOS**: Available on iPhone and iPad
- **Android**: Available on Android smartphones and tablets
- **Web**: Accessible through web browsers

## Pricing

- **Free Version**: Basic Pomodoro timer and task management features
- **Professional Plan**: $3.90 per month

The pro plan unlocks advanced features including detailed statistics, unlimited tasks, and premium customization options.

## Target Users

Pomotodo is particularly well-suited for:
- Creative professionals (designers, writers, artists)
- Freelancers managing multiple projects
- Students needing structured study sessions
- Anyone who benefits from the Pomodoro Technique combined with task lists

## Benefits

By combining the Pomodoro Technique with task management, Pomotodo helps creatives maintain focus while tracking what they accomplish, making it easier to estimate project timelines and improve time management skills.
