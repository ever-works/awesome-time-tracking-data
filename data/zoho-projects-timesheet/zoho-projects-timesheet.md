## Overview

Zoho Projects Timesheet is a built-in time tracking solution within the Zoho Projects platform, designed to help teams accurately track time spent on tasks and projects while maintaining seamless integration with project management workflows.

## Features

- **Multiple Tracking Methods**: Timer widget, manual entry, or bulk timesheet entry
- **Task-Level Tracking**: Log time directly against specific tasks and subtasks
- **Timesheet Approval**: Manager approval workflows with comments and feedback
- **Billable vs Non-Billable**: Categorize hours for accurate client billing
- **Utilization Reports**: View team capacity, allocation, and productivity metrics
- **Invoice Generation**: Convert approved timesheets to invoices automatically
- **Mobile Time Tracking**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go time entry
- **Custom Fields**: Add custom data fields to time entries
- **Reminders**: Automatic notifications for pending timesheet submissions
- **Integration**: Connects with Zoho Invoice, Books, CRM, and third-party tools
- **Time Logs View**: Filter and analyze time logs by project, user, date range, or task
- **Export Options**: Export timesheets to Excel, PDF, or CSV formats

## Ideal For

- Consulting firms and professional services
- Software development teams
- Creative agencies
- Any project-based organization needing integrated time tracking

## Pricing

Zoho Projects pricing includes timesheet functionality:
- **Free Plan**: Up to 3 users with basic time tracking
- **Premium**: $5/user/month (unlimited projects and advanced features)
- **Enterprise**: $10/user/month (includes advanced customization and integrations)

All paid plans include full timesheet capabilities with no additional cost.
