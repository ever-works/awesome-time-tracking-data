## Overview

Crew Console is promoted as the #1 Construction Scheduling, Time-keeping, and Job-tracking app for construction teams. The software is built with a "Field First" philosophy designed for ease of use in construction environments.

## Key Features

- **Mobile Time Tracking**: Makes it easy for team members to clock in and out from the field
- **Faster Reporting**: Field-first design makes reporting faster and more reliable
- **Job Tracking**: Track progress and activities across multiple construction jobs
- **Scheduling**: Manage crew schedules and assignments
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Designed for ease of use by managers, foremen, and field employees

## Target Users

Built specifically for construction managers, foremen, and field employees who need reliable time tracking and job management capabilities while working on construction sites.

## Benefits

- Simplifies time tracking for field crews
- Improves accuracy of time reporting
- Streamlines communication between field and office
- Provides real-time visibility into job progress
