## Overview

Timeular offers a unique time tracking approach combining a physical 8-sided tracking device (ZEI°) with comprehensive digital software. Users track time by simply flipping the Timeular Tracker to different sides, each representing a different activity or project, creating an intuitive and tactile tracking experience.

## The Timeular Tracker (ZEI°)

### Physical Device
- 8-sided geometric tracker
- Bluetooth connectivity
- Each side assigned to activity/project
- LEDs indicate active tracking
- Portable and desk-friendly
- Battery-powered (rechargeable)

### How It Works
1. Assign activities to each side
2. Place tracker with desired activity face-up
3. Time automatically starts tracking
4. Flip to new side to switch activities
5. Data syncs to app/software

## Software Features

- **Activity Management**: Configure what each tracker side represents
- **Automatic Tracking**: No manual timers to start/stop
- **Calendar View**: Visual timeline of day's activities
- **Reports**: Detailed time analytics and breakdowns
- **Mobile & Desktop**: Apps for all platforms
- **Integrations**: Connect with calendars, project tools
- **Team Features**: Shared activities and reporting
- **Offline Mode**: Tracker works without connection, syncs later
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Built-in break reminders
- **Tags and Notes**: Additional context for time entries
