## Overview

Klokki is an automatic time-tracking app for Mac designed specifically for freelancers, living in your menu bar and tracking time spent across applications.

## Features

- Fully automatic time tracking
- Lives in Mac menu bar for easy access
- Tracks time spent in applications and websites
- Manual time entry when needed
- Client and project organization
- Timesheet generation
- Billing and invoicing support
- Privacy-focused design
- Offline functionality

## Pricing

One-time purchase model for Mac. Free trial available.
