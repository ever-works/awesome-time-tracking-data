## Overview

Protection Time Blocking reserves calendar space for priorities before reactive demands fill it.

## Implementation

1. Block recurring focus time weekly
2. Schedule before others can
3. Mark as busy/private if needed
4. Protect blocks from meeting requests
5. Move blocks rather than delete them

## What to Protect

- Deep work sessions
- Learning time
- Strategic planning
- Personal commitments
- Exercise and self-care

## Pricing

Free practice using calendar tools.
