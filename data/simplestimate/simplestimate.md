## Overview

SimplEstimate is estimation software that enables businesses to create accurate estimates quickly and professionally. The platform helps contractors and service businesses move away from manual estimation methods to digital, streamlined processes.

## Features

- **Quick Estimate Creation**: Generate professional estimates in minutes rather than hours
- **Template Library**: Pre-built templates for common project types and industries
- **Cost Calculation**: Automated calculations for labor, materials, and overhead costs
- **Professional Output**: Create polished, client-ready estimates and proposals
- **Digital Delivery**: Send estimates electronically to clients for faster approval
- **Tracking & Follow-up**: Monitor estimate status and follow up on pending proposals

## Use Cases

- Contractors and construction businesses estimating project costs
- Service providers creating quotes for client work
- Businesses needing to standardize their estimation process
- Teams looking to reduce time spent on manual calculations

## Benefits

- Reduces estimation time from hours to minutes
- Improves accuracy through automated calculations
- Creates professional, consistent estimates
- Helps win more business with faster quote turnaround
- Minimizes errors from manual calculations
