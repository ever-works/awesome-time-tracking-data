## Overview

GigaCalculator Work Hours Calculator is a free online tool for calculating work hours, breaks, and overtime without requiring registration or payment. The calculator supports flexible time periods and provides instant payroll-ready calculations.

## Key Features

**Flexible Time Periods:**
- Weekly timesheets
- Biweekly timesheets
- Monthly timesheets (up to 31 days)
- Custom period lengths

**Break Support:**
- Lunch break deductions
- Multiple break periods
- Automatic time calculations with breaks
- Accurate net work hours

**No Registration Required:**
- Completely free to use
- No sign-up or account creation
- No hidden fees or charges
- Instant access

**Calculation Features:**
- Automatic total hours calculation
- Overtime calculations
- Daily and period totals
- Decimal or HH:MM format options

## Use Cases

- Freelancers tracking billable hours
- Small business owners calculating payroll
- Employees verifying timesheet accuracy
- Contractors documenting project time

## Platform

Web-based calculator accessible from any device with a browser. No installation or downloads required.
