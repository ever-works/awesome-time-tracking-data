## Overview

actiTIME is a time tracking and work management tool designed to help organizations monitor employee hours and manage project effort.

## Features

- **Manual & Timer Tracking**: Log work hours manually or use built-in timers
- **Project Linking**: Connect time entries to specific tasks and projects
- **Budget Control**: Monitor project budgets and track spending
- **Performance Analysis**: Comprehensive reporting and analytics
- **Team Management**: Manage team workloads and schedules
- **Timesheet Approval**: Workflow for reviewing and approving timesheets
- **Leave Management**: Track time off and absences
- **Client Reporting**: Generate reports for client billing
- **Integrations**: Connect with other business tools

## Pricing

Offers free trial with paid plans based on team size.
