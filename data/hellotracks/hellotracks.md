## Overview

Hellotracks is a mobile workforce management solution that provides real-time GPS tracking, job dispatching, and time tracking for field teams. It's designed for businesses that need to track mobile employees and optimize routes.

## Features

- **Real-Time GPS Tracking**: Live location tracking of field employees
- **Job Dispatch**: Assign and dispatch jobs to nearest available workers
- **Time Tracking**: Automatic time tracking based on job check-in/out
- **Route Optimization**: Optimize routes for efficiency
- **Geofencing**: Set up location-based alerts and auto check-ins
- **Forms & Checklists**: Digital forms for job completion
- **Photo Documentation**: Attach photos to jobs and time entries
- **Team Communication**: In-app chat and notifications
- **Reporting**: Time, location, and productivity reports
- **Territory Management**: Assign territories to team members
