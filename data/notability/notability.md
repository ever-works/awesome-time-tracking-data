## Overview

Notability is a digital note-taking app for iPad developed by Ginger Labs. It provides a handwriting-first experience with Apple Pencil support, making it a popular choice for digital bullet journaling and analog-style planning.

## Features

- Handwriting input using Apple Pencil with natural pen feel
- Digital notebooks suitable for bullet journal layouts
- Template support for planners, trackers, and collections
- Cloud-synced notes accessible across devices
- Searchable handwritten text
- Unlimited digital pages
- Audio recording synced with notes for meeting contexts
- Photo and PDF import and annotation
- Divider and subject organization for multiple notebooks
- Easy page rearrangement and management

## Use Cases

- Digital bullet journaling with customizable layouts and collections
- Paperless daily and monthly planning
- Meeting notes with searchable content
- Creative journaling and idea organization
- Habit and mood tracking with custom templates

## Pricing

Notability offers a free tier with limited features and a Notability Plus subscription that provides unlimited editing, cross-device sync, and advanced organization features.
