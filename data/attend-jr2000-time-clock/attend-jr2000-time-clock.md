## Overview

The Attend JR2000 is a budget-friendly biometric time clock that brings enterprise-level time tracking capabilities to small businesses at an accessible price point of $179.99.

## Key Features

**Biometric Capabilities**:
- Fingerprint scanner for secure clock-ins
- Stores fingerprint templates locally
- Fast fingerprint verification
- Backup RFID card reader

**Connectivity**:
- WiFi connectivity
- LAN/Ethernet option
- Cloud-based data sync
- Real-time attendance updates

**User Management**:
- Supports multiple employees
- Multiple departments
- Different pay rates
- Shift scheduling

## Benefits for Small Businesses

- **Affordable**: At $179.99, accessible for tight budgets
- **Easy Setup**: Plug and play installation
- **No Buddy Punching**: Biometric verification ensures accuracy
- **Cloud Integration**: Data syncs to cloud platform
- **Payroll Ready**: Exports to popular payroll systems
