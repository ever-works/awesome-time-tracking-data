## Critical Need for Offline Functionality

Construction sites often have terrible cell coverage, making offline time tracking capability essential rather than optional for construction time tracking software in 2026.

## How It Works

Workers can:
- Clock in and out without internet connection
- Switch between tasks and cost codes
- Add detailed notes and photos
- Track equipment usage

All data is stored locally on the device and automatically syncs when connection is restored.

## Industry Impact

This feature prevents lost time data and ensures accurate payroll even in remote locations with poor connectivity. Construction companies have reported significant improvements in timesheet accuracy and reduced administrative burden.

## Best Practices

- Ensure the app stores sufficient data locally
- Use automatic conflict resolution when syncing
- Provide visual indicators of sync status
- Allow manual sync triggering when connection is available
