## Overview

Open Time Clock is powerful, easy-to-use free web-based time clock software designed for businesses of all sizes.

## Features

- **Free Web-Based Platform** - No software installation required, accessible from any web browser
- **GPS Location Tracking** - Track employee locations when they clock in and out
- **Offline Clock-In** - Allow employees to clock in even without internet connectivity
- **Group Clock-In** - Enable multiple team members to clock in simultaneously
- **Website Integration** - Embed time clock functionality directly into company websites
- **Security Features** - Enhanced security measures to prevent time theft
- **Productivity Monitoring** - Track employee hours and productivity metrics
- **Timesheet Management** - Automatic timesheet generation from clock-in/out data

## Use Cases

Suitable for small businesses, retail stores, restaurants, and field service companies needing reliable time clock functionality without subscription costs.
