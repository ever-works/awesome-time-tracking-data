## Overview

Billables AI is an innovative time tracking solution specifically designed for lawyers and legal professionals. The platform uses artificial intelligence to automatically track legal work and generate time entries, addressing the common problem of attorneys losing billable hours due to manual timesheet tracking.

## How It Works

1. **Automatic Tracking**: Software monitors your work activities as you perform legal tasks
2. **AI Analysis**: Machine learning algorithms analyze your workflows to identify billable work
3. **Smart Categorization**: Automatically assigns work to correct clients and matters
4. **Narrative Generation**: Creates professional narrative descriptions of work performed
5. **Review & Submit**: Attorneys review auto-generated entries and submit to billing system

## Problem It Solves

Legal professionals face a critical challenge: According to studies, attorneys capture only about 60-70% of their actual billable time due to the burdens of manual time entry. This "leakage" costs law firms and legal departments significant revenue.

**Common Time Tracking Challenges for Lawyers**:
- Forgetting to record time immediately
- Reconstructing activities at end of day/week
- Interruptions preventing real-time tracking
- Multiple clients and matters throughout day
- Administrative burden of detailed entries
- Difficulty switching between substantive work and admin tasks
