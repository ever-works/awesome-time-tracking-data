## Overview

Energy Level Tracker is a specialized web application designed to help individuals discover their peak productivity hours by systematically tracking energy fluctuations throughout the day.

## Key Features

### Energy Tracking
- Simple hourly energy level logging interface
- Visual energy pattern recognition over days and weeks
- Automated calculation of hourly energy trends
- Identification of consistent peak and low energy periods

### Analysis & Insights
- Easily calculates hourly trends to identify peak performance windows
- Provides actionable insights for task scheduling optimization
- Helps users understand their personal chronotype
- Generates visual reports showing energy patterns

### Implementation Support
- Recommendations for aligning challenging tasks with high-energy periods
- Suggestions for scheduling routine work during low-energy windows
- Guidance on optimizing daily schedule based on energy data

## How It Works

1. Track your energy for one to three weeks
2. Rate energy levels hourly on a consistent scale
3. Review automated trend analysis
4. Identify your biological prime time
5. Restructure your schedule to match energy patterns

## Benefits

- Maximize productivity by working with natural energy rhythms rather than against them
- Reduce burnout by avoiding high-intensity work during low-energy periods
- Improve work quality by tackling complex tasks during peak hours
- Gain self-awareness about personal productivity patterns
