## Overview

FreshBooks simplifies accounting, invoicing, expense tracking, and time tracking for self-employed individuals and small businesses.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracking integrated with invoicing
- **Automated Invoicing**: Create and send professional invoices automatically
- **Recurring Billing**: Automated billing for retainer clients
- **Expense Tracking**: Track and categorize business expenses
- **Payment Processing**: Accept online payments from clients
- **Financial Reporting**: Generate profit and loss statements, tax reports
- **Project Management**: Basic project tracking and organization
- **Mobile App**: Track time and expenses on the go
- **Client Portal**: Let clients view invoices and make payments

## Pricing

Multiple pricing tiers based on number of billable clients and features needed.
