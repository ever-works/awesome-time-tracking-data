## Overview

Fireflies.ai is an automated AI note-taker that records, transcribes, and summarizes calls for over 1 million companies globally. The company achieved a $1 billion valuation in June 2025 and now serves over 20 million users across 500,000 organizations, including 75% of Fortune 500 companies.

## Core Capabilities

- Transcribe, summarize, search, and analyze all team conversations
- 95% transcription accuracy across 100+ languages
- Detailed notes, action items, and customized summaries instantly after every meeting
- Advanced conversation intelligence and analytics

## Recent Innovations (2025-2026)

### Live Assist
Provides real-time suggestions, coaching, and answers during meetings, helping participants stay engaged and informed.

### Talk to Fireflies
A voice-activated AI assistant powered by Perplexity that performs real-time web searches during live meetings, positioning Fireflies as the first meeting assistant to combine conversation intelligence with real-time internet knowledge.

### Mini-Apps
In 2025, Fireflies launched over 200 mini-apps to extract action items or sentiment shifts automatically from conversations.
