## Overview

Allied Time USA specializes in biometric time and attendance systems, offering a comprehensive range of time clocks from basic punch card systems to advanced facial recognition terminals. Their solutions help businesses of all sizes automate attendance tracking and payroll processing.

## Features

- Multiple biometric options (fingerprint, facial recognition, iris scan)
- Badge and proximity card readers
- Cloud-based time and attendance software
- Real-time attendance monitoring
- Automated overtime calculation
- Department transfer tracking
- Job costing capabilities
- Integration with major payroll providers
- Mobile punch options
- GPS location verification
- Scheduled and ad-hoc reporting
- Employee self-service portal

## Pricing

Allied Time offers time clock hardware ranging from budget-friendly basic models to premium biometric terminals. Software is available as a one-time purchase or monthly subscription. Contact Allied Time for product recommendations and pricing based on your business size and requirements.
