## Overview

Cloud-In-Hand Solutions provides straightforward time and attendance software focused on simplicity and ease of use. The cloud-based platform makes employee check-ins and time tracking accessible from anywhere.

## Features

- Simple employee check-in/check-out
- Cloud-based accessibility
- Mobile time clock apps
- Attendance tracking and reporting
- Employee scheduling
- PTO and leave management
- Real-time attendance dashboards
- Automated timesheet calculation
- Payroll integration
- Multi-location support
- Customizable workflows
- Manager approval processes
- Email and SMS notifications
- Compliance reporting
