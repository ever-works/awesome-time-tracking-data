## Overview

Unifocus is a leading workforce management software platform designed specifically for the hospitality industry, including hotels, resorts, casinos, and hospitality operations. The platform provides comprehensive staff scheduling, time and attendance, task management, and labor forecasting capabilities.

## Time & Attendance Features

- **Multi-Device Time Clocks**: Support for web, mobile, kiosk, and biometric devices
- **Biometric Options**: Fingerprint and facial recognition for secure authentication
- **Geofencing**: Location-based clock-in restrictions
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Live visibility into who's working across properties
- **Shift Verification**: Compare scheduled vs actual hours worked
- **Break Compliance**: Meal and rest break tracking and enforcement
- **Overtime Management**: Automatic overtime calculations and alerts
- **Time-Off Tracking**: PTO, vacation, and sick leave management
- **Mobile Time Entry**: Clock in/out from smartphones with GPS verification

## Scheduling Features

- **Demand-Based Scheduling**: AI-powered schedules based on occupancy and events
- **Auto-Scheduling**: Automatically generate optimized schedules
- **Shift Management**: Create, publish, and modify shifts
- **Schedule Templates**: Reusable patterns for recurring schedules
- **Shift Bidding**: Employees bid on available shifts
- **Shift Swapping**: Employee-initiated shift trades with manager approval
- **Open Shift Marketplace**: Fill last-minute openings quickly
- **Multi-Property Scheduling**: Manage schedules across multiple locations

## Labor Forecasting

- **Occupancy-Based Forecasting**: Predict staffing needs from room occupancy
- **Event Impact Analysis**: Adjust forecasts for conferences and events
- **Historical Data**: Use past patterns to predict future needs
- **Budget Alignment**: Schedule within labor budget constraints
- **Department-Specific Models**: Custom forecasting by department (housekeeping, F&B, front desk)

## Task Management

- **Maintenance Tasks**: Schedule and track maintenance work orders
- **Guest Services**: Manage concierge and guest request tasks
- **Quality Inspections**: Schedule and document property inspections

- **Mobile Task Management**: Field staff access tasks from mobile devices
