## Overview

Jibble provides 100% FREE time tracking for nonprofits and volunteers with unlimited users, or offers a 50% lifetime discount on all paid plans.

## Features for Nonprofits

- **Unlimited Users**: Completely free for unlimited volunteers and staff
- **Facial Recognition**: Volunteers snap a photo with each time entry for security
- **Mobile Time Tracking**: Clock in/out from mobile devices
- **GPS Tracking**: Location verification for field volunteers
- **Volunteer Hours Reports**: Track service hours for grants and reporting
- **Project Tracking**: Assign volunteers to specific projects
- **Timesheet Management**: Digital timesheets for all volunteers
- **Grant Compliance**: Accurate records for grant reporting requirements

## Benefits for Nonprofits

- More accurate and secure attendance records
- Simplified volunteer hour tracking
- Free tier eliminates software costs
- Mobile-friendly for volunteers in the field
- Meets grant reporting requirements

## Pricing

Free for unlimited users, or 50% lifetime discount on premium features.

## Platform

Web, iOS, and Android apps available.
