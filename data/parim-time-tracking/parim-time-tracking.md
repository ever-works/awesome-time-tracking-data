## Overview

Parim is a modern time tracking application that prioritizes simplicity and user experience while offering powerful functionality. Designed for teams that want comprehensive time tracking and project management without unnecessary complexity, Parim provides clean interfaces and intuitive workflows.

## Features

### Time Tracking
- **Simple Timers**: One-click start/stop for active tasks
- **Manual Entry**: Quick time logging for past work
- **Project-Based**: Organize time by projects and clients
- **Task-Level Tracking**: Drill down to individual tasks
- **Calendar View**: See time entries in calendar format
- **Mobile Apps**: Track time on iOS and Android

### Project Management
- **Project Organization**: Structure work by projects and clients
- **Task Lists**: Create and assign tasks within projects
- **Team Assignment**: Allocate team members to projects
- **Budgets**: Set time and cost budgets
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor project completion

### Team Features
- **Team Dashboard**: Overview of team time and activity
- **User Management**: Add and manage team members
- **Permissions**: Control what each user can see and do
- **Collaboration**: Share projects and coordinate work
- **Activity Feed**: See what team is working on

### Reporting
- **Time Reports**: Detailed breakdowns by project, user, or date
- **Project Reports**: Time and cost analysis per project
- **Team Reports**: Productivity and utilization metrics
- **Export Options**: CSV and PDF exports
- **Custom Reports**: Build reports with specific parameters

### Billing & Invoicing
- **Billable Hours**: Track billable vs. non-billable time
- **Hourly Rates**: Set different rates by project or role
- **Invoice Generation**: Create invoices from tracked time
- **Client Reporting**: Share time reports with clients
