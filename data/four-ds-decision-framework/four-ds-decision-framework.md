## The Four Ds

When encountering any task, email, or request, choose one of four actions:

### 1. Do
Complete immediately if it takes less than 2-5 minutes.

### 2. Delegate
Assign to someone else if they can do it better, faster, or it's their responsibility.

### 3. Defer
Schedule for a specific time if it requires more time or isn't urgent.

### 4. Delete/Drop
Eliminate if it's not important, not aligned with goals, or won't have impact.

## Benefits

- Simplifies decision-making
- Prevents task accumulation
- Reduces procrastination
- Clarifies priorities
- Maintains clear inboxes

## Application Areas

- Email processing
- Task list reviews
- Meeting requests
- Project intake
- Daily planning

## Decision Criteria

### When to Do
- Takes under 2-5 minutes
- Urgent and important
- Blocking other work
- Quick win available

### When to Delegate
- Someone else has needed skills
- Development opportunity for team member
- Not your core responsibility
- You're not the best person for it

### When to Defer
- Important but not urgent
- Requires extended time
- Needs additional information
- Depends on other completions

### When to Delete
- Low value or impact
- Doesn't align with goals
- Someone else will handle it
- No longer relevant
- Nice-to-have not must-have
