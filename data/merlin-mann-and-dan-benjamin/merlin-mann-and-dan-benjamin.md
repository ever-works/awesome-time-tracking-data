## Overview

Merlin Mann and Dan Benjamin are the co-hosts of Back to Work, an award-winning talk show on the Relay FM network. The podcast explores productivity, communication, work barriers, constraints, tools, and the human side of doing meaningful work.

## Back to Work Podcast

- Award-winning weekly talk show format
- Topics include productivity systems, communication strategies, workflow optimization
- Discusses both tools and the human psychology behind effective work
- Combines practical advice with humor and cultural commentary

## Key Themes

- Inbox management and email processing
- Tool selection and usage philosophy
- Overcoming barriers to productivity
- The relationship between technology and human work habits
- Communication best practices
- Constraints and how they shape productivity
