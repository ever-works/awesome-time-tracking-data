## Overview

Ellie is an AI email assistant that learns your writing style and drafts replies on your behalf, powered by advanced large language models like OpenAI's GPT-4. The tool helps you save time and reduce "reply anxiety" from a constantly overflowing inbox.

## Features

- **Personalized Writing Style**: Learns from your previous sent emails to mimic your writing style authentically
- **Context-Aware Responses**: Uses context from email threads to craft relevant, appropriate replies
- **Multi-Language Support**: Can respond in any language to match the email being replied to
- **Browser Extensions**: Available as Chrome, Firefox, and Edge extensions
- **Email Platform Support**: Fully supports Gmail, Outlook, and Fastmail
- **Role Assignment**: Assign Ellie different roles for different emails, perfect for context switching
- **Privacy-Focused**: Your data and email content remain secure

## How It Works

Ellie handles approximately 80% of repetitive, cognitive work (drafting the email), freeing up the human to focus on the critical 20%—reviewing, refining, and ensuring the final message is accurate, appropriate, and maintains relationships.

## Pricing

Offers a free plan with limited replies, with paid plans available for higher usage needs.

## Who It's For

Best suited for busy professionals, sales and support teams, and anyone who feels their productivity is being held hostage by their inbox.
