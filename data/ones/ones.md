## Overview

Ones is a project management platform that combines task management, agile methodologies, and time tracking in a unified workspace. It provides teams with tools to plan, execute, and track projects while monitoring time investment across initiatives.

## Time Tracking Features

- Task-level time tracking
- Project time budgets
- Time reports and analytics
- Billable vs. non-billable hours
- Team time visibility

## Project Management Capabilities

- Agile board views (Scrum, Kanban)
- Sprint planning
- Backlog management
- Issue tracking
- Roadmap visualization

## Collaboration Tools

- Team workspaces
- Real-time updates
- Comments and discussions
- File sharing
- @mentions and notifications

## Reporting & Analytics

- Time utilization reports
- Project velocity metrics
- Burndown charts
- Resource allocation
- Custom dashboards

## Target Users

Agile development teams, product managers, and project-based organizations needing integrated time tracking within their project management workflow.
