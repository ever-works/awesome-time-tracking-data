## Overview

Quarter-Hour Rule requires 15-minute minimum effort before allowing yourself to stop.

## How It Works

1. Commit to just 15 minutes
2. Set timer
3. Work fully for those 15 minutes
4. After 15 min, can stop guilt-free
5. Usually continue due to momentum

## Why 15 Minutes

- Long enough to build momentum
- Short enough to feel manageable
- Typical time to overcome initial resistance
- Gets past activation energy hump
