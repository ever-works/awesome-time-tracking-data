## Overview

Billable Ninja Calculator is a free tool that helps freelancers, consultants, and professionals calculate billable hours and revenue quickly and accurately.

## Features

- **Simple Interface**: Easy-to-use calculator for billable time
- **Hourly Rate Input**: Enter your hourly rate for automatic revenue calculation
- **Time Increments**: Supports standard billing increments (6-minute, 15-minute, etc.)
- **Multiple Calculations**: Calculate multiple time entries at once
- **Revenue Totals**: Automatically calculate total billable revenue
- **No Registration**: Use immediately without signup
- **Free**: Completely free to use

## Use Cases

Ideal for:
- Freelancers calculating project revenue
- Consultants preparing invoices
- Lawyers tracking billable hours
- Agencies estimating project costs
- Anyone billing by the hour

## Platform

Web-based, accessible from any device with a browser.

## Pricing

Completely free.
