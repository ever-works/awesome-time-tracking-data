## Overview

Timehubzone is a specialized world clock and meeting planning tool designed to simplify the challenge of scheduling across multiple time zones. The platform provides visual, color-coded displays that make it instantly clear when meeting times work for all participants globally.

## Features

- **World Clock Display**: View current times across multiple time zones simultaneously
- **Meeting Planner**: Interactive tool for finding optimal meeting times
- **Color-Coded Interface**: Visual indicators showing suitable meeting windows
- **Multiple Time Zones**: Compare unlimited number of time zones
- **Daylight Saving Time**: Automatic DST handling for all regions
- **Business Hours Highlighting**: Easily identify standard working hours
- **Drag-and-Drop**: Interactive time selection
- **Shareable Links**: Generate links to share meeting time proposals
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works on all device sizes
- **No Registration**: Use immediately without account creation

## Color-Coded System

Timehubzone uses intuitive color coding:
- **Green**: Optimal meeting times (business hours for all locations)
- **Yellow**: Acceptable times (early or late for some participants)
- **Red**: Poor times (outside business hours for one or more locations)
- Visual indicators make it easy to identify the best meeting windows at a glance

## Meeting Planner Tool

### How It Works
1. **Add Locations**: Select cities/time zones for all meeting participants
2. **View Time Grid**: See a visual grid comparing times across all zones
3. **Identify Optimal Times**: Green-highlighted times indicate good meeting windows
4. **Select Time**: Choose the time that works best for all participants
5. **Share**: Generate shareable link or calendar invite

## Best Practices

When using Timehubzone:
- Look for green time slots where all participants are in business hours
- Consider rotating meeting times if no ideal slot exists
- Account for participants' morning/afternoon preferences
- Book meeting rooms in advance once time is selected
- Send calendar invites with time zone details
