## Overview

Affinda extracts data from any timesheets with unmatched accuracy. With their AI agents, you can build custom timesheet automation models in minutes, not months.

## Key Features

### High Accuracy

The system can extract data from any timesheet, in any format or layout, with more than 99% accuracy.

### Automated Workflow

Affinda's AI reads timesheets with impressive contextual understanding, splitting and classifying documents, extracting and validating data, then transforming the output to match business needs.

### Data Transformation

Affinda automatically converts extracted timesheet data into the format your payroll or HRIS system recognizes, ensuring it's ready for immediate use.

### Validation

Users can write validation rules in natural language and apply business logic to timesheet data, ensuring accurate hours tracking, proper overtime calculations and compliant output ready for straight-through payroll processing.

## OCR Technology

For scanned images or documents where textual information is contained within images, Affinda uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to convert the images into machine-readable data ahead of classification and data extraction.

## Integration Capabilities

The platform supports over 400 business systems — including payroll platforms, HRIS and compliance tools. Affinda connects seamlessly with payroll, HRIS, and compliance systems.
