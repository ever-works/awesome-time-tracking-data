## Overview

Timesheet 365 is a comprehensive time tracking solution designed specifically for organizations using Microsoft 365. It automatically creates timesheets based on work activities across Microsoft's ecosystem, reducing manual time entry while improving accuracy.

## Features

- **Deep Microsoft 365 Integration**: Seamlessly works with Outlook, Teams, Planner, SharePoint, and Project
- **AI Screen Tracking**: Intelligently tracks work activities and categorizes time automatically
- **Automated Timesheet Creation**: Generates timesheets from actual work activities
- **Email Tracking**: Logs time spent on emails by project or client
- **Meeting Time Capture**: Automatically records time spent in Teams meetings
- **Task Integration**: Syncs with Planner and Project tasks for accurate time allocation
- **Customizable Timesheets**: Flexible templates to match organizational needs
- **Approval Workflows**: Built-in approval process for managers
- **Reporting and Analytics**: Comprehensive insights into time allocation and productivity
- **Mobile Access**: Track time on the go with mobile apps
- **Offline Capability**: Continue tracking when disconnected from the internet
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Handle international teams and billing
- **Export Options**: Export to Excel, PDF, or integrate with payroll systems
- **Privacy Controls**: Configurable tracking scope to respect employee privacy

## Ideal For

- Organizations already using Microsoft 365
- Professional services firms billing by the hour
- IT departments tracking project time
- Consulting companies needing detailed time records
