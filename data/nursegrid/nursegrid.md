## Overview

Nursegrid is the #1 nurse scheduling app built by nurses, for nurses. The app helps 600,000+ nurses manage shifts, swaps, credentials, and more with an intuitive mobile-first interface.

## Key Features for Nurses (Free App)

### Shift Management
- Enter full 6-week schedules with just a few taps—fast, simple, and flexible
- Track shifts and sync personal events in one calendar view
- Set reminders for upcoming shifts and important dates
- Share schedules with colleagues and family

### Shift Swapping
- Compare calendars with colleagues to find compatible swap partners
- Swap shifts directly through the app
- Make post-shift plans with coworkers

### Credential Tracking
- Enter licenses and certifications with expiration dates
- Receive helpful reminders before credentials expire
- Stay ahead of renewal deadlines

### Calendar Integration
- Combine worksite shifts with personal calendar for complete day/week/month view
- Full integration with personal life planning

### Job Discovery
- Opportunities come to you based on your preferences
- Search for jobs when ready
- Flexible job searching options

## Nursegrid Manager (For Healthcare Organizations)

Nursegrid Manager is smart healthcare staff scheduling software that:
- Enables self-scheduling for nursing staff
- Manages shift swaps efficiently
- Fills open shifts quickly
- Integrates with Nursegrid Mobile app used by nurses

## Platform Availability

- Available on iPhone (App Store)
- Android (Google Play)
- Computer browser
- Apple Watch and Wear OS smartwatch support
- First nurse scheduling app for smartwatches

## Pricing

The Nursegrid app is 100% free for nurses and healthcare workers. Healthcare organizations pay for Nursegrid Manager scheduling software.
