## Overview

SuperSaaS is a flexible appointment scheduling platform that allows businesses to create highly customized booking systems for various scheduling needs including time tracking of scheduled activities.

## Features

- Highly customizable booking pages
- Multiple schedule types (resource, capacity, service)
- Individual, group, and recurring appointments
- Multi-language support (over 30 languages)
- Calendar integration (Google Calendar, Outlook, iCal)
- Payment processing integration
- Automated email and SMS reminders
- Custom fields and forms
- Access control and user permissions
- API for custom integrations
- Waiting lists
- Resource allocation
- Advanced reporting

## Use Cases

- Professional services (consultants, therapists, coaches)
- Healthcare providers
- Educational institutions
- Fitness and wellness centers
- Equipment and room booking
- Event registration

## Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- Free plan available for basic scheduling
- Highly flexible and customizable
- International accessibility
- Reasonable pricing compared to competitors

**Cons:**
- Steep learning curve due to complexity
- Mobile experience can be suboptimal
- Interface may feel dated compared to newer alternatives

## Pricing

Free plan available for up to 50 appointments per schedule. Paid plans start at approximately $9/month with increasing features and capacity.
