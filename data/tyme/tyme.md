## Overview

Tyme is a comprehensive time tracking and billing app available on all Apple platforms (Mac, iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch) with deep system integration.

## Features

- Available on all Apple platforms with sync
- Over 20 actions for Shortcuts app
- Project and task time tracking
- Automatic time tracking options
- Invoicing and billing
- Detailed reports and exports
- Budget tracking
- Calendar integration
- Team collaboration features
- Apple Watch support

## Pricing

One-time purchase or subscription options available. Free version with limited features.
