## Overview

In-Schedule Suggestions is Trevor AI's smart scheduling feature that analyzes multiple factors to recommend the optimal time to schedule each task.

## Factors Considered

### Advanced Mode
- **Predicted Best Day and Time**: Based on task type and your productivity patterns
- **Priority Levels**: Higher priority tasks get better time slots
- **Due Date Urgency**: Approaching deadlines influence scheduling
- **Calendar Pressure**: Available gaps in your schedule
- **Task Duration**: Estimated time requirements
- **Context**: Similar task patterns from history

### Simple Mode
- Sequential scheduling by priority
- Then ordered by due date
- Fills available calendar gaps

## One-Click Acceptance

When Trevor suggests a time:
1. System highlights optimal time slot
2. Shows reasoning (e.g., "Best focus time available")
3. User clicks to accept
4. Task instantly scheduled
5. Calendar updates in real-time

## Benefits

### Time Savings
- Eliminates manual calendar scanning
- Reduces decision fatigue
- Batch scheduling becomes instant

### Optimization
- Better use of productive hours
- Reduced schedule conflicts
- Improved task completion rates

### Learning System
- Improves recommendations over time
- Adapts to user preferences
- Considers feedback from accepts/declines

## Comparison to Manual Scheduling

Manual: Consider 5-10 factors, scan calendar, find gaps, assess fit, drag task
AI-Powered: AI analyzes everything, presents best option, one click to schedule
