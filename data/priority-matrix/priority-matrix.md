## Overview

Priority Matrix is a software implementation of the Eisenhower Matrix (four-quadrant prioritization method), helping individuals and teams visualize and prioritize tasks based on urgency and importance.

## Features

- **4-Quadrant View**: Visual Eisenhower Matrix
- **Drag and Drop**: Move tasks between quadrants
- **Team Collaboration**: Shared matrices and delegation
- **Email Integration**: Convert emails to tasks
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync with Outlook and Google
- **Templates**: Pre-built project templates
- **Delegation**: Assign tasks to team members
- **Reporting**: Priority and productivity reports
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android
- **Multi-Platform**: Windows, Mac, Web

## The Four Quadrants

1. **Critical & Immediate**: Urgent and important
2. **Critical & Strategic**: Important but not urgent
3. **Supporting**: Urgent but less important
4. **Distractions**: Neither urgent nor important
