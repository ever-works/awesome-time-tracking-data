## Overview

Time Champ is a comprehensive employee monitoring and workforce intelligence tool that transforms raw activity data into actionable insights that drive better focus, smarter decisions, and long-term business success.

## Features

- Live screen recording in real-time
- Keystroke tracking to record all keyboard activity
- Daily reports, active hours, activities, and screenshots
- Idle alert system notifying employers when an employee is idle
- Productivity labels for apps and websites
- Captures productivity hours, focus time, idle periods, app and tool usage, attendance trends, and performance patterns
- Automatic employee attendance tracking
- Project management system with goal management and test case management
- Real-time location tracking of mobile devices
- Data securely stored on Google Cloud's advanced infrastructure

## Platform Compatibility

- Windows
- Linux
- Mac
- Android
