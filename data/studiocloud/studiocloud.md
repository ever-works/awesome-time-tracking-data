## Overview

StudioCloud is a free business management software that keeps everything in one place from contacts to schedules to invoices. It's specifically designed for photographers and creative professionals.

## Key Features

- **Client Management**: Organize and manage client information in one central location
- **Scheduling**: Calendar management for sessions, shoots, and appointments
- **Invoicing**: Create and send professional invoices to clients
- **Online Booking**: Allow clients to book services directly through an online booking system
- **Contract Management**: Store and manage client contracts and agreements
- **Lead Generation**: Track and manage potential clients

## Benefits

- **Free to Use**: Award-winning software available at no cost
- **Customizable**: Adapt the system to match your specific business type and workflow
- **Employee Management**: Manage team members alongside client operations
- **User-Friendly**: Intuitive interface designed for creative professionals

## Best For

- Photography studios (wedding, portrait, commercial)
- Creative studios and agencies
- Freelance photographers
- Small creative businesses needing client management

## Limitations

While StudioCloud excels at client management and scheduling, users should note that dedicated time tracking features may be limited compared to specialized time tracking tools.

## Pricing

Free to use with all core features included.
