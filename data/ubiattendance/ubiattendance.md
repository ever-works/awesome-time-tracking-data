## Overview

ubiAttendance is a specialized attendance and leave management platform designed specifically for mobile and distributed workforces. It focuses on GPS verification, geofencing, and real-time tracking - ideal for organizations with field teams.

## Core Features

### Mobile Attendance
- GPS-enabled clock in/out from mobile app
- Photo capture with attendance (selfie verification)
- Location timestamp for all punches
- Offline mode with automatic sync
- Battery-efficient background tracking

### Geofencing
- Create virtual boundaries around office/site locations
- Auto clock-in when entering geofence
- Alerts when employees enter/exit designated areas
- Multiple geofence locations per employee
- Radius-based and custom polygon geofences

### GPS Route Tracking
- Real-time location visibility of field staff
- Route history playback
- Distance traveled calculations
- Visit verification at client locations
- Route optimization insights

### Live Tracking Dashboard
- Real-time map view of all employees
- See who's checked in and where
- Employee status (in office, on field, on leave)
- Activity timeline for each employee
- Team-wise grouping
