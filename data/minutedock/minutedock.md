## Overview

MinuteDock is a streamlined time tracking and billing solution specifically designed for professional services firms and individuals who bill clients for their time. The platform emphasizes simplicity and efficiency, allowing users to record work hours, manage project budgets, and generate client invoices with minimal friction.

## Target Users

- Accountants and bookkeepers
- Agencies and consultants
- Law firms
- Freelancers and independent contractors
- Any professional services business billing for time

## Key Features

### Lightweight Time Entry

- **Conversational Interface**: Use simple, natural language phrases to log time rather than navigating complex forms or dropdown menus
- **One-Click Timer**: Start tracking with a single keystroke—no clicking through multiple forms
- **Effortless Logging**: Type what you're working on in plain language and the system handles the rest

### Invoicing Integration

- **Instant Invoice Generation**: Convert tracked time into accurate invoices in seconds
- **Flexible Billing Rates**: Customize rates by client, project, team member, task, or any combination
- **Automatic Rate Application**: Rates automatically apply when generating invoices, eliminating manual lookups
- **Direct Accounting Integration**: Send invoices through your accounting software without manual data entry

### Accounting Software Integrations

- **Xero**: Full bi-directional sync for seamless workflow
- **QuickBooks**: Integrated time tracking and invoicing
- **Wave**: Direct connection for time-to-invoice conversion
- **MYOB**: Australian accounting software integration
- **FreshBooks**: Time tracking integration for service businesses

### Budget Management

- Project budget tracking and monitoring
- Real-time budget vs. actual comparison
- Alert notifications for budget thresholds
- Resource allocation insights

### Reporting & Analytics

- Billable hours reporting by client, project, or team member
- Time utilization analysis
- Revenue forecasting based on tracked time
- Customizable report generation
