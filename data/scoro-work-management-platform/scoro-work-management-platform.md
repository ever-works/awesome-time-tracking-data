## Overview

Scoro is an end-to-end work management software designed for professional and creative services. It combines projects, sales, billing, and reporting in one comprehensive platform, streamlining operations from lead to invoice.

## Core Capabilities

### Project Management
- Task and project tracking
- Gantt charts and Kanban boards
- Resource allocation
- Collaborative workspaces

### Time Tracking
- Timer-based tracking
- Manual time entry
- Billable vs. non-billable hours
- Project time allocation
- Mobile time tracking

### CRM & Sales
- Lead and opportunity management
- Sales pipeline visualization
- Quote and proposal generation
- Contact management

### Financial Management
- Automated invoicing
- Expense tracking
- Budget management
- Profitability analysis
- Financial reporting

### Resource Planning
- Team capacity overview
- Workload balancing
- Skills-based allocation
- Utilization tracking

## Integrations

- QuickBooks

- Microsoft 365
- Zapier
- And more

## Pricing

Tiered pricing based on features and team size, with plans for essential, standard, pro, and ultimate needs.

All-in-one platform eliminates tool sprawl and data silos, providing complete visibility from sales through delivery to payment.
