## Overview

WorkflowMax is a cloud-based job management solution with comprehensive time tracking, designed for professional services firms and businesses using Xero accounting software.

## Time Tracking

- Timer and manual time entry
- Mobile time tracking
- Job-level time allocation
- Billable vs. non-billable tracking
- Time approvals
- Timesheets

## Job Management

- Job creation and tracking
- Task management
- Document management
- Job templates
- Client portals
- Purchase orders

## Financial Features

- Quoting and estimates
- Invoicing from time
- Expense tracking
- Job costing
- Profitability tracking
- Budget management
- Xero integration

## Reporting

- Time reports
- Job profitability
- Staff utilization
- WIP reports
- Custom reports
