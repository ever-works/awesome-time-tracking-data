## Overview

LeechBlock is a free, open-source productivity tool that blocks time-wasting websites to help users maintain focus and avoid distractions during work or study time.

## Features

- **Website Blocking**: Block access to specific websites or website categories
- **Customizable Schedules**: Set different blocking rules for different times of day
- **Time Limits**: Allow limited access to sites (e.g., 10 minutes per hour)
- **Multiple Block Sets**: Create up to 6 different sets of blocking rules
- **Password Protection**: Prevent yourself from changing settings during focus time
- **Access Delay**: Add countdown delays before accessing blocked sites
- **Keyword Blocking**: Block sites based on keywords in URL or page title
- **Statistics**: Track how much time you've saved by blocking sites
- **Lockdown Mode**: Completely lock settings for a specified period
- **Override Options**: Temporary override with password or delay
- **Import/Export**: Share or backup your blocking configurations

## Browser Support

- Firefox
- Chrome
- Edge
- Other Chromium-based browsers

## Use Cases

- Students needing to focus on studies
- Remote workers avoiding social media during work hours
- Writers maintaining deep focus time
- Anyone struggling with internet distraction

## Advantages

- Completely free and open-source
- No account or registration required
- Highly customizable blocking rules
- Privacy-focused - no data collection
- Works offline
