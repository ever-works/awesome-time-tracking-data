## Overview

Omnicalculator's Billable Hours Calculator allows you to easily calculate billable hours and billable value of your job with a simple, straightforward interface.

## Features

- **Simple Calculation**: Easy-to-use interface for quick calculations
- **Billable Hours**: Calculate total billable hours worked
- **Revenue Calculation**: Determine billable value based on hourly rate
- **Multiple Rates**: Support for different billing rates
- **Time Input**: Flexible time input options
- **Instant Results**: Real-time calculation updates
- **Mobile Friendly**: Works on all devices

## How It Works

Enter your hourly rate and time worked, and the calculator instantly shows your billable revenue.

## Target Users

Ideal for freelancers, consultants, contractors, and service professionals who bill by the hour.

## Platform

Web-based calculator accessible from any browser.

## Pricing

Free to use.
