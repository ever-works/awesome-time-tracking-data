## What is AI Day Drafting?

Structured's AI Day Drafting feature automatically creates a complete daily schedule based on your tasks and available time, transforming planning from a manual process into an AI-assisted workflow.

## How It Works

### Input Analysis
The AI considers:
- Pending tasks from your inbox
- Task priorities and due dates
- Estimated durations
- Calendar commitments
- Your typical scheduling patterns
- Time of day preferences

### Schedule Generation
In seconds, the AI:
1. Analyzes all tasks and constraints
2. Determines optimal sequence
3. Allocates appropriate time blocks
4. Places tasks on your timeline
5. Leaves buffer time between tasks
6. Respects existing calendar events

### User Refinement
The generated schedule is a starting point:
- Review AI-generated plan
- Adjust as needed
- Move tasks to different times
- Add or remove items
- Fine-tune durations

## Benefits

### Speed
- Complete daily plan in seconds vs. minutes
- Eliminates blank-page paralysis
- Reduces planning overhead

### Consistency
- Ensures every task gets scheduled
- Maintains realistic daily capacity
- Prevents over-scheduling

### Better Than Manual
- AI considers more factors simultaneously
- Less prone to wishful thinking
- More realistic time estimates
- Learns from your patterns
