## Overview

Focusmate is a virtual coworking service that pairs you with another person for 50-minute focused work sessions via video, leveraging social accountability to maintain focus.

## How It Works

### Session Structure
1. **Book a session**: Schedule 50-minute slot
2. **Get matched**: Paired with accountability partner
3. **Brief intro** (2 min): State what you'll work on
4. **Work silently** (45 min): Both work on own tasks
5. **Brief close** (3 min): Share what you accomplished

### Key Features

**Social Accountability**
- Another person expecting you
- Commitment to show up
- Gentle peer pressure
- Shared focus time

**Structured Sessions**
- Fixed 50-minute format
- Calendar integration
- Reminder notifications
- Consistent routine

**Community**
- Meet people worldwide
- Regular partners possible
- Diverse work types
- Supportive environment
