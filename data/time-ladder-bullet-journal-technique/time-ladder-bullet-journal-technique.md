## What is the Time Ladder?

The Time Ladder is a popular bullet journal time tracking layout that creates a visual ladder structure for organizing your day.

## How to Create a Time Ladder

1. **Draw Two Parallel Lines**: Rule two vertical lines down the middle or side of your page
2. **Add Time Markers**: Write the hours of your day between the two lines (e.g., 8am, 9am, 10am)
3. **Plot Activities**: Write meetings, appointments, and tasks on either side of the ladder
4. **Track Progress**: Mark completed items or fill in blocks as you work through your day

## Benefits

- Creates a clear visual timeline of your day
- Easy to see time conflicts and gaps
- Simple to set up and maintain
- Flexible for different time increments
- Works well for time-blocking workflows

## Variations

- Horizontal ladder layout across a two-page spread
- Color-coding for different types of activities
- Using symbols or icons to categorize tasks
- Adding notes or reflections alongside time blocks
