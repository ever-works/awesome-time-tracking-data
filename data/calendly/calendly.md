## Overview

Calendly automates appointment scheduling so you can focus on what matters most. The platform eliminates back-and-forth emails by providing a streamlined booking experience for clients, colleagues, and teams.

## Key Features

### Calendar Integration
- Connect up to six calendars including Google, Outlook, and Exchange
- Automatically sync across all connected calendars to avoid double-booking
- Real-time availability updates across all platforms

### Availability Management
- Set detailed availability preferences
- Add buffers between meetings to prevent back-to-back scheduling
- Limit daily meetings to maintain work-life balance
- Custom availability by event type

### Video Conferencing Integration
- Automatically generate unique video conferencing links
- Support for Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, GoToMeeting, and more
- Conference details included in meeting confirmations

### Automated Workflows
- Automate reminders, follow-ups, and scheduling outreach with Calendly Workflows
- Reduce no-shows with automatic reminder emails and texts
- Improve engagement with personalized follow-up communications

### Team Features
- Round-robin distribution to balance meeting loads across team members
- Routing forms to direct meetings to the right team member based on invitee responses
- Collective event types for panel interviews or group meetings
- Team scheduling links and pages

### Payment Integration
- Collect payments at the time of booking
- Reduce invoicing headaches with upfront payment collection
- Integration with payment processors for seamless transactions

## Pricing

- Free Plan: Available with limited features (one event type, one calendar connection)
- Paid Plans: Starting at $10/user/month
- Enterprise: Custom pricing for large organizations

## Use Cases

- Sales teams booking product demos and consultations
- Customer success teams scheduling onboarding calls
- Recruiters coordinating interview schedules
- Consultants and coaches managing client appointments
- Service providers collecting payments at booking

- Reduces no-shows with automated reminders
