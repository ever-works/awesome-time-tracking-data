## Overview

Weekly timesheet view displays an entire week's worth of time entries in a single grid format, allowing users to see patterns, enter time across multiple days simultaneously, and quickly review weekly allocation. This view is preferred by many knowledge workers over daily time entry.

## Layout Structure

- Rows represent projects, tasks, or activities
- Columns represent days of the week (Mon-Sun)
- Cells contain hours for that task/day combination
- Row totals show hours per project
- Column totals show hours per day
- Grand total shows weekly hours

## Key Benefits

- See entire week at a glance
- Identify patterns and imbalances
- Quick data entry across multiple days
- Easier recall of weekly activities
- Efficient for repetitive work patterns
- Natural weekly planning alignment

## Common Features

- Copy time entries across days
- Apply templates to full week
- Bulk editing capabilities
- Color coding by project or status
- Subtotals by client or category
- Comparison to scheduled hours
