## Overview

PTO (Paid Time Off) management software helps organizations track employee time off including paid vacation, unpaid time off, and all types of absences. These systems automate accrual tracking, streamline approval processes, and provide visibility into team capacity.

## Core Features

### Accrual Tracking
- Automatic calculation based on tenure and policy
- Real-time balance updates
- Multiple PTO types (vacation, sick, personal)
- Rollover and expiration rules

### Request and Approval
- Employee self-service requests
- Manager approval workflows
- Team calendar visibility
- Conflict detection

### Integrations
- Payroll system synchronization
- HR management platforms
- Calendar applications
- Time tracking software

## Benefits

- Reduced administrative burden on HR
- Accurate accrual calculations
- Transparent balance visibility for employees
- Simplified compliance tracking
- Better workforce planning

## Use Cases

- Small businesses tracking employee PTO
- HR departments in medium to large companies
- Remote teams managing distributed time off
- Organizations with complex PTO policies
- Companies requiring compliance documentation
