## Overview

Paismo is a versatile HR software that provides easy-to-customize, localized HR, Payroll & Benefits management. The platform empowers organizations, particularly in the GCC region, to simplify HR management through integrated biometric attendance and comprehensive workforce tools.

## Biometric Time Attendance Features

- Integrates biometric devices to capture employee data in real-time
- Automatic attendance logging with timestamps that sync to HR or payroll system
- Seamless data transfer through connection with time-tracking machines (biometrics)
- Geofencing attendance system for remote employees clocking in via the app
- Prevents time fraud and buddy punching
- Eliminates manual errors in attendance tracking

## Flexible Time Tracking Options

- Biometric devices (fingerprint, facial recognition)
- Mobile apps with geo-location tracking
- Manual time clocks
- Self-service kiosks

## HR & Payroll Management

- Employee records management
- Payroll processing
- Benefits administration
- Performance tracking
- Leave management

## Benefits

- Speeds up check-in/check-out processes
- Minimizes administrative workload
- Ensures accurate time and attendance data
- Improves compliance with labor regulations
- Suitable for organizations with remote and field workers
