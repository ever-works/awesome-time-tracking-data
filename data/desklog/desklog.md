## Overview

Desklog is an automation-first time tracking and productivity monitoring tool built for accuracy without manual effort. It automatically tracks work hours in the background, making it ideal for freelancers and remote teams who want precise time data without the burden of starting and stopping timers.

## Features

- **Automatic Time Tracking**: Tracks work hours automatically based on computer activity
- **App & Website Monitoring**: Records time spent in applications and websites
- **Productivity Analytics**: Categorizes activities as productive, unproductive, or neutral
- **Screenshots**: Optional screenshot capture at configurable intervals
- **Idle Time Detection**: Automatically detects and removes idle time
- **Project & Task Tracking**: Assign time to specific projects and tasks
- **Attendance Management**: Track employee attendance and leaves
- **Timesheets**: Automatic generation of detailed timesheets
- **Reports**: Comprehensive productivity and time usage reports
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked billable hours

## Pricing

Starts at $1.99/user/month for basic time tracking. Premium plan at $3.99/user/month includes productivity monitoring and screenshots. Enterprise plan available with custom pricing.
