## Overview

Knack is a no-code, cloud-based relational database platform that enables businesses to build fully customized time tracking and timesheet software without writing a single line of code. It offers exceptional flexibility for organizations that need time tracking solutions tailored to their specific workflows.

## Features

- **No-Code Builder**: Intuitive drag-and-drop interface to create custom forms, tables, and dashboards
- **Custom Database**: Build relational databases for time tracking, approvals, and comprehensive reporting
- **Workflow Automation**: Create and modify workflows without coding expertise
- **Integration Hub**: 500+ SaaS integrations including QuickBooks, Zapier, Slack, Mailchimp, and Google Sheets
- **Custom Forms**: Design time entry forms that match your business processes
- **Approval Workflows**: Build multi-level approval systems customized to your organization
- **Real-Time Reporting**: Create custom dashboards and reports for project tracking and analysis
- **Role-Based Access**: Configure user permissions and access controls
- **Mobile Responsive**: Access custom time tracking apps from any device

## Use Cases

- Consultancies requiring custom billing and project tracking
- Startups with unique workflow requirements
- Professional services firms needing integrated client management
- Organizations outgrowing traditional timesheet software
- Businesses requiring tight integration with existing tools
