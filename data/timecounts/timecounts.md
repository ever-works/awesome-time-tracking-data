## Overview

Timecounts is a contemporary volunteer management platform built for modern nonprofits, offering an intuitive interface and mobile-first experience for both volunteer coordinators and volunteers.

## Features

- **Volunteer Scheduling**: Create opportunities and shifts with easy signup workflows
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Responsive design works seamlessly on smartphones and tablets
- **Hour Tracking**: Automatic and manual hour logging with approval workflows
- **Communication Hub**: Centralized messaging for volunteer announcements and updates
- **Volunteer Profiles**: Detailed volunteer information including skills, interests, and availability
- **Impact Reporting**: Measure and report on volunteer hours, economic value, and program outcomes
- **Check-In Tools**: QR code and mobile check-in for volunteer events
- **Volunteer Portal**: Self-service access for volunteers to manage their involvement
- **Skills Matching**: Match volunteers to opportunities based on skills and preferences
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync volunteer schedules with personal calendars
- **Customizable Forms**: Build custom volunteer applications and waivers
- **Group Coordination**: Manage corporate volunteer groups and team events
- **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports for grant applications and board presentations

## Modern Approach

Designed with contemporary user experience principles, making it easy for volunteers to engage and for coordinators to manage programs without extensive training.

## Benefits

Increases volunteer participation through user-friendly tools, reduces no-shows with automated reminders, and provides compelling impact data for fundraising and grants.

## Pricing

Tiered pricing based on organization size and features needed. Free trial available to test the platform.
