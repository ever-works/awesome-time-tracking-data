## Overview

The Timeular Tracker (formerly called ZEI) is an eight-sided physical dice that makes time tracking instant and fun. Each side can be assigned to a different task or project, and simply flipping the device automatically tracks time.

## How It Works

### Physical Tracking
1. Assign tasks to each of the 8 sides
2. Place the dice with desired task facing up
3. Tracker automatically starts timing that task
4. Flip to another side to switch tasks
5. Device logs time spent on each activity

### Wireless Connection
Connects via Bluetooth to:
- Desktop app (macOS, Windows, Linux)
- Mobile apps (iOS, Android)
- Real-time synchronization
- Cloud backup

## Benefits

### Tactile Tracking
- Physical interaction more engaging than digital timers
- Visible reminder to track time
- No need to remember to start/stop timers
- Satisfying physical flip action

### Simplicity
- One flip to switch tasks
- No complex interfaces during work
- Intuitive operation
- No learning curve

## Use Cases

- Freelancers juggling multiple clients
- Professionals switching between project types
- People who forget to use digital timers
- Visual/kinesthetic learners
- Teams wanting physical time awareness

## Kickstarter Origin

Originally launched through a successful Kickstarter campaign, gaining popularity as an innovative approach to time tracking.

## Pricing

Physical device sold separately, requires subscription to Timeular software for full functionality.

## Key Innovation

Brings time tracking into the physical world, making it more tangible and harder to forget than purely digital solutions.
