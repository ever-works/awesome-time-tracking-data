## Overview

Focus To-Do combines the Pomodoro Technique with task management to help users boost productivity. The app tracks time spent at work while helping manage tasks effectively.

## Features

- Pomodoro timer with customizable work/break intervals
- Task and project management with subtasks
- Time tracking and productivity statistics
- Cross-platform synchronization (iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, browser)
- Recurring tasks and reminders
- Daily/weekly/monthly productivity reports
- White noise for concentration

## Pricing

Offers a free plan with basic features. Premium subscription available for advanced features like unlimited projects and enhanced reports.
