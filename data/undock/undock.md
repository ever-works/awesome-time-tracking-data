## Overview

Undock is a predictive scheduling platform that functions like a spell-checker for time management, proactively suggesting mutually available times directly within your workflow.

## Key Features

### Smart Scheduling
- AI-driven calendar assistant that learns your preferences
- 'Time Radar' for visualizing mutual availability in real time
- Cross-platform synchronization that prevents double-booking
- Set preferred meeting times, daily limits, and custom rules

### Platform Integration
- Access your schedule through Gmail, Outlook 365, and Superhuman
- Schedule meetings through Slack, Twitter, and LinkedIn
- Mobile keyboard integration for calendar access in any app
- Chrome extension for seamless inbox scheduling

### Video Conferencing
- Built-in video conferencing with browser-based access
- Audio-only or in-person meeting options
- Screen sharing capabilities
- Call recording features

### Meeting Management
- Set daily and weekly meeting caps to avoid overwhelm
- Customizable scheduling windows and availability rules
- Built-in scheduling assistant for inbox-based coordination
