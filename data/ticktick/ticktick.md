## Overview

TickTick is a powerful task management and productivity application that combines to-do lists, calendar, habit tracking, and time management features in one comprehensive platform. It's designed for both individuals and teams who want to stay organized and productive.

## Features

- **Smart Lists**: Create custom lists with filters and tags for better organization
- **Calendar View**: See all tasks and events in one unified calendar
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Built-in focus timer to help you work in focused intervals
- **Timeboxing Support**: Allocate specific time blocks to tasks directly in your schedule
- **Habit Tracking**: Build and monitor daily habits alongside your tasks
- **Natural Language Input**: Add tasks quickly using natural language processing
- **Collaboration Tools**: Share lists and assign tasks to team members
- **Multiple Reminders**: Set location-based, time-based, and recurring reminders
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Available on iOS, Android, Web, Windows, Mac, and browser extensions
- **Integration**: Connects with Outlook, Slack, Gmail, and other popular tools
- **Priority Levels**: Four priority levels to help focus on what matters most
- **Sub-tasks and Checklists**: Break down complex tasks into manageable steps
- **Duration Tracking**: Estimate and track how long tasks take

## Pricing

Free tier available with basic features. Premium subscription starts at $2.99/month or $27.99/year, offering additional features like calendar view, custom smart lists, and more storage.
