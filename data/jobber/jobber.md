## Overview

Jobber is field service management software designed for home service businesses like plumbing, HVAC, landscaping, and cleaning services. The platform combines scheduling, dispatching, invoicing, and time tracking in one solution.

## Time Tracking Features

**Job Costing:**
- Real-time profitability tracking
- Track materials and labor costs
- Compare estimated vs. actual time
- Job-level cost analysis

**Time Management:**
- Employee time tracking
- Job duration logging
- Break time tracking
- Integrated with scheduling

## Key Features

**Scheduling & Dispatching:**
- Drag-and-drop schedule management
- Route optimization
- Team member assignments
- Client appointment booking

**Invoicing & Payments:**
- Automated invoice generation
- Online payment processing
- Quote creation and tracking
- Payment reminders

**Client Management:**
- Client database and history
- Communication tools
- Service history tracking
- Follow-up automation

## Pricing (2026)

- Solo: $25/month (annual billing)
- 5 users: $109/month (annual billing)
- Additional plans available for larger teams

## Target Market

Best suited for growing mid-sized home service businesses that need comprehensive field service management with strong time tracking and job costing capabilities.

## Value Proposition

Jobber offers the best value for growing businesses, providing enterprise-level features at competitive pricing for teams that have outgrown simple scheduling tools but don't need enterprise-scale complexity.
