## Overview

TimeBolt is an AI-powered video editing tool designed to streamline the editing process for videos and podcasts by automatically removing silences, speeding up scenes, and rapidly cutting commentary. It can remove silence and jump cut an hour of video in just 13 seconds.

## Key Time-Saving Features

### Automatic Silence Detection
TimeBolt uses on-device waveform analysis to detect silence with 0.01s precision—not AI transcriptions. Algorithms cut silence for you saving 50% of manual edit time.

### Additional Features
- Removes filler words, phrases, and bad takes with AI-powered UMCHECK™
- Local processing means your footage never leaves your machine with no cloud upload
- Export options to Premiere, DaVinci, or Final Cut

## Real User Time Savings

Users report significant time reductions. One user noted: manually cutting out silence takes about 5 minutes for every 1 minute of video, but TimeBolt did it in a little over 1 minute for a 4K video.
