## Overview

Dr. Neil Fiore is a psychologist, author, and speaker who specializes in productivity, stress management, and overcoming procrastination. His work brings psychological insights to time management practices.

## Professional Background

- PhD in Psychology from the University of Maryland
- Former captain in the U.S. Army
- Over 30 years of experience as a coach and therapist
- Speaker and consultant to corporations and universities

## The Now Habit

His most famous contribution is The Now Habit system, which reframes procrastination as a psychological coping mechanism rather than a character flaw. The system addresses:
- Fear and anxiety around work
- Perfectionism and fear of failure
- Resentment from feeling overworked
- Lack of clear priorities

## Key Insights

Fiore's work reveals that procrastination often stems from:
- Using work to fill all available time, leading to burnout
- Lack of guilt-free leisure creating resentment
- Overwhelming tasks that trigger avoidance
- Unrealistic expectations about productivity

## Published Works

- "The Now Habit: A Strategic Program for Overcoming Procrastination and Enjoying Guilt-Free Play" (1988)
- "The Now Habit at Work: Perform Optimally, Maintain Focus, and Ignite Motivation in Yourself and Others" (2010)
- "Awaken Your Strongest Self: Break Free of Stress, Inner Conflict, and Self-Sabotage" (2006)

## Legacy

Fiore's psychological approach to time management has influenced how we understand the emotional and mental aspects of productivity, moving beyond purely mechanical systems.
