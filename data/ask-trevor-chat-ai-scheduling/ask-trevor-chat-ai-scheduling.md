## Overview

Ask Trevor Chat represents Trevor AI's natural language interface for task and schedule management, allowing users to manage their time through conversation rather than manual data entry.

## Context-Aware Intelligence

Ask Trevor understands:
- **Your Tasks**: Complete task list with priorities and due dates
- **Calendar Events**: Existing commitments and availability
- **Scheduling Preferences**: Preferred work times and patterns
- **Personal Context**: Work habits and productivity patterns

## Example Commands

### Task Management
- "Schedule my highest priority tasks for tomorrow morning"
- "Move all Friday tasks to next week"
- "Find time for the design review before the deadline"

### Complex Scheduling
- "Block out focus time each morning this week"
- "Reschedule everything from today that I didn't complete"
- "Show me what I can realistically finish by Friday"

### Analysis & Insights
- "When am I most productive?"
- "How much time did I spend on Project X this week?"
- "Do I have time for a 2-hour task tomorrow?"

## Advantages Over Manual Scheduling

### Speed
- Complex operations in seconds
- Batch scheduling multiple tasks
- Instant rescheduling

### Intelligence
- Considers multiple factors simultaneously
- Learns from your patterns
- Suggests optimal times

### Reduced Friction
- Natural language instead of clicks
- No need to remember specific commands
- Conversational interaction feels intuitive

## Comparison to Traditional AI Assistants

Unlike general AI assistants, Ask Trevor has deep integration with your specific productivity data, enabling more accurate and useful scheduling suggestions.
