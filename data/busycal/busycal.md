## Overview

BusyCal is the most powerful, flexible, reliable calendar app for macOS. It's packed with innovative, time-saving features including customizable views, calendar sets, integrated tasks, travel time, smart filters, natural language input, weather, moons, graphics, tags, do not disturb mode and much more.

## Key Features

- **Time Blocking & Travel Time**: Block out time for walking, driving or taking mass transit to an event or location with automatic calculation using Location Services
- **Integrated Tasks**: Display tasks right in your calendar on their due dates and carry them forward until completed
- **Natural Language Input**: Create events and tasks with preview of event details displayed while you type
- **Smart Filters**: Stitch together complex search combinations, toggle between calendar sets, and memorize view settings
- **Menu Bar Extension**: Navigate freely across months, drag out to floating window, infinite scrolling in agenda list
