## Overview

The stride80 is Accu-Time Systems' flagship biometric time clock featuring Only|You palm vein scan technology, unveiled for the 2026 market as a premium touchless authentication solution for workforce time and attendance tracking.

## Technology

### Only|You Palm Vein Recognition
- Scans vein patterns beneath skin surface using near-infrared light
- Highly secure authentication that cannot be replicated
- Completely touchless operation for maximum hygiene
- Recognition speed under 0.3 seconds in 1:1 verification mode
- Under 1 second in 1:N identification mode for databases up to 6,000 people

## Key Features

- **Superior Hygiene**: Zero contact required, ideal for post-pandemic workplace safety
- **High Accuracy**: Virtually eliminates false accepts and false rejects
- **Fast Processing**: Sub-second authentication for high-traffic environments
- **Liveness Detection**: Confirms the presence of actual blood flow
- **Weather Resistant**: Works regardless of hand conditions (wet, dry, dirty)
- **Privacy Friendly**: Only scans vein patterns, not personal characteristics

## Advantages Over Alternative Biometric Methods

### vs. Fingerprint
- More hygienic (touchless)
- Works with gloves or damaged fingertips
- Not affected by dry or oily skin

### vs. Facial Recognition
- Not affected by masks, glasses, or facial hair
- More consistent in varying lighting conditions
- Higher accuracy and security

### vs. RFID Cards
- Prevents buddy punching (cannot share vein pattern)
- No cards to lose or forget
- Cannot be stolen or duplicated
