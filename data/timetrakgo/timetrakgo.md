## Overview

TimeTrakGO is a mobile time clock app with GPS location tracking designed for field service businesses. It combines time tracking with location verification to ensure accurate attendance and job costing for mobile workers.

## Features

- Mobile GPS time clock
- Geofencing and location verification
- Real-time employee location tracking
- Job site assignment
- Photo punch verification
- Offline time tracking
- Automated timesheet generation
- Job costing capabilities
- Mileage tracking
- RFID proximity badge support
- Scheduling integration
- Payroll export
- Manager alerts and notifications
- Multi-device support

## Pricing

Affordable per-user pricing with monthly or annual billing. Various plan levels based on features needed. Free trial available to test the platform.
