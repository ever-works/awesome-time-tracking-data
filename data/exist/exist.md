## Overview

Exist is a comprehensive personal analytics service that connects to dozens of apps and devices to automatically track your life, then uses machine learning to find meaningful correlations and insights in your data.

## Features

- **Automatic Tracking**: Integrates with 40+ services including RescueTime, Fitbit, Apple Health, Spotify, and more
- **Manual Tracking**: Add custom attributes to track anything not automatically captured
- **Correlation Discovery**: Machine learning algorithms identify correlations between different aspects of your life
- **Mood Tracking**: Track daily mood and see how it correlates with sleep, activity, and productivity
- **Productivity Insights**: Understand what factors affect your productive time and focus
- **Health Analytics**: Correlate health metrics with lifestyle factors
- **Custom Attributes**: Create unlimited custom tracking attributes for anything you want to measure
- **Data Visualization**: Beautiful charts and graphs to visualize trends over time
- **Weekly Reports**: Automated weekly insights delivered via email
- **Data Export**: Export all data for external analysis
- **Privacy-Focused**: You control what data is tracked and can delete anytime
