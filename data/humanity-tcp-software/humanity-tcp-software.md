## Overview

Humanity (now part of TCP Software) is an enterprise workforce management platform offering advanced scheduling, time and attendance, and labor optimization features for mid-market and enterprise organizations.

## Key Features

- **Advanced Scheduling**: Complex shift patterns and rotation management
- **Demand Forecasting**: AI-powered staffing predictions based on historical data
- **Auto-Scheduling**: Automatically generate optimized schedules
- **Time & Attendance**: Integrated time clock with multiple punch options
- **Compliance Engine**: Automated compliance with labor laws and union rules
- **Skills-Based Scheduling**: Match workers to shifts based on certifications and skills
- **Shift Marketplace**: Self-service shift trading and bidding
- **Absence Management**: PTO, sick leave, and absence tracking
- **Fatigue Management**: Prevent overwork and ensure adequate rest
- **Multi-Location**: Manage scheduling across multiple sites and departments
- **API Integration**: Connect with ERP, HRIS, and payroll systems
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Advanced workforce analytics and insights
