## Overview

Neon CRM is an all-in-one nonprofit management platform that uniquely combines volunteer management with donor management, event registration, and fundraising tools, enabling organizations to see the full picture of constituent engagement.

## Features

- **Volunteer Management**: Recruit, schedule, and track volunteers with dedicated tools
- **Hour Tracking**: Log volunteer hours and generate service reports
- **Donor Management**: Complete CRM for tracking donors, gifts, and engagement
- **Fundraising**: Online donation forms, peer-to-peer fundraising, and campaigns
- **Event Management**: Registration, ticketing, and check-in for events
- **Email Marketing**: Segment and communicate with volunteers, donors, and members
- **Membership Management**: Track memberships, renewals, and member benefits
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive analytics on fundraising, volunteer impact, and engagement
- **Constituent Profiles**: Unified view of each person's volunteer hours, donations, and involvement
- **Mobile Apps**: Access volunteer and donor information on mobile devices
- **Integration**: Connect with accounting, email, and other nonprofit tools
- **Custom Fields**: Flexible data structure for organization-specific needs

## Unique Value

The integration of volunteer and donor management allows nonprofits to understand the full relationship with each constituent, identifying volunteers who might become donors and donors who might want to volunteer.

## Benefits

Eliminates data silos by managing volunteers, donors, and members in one system, providing a complete view of constituent engagement and enabling more strategic relationship building.
