## Overview

Everhour is a time tracking solution designed to work seamlessly within existing project management tools. Rather than being a standalone application, Everhour embeds time tracking controls directly into platforms like Asana, Jira, Trello, and ClickUp, eliminating context switching and streamlining workflow.

## Key Features

- **Native integrations** - Time tracking controls appear directly in your project management tasks (Asana, Jira, ClickUp, Monday, Trello, Basecamp, Notion, GitHub)
- **Automatic syncing** - Projects and tasks automatically sync from connected platforms
- **Timer and manual entry** - Track time with one-click timers or add time manually
- **Project budgeting** - Set time and cost budgets with threshold alerts
- **Billing & invoicing** - Track billable hours and generate invoices
- **Team scheduling** - Plan team capacity and resource allocation
- **Comprehensive reporting** - Analyze time data across projects, clients, and team members
- **Browser extension** - Access time tracking from anywhere on the web
- **Cross-platform tracking** - Track time across multiple tools simultaneously and aggregate in one place

## Use Cases

- Teams already using project management tools who need time tracking
- Agencies managing multiple clients across different platforms
- Professional services tracking billable hours by project
- Organizations wanting to avoid switching between multiple apps

## Pricing

Everhour offers tiered pricing based on team size with a free trial available. Plans include time tracking, budgeting, invoicing, and integrations.

Everhour is particularly valuable for teams that want time tracking integrated into their existing workflow rather than adding another standalone tool to their stack.
