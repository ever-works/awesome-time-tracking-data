## Overview

OLOID is an enterprise-grade facial recognition platform that provides contactless workforce authentication and time tracking. Their AI-powered biometric technology enables accurate, touchless employee clock-in/out while preventing time theft and buddy punching.

## Features

- Advanced AI facial recognition (sub-second identification)
- Contactless biometric authentication
- Mobile and kiosk-based clock-in options
- Multi-factor authentication support
- Mask detection and temperature screening
- Works in various lighting conditions
- Privacy-focused with encrypted biometric data
- Integration with workforce management systems
- Real-time attendance tracking
- Audit trails and compliance reporting
- Scalable for large enterprises
- Cloud-based or on-premise deployment
