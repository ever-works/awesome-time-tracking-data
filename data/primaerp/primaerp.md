## Overview

PrimaERP is a comprehensive cloud-based ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solution that includes project management and time tracking functionality designed for small to medium-sized businesses.

## Key Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track employee hours on projects and tasks
- **Project Management**: Plan, execute, and monitor projects with integrated tools
- **Resource Management**: Allocate and track resource utilization
- **Financial Management**: Manage budgets, expenses, and project profitability
- **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports on time, costs, and project performance

## Time Tracking Capabilities

- Employee timesheet management
- Project-based time allocation
- Billable vs non-billable hour tracking
- Integration with project budgets and invoicing

## Benefits

- Centralized platform for project and business management
- Real-time visibility into project costs and profitability
- Streamlined workflow from time tracking to invoicing
- Cloud accessibility from anywhere

## Best For

- Small to medium-sized businesses
- Project-based organizations
- Companies needing ERP functionality with time tracking
- Teams requiring integrated resource and financial management
