## Overview

Replicon is an enterprise-grade time intelligence platform that combines time tracking, project management, and workforce analytics with AI-powered automation to help large organizations manage time, ensure compliance, and optimize labor costs.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Timesheets**: Automatically suggest time entries based on calendar, emails, and work patterns
- **Project Time Tracking**: Track time across multiple projects with task-level granularity
- **Geofencing**: Location-based time tracking for field workers and remote teams
- **Compliance Management**: Automated compliance with labor laws across multiple jurisdictions
- **Workforce Analytics**: Deep insights into productivity, utilization, and labor costs
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps with offline capability for field workers
- **Client Billing**: Track billable vs non-billable time and generate client invoices
- **Leave Management**: Integrated time-off tracking and approval workflows
- **Payroll Integration**: Direct integration with major payroll providers globally
- **Multi-Currency**: Support for global teams with multiple currencies and tax rates
