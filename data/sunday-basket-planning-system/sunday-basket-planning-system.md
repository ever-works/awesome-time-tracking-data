## Overview

The Sunday Basket is a comprehensive weekly planning system developed by Organize 365 that helps people transition from cluttered counters and mental overwhelm to clarity and confidence by establishing a new weekly rhythm.

## How It Works

Instead of dealing with each paper and task as it enters your home or life, you collect those items in a centralized basket to process on one set day each week, typically Sunday. This approach saves time and ensures each item receives the attention it needs.

## Core Components

**Weekly Planning Sheets**: Dedicated sheets with sections for each day of the week, meal planning, organizing plans, top 3 tasks, goals, and space for appointments or meetings.

**Slash Pockets System**: The 1.0 Slash Pockets manage categories like errands, finances, dates to add to calendar, items you're waiting on, and tasks that must be completed this week.

**Processing Time**: Most people dedicate 1-2 hours on Sundays to plan the week ahead, regardless of other commitments.

## Key Benefits

- Reduces daily decision fatigue
- Creates a single source of truth for weekly tasks
- Eliminates counter clutter
- Establishes consistent weekly planning rhythm
- Helps prioritize what truly matters
- Reduces mental load throughout the week

## Implementation

1. Get a physical basket or container
2. Collect papers and tasks throughout the week
3. Set aside 1-2 hours on Sunday (or your chosen day)
4. Process all items using the planning sheets
5. Set up slash pockets for different categories
6. Review and plan the upcoming week

## Best For

- Busy families managing multiple schedules
- People overwhelmed by paper clutter
- Anyone wanting structured weekly planning
- Those who prefer physical organization systems
