## Overview

Timely is an AI-powered automatic time tracking solution that eliminates manual timers by creating a comprehensive, private record of your computer activity. It then uses AI to suggest how that time should be allocated to projects, making timesheet creation effortless and accurate.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Activity Capture**: Records all applications, websites, and documents used
- **Memory Timeline**: Private, searchable record of your digital workday
- **AI Suggestions**: Smart recommendations for time allocation
- **One-Click Timesheets**: Drag activities to projects instead of running timers
- **Privacy-First**: All data stays private; only allocated time is shared
- **No Screenshots**: Tracks activity without invasive monitoring
- **Cross-Platform**: macOS, Windows, Linux, mobile apps
- **Project Management**: Built-in project and task organization
- **Team Features**: Capacity planning, scheduling, and reporting
- **Integrations**: Connects with Google Calendar, Asana, GitHub, and more

## How It Works

1. **Background Tracking**: Timely runs quietly, recording what you work on
2. **AI Processing**: Creates timeline and suggests project allocations
3. **Review & Allocate**: At day's end, drag activities to correct projects (takes 1-2 minutes)
4. **Automatic Timesheets**: Your timesheet is created automatically
