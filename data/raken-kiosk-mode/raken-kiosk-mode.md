## Overview

Raken Kiosk Mode transforms any tablet or shared device into a centralized time clock for construction crews. Employees can clock in and out from a single device at the job site, eliminating the need for individual employee devices or expensive dedicated time clock hardware.

## Features

- AI-powered photo ID verification at each clock-in/out
- Automated geolocation capture
- Buddy punching prevention through facial recognition
- Multi-worker support on single device
- Offline functionality for areas without connectivity
- Real-time sync when connection restored
- Integration with daily reports and project documentation
- Supervisor approval workflows
- Export to payroll systems

## AI Photo Verification

When workers clock in, Raken's AI captures and verifies their photo against their profile. This automated verification prevents time theft by ensuring the person clocking in is actually the employee they claim to be, without requiring manual verification by supervisors.

## Geolocation & Site Tracking

Each time entry automatically captures GPS coordinates, confirming workers are actually at the job site when clocking in. This provides documentation for client billing, compliance audits, and project tracking.

## Use Cases

Ideal for construction companies, general contractors, and subcontractors who need to track time for crews working at multiple job sites. Particularly valuable for companies with workers who don't have company-provided devices or access to smartphones.

## Integration

Raken Kiosk Mode integrates with the broader Raken platform, which includes daily reports, safety toolbox talks, photo documentation, and project management features, creating a comprehensive solution for construction field management.
