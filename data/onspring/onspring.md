## Overview

Onspring is a cloud-based governance, risk and compliance (GRC) platform that helps streamline business processes and enhance efficiency across various industries. The platform caters to enterprises and government agencies seeking to automate their GRC efforts such as risk management, compliance, internal audit, and third-party vendor risk.

## Features

- **Internal Audit Management**: Seamless process automation from planning to reporting with real-time analytics
- **No-Code Platform**: Create new applications, surveys, and reports without custom coding
- **Process Automation**: Automate workflows and business processes
- **Live Analytics**: Real-time reporting and analytics dashboards
- **SOC 2 Audit Support**: Manage compliance for privacy, security, availability, processing integrity, or confidentiality controls
- **IT Audit Capabilities**: Track and report across business units with specialized programs including GDPR, NIST, and ISO regulations
- **Risk Management**: Comprehensive risk assessment and mitigation tracking
- **Compliance Management**: Ensure adherence to regulatory requirements
- **Vendor Management**: Third-party vendor risk assessment and monitoring
- **Business Continuity**: Disaster recovery and business continuity planning
- **Incident Management**: Track and resolve incidents and issues
- **Decentralized Team Management**: Coordinate distributed audit teams

## Target Industries

Onspring serves various sectors including financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, government agencies, and other highly regulated industries.
