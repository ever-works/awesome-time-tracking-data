## Overview

TalentDesk is an all-in-one freelancer and contractor management platform designed for companies working with independent contractors globally. It streamlines onboarding, contract management, time tracking, invoicing, payments, and compliance in a single integrated system.

## Core Features

- Contractor onboarding and profile management
- Contract creation and e-signature
- Time tracking and timesheet management
- Project and task tracking
- Invoice generation and approval
- Global payment processing
- Compliance and tax management
- Agent of Record (AOR) services
- Reporting and analytics
- Integration with accounting and HR systems
