## Overview

Same-day time logging is recognized as the single most effective practice for improving time tracking data accuracy. This approach requires employees to log their time daily rather than waiting until the end of the week.

## Key Principles

- Log time entries on the same day work is performed
- Reduce reliance on memory for accurate time capture
- Enforce through automated end-of-day reminders
- Use calendar pre-population to reduce friction
- Enable mobile entry for commutes and transitions
- Create organizational habit around daily logging

## Benefits

- Significantly improved time tracking accuracy
- Reduced timesheet errors and corrections
- Better billable hour capture
- More reliable project data
- Easier approval processes
- Reduced administrative burden
- Improved compliance with billing requirements

## Implementation

Effective same-day logging requires organizational commitment, automated reminders, mobile accessibility, and manager reinforcement of the daily logging expectation.
