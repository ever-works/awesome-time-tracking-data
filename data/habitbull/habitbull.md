## Overview

HabitBull is a simple yet powerful habit tracking application for iOS and Android that helps users build streaks, track multiple habits simultaneously, and visualize progress with charts and statistics.

## Features

- **Unlimited Habits**: Track as many habits as you want with customizable goals
- **Streak Tracking**: Build and maintain streaks to stay motivated
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Set habits for daily, weekly, or custom schedules
- **Reminders**: Customizable notifications to remind you to complete habits
- **Calendar View**: Visual calendar showing habit completion with color coding
- **Charts & Statistics**: Track progress over time with graphs and success percentages
- **Notes**: Add notes to habit entries to track context and insights
- **Cloud Backup**: Sync data across devices with cloud backup
- **Motivational Quotes**: Daily inspirational quotes to keep you motivated
- **Habit Groups**: Organize related habits into categories

## Pricing

Free version with ads supports up to 5 habits. Premium version ($4.99 one-time or subscription) removes ads, allows unlimited habits, and includes additional features like habit groups and extended statistics.
