## Overview

Tim is a fully native time tracking application designed specifically for macOS. Created by Neat Software, it emphasizes simplicity, privacy, and seamless integration with the macOS ecosystem.

## Key Features

- **Menu Bar Integration**: See tracked time directly in the macOS menu bar
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Control time tracking without leaving your keyboard
- **Visual Analytics**: Beautiful charts showing hours worked, averages, and revenue
- **Idle Detection**: Prompts to handle time when you return from being away
- **Export Options**: CSV, JSON, or iCal formats
- **Light & Dark Mode**: Native support for macOS appearance modes
- **Complete Privacy**: Data stays entirely on your device, no account or internet required

## System Requirements

- macOS 12.4 or later
- Compatible with both Intel-based and Apple Silicon Macs

## Pricing

## Data Privacy

Tim prioritizes user privacy:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No user accounts required
- No internet connection needed
- You maintain complete ownership and control of your time tracking data
