## Overview

ClickTime transforms work hours into accurate, finance-ready data by capturing labor costs structured by project, client, cost center, and work type at the moment of entry. This immediate cost association enables real-time financial visibility and control.

## Labor Cost Capture

- Associate labor rates with employees, roles, or projects
- Capture costs at time of entry, not retrospectively
- Structure costs by multiple dimensions simultaneously
- Enable accurate project costing and profitability analysis

## Key Dimensions

**By Project**: Track costs per project for profitability

**By Client**: Understand client-specific labor expenses

**By Cost Center**: Allocate costs to departments or divisions

**By Work Type**: Differentiate between billable, non-billable, internal

## Use Cases

**Professional Services**: Bill clients accurately based on actual labor costs

**Project Management**: Monitor project budgets against labor spend

**Finance Teams**: Get real-time visibility into labor expense

**Resource Planning**: Understand true cost of resource allocation

## Integration

- Export to accounting systems (QuickBooks, etc.)
- Connect with payroll for reconciliation
- Feed financial reporting and analytics
- Support billing and invoicing workflows

## Benefits

- Real-time cost visibility
- Accurate project profitability analysis
- Better budget management
- Improved cost forecasting
- Finance-ready data without manual processing
