## Overview

Loop Habit Tracker is a completely open-source habit tracking app designed to help users build and maintain long-term positive habits. It stands out for its commitment to privacy, ad-free experience, and flexible tracking capabilities.

## Key Features

- **Open Source & Privacy**: Published under GPLv3 license with complete source code available on GitHub. Loop doesn't require an Internet connection or online account registration, and all data stays on your device
- **Ad-Free Forever**: Absolutely no advertisements, annoying notifications, or intrusive permissions - and there will never be
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Supports both simple daily habits and complex schedules including:
  - 3 times every week
  - One time every other week  
  - Every other day
  - Custom frequencies
- **Smart Reminders**: Create individual reminders for each habit at a chosen hour of the day. Easily check, dismiss, or snooze habits directly from the notification without opening the app
- **Beautiful Visualizations**: Track progress with colorful charts and graphs showing your habit streaks and completion patterns
- **Detailed Statistics**: View comprehensive data about your habits including:
  - Current streaks
  - Historical performance
  - Success rates
  - Trends over time
- **Home Screen Widgets**: Monitor and track your habits directly from your Android home screen with beautiful and colorful widgets
- **No Account Required**: No registration, no login, no data syncing to cloud servers

## Platform Availability

- **Android Only**: Available on Android devices
- **Download Options**:
  - Google Play Store
  - F-Droid (open-source app repository)
  - Direct APK download
  - Source code on GitHub (github.com/iSoron/uhabits)

## Pricing

**Completely Free**: Loop Habit Tracker is 100% free with no premium tiers, in-app purchases, or subscriptions. All features are available to all users.
