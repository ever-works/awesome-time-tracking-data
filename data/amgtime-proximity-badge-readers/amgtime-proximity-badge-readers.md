## Overview

AMGtime proximity badge readers verify employee identity by reading RFID cards, with employees only needing to pass within a couple of inches of the reader.

## Key Features

- **Proximity Technology**: Read badges from 2-4 inches away
- **Multiple Frequencies**: Support for 125kHz and 13.56MHz
- **HID Compatible**: Works with HID proximity cards
- **Fast Reads**: Instant badge recognition
- **Durable Construction**: Built for industrial environments
- **LED Indicators**: Visual feedback for successful reads
- **Audio Feedback**: Beep confirmation for clock-ins
- **TCP/IP Connectivity**: Network-connected for real-time data
- **PoE Support**: Power over Ethernet options available

## Models Available

- Wall-mounted readers
- Desktop units
- Outdoor weatherproof models
- Combined biometric and proximity readers

## Integration

- Works with AMGtime software platforms
- Integrates with major payroll systems
- API available for custom integrations

## Use Cases

Ideal for businesses wanting contactless time tracking without fingerprint or facial recognition.
