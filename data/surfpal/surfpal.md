## Overview

SurfPal is a browser-based time tracking tool designed to track, analyze, and limit time spent on each website. It helps users understand their browsing habits and improve productivity by providing detailed insights into web activity patterns.

## Features

- **Website Time Tracking**: Automatically tracks time spent on every website
- **Activity Analysis**: Analyze browsing patterns and habits
- **Time Limits**: Set limits on time spent on specific websites
- **Productivity Categorization**: Categorize websites as productive or distracting
- **Detailed Reports**: View reports on daily, weekly, and monthly web usage
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: See current session time on active website
- **Alerts & Notifications**: Get notified when approaching time limits
- **Privacy-Focused**: Data stored locally on your device
- **Browser Integration**: Works seamlessly within your browser
- **Focus Mode**: Block distracting websites during focus sessions

## Use Cases

- Understanding time wasted on social media and entertainment sites
- Limiting time on distracting websites during work hours
- Tracking research time spent on various sites
- Analyzing productivity patterns throughout the day
- Maintaining work-life balance by monitoring after-hours browsing

## Target Audience

Remote workers, students, freelancers, and anyone looking to better understand and control their web browsing habits.
