## Overview

Manganum is a Chrome extension designed to make you more productive by consolidating your most-used productivity tools into an elegant sidebar interface.

## Features

- **Google Services Integration**: Access Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive, Google Keep, and Google Tasks without leaving your current tab
- **AI Integration**: Built-in access to ChatGPT and other AI assistants for quick queries and assistance
- **One-Click Access**: Quickly toggle between different apps and services with a single click from the sidebar
- **Sleek Design**: Modern, minimalist interface that doesn't clutter your browsing experience
- **Time Management**: Keep track of calendar events and tasks while working on other tasks

## Benefits

By reducing context switching and keeping essential tools accessible, Manganum helps users save time and maintain focus throughout their workday.

## Availability

Available as a Chrome browser extension, compatible with all Chromium-based browsers.

## Pricing

Free version available with premium features offered through subscription.
