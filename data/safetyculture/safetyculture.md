## Overview

SafetyCulture is an all-in-one operations platform that combines safety inspections, quality control, time tracking, and team communication tools to help organizations maintain compliance and improve operational efficiency.

## Time Tracking Features

- **GPS Time Tracking**: Track employee location when clocking in and out
- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for field workers using smartphones and tablets
- **Geofencing**: Ensure employees are at correct job sites when tracking time
- **Photo Verification**: Capture photos during clock-in for additional verification
- **Timesheet Management**: Review, approve, and export timesheets
- **Integration with Inspections**: Link time tracking with safety inspections and audits
- **Offline Mode**: Track time even without internet connectivity
- **Real-Time Notifications**: Alerts for missed clock-ins or policy violations

## Additional Capabilities

- Digital inspections and audits
- Issue management and corrective actions
- Training and competency management
- Asset and equipment tracking
- Safety documentation and reporting
- Team communication and collaboration
