## Overview

FreelanceHourlyRate.com provides a straightforward calculator to help freelancers determine their ideal hourly charge. The tool considers income goals, experience level, and industry standards to generate appropriate pricing recommendations.

## Key Features

- Simple, user-friendly interface
- Income goal-based calculations
- Experience level considerations
- Industry-specific benchmarks
- Instant rate recommendations
- No signup required
- Free to use

## Input Parameters

- Desired income goal
- Current experience level
- Industry or field
- Work schedule preferences
- Geographic location (optional)

## How It Works

1. Enter your annual income target
2. Select your experience level (beginner, intermediate, expert)
3. Choose your industry or field
4. Input expected working hours
5. Receive instant hourly rate calculation
