## Overview

Atto is a mobile-first time tracking app built for teams on the move, specifically designed for field-based operations. With GPS tracking, team scheduling, and real-time clock-ins, it's designed for industries like construction, home services, cleaning, and other field-based operations.

## Key Features

### GPS & Location Tracking
- Real-time GPS tracking to monitor employee locations during work hours
- Live GPS tracking and geofencing for accurate, location-based clock-ins
- Automated mileage logging to track travel between job sites
- Location history and route tracking for accountability

### Time Tracking
- Mobile-first time clock for employees to clock in/out via iOS or Android app
- Automatic timesheet generation for payroll processing
- Real-time visibility into who's working and where
- Overtime tracking and alerts

### Team Management
- Scheduling and shift management capabilities
- Break tracking and management
- Team calendar and availability views
- Mobile notifications for schedule changes

### Reporting & Payroll
- Detailed timesheets for accurate payroll processing
- Export data to popular payroll systems
- Customizable reports for labor costs and productivity
- Manager web dashboard for oversight

## Platform

Atto is a mobile-first tool where employees use the iOS or Android app to track time, while managers access reports and settings via the web dashboard.
