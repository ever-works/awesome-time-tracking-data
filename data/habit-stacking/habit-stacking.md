## What is Habit Stacking?

Habit stacking is identifying a current habit and stacking a new behavior on top. Format: "After I [CURRENT HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT]."

## Examples

- After I pour my morning coffee, I will meditate for 60 seconds
- After I sit down at my desk, I will write down my top three priorities
- After I close my laptop for lunch, I will do 10 pushups

## Why It Works

- Leverages existing neural pathways
- Removes need to remember separately
- Creates automatic behavioral chains
- Lower willpower requirement

## Best Practices

- Stack habits of similar duration
- Choose obvious trigger habits
- Make new habit specific
- Start very small
- Be consistent with the stack
