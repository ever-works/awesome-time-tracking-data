## Overview

Brain.fm is a focus music app that uses patented audio technology scientifically proven to enhance concentration, reduce stress, and improve sleep quality.

## How It Works

Brain.fm impacts the prefrontal cortex, the brain region responsible for thinking and creativity. The technology uses functional music designed to affect your brain and optimize your performance, unlike traditional music which is designed to please your ears.

## Features

- Science-backed audio technology proven more effective than binaural beats, Spotify, and YouTube focus playlists
- Multiple modes: Focus, Relax, and Sleep
- Adjustable session lengths and intensity levels
- Cross-platform availability (web, iOS, Android, desktop)
- Background playback support
- Personalized soundscapes based on user preferences

## Use Cases

- Deep work sessions requiring intense concentration
- Studying and learning new material
- Creative work and brainstorming
- Meditation and relaxation
- Improved sleep quality
