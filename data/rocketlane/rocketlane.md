## Overview

Rocketlane is a professional services automation platform designed for customer-facing teams, particularly those managing implementations, onboarding, and consulting engagements. It integrates time tracking with project management to provide a comprehensive solution for service delivery.

## Features

- **Integrated Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracking within project management workflows
- **Project Templates**: Pre-built templates for common consulting engagements
- **Client Collaboration**: Portals for client communication and collaboration
- **Resource Management**: Allocate and track consultant availability across projects
- **Billable Hours Tracking**: Capture and categorize time for accurate client billing
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Real-time insights into project health and time utilization
- **Budget Tracking**: Monitor time budgets against project estimates
- **Integration Capabilities**: Connect with CRM, invoicing, and other business tools

## Use Cases

- Customer onboarding and implementation projects
- Professional services consulting engagements
- Customer success initiatives requiring time tracking
- Implementation consulting for SaaS companies
- Managed services delivery

## Benefits

- Streamlines both project delivery and time tracking in one platform
- Improves visibility into project profitability
- Enhances client communication and transparency
- Reduces time spent on administrative tasks
- Provides accurate data for resource planning
