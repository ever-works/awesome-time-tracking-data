## Overview

Replicon CloudClock is a cloud-enabled kiosk application that transforms an ordinary iPad into a powerful touchless time clock using facial recognition and voice-assisted technology.

## Key Features

### Contactless Operation
- Touchless facial recognition enables employees to simply walk up to their time clock and automatically punch in
- No need to touch any clock surfaces
- Sophisticated combination of facial recognition and voice-assisted technology
- Employees can punch in and out six times faster than traditional electronic time clocks

### Anti-Fraud Measures
- Captures an image with each punch
- Runs a photo audit trail to deter time theft
- Eliminates "buddy punching" (when one employee clocks in for another)
- Deploys cutting-edge biometrics based on facial recognition technology

### Cost-Effective Implementation
- Download and install the CloudClock app on iPad
- No additional hardware cost of fingerprint readers
- Fast and frictionless automatic log in for employees
- Any iOS tablet device can be converted into a self-service time clock

### Self-Service Capabilities
Employees can:
- View their schedules
- Request time off
- View punch history
- Access real-time information via the CloudClock interface

## Technical Details

### Setup Process
1. Download the CloudClock app on iPad
2. Connect the clock to your Deltek | Replicon account
3. Initiate time capture
4. Employees register their faces in the system

### Platform Requirements
- iOS tablet device (iPad)
- Internet connection for cloud synchronization
- Deltek | Replicon account
