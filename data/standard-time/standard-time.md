## Overview

Standard Time is a comprehensive project time tracking and management solution designed for professional services, consultancies, and project-based organizations. It combines time tracking with project management, task tracking, and billing capabilities in a single integrated platform.

## Features

- **Project Time Tracking**: Track time against projects, tasks, and milestones with multiple timer options
- **Task Management**: Create, assign, and track tasks with dependencies and resource allocation
- **Expense Tracking**: Record project-related expenses and attach receipts
- **Billing & Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked time and expenses with customizable rates
- **Resource Management**: Allocate team members across projects and monitor capacity
- **Timesheet Management**: Flexible timesheet entry with approval workflows
- **Project Templates**: Reusable project structures for common engagement types
- **Custom Fields**: Add custom data fields to projects, tasks, and time entries
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync with Outlook and other calendar systems
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go time tracking

## Reporting Capabilities

- Project profitability analysis
- Resource utilization reports
- Time and expense summaries
- Budget vs. actual tracking
- Client billing reports
- Employee productivity metrics

## Benefits

- Improves project profitability through accurate tracking
- Streamlines billing and invoicing processes
- Provides visibility into resource allocation and utilization
- Supports multiple billing models (hourly, fixed-fee, retainer)
- Reduces administrative overhead

## Use Cases

- Professional services firms (consulting, accounting, legal)
- IT services and managed service providers
- Marketing and creative agencies
