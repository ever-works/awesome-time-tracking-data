## Overview

Zenefits is a comprehensive cloud-based HR platform that includes time and attendance tracking as part of an all-in-one solution for managing employees, benefits, payroll, and compliance.

## Time & Attendance Features

- Mobile time clock with GPS tracking
- Web-based punch clock
- Automatic overtime calculation
- Timesheet approval workflows
- PTO request and tracking
- Integration with payroll

## HR Platform Features

- Employee onboarding
- Benefits administration
- Payroll processing
- Compliance management
- Document management
- Performance reviews

## Target Users

- Small to mid-sized businesses (10-500 employees)
- Companies wanting integrated HR and time tracking
- Businesses needing compliance support

## Pricing

Tiered pricing based on features and number of employees.
