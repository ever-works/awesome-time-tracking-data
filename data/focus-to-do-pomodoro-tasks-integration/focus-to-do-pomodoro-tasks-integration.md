## Overview

Focus To-Do masterfully merges a task manager with a Pomodoro timer, letting you assign focus sessions directly to your tasks. This integration eliminates the need to switch between task planning and time tracking tools.

## Key Features

- **Task-Timer Integration**: Attach Pomodoro sessions directly to specific tasks
- **Planning Workflow**: Estimate how many Pomodoros each task will require
- **Execution Tracking**: Track actual Pomodoros completed per task
- **Progress Visualization**: See completion status for each task
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Access tasks and timers across all devices
- **Productivity Statistics**: Analyze Pomodoro completion patterns

## Why It Stands Out

Unlike standalone Pomodoro apps or separate task managers:

- No context switching between planning and doing
- Clear connection between time invested and task completion
- Accurate estimation of task duration based on Pomodoro history
- Integrated approach to time management and task organization

## Use Cases

- Students planning study sessions for different subjects
- Professionals managing multiple projects and clients
- Freelancers tracking time across various tasks
- Anyone wanting seamless integration between planning and execution

## Availability

Available on iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, and web platforms with full synchronization across devices.
