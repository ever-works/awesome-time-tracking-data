## Overview

Clio Duo is an AI-powered time tracking assistant integrated into Clio Manage, the leading cloud-based legal practice management software. It automatically identifies unlogged billable work—such as phone calls, emails, notes, and document work—and suggests time entries, helping attorneys capture more billable time without manual tracking overhead.

## AI-Powered Time Suggestions

### Automatic Detection
- Monitors emails sent and received
- Tracks phone calls made
- Detects notes and file activity
- Identifies document work
- Recognizes meeting attendance

### Intelligent Suggestions
- Suggests time entries for unlogged work
- Recommends matter association
- Proposes appropriate time amounts
- Learns from attorney behavior
- Improves suggestions over time

### One-Click Capture
- Review suggestions in dashboard
- Accept with single click
- Edit if needed before accepting
- Reject suggestions not billable
- Batch process multiple entries
