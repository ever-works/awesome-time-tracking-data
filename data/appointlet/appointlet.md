## Overview

Appointlet is a straightforward scheduling solution that works well for individual professionals and small teams who need basic appointment booking without complex features or steep learning curves.

## Features

- Meeting scheduling with automated booking
- Google Calendar integration
- Time zone detection
- Customizable booking pages
- Email notifications and reminders
- Meeting types and durations
- Availability rules
- Buffer times between meetings
- Team scheduling
- Embed options for websites
- Custom confirmation messages
- Integration with various third-party tools

## Key Strengths

- Clean, simple user interface
- Easy setup and onboarding
- Seamless Google Calendar integration
- Broader range of integrations compared to some competitors
- Affordable pricing for small teams
- Good balance of features without overwhelming complexity

## Ideal For

- Solo professionals (consultants, coaches, therapists)
- Small service businesses
- Sales teams
- Customer success teams
- Anyone needing simple appointment booking

## Comparison Notes

Compared to more complex platforms like SuperSaaS, Appointlet offers a cleaner and simpler user experience. It focuses on doing the essentials well rather than offering every possible feature.

## Pricing

Free plan available with basic features. Paid plans start at $8/month, offering additional customization, integrations, and team features.
