## Overview

Focus Booster is a time tracking and productivity app built around the Pomodoro Technique. It helps you maintain focus through timed work sessions while automatically tracking time for billing and reporting purposes.

## Features

- **Pomodoro Timer**: Built-in 25-minute focus sessions with breaks
- **Time Tracking**: Automatic time tracking during Pomodoro sessions
- **Customizable Sessions**: Adjust Pomodoro and break durations
- **Project Tracking**: Assign sessions to projects and clients
- **Session History**: Review all completed Pomodoro sessions
- **Reports**: Daily, weekly, and monthly productivity reports
- **Charts and Graphs**: Visual analytics of time spent
- **Export Data**: Export time data for invoicing
- **Reminders**: Notifications to keep you on track
- **Multi-Device**: Works on desktop and mobile
- **Offline Mode**: Track time without internet
- **Session Notes**: Add notes to individual sessions
