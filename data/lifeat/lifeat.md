## Overview

LifeAt offers focus music, task management, to-do lists, Pomodoro timer, virtual spaces, and co-working, all in one place. In the era of remote work, LifeAt serves as your virtual office.

## Features

- **400+ Immersive Backgrounds**: Realistic videos and sounds across nature, lofi, cafes, and other productive environments
- **Virtual Co-working**: Join thousands of users live every day for accountability and body-doubling
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Built-in focus timer for structured work sessions
- **Todo List**: Integrated task management
- **Spotify Integration**: Connect and play Spotify playlists while working
- **Daily Fortune**: Motivational element to start your day
- **Productivity Streak Tracking**: Monitor your consistency over time
- **Customizable Ambient Sounds**: Rain, wind, cafe, office, and more

## Community & Collaboration

LifeAt is where users come together live every day to chat, interact, and stay productive together. The platform enables virtual co-working through immersive backgrounds combined with productivity tools.

Google Calendar integration for seamless schedule management.
