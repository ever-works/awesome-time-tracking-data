## Overview

Zoho Invoice is a time tracking and timesheet billing software designed for freelancers and small businesses. It automatically adds all logged hours and billable expenses to invoices with just a click.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track time spent on projects and tasks with built-in timers
- **Automatic Invoicing**: Convert tracked time and expenses into professional invoices automatically
- **Project Management**: Organize work by projects with task lists and milestones
- **Expense Tracking**: Log project expenses and add them to client invoices
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Bill clients in multiple currencies with automatic conversion
- **Payment Gateways**: Accept online payments through integrated payment processors
- **Client Portal**: Allow clients to view invoices, make payments, and track project status
- **Recurring Invoices**: Set up automatic recurring billing for retainer clients
- **Mobile Apps**: Track time and send invoices from iOS and Android devices

## Pricing

Zoho Invoice offers a free plan for businesses with annual revenue under $50,000. Paid plans start at $9/month for premium features, with additional tiers for larger businesses requiring advanced functionality.
