## Overview

The official Eisenhower Matrix app (updated January 12, 2026) brings proven time management methodology directly to Android, transforming overwhelming to-do lists into clear, actionable priorities.

## Features

- **Four Quadrants**: Organize tasks by urgent/important, important/not urgent, urgent/not important, not urgent/not important
- **Task Management**: Add, edit, and organize tasks within quadrants
- **Priority Visualization**: See at a glance what needs immediate attention
- **Quick Sorting**: Easily move tasks between quadrants
- **Multiple Projects**: Manage up to 5 projects on free plan
- **Task Limits**: 100 tasks on free tier
- **Clean Interface**: Minimalist design focused on clarity
- **Cross-Device Sync**: Keep tasks synced across devices

## Methodology

Based on President Eisenhower's decision-making framework: "What is important is seldom urgent and what is urgent is seldom important."

## Pricing

Free for 5 projects and 100 tasks, no credit card required.

## Platform

Android with web version available.
