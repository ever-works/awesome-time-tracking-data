## Overview

TagTrack is an iOS app that brings physical interaction to digital time and habit tracking. By placing NFC tags or QR codes around your environment, you can instantly start tracking activities with a simple tap or scan, reducing friction and making time tracking more automatic and mindful.

## Key Features

- NFC tag support for iPhone devices with NFC capability
- QR code scanning for universal compatibility
- Instant timer activation via tag tap or code scan
- Habit tracking with physical triggers
- Custom tag assignments for different activities
- Historical tracking and analytics
- Widgets for quick access to recent activities
- No login required - privacy-focused design
- Works offline with local data storage

## Use Cases

- **Workspace transitions** - NFC tags on desk areas to track deep work sessions
- **Exercise routines** - Tags on gym equipment to log workout time
- **Home activities** - QR codes in rooms to track cleaning, cooking, or hobbies
- **Reading habits** - Tag on bookshelf to log reading sessions
- **Practice sessions** - Tags on musical instruments for practice time
- **Task batching** - Different tags for different task categories

## Benefits of Physical Tracking

- Eliminates need to unlock phone and navigate to app
- Creates environmental cues that prompt behavior
- Reduces cognitive load compared to manual entry
- Makes tracking feel more tangible and intentional
- Encourages consistent tracking through ease of use
- Leverages implementation intentions (if-then planning)

## Hardware Requirements

- iPhone with iOS compatibility
- NFC capability for NFC tag support (iPhone 7 and later)
- NFC tags (NTAG213, NTAG215, or NTAG216 recommended)
- Printed QR codes for non-NFC tracking points

## Implementation Tips

- Place tags in locations where activities naturally begin
- Use waterproof NFC stickers for outdoor or wet areas
- Label tags with activity names for visual confirmation
- Start with 3-5 key activities before expanding
- Position tags within easy reach of natural workflow
- Combine with visual reminders or color coding

## Privacy and Data

TagTrack emphasizes privacy with local data storage on device. No account creation or cloud sync required, though users can enable optional backup features.
