## Overview

Billables AI is an automated time-tracking platform specifically designed for legal professionals, using artificial intelligence to help firms recover billable hours that would otherwise be lost to manual tracking errors.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Time Capture**: Automatically reviews emails, calendar events, and documents to identify billable activities
- **Intelligent Categorization**: Uses machine learning to assign time entries to the correct client and matter
- **Narrative Generation**: Automatically generates time entry descriptions based on activity context
- **Integration**: Works with common legal practice management systems and email platforms
- **Compliance**: Ensures entries meet client billing guidelines and ABA requirements
- **Recovery Analytics**: Provides insights into time leakage and recovery rates

## Benefits

The ABA estimates that legal professionals lose up to 10% of billable hours to time tracking errors. Billables AI helps firms recover this lost revenue through automated, accurate time capture.
