## Overview

John Lee Dumas is the founder and host of Entrepreneurs On Fire, an award-winning daily podcast that has featured interviews with over 2,000 inspiring entrepreneurs.

## Entrepreneurs On Fire

- Award-winning daily podcast format
- Over 2,000 guest interviews with successful entrepreneurs
- Focus on extracting patterns and habits of highly productive individuals
- Known for his signature seven-question interview format

## Productivity Philosophy

Dumas is known for his disciplined approach to entrepreneurship and productivity, emphasizing consistency, daily habits, and the importance of creating systems for sustainable business growth.
