## Overview

Align is the ultimate time blocking planner and visual schedule maker designed to help users plan their day, organize their life, and boost productivity through beautiful visual time blocks.

## Key Features

### Free Features
- **Unlimited Time Blocks**: Create unlimited time blocks to visualize your entire day and turn your daily task list into beautiful blocks
- **Calendar Sync**: Seamlessly integrate existing calendars for a unified view
- **Personalized Task System**: Customize tasks with unique icons and colors
- **Smart Reminders**: Intelligent notifications to keep you on track

### Advanced Capabilities
- **Resizable Time Blocks**: Flexible block sizing with intelligent overlap avoidance that automatically adjusts your schedule to prevent conflicts
- **Notes Integration**: Add detailed notes to any time block for context and information
- **Recurring Time Blocks**: Build routines without re-creating tasks by setting up recurring blocks
- **iCloud Sync**: Synced via iCloud across iPhone (iPad & Mac coming soon)

## Visual Design

Align transforms your task list into visually appealing time blocks with:
- Color-coded categories for easy visual differentiation
- Custom icons for task types
- Clean, intuitive interface that makes planning enjoyable
- Drag-and-drop functionality for easy rescheduling

## Platform Availability

- **Current**: iOS 17.0 or later (iPhone)
- **Coming Soon**: iPadOS, watchOS, and macOS
- **Distribution**: Available on the App Store

## Smart Scheduling

Align's intelligent scheduling system prevents time block overlaps by automatically adjusting your schedule when conflicts arise, ensuring your day remains organized and realistic.

## Routine Building

With recurring time blocks, users can establish consistent daily or weekly routines without the tedium of manually creating the same blocks repeatedly. This feature is particularly helpful for:
- Morning and evening routines
- Regular work blocks
- Exercise and wellness activities
- Recurring meetings or commitments

## Pricing

Align offers free core features with premium capabilities available through in-app purchase or subscription.
