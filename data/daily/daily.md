## Overview

Daily is a time tracking app for Mac that makes time tracking effortless by periodically asking what you're doing, eliminating the need to manually toggle timers. It's specifically designed for individual use with a focus on simplicity.

## Features

### Effortless Tracking
- Periodically prompts you to log what you're working on
- No manual timer starting or stopping required
- Quick, frictionless time entry
- Minimal interruption to your workflow

### Mac-Specific Design
- Native macOS application
- Designed specifically for Mac users
- Clean, minimal interface
- Integrates seamlessly with macOS

### Individual Focus
- Tailored for personal productivity
- Simple project and task organization
- Easy-to-read time reports
- No unnecessary team features

## How It Works

Instead of requiring you to remember to start and stop timers, Daily periodically checks in with you throughout the day, asking what you're currently working on. This approach combines the accuracy of active time tracking with the convenience of periodic logging.

## Benefits

- **No Timer Management**: Forget about starting and stopping timers
- **Reduced Mental Load**: The app reminds you to log time, not the other way around
- **Accurate Records**: Regular check-ins ensure comprehensive time tracking
- **Mac-Native Experience**: Built specifically for macOS users

## Platform

- macOS only
- Native Mac application

## Ideal For

Freelancers, consultants, and individual professionals who want accurate time tracking without the burden of constant timer management.
