## Overview

AutoTrack by TrackingTime is an AI-powered time tracking system designed for efficient teams. The platform provides AI-powered suggestions to help log hours privately—no timers, no monitoring—and helps build accurate, up-to-date timesheets without relying on memory or manual input.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Suggestions**: Automatically suggests time entries based on detected work patterns and application usage
- **Privacy-First Approach**: No screenshots, no surveillance, no micromanagement—all data stays private until users choose to log it
- **Pattern Learning**: Learns from your work habits to provide increasingly accurate time entry suggestions
- **Seamless Integration**: Works in the background without interrupting workflow
- **Memory-Free Tracking**: Eliminates the need to remember what you worked on throughout the day
- **Team Productivity Insights**: Provides real-time insights into team productivity without invasive monitoring

## Use Cases

Ideal for consultancies, agencies, and professional services firms that need accurate billable hour tracking while maintaining employee privacy and autonomy.
