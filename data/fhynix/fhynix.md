## Overview

Fhynix is a 2026 agenda planner and productivity app that helps users organize tasks, events, routines, reminders, and goals—all in one clean timeline. The app stands out for its AI-powered natural language input and unique WhatsApp integration.

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Natural Language Task Entry
Fhynix uses AI to turn everyday sentences into structured calendar entries. Users can type in plain English such as "Gym every alternate day at 7 am" or "Schedule dentist appointment next Friday at 11 am," and Fhynix automatically understands the context, deadlines, and priorities without complicated setup.

The AI automatically:
- Scans and places entries on your timeline
- Understands recurring patterns
- Recognizes time specifications
- Determines priorities

### WhatsApp Integration
Fhynix is currently the only major productivity app with native WhatsApp integration. Key capabilities include:
- Sending reminders for calendar events directly to WhatsApp
- Instant event sharing through WhatsApp
- Never missing important deadlines or study sessions
- Convenient notification delivery through a familiar platform

### Smart AI Scheduling
Fhynix uses AI to automatically plan your day by:
- Placing tasks and events into your calendar timeline
- Considering task urgency
- Evaluating available time slots
- Respecting user-defined priorities
- Creating realistic daily schedules

### Calendar Integration
Users can import events from multiple calendar sources with a single tap:
- Google Calendar
- iCloud Calendar  
- Outlook Calendar
- Seamless synchronization across platforms

### Unified Timeline View
All productivity elements are organized in one clean, visual timeline:
- Tasks and to-dos
- Calendar events
- Recurring routines
- Reminders
- Long-term goals
