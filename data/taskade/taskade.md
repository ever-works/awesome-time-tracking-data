## Overview

Taskade is an AI-powered productivity workspace that combines task management, project planning, and time tracking in one unified platform. It's known for its Flowtime Technique implementation and AI-powered features that help teams and individuals work more efficiently.

## Flowtime Technique Integration

Taskade provides comprehensive guidance on the Flowtime Technique (also called Flowmodoro), a flexible time management method designed to maximize periods of deep, uninterrupted focus. Unlike rigid Pomodoro intervals, Flowtime encourages working on a single task until concentration naturally begins to wane.

### Flowtime Features
- Track flow state duration
- Flexible work/break ratios
- Focus session analytics
- Productivity pattern insights
- Deep work optimization
