## Overview

TimeCalculatorTool.com is a free online utility created to help people calculate time accurately and efficiently. The tool allows users to calculate total hours worked, track clock in and clock out times, and account for breaks or overtime across any time period.

## Features

- Multiple time format support (12-hour, 24-hour, decimal)
- Break time deduction
- Overtime calculation
- Weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly timesheet views
- Print and export capabilities
- Mobile-friendly interface
- No registration required

## Calculation Types

- Total hours worked per day and week
- Break time deductions
- Overtime hours (daily and weekly)
- Gross pay calculations with hourly rates
- Time duration between two timestamps

## Use Cases

- Employee timesheet management
- Freelancer hour tracking
- Payroll preparation
- Project time estimation
- Billing and invoicing support

## Pricing

Completely free to use with no limitations.
