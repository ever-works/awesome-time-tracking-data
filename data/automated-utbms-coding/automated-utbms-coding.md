## Overview

Automated UTBMS (Uniform Task-Based Management System) coding uses AI to apply standardized billing codes to legal time entries automatically.

## The Problem

Manual UTBMS coding:
- Time-consuming (2-5 minutes per entry)
- Error-prone
- Inconsistent
- Creates billing disputes
- Slows down invoicing

## Automated Solution

**AI Analysis**
Analyzes time entry narratives and activities.

**Code Assignment**
Automatically applies appropriate UTBMS codes.

**Compliance Check**
Verifies codes match client billing guidelines.

**Speed**
6x faster than manual coding.

## Benefits

**Time Savings**
Review and approve pre-bills over 6x faster.

**Consistency**
Standardized coding across all entries.

**Compliance**
Automatic adherence to client guidelines.

**Reduced Disputes**
Accurate codes prevent billing disputes.

**Better Realization**
Compliant billing improves collection rates.
