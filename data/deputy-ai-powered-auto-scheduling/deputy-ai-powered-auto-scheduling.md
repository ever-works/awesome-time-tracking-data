## AI-Powered Scheduling

Deputy's auto-scheduling uses artificial intelligence to create optimal staff schedules that balance business needs with labor costs, transforming what used to take hours into a process that takes minutes.

## How It Works

### Labor Demand Forecasting
Deputy's AI analyzes:
- Historical sales data
- Foot traffic patterns
- Seasonal trends
- Day of week variations
- Special events
- Weather impact (for relevant businesses)

### Optimal Schedule Generation
Based on forecasts, the AI:
1. Determines required staffing levels by hour
2. Matches employee skills to positions
3. Respects employee availability and preferences
4. Complies with labor laws and break requirements
5. Minimizes overtime and unnecessary labor costs
6. Balances fairness in shift distribution

### Schedule Output
- Draft schedule ready for review
- Highlights potential issues
- Suggests adjustments for optimization
- One-click publish to team
