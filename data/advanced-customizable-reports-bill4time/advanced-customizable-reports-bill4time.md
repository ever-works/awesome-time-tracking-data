## Overview

Advanced Customizable Reports represent a significant 2026 enhancement to Bill4Time's legal billing software, providing Legal Pro and Enterprise users with unprecedented control over how firm data is viewed, filtered, and analyzed for strategic decision-making.

## Key Features

### Unified Data View
Bring together multiple data streams:
- Productivity metrics (billable hours, utilization rates)
- Profitability analysis (margins, realization rates)
- Cash flow tracking (collections, aging receivables)
- Performance indicators (by attorney, practice area, client)

### Real-Time Analysis
- Live data updates as work is tracked and billed
- No lag between activity and reporting
- Current performance visibility for immediate adjustments
- Trend analysis showing performance trajectories

### Advanced Filtering
Customize reports with granular control:
- Filter by attorney, practice group, or office
- Date range selection with custom periods
- Client and matter type segmentation
- Billing rate and fee arrangement filters
- Multi-dimensional cross-filtering
