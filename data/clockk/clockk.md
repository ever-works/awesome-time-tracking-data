## Overview

Clockk is an innovative AI time tracking solution built for busy professionals who juggle multiple clients and projects. Unlike traditional time trackers that require starting and stopping timers, Clockk automatically tracks all work activity in the background and uses artificial intelligence to organize time entries intelligently.

## Features

- **Automatic Activity Tracking**: Monitors all applications, websites, and documents you work on throughout the day
- **AI-Powered Categorization**: Machine learning automatically assigns activities to the correct clients and projects
- **No Manual Timers**: Never worry about forgetting to start or stop a timer
- **Multi-Client Management**: Seamlessly track time across unlimited clients and projects
- **Intelligent Learning**: AI improves accuracy over time by learning your work patterns
- **Privacy Focused**: No screenshots or invasive monitoring
- **Flexible Editing**: Review and adjust AI suggestions before finalizing timesheets
- **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports for client billing and productivity analysis
- **Integrations**: Connects with popular productivity and accounting tools

## How Clockk Works

1. **Track**: Clockk runs in the background, recording which applications, websites, and files you use
2. **Categorize**: AI analyzes your activity and automatically assigns it to the appropriate client and project
3. **Review**: At day's end, review the AI-suggested time entries in an intuitive timeline view
4. **Approve**: Accept, adjust, or reject suggestions before creating final timesheets
5. **Bill**: Export time data to invoices or accounting systems
