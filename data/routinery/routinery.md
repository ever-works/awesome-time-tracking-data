## Overview

Routinery is a timer-based routine planner specifically designed with a time-blocked approach where you can have separate morning, midday, and evening routines, each with its own sequence of timed steps. The app guides you through routine sequences using push notifications and optional voice alerts (text-to-speech).

## Features

- **Habit Chains**: Step-by-step sequences like "Make bed (2 min)" → "Brush teeth (3 min)" → "Meditate (10 min)"
- **Multiple Routines**: Separate sequences for morning, midday, and evening
- **Voice Alerts**: Optional text-to-speech announcements for each step
- **Push Notifications**: Gentle reminders to move to the next task
- **Timed Steps**: Each routine step has a specific duration
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor routine completion and streaks
- **Customizable Timers**: Adjust duration for each activity
- **Visual Progress**: See how far through your routine you are
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Start routines manually or on schedule
- **ADHD-Friendly**: Structured prompts prevent getting stuck on single tasks
- **Routine Templates**: Pre-made routines to get started quickly
- **Statistics**: Track consistency and total time spent on routines

## How It Works

1. **Build Routine**: Create a sequence of timed activities
2. **Set Durations**: Assign time limits to each step
3. **Start Sequence**: Begin your routine when ready
4. **Follow Prompts**: Voice or notification guides you through each step
5. **Stay on Track**: Timer prevents lingering too long on any task
6. **Complete Routine**: Finish all steps in the sequence
