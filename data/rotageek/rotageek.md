## Overview

Rotageek is an intelligent workforce management platform that uses artificial intelligence to create optimized employee schedules while tracking time and attendance, designed for retail, hospitality, and healthcare industries.

## Features

- **AI Scheduling**: Machine learning algorithms create optimal schedules automatically
- **Demand Forecasting**: Predicts staffing needs based on historical data and trends
- **Employee Preferences**: Incorporates employee availability and preferences into schedules
- **Time & Attendance**: Integrated time clock with mobile punch in/out
- **Shift Swapping**: Employees can swap shifts within compliance rules
- **Labor Cost Control**: Real-time tracking of labor costs against budget
- **Compliance Management**: Ensures schedules comply with labor laws and contracts
- **Mobile App**: iOS and Android apps for employees to view schedules and clock in
- **Analytics**: Deep insights into scheduling efficiency and labor productivity
- **Integration**: Connects with major payroll and HR systems
