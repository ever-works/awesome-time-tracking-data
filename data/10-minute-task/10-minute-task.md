## Overview

The 10 Minute Task technique is a task management rule designed to eliminate procrastination by making every task feel approachable.

## The Rule

Every single task on your to-do list should take no longer than 10 minutes to complete. If a task would take longer, you must break it down into smaller steps.

## How It Works

- Review each item on your to-do list
- If a task will take more than 10 minutes, break it into smaller sub-tasks
- Each sub-task should be completable within 10 minutes
- This forces task decomposition and makes progress feel more achievable

## Benefits

- Reduces task paralysis by making items feel manageable
- Encourages systematic breakdown of complex projects
- Creates a sense of momentum through frequent completions
