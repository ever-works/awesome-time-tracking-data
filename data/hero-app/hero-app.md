## Overview

Hero is an AI assistant app that maximizes productivity by handling calendar, tasks, notes, routines, and partner sharing all in one place, significantly reducing fatigue from switching between apps.

## Core Features

### All-in-One Platform

- **Calendar Management**: Integrated calendar for scheduling and time blocking
- **Task Management**: To-do lists and task tracking
- **Note Taking**: Capture ideas and information
- **Routine Building**: Create and track daily routines
- **Partner Sharing**: Coordinate schedules and tasks with partners

### AI Assistance

The AI assistant helps users:

- Organize their day efficiently
- Prioritize tasks automatically
- Suggest optimal scheduling
- Reduce decision fatigue
- Streamline workflows

## Key Benefits

### Reduced App Switching

By consolidating multiple productivity tools into one:

- Eliminates mental overhead of switching apps
- Reduces context switching
- Improves focus and flow
- Saves time on app navigation
- Provides unified view of all commitments

### Productivity Gains

- Faster daily planning (10-20 minutes)
- Better visibility of tasks and time
- Improved coordination with partners
- Streamlined routine management
- Centralized information hub

## Use Cases

- Individual productivity management
- Couple coordination and shared planning
- Daily routine tracking
- Work-life balance management
- Time blocking and scheduling
