## Overview

Hour Timesheet is a cloud-based time tracking solution specifically designed for small to midsize businesses that need to maintain Defense Contract Audit Agency (DCAA) compliance while efficiently managing employee time, attendance, and payroll processes. The platform provides comprehensive timekeeping capabilities with built-in regulatory compliance features.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking & Attendance

- **Employee Time Tracking**: Track billable hours, worker locations, and employee time at work or remote locations
- **Daily Timesheet Reminders**: Automated reminders ensure timely timesheet submission
- **Job Cost Tracking**: Monitor costs associated with specific jobs and projects
- **Overtime Management**: Automatic overtime calculation and tracking
- **Leave Management**: Integrated employee leave and absence tracking

### DCAA Compliance

- **Government Contractor Focus**: Specifically designed for businesses working with government contracts
- **Audit-Ready Records**: Maintains detailed time records that meet DCAA audit requirements
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Built-in features ensure compliance with federal timekeeping regulations
- **Manager Approvals**: Approval workflows for timesheet verification and authorization

### Integration Capabilities

- **QuickBooks Integration**: One-click, bi-directional sync with both QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Desktop
- **Payroll Export**: Export data to ADP and Paychex payroll systems
- **Full-Service Payroll**: Built-in payroll processing functionality

### Mobile Access

- **Multi-Device Support**: Access from Android phones, tablets, iPads, and iPhones
- **On-the-Go Tracking**: Track time from any location using mobile devices
- **Real-Time Updates**: Instant synchronization across all devices

### Workforce Management

- **Employee Scheduling**: Create and manage employee work schedules
- **1099 Contractor Support**: Track time for both W-2 employees and 1099 contractors
- **Remote Work Tracking**: Monitor time for on-site and remote employees
- **Location Tracking**: GPS-based verification of employee work locations

## Pricing & Flexibility

- **No Hidden Fees**: Transparent, straightforward pricing
- **No Long-Term Contracts**: Month-to-month service with no commitment
- **Cancel Anytime**: Flexible cancellation policy
- **Free Trial**: 30-day free trial to test all features before committing
