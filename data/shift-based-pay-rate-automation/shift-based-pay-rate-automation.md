## Overview

Shift-Based Pay Rate Automation represents a sophisticated payroll calculation feature in modern workforce management systems that automatically computes employee compensation based on complex shift-specific rules, including differential pay rates, overtime multipliers, holiday bonuses, and specialized shift premiums.

## Core Functionality

### Automatic Salary Calculation
- Links hourly pay rates directly to shift definitions
- Calculates total compensation from tracked work hours
- Applies differential rates automatically
- Generates payroll-ready summaries

### Multiple Rate Types
- **Base hourly rate**: Standard shift compensation
- **Overtime rates**: Time-and-a-half, double-time
- **Holiday rates**: Bank holiday multipliers
- **Shift differentials**: Night shift, weekend premiums
- **Skill-based rates**: Specialized role compensation
- **Location differentials**: Geographic pay variations

## Advanced Rate Rules

### Conditional Multipliers
- After X hours per day → overtime rate
- After Y hours per week → higher overtime tier
- Specific days → weekend/holiday rates
- Time ranges → night shift differentials
- Multiple conditions → stacked multipliers

### Customizable Rate Structures
Supports complex scenarios:
- Progressive overtime (different rates at different thresholds)
- Compounding multipliers (holiday + night shift)
- Role-specific premium pay
- Tenure-based rate increases
- Performance-based bonuses
