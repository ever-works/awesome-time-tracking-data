## Overview

WebWork stands out for balancing AI-powered analytics with affordability at $3.99/user/month, making it an accessible option for companies focused on scalable remote team time tracking.

## Features

- **Automatic Time Tracking**: AI-powered automatic tracking of work activities
- **Screenshots & Activity Monitoring**: Optional screenshot capture and activity level tracking
- **Productivity Analytics**: AI-generated insights into team productivity patterns
- **Project & Task Tracking**: Organize work by projects and tasks with time allocation
- **Attendance Management**: Track employee attendance and working hours
- **Detailed Reports**: Comprehensive reporting on time usage, productivity, and activity levels
- **Integrations**: Connect with popular project management and communication tools
- **Mobile Apps**: Track time on iOS and Android devices

## Pricing

WebWork offers competitive pricing at $3.99 per user per month, making it one of the most affordable AI-powered time tracking solutions for remote teams. A free trial is available to test the platform.
