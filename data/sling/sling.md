## Overview

Sling is a free employee scheduling platform that combines shift planning, time tracking, team communication, and task management for shift-based businesses.

## Core Features (Free)

**Scheduling**:
- Drag-and-drop shift scheduling
- Shift templates
- Availability management
- Schedule notifications

**Time Clock**:
- Mobile time clock app
- Geofencing
- Overtime alerts
- Timesheet export

**Communication**:
- Group and direct messaging
- Announcements
- News feed

**Task Management**:
- Daily task lists
- Task assignment
- Completion tracking

## Premium Features

- Advanced reporting
- Labor cost optimization
- Shift trading
- Time-off management
- Payroll integration

## Target Industries

- Retail
- Restaurants
- Healthcare
- Hospitality
- Any shift-based business

## Pricing

- Free plan available
- Premium plans starting at $2/user/month
