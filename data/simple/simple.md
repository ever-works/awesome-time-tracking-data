## Overview

Simple is a streamlined time tracking and task management app that lets you start timers with one click and organize your to-dos in a clean, structured list. It provides basic reporting so you can see exactly how much time you've spent on specific tasks, projects, or clients on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis.

## Features

- One-click start timers
- Structured task list for to-do organization
- Manual time entry for forgotten timer starts
- Group tasks by projects or clients
- View and edit completed tasks
- Basic reporting (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Real-time sync across web, mobile, and tablet
- Time tracking per task, project, or client

## Pros

- Simple interface with a free plan available
- Clear and uncluttered design
- No bells, whistles, or integration complexity

## Cons

- No additional features beyond basic time tracking
- No integrations with other apps or tools
- Not suitable if you need connections to project management suites, accounting software, or invoicing tools
