## Overview

Fiverr Workspace is a comprehensive platform owned by Fiverr that provides freelancers with a variety of business management tools including time tracking, invoicing, proposals, and client communication.

## Features

- Time tracking with activity, client, and project grouping
- Real-time timesheet sharing with clients
- Invoice generation and management
- Professional proposal creation
- Contract templates
- Client relationship management (CRM)
- Task and project management
- Financial tracking and reporting
- Expense tracking
- Payment processing
- Mobile app for iOS and Android
- Chrome extension for browser-based tracking

## Time Tracking Capabilities

You can take complete control over your time tracking and group them by activity, client, project, or not at all. Timesheets can be shared with clients to show work completed and updated in real-time, providing transparency in freelance relationships.

## Browser Extension

Fiverr Workspace Pro Chrome extension is described as the ultimate productivity tool for freelancers working on Fiverr, Upwork, and other platforms, allowing seamless time tracking across different freelance marketplaces.

## Ideal For

- Independent freelancers
- Solopreneurs
- Service providers on Fiverr and other platforms
- Consultants needing all-in-one solution
- Freelancers wanting client transparency

## Pricing

Free plan available for freelancers with only 1 client. The all-in-one plan costs $18/month for freelancers managing multiple clients and needing full feature access.
