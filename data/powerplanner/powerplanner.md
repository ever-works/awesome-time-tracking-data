## Overview

PowerPlanner is a comprehensive student planner application that combines class scheduling, assignment tracking, grade calculation, and time tracking to help students manage their academic workload effectively.

## Features

- **Class Schedule**: Manage rotating schedules with support for A/B day schedules and custom time slots
- **Assignment Tracking**: Track homework, projects, and exams with due dates and priorities
- **Grade Calculator**: Automatically calculate current grades based on completed assignments and weights
- **Time Tracking**: Log study time for each class and assignment to analyze time management
- **Reminders**: Customizable notifications for upcoming assignments and exams
- **Calendar View**: See all assignments and exams in calendar format with color coding by class
- **GPA Tracking**: Track cumulative GPA across semesters and years
- **Offline Support**: Works offline with automatic sync when connected
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on Windows, iOS, Android, and web
- **Cloud Sync**: Synchronize data across all devices

## Pricing

One-time payment of $1.99 for premium features. Free version available with basic scheduling and assignment tracking. Premium adds grade calculation, time tracking, and advanced customization.
