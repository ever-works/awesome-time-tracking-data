## Overview

BeforeSunset AI is a web-based application designed to help teams and busy professionals with daily planning and time management. It's a daily planner that helps users manage time, tasks, and schedules collaboratively using AI to optimize productivity.

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Planning
- Users can talk or type about their day, and AI turns it into actionable tasks effortlessly
- BeforeSunset AI creates the perfect schedule by syncing your calendar and to-do list without manual organization
- Hit "Plan My Day" and let AI create the perfect schedule with tasks arranged by priority and time efficiency

### Task Management
- The AI assistant creates subtasks and turns to-dos into actionable tasks
- Automatic task prioritization based on importance and deadlines
- Real-time schedule optimization

### Focus & Productivity Tools
- AI generates an ideal workspace to help users stay focused and motivated
- Pomodoro timer integration
- Note-taking capabilities
- Customizable ambient sounds for a distraction-free environment

### Integrations
- Seamless Google Calendar integration
- Multi-platform synchronization (mobile, tablet, desktop)

## Platform Availability

Tasks and plans stay in sync across:
- Web application
- Desktop app (Mac, Windows)
- Mobile apps (iOS and Android via App Store and Google Play)
