## Overview

MyCase is an all-in-one legal practice management software designed for small to mid-sized law firms. It combines time tracking, billing, case management, client communication, and document management in one cloud-based platform.

## Features

- **Smart Time Finder**: AI tool that captures an additional 579,665 hours in 2023 for customers
- **Time Tracking**: Multiple time entry methods with activity-based suggestions
- **Billing & Invoicing**: Flexible billing with trust accounting
- **Case Management**: Organize cases, contacts, and documents
- **Client Portal**: Secure client communication and document sharing
- **Document Management**: Store and organize all case documents
- **Calendar**: Deadline tracking and court date management
- **Text Messaging**: Communicate with clients via text
- **E-Signature**: Send documents for electronic signature
- **Mobile App**: Full-featured iOS and Android apps

## Smart Time Finder

Generates a list of common billable activities to help recover missed time. This feature alone helped customers capture significant additional billable hours.
