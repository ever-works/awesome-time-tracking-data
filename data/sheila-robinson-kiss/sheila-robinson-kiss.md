## Overview

Sheila Robinson-Kiss is a mental health educator, therapist, author, and speaker who integrates mental wellness with productivity and life balance.

## The Rebalancing America and Beyond Initiative

A national mental health and wellness platform dedicated to educating individuals about self-health and resiliency models.

## Key Focus Areas

- Mental health and wellness in the workplace
- Work-life integration and balance
- Self-health strategies
- Resiliency models for sustained functioning
- Therapeutic approaches to productivity challenges
