## Overview

Lindy AI is an artificial intelligence-powered scheduling assistant that automates calendar management and meeting coordination. It acts as a virtual assistant to handle scheduling tasks, optimize time allocation, and manage availability across multiple calendars.

## Features

- AI-powered meeting scheduling and coordination
- Natural language processing for scheduling requests
- Automatic calendar optimization
- Multi-calendar synchronization
- Intelligent conflict detection and resolution
- Meeting preferences learning and application
- Buffer time management between meetings
- Time zone coordination for global teams
- Email integration for scheduling communications
- Customizable scheduling rules and preferences

## AI Capabilities

- Learns from scheduling patterns and preferences
- Suggests optimal meeting times based on productivity patterns
- Automatically handles back-and-forth scheduling emails
- Adapts to changing priorities and urgency levels
- Predicts best meeting durations based on topic and attendees
- Identifies and protects focus time blocks

## Use Cases

- Busy professionals managing multiple meeting requests
- Teams coordinating across different time zones
- Sales teams scheduling client meetings
- Consultants managing client availability
- Executives requiring sophisticated calendar management

## Integration

- Google Calendar
- Microsoft Outlook

- Email clients (Gmail, Outlook)

## Benefits

- Eliminates back-and-forth scheduling emails
- Reduces time spent on calendar management
- Prevents scheduling conflicts automatically
- Optimizes calendar for productivity
