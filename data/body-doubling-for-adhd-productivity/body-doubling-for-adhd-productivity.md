## What is Body Doubling

Working in the presence of another person (virtual or in-person) to help initiate and sustain focus on tasks.

## Why It Works for ADHD

**External Accountability**
Another person's presence creates implicit accountability.

**Social Pressure**
Mild social pressure to stay on task.

**Mirror Neurons**
Seeing others work triggers work behavior.

**Reduced Overwhelm**
Shared space reduces isolation anxiety.

## Implementation Methods

**In-Person**
- Coffee shops
- Libraries
- Shared offices
- Friend working nearby

**Virtual**
- Focusmate (scheduled sessions)
- Virtual co-working rooms
- Video calls while working
- Discord study servers

## Benefits

- Easier task initiation
- Sustained focus
- Reduced procrastination
- Social connection
- Accountability

## Platforms

- Focusmate (scheduled 1:1 sessions)
- Flow Club (group sessions)
- Study Together (YouTube live streams)
- Discord study servers

## Best Practices

- Match work styles
- Minimize conversation
- Use video when virtual
- Schedule regular sessions
- Choose compatible partners
