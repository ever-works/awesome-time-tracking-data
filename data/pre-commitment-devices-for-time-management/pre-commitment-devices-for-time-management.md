## Overview

Pre-Commitment Devices lock in decisions before temptation arises, removing choice at crucial moments.

## Types

### Social Commitments
- Accountability partners
- Public declarations
- Group activities

### Technical Commitments
- Website blockers
- App limits
- Automatic scheduling

### Financial Commitments
- Paid classes/coaching
- Deposits
- Penalty systems

## Examples

- Schedule deep work blocks (calendar commitment)
- Use Freedom app 9-11am daily (technical)
- Tell team your focus hours (social)

Free strategy, though some tools have costs.
