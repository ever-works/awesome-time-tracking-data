## Overview

Timer Tomato is a flexible and easy-to-use online Pomodoro technique timer designed to help boost productivity through structured time management. It offers a straightforward approach to implementing the Pomodoro Technique without unnecessary complexity.

## Key Features

- Simple, clean interface
- Flexible timer configuration
- Easy-to-use controls
- Browser-based accessibility
- No signup required
- Free to use
- Mobile-responsive

## Pomodoro Technique Implementation

### Standard Intervals
- 25-minute focus sessions
- 5-minute short breaks
- 15-minute long breaks after 4 pomodoros
- Customizable durations

## Benefits

- Boosts productivity through structured intervals
- Prevents burnout with regular breaks
- Improves focus and concentration
- Simple and distraction-free
- Flexible to your workflow
- Accessible from anywhere

## How It Works

1. Visit timertomato.com
2. Set your preferred work interval
3. Start the timer
4. Focus on your task
5. Take a break when the timer ends
6. Repeat the cycle
