## Overview

Flowlu is an all-in-one business management platform that brings together project management, CRM, invoicing, time tracking, and knowledge management, providing a comprehensive solution for growing businesses.

## Features

- **Project Management**: Tasks, milestones, Gantt charts, and Kanban boards
- **Time Tracking**: Timers and timesheets with billable hour tracking
- **CRM**: Lead and customer relationship management with sales pipeline
- **Invoicing**: Create and send invoices with online payment acceptance
- **Financial Management**: Track income, expenses, and profitability
- **Knowledge Base**: Internal wiki and documentation for team knowledge
- **Collaboration**: Team chat, file sharing, and comments
- **Resource Planning**: Team workload and capacity management
- **Custom Workflows**: Automate business processes with custom rules
- **Reporting**: Dashboards and reports on projects, finances, and sales
- **Client Portal**: Branded portal for client collaboration
- **Mobile Apps**: Manage work from iOS and Android devices
- **Integrations**: Connect with email, calendars, and third-party tools
- **White Label**: Custom branding options for agencies

## Target Audience

Designed for agencies, consultancies, and service businesses that need to manage both client-facing work and internal operations in one system.

## Benefits

Reduces software sprawl by consolidating multiple business functions into one platform, ensuring data consistency and eliminating duplicate data entry across systems.

## Pricing

Flexible pricing with a free plan for individuals and small teams, with paid tiers adding features like CRM, financial management, and increased storage.
