## Overview

Getting Things Done (GTD) by David Allen introduces a comprehensive system for managing tasks and projects, enabling stress-free productivity through clarity and efficient organization.

## The Five Steps of GTD

1. **Capture**: Collect everything that has your attention
2. **Clarify**: Process what each item means and what to do about it
3. **Organize**: Put it where it belongs
4. **Reflect**: Review frequently
5. **Engage**: Simply do

## Core Principles

- **Mind Like Water**: Achieve a calm, ready state
- **Next Actions**: Define concrete next steps for everything
- **Contexts**: Organize by where/how tasks can be done
- **Projects**: Track multi-step outcomes
- **Weekly Review**: Regular system maintenance
- **Someday/Maybe**: Capture ideas without commitment

## Impact

GTD has become one of the most influential productivity methodologies, spawning countless apps, books, and dedicated practitioners worldwide.

## Target Audience

Knowledge workers overwhelmed by tasks, projects, and commitments seeking a comprehensive organizational system.
