## Overview

Justin Hale is a dynamic keynote speaker, trainer, and productivity guru that focuses on practical workplace productivity and interpersonal communication.

## Focus Areas

- **Personal Productivity** - Systems and habits for individual effectiveness
- **Habit Change** - Strategies for building and breaking habits
- **Crucial Conversations** - Navigating difficult workplace discussions
- **Team Accountability** - Building accountability structures in teams
- **Change Management** - Guiding organizations through transitions
- **Generational Gaps** - Bridging differences between workplace generations
