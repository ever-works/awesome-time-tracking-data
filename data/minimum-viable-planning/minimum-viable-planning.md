## Overview

Minimum Viable Planning applies lean startup principles to personal productivity—find the smallest system that works.

## Core Principle

The best planning system is the one you'll actually use consistently. Complexity is the enemy of sustainability.

## Essential Elements

1. Weekly planning session (30-60 min)
2. Daily priorities list (3-5 items max)
3. Calendar for time-specific commitments
4. Capture tool for ideas/tasks
5. Weekly review (optional but recommended)

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Complex categorization systems
- Elaborate tagging schemes
- Multiple tools requiring sync
- Detailed time estimates
- Rigid hourly schedules

## Benefits

- Low friction to maintain
- Easy to restart after breaks
- Flexible and adaptive
- Sustainable long-term
- Focuses energy on doing, not planning
