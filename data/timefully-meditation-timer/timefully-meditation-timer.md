## Overview

Timefully is a meditation timer app for Transcendental Meditation, Loving-Kindness Meditation, Body Scan, Breath Awareness Meditation, Zen Meditation, Mindfulness Meditation, and all types of Yoga. Designed for serious practitioners who maintain a regular meditation practice.

## Key Features

- Customizable meditation timers
- Session tracking and statistics
- Progress milestones
- Minimalist, distraction-free interface
- Support for various meditation types
- Daily practice reminders
- Practice streaks and insights

## Target Audience

Perfect for committed meditators who practice unguided meditation and need a reliable timer without the distractions of guided meditations or social features.

## Pricing

Available on iOS App Store with free and premium options.
