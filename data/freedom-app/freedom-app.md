## Overview

Freedom is a cross-platform productivity app that blocks distracting websites and applications to help users focus on deep work. Unlike passive time tracking, Freedom actively prevents access to specified distractions during scheduled focus sessions.

## Key Features

**Website & App Blocking:**
- Block distracting websites across all browsers
- Block applications on desktop and mobile
- Custom blocklists for different work modes
- Schedule-based automatic blocking

**Cross-Platform Sync:**
- Works on iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows
- Blocks sync across all devices
- Start sessions on one device, apply to all
- Unified dashboard for all platforms

**Scheduling:**
- Recurring block sessions (e.g., weekday mornings)
- One-time focused work sessions
- Customizable session lengths
- Auto-start at specified times

**Locked Mode:**
- Prevents users from ending sessions early
- Forces commitment to focus periods
- Builds discipline and consistency
- Can't be bypassed during active sessions

**Productivity Tracking:**
- Track time spent in focus mode
- Monitor blocked attempts to access distractions
- Visualize productivity patterns
- Build streaks of productive days

## Use Cases

- Writers eliminating social media during creative time
- Students blocking entertainment during study sessions
- Professionals maintaining focus during deep work
- Anyone struggling with digital distractions

## Platform Support

- Mac (macOS)
- Windows (PC)
- iOS (iPhone/iPad)
- Android (phones/tablets)
- Chrome Extension

## Market Position (2026)

Freedom ranks as one of the top third-party screen-limiting apps alongside RescueTime and Forest, offering more aggressive blocking than built-in iOS Screen Time or Android Digital Wellbeing features.

## Pricing

Freedom offers both free and premium subscription tiers, with premium features including advanced scheduling and cross-device synchronization.
