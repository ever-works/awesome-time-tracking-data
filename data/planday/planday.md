## Overview

Planday is a cloud-based workforce management platform that combines employee scheduling, time tracking, and payroll integration to help businesses optimize their staff operations. The platform is particularly well-suited for small businesses with straightforward scheduling needs.

## Key Features

**Employee Scheduling:**
- Drag-and-drop shift planning interface
- Template-based scheduling for recurring shifts
- Open shift management and shift trading
- Mobile access for employees to view schedules

**Time Tracking:**
- Clock in/out functionality
- Break management
- Overtime tracking and alerts
- GPS location verification (optional)

**Payroll Integration:**
- Seamless export to payroll systems
- Automated timesheet approvals
- Integration with popular accounting software
- Wage calculation support

**Communication:**
- Built-in messaging for team communication
- Shift notifications and reminders
- Availability management
- Time-off requests and approvals

**Reporting & Analytics:**
- Labor cost analysis
- Attendance reports
- Schedule adherence metrics
- Export capabilities for further analysis

## Target Industries

- Retail stores
- Restaurants and hospitality
- Service-based businesses
- Healthcare facilities
- Small to medium-sized enterprises

## Performance (2026)

According to 2026 comparisons, Planday has an analyst rating of 81 and a user sentiment rating of 87 based on 154 reviews. The platform is recognized for its user-friendly interface and strong payroll integration capabilities.

## Competitive Position

Planday competes with platforms like Shiftboard, 7shifts, and When I Work. While Shiftboard excels at labor cost control for mission-critical industries, Planday focuses on simplicity and ease of use for small businesses with more straightforward scheduling requirements.

## Platform Accessibility

Planday offers web-based access along with mobile apps for iOS and Android, enabling managers and employees to manage schedules from any device.
