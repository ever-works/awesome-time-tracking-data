## Overview

TimeDox specializes in biometric time clock systems, offering a range of hardware devices and cloud-based software for businesses that need reliable, fraud-proof time and attendance tracking using fingerprint and facial recognition technology.

## Features

- **Fingerprint Recognition**: High-accuracy fingerprint scanners for employee identification
- **Facial Recognition**: Advanced face detection and matching algorithms
- **Multiple Hardware Options**: Various time clock models for different environments and budgets
- **Cloud-Based Software**: Access time and attendance data from anywhere
- **Buddy Punch Prevention**: Biometric authentication eliminates time theft
- **Badge & PIN Options**: Alternative authentication methods when biometrics aren't suitable
- **Real-Time Data**: Instant synchronization of clock-in/out data to the cloud
- **Overtime Tracking**: Automatic calculation of regular and overtime hours
- **Department Tracking**: Track employee time across different departments or projects
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive time and attendance reports for payroll processing
- **Payroll Integration**: Export data to major payroll systems
- **Mobile Access**: View reports and manage settings via mobile browser
- **WiFi & Ethernet**: Flexible network connectivity options

## Hardware Options

Offers various time clock models including fingerprint-only, face recognition, combination units, and outdoor-rated devices for harsh environments.

## Pricing

Hardware and software pricing varies by model and subscription plan. One-time hardware costs plus monthly or annual software subscription.
