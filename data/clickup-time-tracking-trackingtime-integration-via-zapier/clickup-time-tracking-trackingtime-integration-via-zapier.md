## ClickUp Time Tracking (TrackingTime Integration via Zapier)

### Description
A Zapier-based integration that connects TrackingTime with ClickUp so you can track time spent on ClickUp tasks, automate the transfer of time entries, and use TrackingTime’s reporting and analytics on your ClickUp work.

---

### Features
- **Time tracking inside ClickUp (via Zapier)**  
  Add TrackingTime’s time tracking capabilities to ClickUp using Zapier as the connector.

- **Automatic data transfer**  
  Automates the transfer of time tracking data between TrackingTime and ClickUp, reducing manual entry and the risk of errors.

- **Automatic timesheets**  
  Use Zapier’s pre-built templates to generate automatic timesheets from your ClickUp tasks and TrackingTime entries.

- **TrackingTime analytics on ClickUp work**  
  Analyze time spent on ClickUp tasks using TrackingTime’s analytics and reporting features.

- **Project and operations monitoring**  
  Use TrackingTime to track time and monitor projects tied to your ClickUp tasks.

- **Compliance and reporting**  
  Support internal and external requirements by reporting on time spent on ClickUp tasks through TrackingTime’s reporting tools.

- **Field mapping between tools**  
  Match TrackingTime data (such as tasks or time entries) to specific ClickUp fields within Zapier.

- **Configurable automation**  
  Customize triggers and actions in Zapier (e.g., when a task is created or updated in ClickUp, create or update a corresponding record in TrackingTime).

- **Multi-app time tracking**  
  Extend the same time tracking setup to other tools your team uses every day by synchronizing TrackingTime with additional apps via Zapier.

---

### How It Works / Setup
1. **Sign up**  
   Create accounts in TrackingTime and Zapier.

2. **Create your Zap**  
   In Zapier, click **“Create Zap”**, then connect your TrackingTime and ClickUp accounts.

3. **Connect, match, and automate**  
   - Grant Zapier access to both services.  
   - Configure trigger and action criteria.  
   - Map TrackingTime data to ClickUp fields.  
   - Test the Zap for accuracy and activate it to automate time tracking for ClickUp.

---

### Integrations
- **Primary integration:** ClickUp ↔ TrackingTime (via Zapier)
- **Platform:** Zapier (for triggers, actions, templates, and automation)
- **Extended ecosystem:** TrackingTime can additionally be synchronized with other apps your team uses daily (via Zapier and other integrations) so users can track time without switching between tools.
