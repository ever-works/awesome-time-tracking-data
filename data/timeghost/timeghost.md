## Overview

Timeghost is a time tracking solution that embeds directly into popular project management tools, allowing teams to track time without leaving their workflow.

## Key Features

- **Native Integration**: Works directly within Asana, Jira, and other project management tools
- **One-Click Tracking**: Start and stop timers directly from tasks and issues
- **Automatic Synchronization**: Time entries sync automatically with project data
- **Reporting**: Generate comprehensive time reports based on projects, clients, and team members
- **Budget Tracking**: Monitor project budgets in real-time
- **Team Collaboration**: See team capacity and workload at a glance

## Supported Integrations

- Asana
- Jira
- GitHub
- Additional project management platforms

## Benefits

- Eliminates context switching between tools
- Increases time tracking adoption through ease of use
- Provides accurate project costing and profitability insights
- Improves resource allocation decisions

## Best For

- Teams already using Asana or Jira
- Agencies tracking billable hours across multiple clients
- Software development teams needing issue-level time tracking
- Organizations wanting non-intrusive time tracking
