## Overview

Growlio Billable Hours Calculator is a free, advanced online tool that goes beyond basic time-to-money calculations. It provides comprehensive features for tracking billable hours, managing team time, analyzing profitability, and generating professional reports—all without requiring account creation or payment.

## Features

- **Billable Hours Calculation**: Instantly calculate total billable hours and revenue
- **Multiple Hourly Rates**: Support for different rates per person, project, or task
- **Team Management**: Track time for multiple team members simultaneously
- **Profit Analysis**: Calculate gross profit after expenses
- **Utilization Rates**: Monitor billable vs. non-billable time percentages
- **Time Entry**: Log hours with dates, descriptions, and categories
- **Report Export**: Download calculations as PDF or CSV
- **Project Tracking**: Organize time by client, project, or task
- **Expense Tracking**: Include expenses in profitability calculations
- **Tax Considerations**: Factor in tax rates for net revenue estimates
- **No Signup Required**: Use immediately without registration
- **Mobile Friendly**: Works on all devices
