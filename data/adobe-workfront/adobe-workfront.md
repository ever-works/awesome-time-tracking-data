## Overview

Adobe Workfront is a comprehensive enterprise work management solution that offers a broad range of features for tracking tasks, resources, and time. It's the leader in enterprise work management, connecting strategy to delivery and integrating people and data across the enterprise.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Robust time tracking capabilities integrated with project management
- **Resource Management**: Advanced resource allocation and capacity planning
- **Portfolio Management**: Strategic planning and prioritization tools
- **Workflow Automation**: Customizable workflows and approval processes
- **Real-Time Reporting**: Performance analytics and dashboards
- **Integration Ecosystem**: Connects with Adobe Creative Cloud and other enterprise systems
- **Task Management**: Comprehensive task assignment and tracking
- **Document Management**: Centralized file storage and version control

## Use Cases

- Large enterprise project management
- Marketing and creative team workflows
- IT and product development tracking
- Professional services time and billing
- Cross-functional team coordination

## Target Audience

Enterprises with 100+ employees, particularly those in marketing, creative services, IT, and professional services sectors.

## Pricing

Enterprise pricing available upon request. Typically positioned as a premium enterprise solution.
