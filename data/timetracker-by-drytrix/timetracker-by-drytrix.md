## Overview

TimeTracker is an open-source time tracking solution that can be self-hosted, giving users complete control over their time tracking data. It's designed for individuals, freelancers, and teams who prefer managing their own infrastructure.

## Features

- Self-hosted deployment for complete data ownership
- Project-based time tracking
- Client management
- Invoice generation
- Detailed time reports and analytics
- Multi-user support
- API access for integrations
- Customizable to specific business needs

## Pricing

Free and open-source. Hosting costs vary depending on infrastructure choices.
