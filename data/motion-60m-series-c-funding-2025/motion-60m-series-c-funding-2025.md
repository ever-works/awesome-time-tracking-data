## Funding Round Details

In December 2025, Motion secured $60 million in Series C funding at a $550 million valuation, signaling significant investor confidence and accelerated product development plans for 2026.

## Company Evolution

Motion has undergone a major strategic transformation:

### 2023-2024: AI Scheduler
- Focused primarily on intelligent calendar management
- Automated meeting scheduling and time blocking
- Individual productivity tool

### 2025-2026: AI Employee SuperApp
- Expanded into comprehensive work management
- AI Employees that automate entire business workflows
- Enterprise-focused features
- Integrated projects, tasks, calendar, meetings, docs, notes, reports, and workflows

## Product Positioning 2026

Motion now positions itself as "The #1 Rated Productivity Platform for the AI Era" with:
- AI Projects
- AI Tasks
- AI Calendar
- AI Meetings
- AI Docs
- AI Notes
- AI Reports
- AI Workflows

## Market Impact

The substantial Series C funding indicates:
- Strong product-market fit
- Aggressive growth trajectory
- Expansion from individual users to enterprise teams
- Increased R&D investment in AI capabilities

## Competitive Landscape

The funding positions Motion to compete more aggressively with established players in the productivity and work management space, particularly in AI-powered automation and scheduling.
