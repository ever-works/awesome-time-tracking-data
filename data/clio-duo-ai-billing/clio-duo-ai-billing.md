## Overview

Clio Duo is an AI-powered feature within the Clio legal practice management platform designed to automate billing tasks and improve the efficiency of law firm operations.

## Key Features

- **Automated Invoice Generation**: Clio Duo uses AI to automatically generate draft invoices based on client activity, eliminating manual invoice creation for routine billing
- **Time Entry Enhancement**: AI assists in creating more detailed and accurate time entries
- **Revenue Capture**: Helps firms capture more billable time by surfacing work that might otherwise go unbilled
- **Context-Aware Processing**: The AI understands legal workflows and client matter context to generate appropriate billing items

## Benefits for Law Firms

- Reduces administrative overhead for small and solo practices
- Improves billing accuracy and defensibility
- Saves significant time on routine invoicing tasks
- Increases revenue by ensuring all billable work is captured and invoiced

## Integration

Clio Duo is built directly into the Clio platform and works seamlessly with Clio Manage's time tracking, expense tracking, and practice management features.

## Pricing

Clio Duo is available as part of select Clio subscription plans. Pricing varies based on firm size and features required.
