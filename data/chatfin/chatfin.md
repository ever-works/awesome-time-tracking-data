## Overview

ChatFin provides an AI assistant for finance teams that connects to accounting, ERP, and BI tools to answer questions and automate routine tasks.

## Timesheet Automation Features

With ChatFin AI, teams capture time automatically, reconcile it to projects, and surface anomalies for review in finance AI chat.

### Automated Time Capture

AI agents passively track activity—calendar meetings, emails, document edits—and draft the timesheet for employees.

### Policy Enforcement

AI document processing finance can ingest vendor timesheets; AI controller rules enforce rate cards and overtime policies.

### Reconciliation

Reconciliation AI agents compare time logs to badge data, project plans, and contracts. Suspect entries route to managers with evidence.

## Security & Compliance

SOC 2 controls, role-based access, and masked PII keep labor data safe while FP&A still sees needed signals.

## Benefits

By automating timesheets with AI, you stop leaking revenue and start getting a true picture of your business's efficiency. AI timesheet automation plus reconciliation keeps labor clean, auditable, and ready for FP&A. Finance gains confidence without adding headcount.

The platform is designed to handle AP, AR, FP&A, and controllership tasks and is trusted by global enterprises for mission-critical operations.
