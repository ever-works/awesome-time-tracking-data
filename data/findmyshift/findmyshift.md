## Overview

Findmyshift is a cloud-based employee scheduling and time tracking platform designed for shift-based businesses in retail, hospitality, healthcare, and other industries.

## Scheduling Features

- Drag-and-drop schedule builder
- Recurring shifts and templates
- Shift swapping and coverage
- Open shift bidding
- Availability tracking
- Schedule publishing

## Time & Attendance

- Mobile and web time clock
- GPS verification
- Break tracking
- Timesheet approvals
- Overtime calculation

## Employee Features

- Mobile app access
- Shift notifications
- Availability updates
- Time-off requests
- Shift swap requests

## Management Tools

- Labor cost tracking
- Reporting and analytics
- Multi-location support
- Integration with payroll

## Pricing

Affordable per-employee pricing with free trial.
