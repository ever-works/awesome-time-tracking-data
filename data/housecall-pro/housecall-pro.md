## Overview

Housecall Pro is a field service management platform designed for small service businesses. Known for its user-friendly interface and quick deployment, the platform offers comprehensive time tracking alongside scheduling, dispatching, and invoicing.

## Time Tracking Features

**Core Time Tracking:**
- Employee time tracking integration
- Job duration logging
- Clock in/out functionality
- Integrated with scheduling system

**GPS Tracking:**
- Live GPS tracking of technicians
- Real-time vehicle location monitoring
- Enhanced dispatching through location awareness
- Travel time tracking

## Key Features

**Scheduling & Dispatching:**
- Calendar-based scheduling
- Real-time dispatch optimization
- Technician assignment
- Automated appointment reminders

**Customer Management:**
- Client database
- Service history
- Communication tools
- Online booking portal

**Payment Processing:**
- Mobile payment acceptance
- Invoice generation
- Automated payment reminders
- Multiple payment method support

## Pricing (2026)

- Solo: $59/month (annual billing)
- 5 users: $149/month (annual billing)
- Additional tiers for larger teams

## Target Market

Ideal for small field service teams needing a user-friendly solution for quick deployment. The platform provides the most accessible experience for small teams without complex requirements.

## Competitive Advantage

Housecall Pro differentiates through ease of use and rapid implementation, making it the top choice for small teams that prioritize simplicity and quick onboarding over advanced enterprise features.
