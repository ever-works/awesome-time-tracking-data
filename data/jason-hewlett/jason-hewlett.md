## Overview

Jason Hewlett is a leadership expert, author, and inductee of the Speaker Hall of Fame recognized for his impactful presentations on leadership and personal development.

## Notable Works

- **Signature Moves: How To Stand Out In A Sit Down World** - A book on developing distinctive leadership skills
- **The Promise: Become a Legendary Leader and Discover Your Signature Moves** - Award-winning presentation

## Approach

Hewlett's methodology focuses on helping leaders discover and develop their unique "signature moves" - the distinctive behaviors and approaches that set them apart and enable them to lead effectively.
