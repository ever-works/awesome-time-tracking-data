## Overview

Minutes.io is a straightforward, web-based tool designed for capturing meeting minutes efficiently, perfect for users looking for simplicity and speed without sacrificing functionality.

## Key Features

- **Attendance Management**: Track who attends meetings and manage participant lists
- **Time Tracking**: Global and by-topic time tracker to monitor meeting duration and time spent on each agenda item
- **Decision Support**: Propose and support decisions during meetings with structured workflows
- **Meeting Minutes**: Efficient capture and distribution of meeting notes
- **Collaboration**: Real-time collaborative note-taking and editing

## Enterprise Adoption

Widely used in KPMG global offices for both internal meetings and client meetings, demonstrating enterprise-grade reliability and compliance.

## Benefits

- Simple and fast meeting documentation
- Structured decision-making process
- Time accountability through topic-level tracking
- Attendance tracking for compliance and record-keeping
- Professional meeting minutes output
