## Overview

TimeSentry is time tracking and billing software that uses artificial intelligence to auto-populate timesheets. The software connects to email, calendars, and other data sources to track time spent on projects and suggest narrative time entries for professionals to review and submit for client billing.

## Features

- AI-powered timesheet auto-population from multiple data sources
- Integration with email, calendar, and address book applications
- Project management tool integration (Asana and others)
- Analytics and reporting capabilities
- Billing and invoicing functionality
- Team management features
- Narrative time entry suggestions for client billing

## Benefits

TimeSentry aims to reduce the administrative burden of time tracking so professionals can focus on client work rather than timesheet tasks. The software promises to significantly reduce the time spent on tracking work hours and creating invoices, enhance billing accuracy, and provide valuable insights into team productivity and project profitability.

## Use Cases

Ideal for professional services firms, consultants, lawyers, and other professionals who need to track billable hours accurately while minimizing time spent on administrative tasks.
