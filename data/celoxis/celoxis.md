## Overview

Celoxis is a robust project management and time tracking platform designed for enterprises and mid-market companies managing complex project portfolios. It combines powerful time tracking capabilities with resource management, financial tracking, and portfolio analytics in a unified system optimized for teams and professional services organizations.

## Time Tracking Capabilities

### Flexible Time Entry
- **Timesheet Interface**: Weekly grid view for quick batch entry
- **Task-Based Tracking**: Enter time against specific project tasks
- **Timer Functionality**: Real-time tracking with start/stop capability
- **Mobile Time Entry**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go tracking
- **Bulk Operations**: Update multiple time entries simultaneously
- **Automatic Reminders**: Email prompts for missing timesheets

### Time Categorization
- **Project & Task Allocation**: Granular time assignment
- **Billable vs Non-Billable**: Separate client from internal time
- **Overtime Tracking**: Monitor and flag excess hours
- **Activity Codes**: Custom classifications for time types
- **Client & Department**: Multi-dimensional time analysis
- **Work Types**: Distinguish development, testing, documentation, etc.

### Approval Workflows
- **Multi-Level Approvals**: Hierarchical timesheet review
- **Automated Routing**: Intelligent workflow based on org structure
- **Batch Approval**: Approve multiple timesheets at once
- **Rejection & Resubmission**: Workflow for corrections
- **Approval Notifications**: Email and in-app alerts
- **Audit Trail**: Complete history of approvals and changes

## Resource Management

### Capacity Planning
- **Resource Availability**: Track vacation, holidays, availability
- **Workload View**: Visual representation of team capacity
- **Allocation vs Actuals**: Compare planned assignments to time tracked
- **Skills Matrix**: Match resources by capabilities
- **Resource Requests**: Workflow for requesting team members
- **Forecasting**: Predict future resource needs

### Utilization Tracking
- **Utilization Reports**: Billable vs non-billable percentages
- **Target Utilization**: Set goals and track against them
- **Bench Management**: Identify underutilized resources
- **Efficiency Metrics**: Estimated vs actual time analysis
- **Individual & Team Views**: Drill down from portfolio to person

## Financial Integration

### Cost Tracking

### Billing & Invoicing
- **Billable Hours Export**: Send time data to invoicing systems
- **Time & Materials**: Track for T&M billing arrangements
