## Overview

Shiftie is a user-friendly staff scheduling and management platform built specifically for the restaurant, cafe, and bar industry, helping hospitality managers create schedules, track time, and communicate with their teams efficiently.

## Features

- **Easy Scheduling**: Build schedules quickly with templates and drag-and-drop interface
- **Shift Management**: Publish, edit, and manage shifts from any device
- **Time Tracking**: Employees clock in and out via mobile app
- **Availability**: Staff can set their availability and request time off
- **Shift Swapping**: Employees can offer and pick up available shifts
- **Team Chat**: Built-in messaging for team communication
- **Notifications**: Automatic shift reminders and schedule change alerts
- **Labor Cost Tracking**: Monitor wages and labor costs in real-time
- **Multiple Locations**: Manage staff across multiple restaurant locations
- **Mobile Apps**: Native iOS and Android apps for managers and staff
- **Reporting**: Labor reports and attendance summaries
- **Integrations**: Connect with payroll and POS systems

## Target Audience

Designed for independent restaurants, cafe chains, bars, and other hospitality venues that need simple yet powerful scheduling tools without overwhelming complexity.

## Pricing

Affordable pricing plans starting with a free tier for small teams, with paid plans offering additional features and unlimited staff.
