## Overview

Intapp Time is a specialized time tracking solution built for professional services firms that require accurate billing, compliance tracking, and integration with financial and practice management systems.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Time Capture**: Automatically suggests time entries based on calendar events, emails, and work patterns
- **Mobile Time Tracking**: Native iOS and Android apps for capturing time anywhere
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync with Outlook and Google Calendar to pre-populate time entries
- **Narrative Generation**: AI-assisted descriptions for time entries
- **Approval Workflows**: Multi-level approval processes for time and expense submissions
- **Compliance**: Built-in controls for legal and regulatory requirements
- **Integration**: Seamless connection with Intapp's practice management and financial systems
- **Reporting**: Advanced analytics and reporting for firm leadership
- **Offline Mode**: Continue tracking time without internet connectivity
