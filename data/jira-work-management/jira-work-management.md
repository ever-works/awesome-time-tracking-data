## Overview

Jira Work Management facilitates time tracking by allowing teams to assign tasks, track progress, and log work hours against specific projects and issues.

## Features

- Log time against tickets for real-time visibility into task durations
- Dashboards and reports showing time spent, work remaining, and deadlines
- Break down projects into tickets and subtasks with due dates
- Kanban boards for streamlining workflows and managing bottlenecks
- Sprint Progress Insights for quick views of progress
- Timelines for planning work and tracking progress in a single view

## Use Cases

- Identify tasks running over estimates and adjust resources
- Improve project planning with accurate time data
- Enhance resource allocation and prevent workload imbalances
- Analyze time data for better sprint planning and estimates
