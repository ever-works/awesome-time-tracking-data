## Overview

WorkTime is described as the only unique non-invasive, pure productivity monitoring software on the market, focusing exclusively on productivity metrics while respecting employee privacy.

## Key Features

### Non-Invasive Monitoring
- NO screenshots
- NO keystroke content  
- NO email or chat data
- NO camera tracking

### What It Tracks
- Active and idle time
- Login/logout times
- Application and website usage
- Attendance
- 80+ productivity reports
