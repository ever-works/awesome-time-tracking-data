## Overview

Civic Champs is a comprehensive volunteer management platform designed for nonprofits and charitable organizations. The platform handles the full volunteer lifecycle, from recruitment and scheduling to tracking volunteer hours and generating reports.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Hour Tracking**: Uses location to prompt check-in and check-out, automatically logging volunteer hours
- **Volunteer Scheduling**: Create and manage volunteer shifts and events
- **Self-Service Portal**: Volunteers can sign up for opportunities, track their hours, and manage their profiles
- **Communication Tools**: Send targeted messages and updates to volunteer groups
- **Impact Reporting**: Generate reports on volunteer hours and community impact
- **Background Checks**: Integrated volunteer screening capabilities
- **Mobile App**: iOS and Android apps for volunteers and coordinators

## Benefits for Nonprofits

- Reduces administrative burden of manual hour tracking
- Provides accurate data for grant applications and funding reports
- Improves volunteer retention through better engagement
- Streamlines volunteer onboarding and training
- Calculates economic value of volunteer time

## Value Tracking

At $34.79 per hour (2026 standard), volunteer time represents enormous value for nonprofits. Civic Champs helps organizations quantify and report this impact.
