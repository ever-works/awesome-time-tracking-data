## Overview

Custom Billable Rate Configuration allows organizations to set different hourly rates based on multiple criteria including client type, matter complexity, task category, and team member, enabling flexible and value-based billing strategies.

## Configuration Options

### By Client
- Premium clients: Higher rates
- Non-profit organizations: Discounted rates
- Long-term partners: Special pricing
- New client incentives: Promotional rates

### By Matter/Project Type
- Complex litigation: Premium rates
- Routine matters: Standard rates
- Pro bono: No charge
- Fixed fee work: Custom arrangements

### By Team Member
- Partners: $400-800/hour
- Senior associates: $250-400/hour
- Junior associates: $150-250/hour
- Paralegals: $75-150/hour

### By Task Type
- Court appearances: Premium
- Research: Standard
- Document review: Lower rate
- Administrative: Non-billable
