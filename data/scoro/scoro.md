## Overview

Scoro is a comprehensive work management platform designed specifically for agencies, consultancies, and professional service firms.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track billable and non-billable hours
- **Project Management**: Complete project planning and execution tools
- **Financial Control**: Real-time visibility into revenue and costs
- **Quote Building**: Create and manage client quotes
- **Task Assignment**: Distribute work across team members
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked time and expenses
- **Budget vs. Actuals**: Compare planned budgets to real-time actuals
- **Resource Planning**: Allocate and manage team resources
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive business intelligence and analytics
- **CRM**: Client relationship management features
