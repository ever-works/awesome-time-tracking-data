## Overview

Scribe is a process-documentation tool that automatically captures your on-screen actions and converts them into a clean, structured tutorial—complete with steps, text descriptions, and screenshots. Instead of taking manual notes or screenshots, Scribe generates everything for you in seconds.

## How Scribe Works

1. Install the extension
2. Click record
3. Perform your workflow
4. Scribe automatically captures each action with annotated screenshots

Every time you click a button, open a menu, or enter information, Scribe captures it with a screenshot and an auto-generated step description. When you're done, Scribe instantly generates a clean tutorial.
