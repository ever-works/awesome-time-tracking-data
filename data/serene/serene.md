## Overview

Serene is a macOS application designed to help users stay concentrated and achieve their goals by blocking distractions, organizing daily priorities, and creating an optimal focus environment. When you "Go Serene" (start a session), the app creates a comprehensive focus environment by blocking apps and websites, playing focus music, and managing your goals.

## Key Features

### Distraction Blocking
- **Website Blocker**: Blocks distracting websites during focus sessions
- **App Blocker**: Closes and blocks distracting macOS applications
- **Automatic Blocking**: Distractions are blocked automatically when starting a session
- **Browser Extensions**: Free extensions for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge to extend blocking capabilities

### Session Management
- **"Go Serene" Sessions**: One-click to start comprehensive focus mode
- **Countdown Timers**: Visual countdown showing remaining focus time
- **Pomodoro Timers**: Built-in Pomodoro technique support
- **Custom Durations**: Flexible session lengths to match your work style

### Goal Setting & Planning
- **Daily Goal Setting**: Define one primary goal that matters each day
- **To-Do Lists**: Organize tasks and subtasks for the day
- **Task Prioritization**: Focus on what truly matters
- **Goal Tracking**: Monitor progress on daily and weekly goals

### Focus Enhancement
- **Focus Music**: Soothing background music designed to enhance concentration
- **Ambient Sounds**: Rain, forest, ocean, white noise, and other calming soundscapes
- **Layered Sounds**: Mix multiple ambient sounds to create custom environments
- **Volume Control**: Adjust individual sound layers

### Phone Integration
- **Phone Silencer**: Automatically silence phone notifications during focus sessions (with proper setup)
- **Cross-Device Coordination**: Reduce interruptions across all devices

## Workflow

1. **Set Your Daily Goal**: Start your day by defining the one thing that matters most
2. **Add Tasks**: Break down your goal into actionable to-do items
3. **Go Serene**: Click to start a focus session
4. **Focus Environment Activated**:
   - Countdown timer appears
   - Focus music begins playing
   - Distracting apps close and get blocked
   - Websites on blocklist become inaccessible
5. **Work Uninterrupted**: Complete tasks in your protected focus environment
6. **Take Breaks**: End sessions for scheduled breaks between focus blocks

## Use Cases

- **Writers**: Eliminate social media and news distractions during writing sessions
- **Developers**: Block communication apps during deep coding work
- **Students**: Create distraction-free study environments
- **Designers**: Maintain flow state during creative work
- **Remote Workers**: Structure work-from-home days with focused sessions

## User Feedback & Limitations

### Positive Aspects
- Comprehensive approach to focus (blocking + music + goals)
- Clean, intuitive macOS interface
- Automatic blocking when starting sessions
- Affordable compared to some competitors

### Reported Issues
- Some users report the browser extension doesn't always block websites reliably
- macOS-only limits users with multi-platform setups
- Blocking can sometimes be bypassed by determined users
- Fewer advanced customization options than dedicated blocking tools

## Comparison with Alternatives

### vs. Freedom
- Lower price point ($40/year vs $50+)
- More integrated goal-setting features
- macOS only (Freedom supports more platforms)
- Less mature blocking technology

### vs. Focus (Mac App)
- More comprehensive features (Focus is simpler website blocker)
- Goal management built-in (Focus doesn't have this)
- Similar price range
- Both macOS-exclusive

### vs. Cold Turkey
- More user-friendly interface
- Better design and UX
- Weaker blocking enforcement (Cold Turkey is harder to bypass)
- Goal-oriented approach (Cold Turkey is pure blocking)

## Best For

- Mac users who want an all-in-one focus solution
- People who respond well to ambient sounds and music
- Users who prefer goal-oriented daily planning
- Those wanting moderate blocking without extreme lockdown
- Individuals seeking an attractive, well-designed Mac app

## Not Ideal For

- Windows or Linux users
- People needing bullet-proof blocking (use Cold Turkey)
- Users wanting browser-only solution (extensions alone may not be reliable)
- Those who prefer minimal, distraction-free interfaces
