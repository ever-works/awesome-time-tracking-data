## Overview

Kiosk Mode Time Tracking transforms a single shared device (tablet, computer, or dedicated terminal) into a digital time clock where multiple employees can clock in and out. This solution is ideal for businesses with shift-based work, centralized work locations, or environments where employees don't have individual devices.

## How It Works

### Basic Operation
1. A tablet, computer, or terminal is set up in kiosk mode
2. The device remains locked to the time tracking app
3. Employees authenticate themselves (PIN, QR code, facial recognition, or name selection)
4. They clock in/out with a single tap
5. The system records timestamp, location, and employee details

### Authentication Methods

**PIN Entry**: Each employee has a unique numeric PIN code for quick access

**QR Code**: Employees scan a personal QR code from their badge or phone

**Name Selection**: Employees select their name from a list (with optional photo verification)

**Biometric**: Facial recognition or fingerprint scanning for maximum security

**Universal PIN**: Optional fallback PIN for any employee to clock in

## Key Features

### Multi-Employee Support
- Unlimited employees can use the same device
- Quick switching between users
- Photo identification to prevent buddy punching

### Break Tracking
- Start and end breaks directly from the kiosk
- Automatic calculation of break duration
- Configurable break types (lunch, rest, etc.)

### Project/Task Selection
- Employees can switch between projects or tasks
- Select the work area or department
- Assign time to specific clients or jobs

### Offline Capability
- Continue tracking even without internet connection
- Data syncs automatically when connection restores
- No lost time entries

## Use Cases

### Retail Stores
- Place kiosk at employee entrance/break room
- Track shifts for cashiers, stockers, managers
- Monitor break compliance

### Restaurants & Hospitality
- Kitchen, front-of-house, and bar staff clock in at central location
- Track both regular hours and tip-eligible hours
- Monitor split shifts

### Manufacturing Facilities
- Shop floor kiosk for production workers
- Track time by production line or work order
- Switch between different job codes

### Construction Job Sites
- Rugged tablet at site entrance
- All crew members use same device
- Track time by project phase or area

### Healthcare Facilities
- Nurses and staff clock in at nursing station
- Track time by department or patient unit
- Monitor shift coverage

## Advantages

**Cost-Effective**: One device serves entire workforce instead of individual apps for each employee

**Simplicity**: Employees don't need to download apps or manage accounts

**Centralized**: All time punches happen at a known, controlled location

**Compliance**: Built-in geofencing confirms employees are on-site

**Security**: Prevents off-site or unauthorized clock-ins

## Security Features

### Buddy Punching Prevention
- Photo verification at clock-in
- Biometric authentication
- Geofencing to confirm location
- Manager alerts for suspicious activity

### Device Lockdown
- Kiosk mode prevents access to other apps
- Auto-locks after each transaction
- Password-protected admin settings
- Remote device management

## Implementation Considerations

### Hardware Selection
- **Tablet**: Portable, wall-mountable, touch-friendly
- **Desktop Computer**: Larger screen, more durable for high-traffic areas
- **Dedicated Terminal**: Purpose-built, most secure, weather-resistant options available

### Placement
- High-traffic area for easy access
- Secure location to prevent tampering
- Protected from weather (outdoor installations)
- Near entrance/exit for natural flow

### Customization Options
- Company branding and logo
- Custom fields (department, job code, equipment)
- Language selection for multilingual workforces
- Custom break types and policies

## Best Practices

- Position device at employee eye level for easy access
- Provide clear signage and instructions
- Train employees on proper clock-in procedure
- Regular device cleaning (especially biometric sensors)
- Backup authentication method (if primary fails)
- Monitor for unusual patterns (e.g., same employee always clocking in others)

## Pricing
