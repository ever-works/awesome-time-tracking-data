## Overview

Wagepoint, a Calgary-based payroll fintech company, launched built-in Timesheets functionality and a new employee mobile app in February 2026, bringing time tracking and employee self-service capabilities directly into their payroll platform.

## Features

- **Integrated Timesheets**: Time tracking built directly into the Wagepoint payroll system
- **Employee Mobile App**: New mobile application for employee self-service
- **Payroll Integration**: Seamless connection between time tracking and payroll processing
- **Employee Self-Service**: Allows employees to manage their own time entries
- **Canadian-Focused**: Built by a Canadian fintech company with Canadian payroll compliance in mind

## Target Market

Wagepoint Timesheets is designed for Canadian small and medium-sized businesses that use Wagepoint for payroll and want integrated time tracking without switching between multiple platforms.

## Launch

Announced and launched in February 2026.

## Platform

Web-based platform with mobile app for employee access.
