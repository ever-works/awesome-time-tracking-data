## Overview

Timewatch is a professional services automation (PSA) platform that combines resource scheduling, time tracking, project costing, billing, and financial integration. It's designed for consulting firms, agencies, and professional services organizations.

## AI-Powered Automation

Timewatch uses artificial intelligence to analyze calendar data and automatically generate timesheets:
- Converts Outlook, Teams, and Google Calendar appointments into timesheet entries
- AI analyzes appointment data to categorize and allocate time
- Reduces manual timesheet entry by up to 2+ hours per week per employee
- Employees continue using their normal calendar tools
- Automatic sync keeps timesheets current

## Time Tracking Features

- Multiple timesheet entry methods (automated, manual, timer)
- Calendar integration (Outlook, Google Calendar, Teams)
- Mobile time entry
- Offline mode with sync
- Configurable approval workflows
- Time allocation by project, task, and activity
- Billable and non-billable time tracking
