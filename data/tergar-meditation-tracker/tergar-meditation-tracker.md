## Overview

The Tergar Meditation Tracker app helps you start and maintain a regular meditation practice by tracking your sessions as you progress on the journey of training your mind. Part of the Tergar Meditation Community founded by Yongey Mingyur Rinpoche.

## Features

- Session tracking and logging
- Progress visualization
- Community connection
- Meditation insights and teachings
- Practice reminders
- Streaks and milestones
- Integration with Tergar's learning programs

## Background

Developed by Tergar International, an organization dedicated to making the ancient practice of meditation accessible to the modern world through the teachings of Tibetan meditation master Yongey Mingyur Rinpoche.

## Target Audience

Designed for students of Tergar's meditation programs and anyone interested in building a consistent meditation practice rooted in authentic Buddhist teachings.

## Pricing

Free app available on iOS and Android.
