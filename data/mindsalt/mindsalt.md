## Overview

MindSalt provides online approval workflow software for employee timesheets and expense reports. The platform allows organizations to create custom approval workflows that accommodate complex multilevel hierarchies, ensuring proper review and authorization of time entries and expenses.

## Features

- **Customizable Approval Workflows**: Create complex multilevel approval processes tailored to organizational structure
- **Timesheet Management**: Track employee work hours with web-based timesheet entry
- **Expense Report Management**: Submit and approve expense reports alongside timesheets
- **Email Notifications**: Automated reminders and alerts for pending approvals
- **Audit Trails**: Complete logging of all timesheet and expense report changes
- **Multi-Level Approvals**: Support for hierarchical approval chains
- **Web-Based Access**: Access from any device with a web browser
- **Integration Capabilities**: Export data to accounting and payroll systems

## Workflow Automation

MindSalt's workflow engine automatically routes timecards and expense reports to the appropriate managers based on preset business rules, with notifications sent when timesheets are pending approval.

## Pricing

MindSalt pricing starts at $6 per user per month, with pricing based on the number of users and features required.
