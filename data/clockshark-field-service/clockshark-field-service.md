## Overview

ClockShark is a mobile-first time tracking solution built specifically for construction and field service businesses, featuring GPS tracking, geofencing, scheduling, and seamless QuickBooks integration.

## Features

- GPS time clock with location tracking
- Geofencing for automatic clock-in/out
- Team scheduling and dispatch
- Job and task tracking
- PTO management
- Timesheet approvals
- QuickBooks integration
- Offline mode

## Field Service Capabilities

- Real-time crew location visibility
- Mileage tracking
- Photo attachments
- Job costing
- Certified payroll reports
