## Overview

ClickTime is a comprehensive employee time tracking and resource management platform designed for professional service firms, agencies, and project-based organizations. With robust timesheet approval workflows, budget tracking, and resource planning capabilities, ClickTime helps organizations accurately capture time, control costs, and optimize resource allocation.

## Features

### Time Tracking
- **Flexible Entry Methods**: Timers, manual entry, or bulk timesheet entry
- **Project & Task Tracking**: Multi-level project hierarchies with detailed task breakdowns
- **Activity Codes**: Customizable activity types for granular time categorization
- **Timesheet Views**: Daily, weekly, or monthly timesheet layouts
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for time entry anywhere
- **Offline Mode**: Track time without internet, sync when connected
- **Time Entry Templates**: Save and reuse common entry patterns

### Timesheet Approval Workflows
- **Project-Level Approval**: Managers approve time for their specific projects
- **Multi-Level Approvals**: Sequential approval chains (Manager → Director → Finance)
- **Delegated Approval**: Temporary approval authority for backup approvers
- **Bulk Approval**: Approve multiple timesheets simultaneously
- **Exception Handling**: Flagging and resolution of timesheet issues
- **Approval Status Tracking**: Real-time visibility into approval pipeline
- **Deadline Management**: Configurable submission and approval deadlines

### Compliance and Organization
- **Standardized Format**: Consistent timesheet structure across organization
- **Policy Enforcement**: Business rules ensure compliance
- **Required Fields**: Mandate necessary information for each entry
- **Audit Trail**: Complete history of submissions, approvals, and modifications
- **Labor Law Compliance**: Track breaks, overtime, and regulatory requirements
- **Client Requirements**: Adapt to specific client billing standards

### Expense Tracking
- **Expense Entry**: Log business expenses with receipts
- **Receipt Capture**: Mobile app photo capture of receipts
- **Expense Categories**: Customizable expense types
- **Billable Expenses**: Flag expenses for client billing
- **Mileage Tracking**: Built-in mileage calculator
- **Credit Card Integration**: Import expenses from card statements
- **Expense Approval**: Separate approval workflow for expenses

### Resource Planning
- **Capacity Planning**: Visualize team availability and workload
- **Resource Allocation**: Assign people to projects based on capacity
- **Skill Matching**: Match resources to projects by skillset
- **Utilization Tracking**: Monitor billable vs non-billable time
- **Forecasting**: Plan future resource needs
- **What-If Scenarios**: Model different allocation strategies
- **Availability Management**: Track PTO and availability

### Budget Management
- **Project Budgets**: Set time and cost budgets per project
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor budget consumption as time is tracked
- **Budget Alerts**: Warnings when approaching or exceeding budgets
- **Budget vs Actual**: Compare planned to actual time and costs
- **Multi-Currency**: Handle international projects and rates
- **Rate Management**: Different billing rates by role, person, or project

### Reporting and Analytics
- **Standard Reports**: 50+ pre-built report templates
- **Custom Reports**: Build tailored reports with report builder
- **Dashboards**: Real-time visibility into key metrics
- **Time Analysis**: Understand how time is spent across projects and activities
- **Utilization Reports**: Billable percentage and capacity metrics
- **Profitability Analysis**: Project-level profit and loss
- **Trend Analysis**: Historical patterns and forecasting
- **Export Options**: PDF, Excel, CSV formats

## Approval Workflow Details

### Project Manager Approval
- **Scope**: Approve time entries for assigned projects only
- **Visibility**: See only relevant project time entries
- **Authority**: Accept, reject, or request changes to entries
- **Comments**: Add feedback or questions to time entries
- **Batch Actions**: Approve multiple entries for a project at once

### Hierarchical Approval
- **First Level**: Project or team manager reviews
- **Second Level**: Department head or director approves
- **Final Level**: Finance or executive approval (optional)
- **Conditional Routing**: Route based on entry characteristics

### Organized Submissions
- **Standardized Format**: All timesheets follow same structure
- **Required Information**: Enforced fields ensure completeness
- **Clear Attribution**: Who, what, when, why always visible
- **Supporting Details**: Notes and justifications included
- **Compliance Checks**: Automated validation against guidelines
