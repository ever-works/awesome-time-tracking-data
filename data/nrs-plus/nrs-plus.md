## Overview

NRS Plus is a complete point-of-sale system designed for small retail stores, convenience stores, and bodegas, with integrated employee time clock features that track staff hours directly through POS terminals.

## Features

- **Integrated Time Clock**: Employees clock in/out using the POS terminal
- **Employee Tracking**: Monitor which employee made which sales
- **Time Clock Only Users**: Create accounts for employees who don't process sales
- **Automatic Hour Calculation**: System calculates total hours worked for payroll
- **Shift Reports**: View employee hours by shift or pay period
- **Point of Sale**: Full POS functionality including barcode scanning and inventory
- **Inventory Management**: Track stock levels and automatically reorder
- **Sales Reporting**: Detailed reports on sales, inventory, and employee performance
- **Multi-Store Support**: Manage multiple locations from central dashboard
- **Cloud-Based**: Access reports and data from anywhere
- **Payment Processing**: Accept credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payments
- **Low Cost**: Affordable pricing designed for small businesses

## Target Market

Independent retailers, convenience stores, smoke shops, liquor stores, and other small retail operations that need simple, affordable POS with basic time tracking.

## Benefits

Eliminates the need for separate time clock hardware by using the POS terminal for both sales and time tracking, reducing hardware costs and simplifying operations.

## Pricing

Affordable flat-rate pricing with no percentage of sales fees. Hardware and software packages available.
