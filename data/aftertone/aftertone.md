## Overview

Aftertone is a modern time blocking and task management app built specifically for Mac that uses AI to help you plan your day more effectively and protect your focus time.

## Features

- AI-powered task scheduling
- Automatic time blocking
- Calendar integration (Google Calendar, iCloud)
- Smart suggestions for task placement
- Focus time protection
- Native macOS design and performance
- Menu bar quick access
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Task prioritization
- Daily planning assistance
- Buffer time management
- Meeting optimization

## AI Capabilities

- Learns your work patterns
- Suggests optimal times for tasks
- Automatically adjusts schedule when plans change
- Identifies best focus time blocks
- Balances deep work with meetings

## Platform

- macOS native application
- Designed for Apple Silicon and Intel Macs
- Follows Apple Human Interface Guidelines
- Integrates with macOS calendar and notifications
