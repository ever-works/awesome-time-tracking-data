## Overview

Shiftboard is an enterprise-grade workforce scheduling platform that helps organizations manage complex shift-based operations. It's particularly popular in healthcare, manufacturing, hospitality, and logistics industries.

## Features

- AI-powered shift scheduling
- Demand forecasting and labor optimization
- Mobile app for shift management
- Time and attendance tracking
- Compliance management (overtime, break rules)
- Employee self-service portal
- Shift bidding and trading
- Automated shift notifications
- Integration with HRIS and payroll systems
- Real-time labor cost tracking
- Certification and credential tracking
- Multi-location support
