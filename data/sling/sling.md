## Overview

Sling is an all-in-one workforce management platform built specifically for restaurants, retail stores, and businesses with hourly shift workers. It combines scheduling, time tracking, team communication, and task management in a single intuitive application designed to save managers time while improving team coordination.

## Features

### Smart Scheduling
- **Drag-and-Drop Interface**: Create schedules quickly with visual builder
- **Availability Tracking**: Employees set when they can and cannot work
- **Conflict Alerts**: Automatic warnings for double-bookings and availability conflicts
- **Schedule Templates**: Save and reuse common schedule patterns
- **Shift Copy**: Duplicate previous week's schedules with one click
- **Color Coding**: Visual organization by role, shift type, or location
- **Schedule Publishing**: Send schedules to entire team with notifications
- **Multi-Location**: Manage multiple store or restaurant locations

### Time Clock
- **Mobile Clock-In**: Employees punch in/out from their smartphones
- **Kiosk Mode**: Use tablet as shared time clock for team
- **GPS Geofencing**: Ensure employees are on-site when clocking in
- **Facial Recognition**: Prevent buddy punching with photo verification
- **Break Tracking**: Automatic meal and rest break recording
- **Time-Off Requests**: Employees request time off through the app
- **Timesheet Editing**: Managers can adjust entries with audit trail
- **Overtime Tracking**: Monitor hours and receive overtime warnings

### Labor Cost Management
- **Real-Time Labor Costs**: See current labor spending as schedules are built
- **Budget Targets**: Set daily, weekly, or monthly labor budgets
- **Forecasting**: Predict labor needs based on historical data and sales
- **Cost Alerts**: Notifications when approaching budget limits
- **Labor Reports**: Detailed analysis of labor costs by department, location, or time period
- **Sales Integration**: Connect with POS systems for labor-to-sales ratio tracking

### Team Communication
- **Group Messaging**: Chat with entire team or specific groups
- **Announcements**: Broadcast important updates with read receipts
- **Direct Messages**: One-on-one communication between team members
- **Shift Notes**: Attach information to specific shifts
- **File Sharing**: Distribute menus, procedures, and training materials
- **Message Scheduling**: Schedule announcements for future delivery

### Shift Management
- **Shift Trading**: Employees can offer shifts to coworkers
- **Manager Approval**: Control which trades happen automatically
- **Open Shifts**: Post available shifts for employees to claim
- **Shift Reminders**: Automated notifications before shifts begin
- **Late Clock-In Alerts**: Notify managers of tardiness
- **Callout Management**: Track no-shows and late arrivals

### Task Management
- **Task Lists**: Create daily to-do lists for shifts and employees
- **Recurring Tasks**: Set up regular duties that repeat daily, weekly, or monthly
- **Task Assignment**: Assign specific tasks to roles or individuals
- **Completion Tracking**: Employees check off completed tasks
- **Photo Verification**: Employees can attach photos to completed tasks
- **Shift Notes**: Add context and instructions to tasks

## Industry-Specific Features

### For Restaurants
- **Position-Based Scheduling**: Schedule by role (servers, cooks, hosts)
- **Shift Meal Tracking**: Track employee meals for inventory and accounting
- **Section Assignment**: Assign servers to specific sections
- **Kitchen vs Front of House**: Separate scheduling views by area
- **POS Integration**: Connect with Toast, Square, Clover, and other restaurant POS systems

### For Retail
- **Peak Hour Scheduling**: Ensure adequate coverage during busy times
- **Sales-Based Forecasting**: Schedule based on predicted foot traffic
- **Multi-Department**: Manage schedules across different store departments
- **Seasonal Staffing**: Handle fluctuating staffing needs

## Mobile Apps

- **iOS and Android**: Native apps for employees and managers
- **Manager View**: Full scheduling and management capabilities on mobile
- **Employee View**: Simplified interface for viewing schedules and clocking in
- **Offline Access**: View schedules without internet connection
- **Push Notifications**: Instant alerts for schedule changes and messages

## Integration Options

- **Payroll**: QuickBooks, Gusto, ADP, Paychex, Square Payroll
- **POS Systems**: Toast, Square, Clover, Lightspeed, Revel
- **Accounting**: QuickBooks, Xero
- **HR Systems**: BambooHR, Namely

## Benefits

- **Save Scheduling Time**: Build schedules in minutes, not hours
- **Reduce Labor Costs**: Real-time visibility prevents overstaffing
- **Improve Communication**: Keep team connected and informed
- **Prevent Time Theft**: GPS and facial recognition ensure accurate time tracking
- **Increase Accountability**: Task lists ensure critical duties are completed
- **Simplify Compliance**: Automatic break and overtime tracking
- **Reduce No-Shows**: Shift reminders decrease missed shifts
