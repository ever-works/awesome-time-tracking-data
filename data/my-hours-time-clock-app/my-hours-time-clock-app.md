### Overview
The **My Hours Time Clock App** is a time-tracking solution focused on employee attendance and absence tracking. It lets employees clock in and out, while managers monitor working hours and presence. The app connects with the broader My Hours platform to support reporting and payroll-related workflows.

### Features
- **Clock-in / clock-out tracking** – Employees can record the start and end of their work periods using a simple time clock interface.
- **Attendance tracking** – Captures when employees are present at work, providing an overview of who is working and when.
- **Absence tracking** – Tracks time off and other absences alongside regular working hours.
- **Manager oversight of working hours** – Managers can review logged work hours to monitor presence and ensure coverage.
- **Presence monitoring** – Supports visibility into which employees are currently clocked in.
- **Integration with My Hours platform** – Time entries from the time clock app flow into the broader My Hours system.
- **Reporting support** – Recorded time can be used within My Hours reporting tools for analysis of worked hours.
- **Payroll-related workflows** – Tracked hours can be used to support payroll calculations and related processes.

### Integrations
- **My Hours platform** – Native integration with the main My Hours environment for reporting and payroll workflows.
