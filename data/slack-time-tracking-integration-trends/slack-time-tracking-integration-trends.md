## Overview

By embedding time tracking into Slack where work discussions happen, teams achieve better adoption, cleaner data, and more precise insights into performance and resource allocation.

## User Base

As of 2025–2026, Slack has grown to approximately 47.2 million daily active users and 79 million monthly active users.

## Key Benefits

- **Contextual Tracking**: Track time where work discussions occur
- **Improved Accuracy**: Immediate tracking reduces forgotten time entries
- **Reduced Friction**: No context switching to separate time tracking app
- **Better Adoption**: Seamless integration improves team compliance
- **Faster Entry**: Simple slash commands for starting/stopping timers
- **Channel Integration**: Link Slack channels to specific projects automatically

## Popular Integrations

- **Toggl Track**: Start/stop timers, log entries, link channels to projects
- **TimeCamp**: Track hours during discussions without interrupting flow
- **Harvest**: Simple slash commands for time entry
- **Jibble, TimeDoctor, Timely**: Additional Slack-integrated options

## Impact

Integrated time tracking improves accuracy, reduces administrative overhead, and ensures successful project execution through better time management.
