## Overview

Intend (formerly known as Complice) is described as an online todo list/journal/coworking hybrid that uses intentions for today rather than traditional task lists. The app focuses on helping users align daily actions with long-term goals through intention-setting.

## Key Features

- **Intentions-Based Planning**: Set daily intentions tied to specific goals rather than disconnected tasks
- **Goal Tracking**: Create goals with optional milestones and link daily work to them
- **Virtual Coworking**: Work in a virtual coworking space with other users to enhance focus and accountability
- **Regular Reviews**: Built-in system for daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly reviews
- **Progress Evaluation**: Daily evaluation of progress toward each goal with "enough" or "not enough" ratings
- **Timeline Feature**: Track your progress over time
- **Reflections**: End-of-day, week, month, and year reflection prompts

## How It Works

Users set up goals with optional milestones and reviews. The key feature is daily intention-setting where you determine your todos or intentions for the day, with each intention linked to a specific goal. This creates a clear connection between daily work and long-term objectives.

## Use Cases

Ideal for individuals who:
- Want to connect daily actions to bigger picture goals
- Prefer intention-setting over traditional task management
- Benefit from accountability through virtual coworking
- Value regular reflection and review processes
- Seek alignment between daily efforts and long-term vision
