## Overview

CosmoLex is a comprehensive legal practice management and accounting software designed for law firms. It handles both case management and back-office accounting within a single platform, eliminating the need for third-party integrations.

## Key Features

**Time Tracking & Billing:**
- Built-in time tracking for billable hours
- Automated invoice generation
- Electronic payment processing
- Expense tracking and reimbursement

**Trust Account Management:**
- IOLTA and trust account safeguards
- Automatic three-way reconciliation
- Transaction logs for compliance
- Built-in compliance checks

**Case Management:**
- Document management and storage
- Client communication tools
- Calendar and deadline tracking
- Task management

**Accounting Integration:**
- General ledger accounting
- Financial reporting and dashboards
- Track casework and financials in one view
- No third-party accounting software needed

## Target Market

Ideal for solo attorneys and small law firms that need comprehensive practice management and accounting in a single solution without maintaining separate systems.

## Compliance

CosmoLex is specifically built to handle the strict rules around IOLTA and trust account management, with safeguards that help law firms maintain compliance with bar association requirements.
