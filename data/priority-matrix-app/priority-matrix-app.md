## Overview

Priority Matrix® by Appfluence is a priority and task management tool based on the Eisenhower method,  helping users organize and prioritize tasks across four customizable quadrants to focus on what truly matters.

## The Four Quadrants

1. **Critical & Immediate** - Do these tasks first
2. **Critical & Not Immediate** - Schedule time for these important tasks
3. **Not Critical & Immediate** - Delegate or minimize time spent
4. **Uncategorized Inbox** - Review and categorize new items
