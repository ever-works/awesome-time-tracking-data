## Overview

TickTick's Habit Gallery provides a curated library of over 60 common habits, making it easy for users to start tracking without having to design their habit tracking system from scratch.

## Pre-Made Habits Include

### Health & Fitness
- Daily exercise
- Drink 8 glasses of water
- Get 8 hours of sleep
- Take vitamins
- Stretch/yoga

### Productivity
- Morning routine
- Review goals
- Plan tomorrow
- Clear inbox
- Focus time

### Personal Development
- Read for 30 minutes
- Learn a language
- Practice meditation
- Journal
- Express gratitude

### Social & Lifestyle
- Call family
- No social media
- Spend time outdoors
- Practice hobby

## How It Works

1. **Browse Gallery**: Explore 60+ habit templates
2. **Select Habits**: Choose relevant habits to track
3. **Customize**: Adjust frequency, reminders, and goals
4. **Track Progress**: Mark habits complete each day
5. **View Statistics**: See streaks, completion rates, and patterns

## Feedback System

Detailed statistics and feedback help users:
- Build streaks for motivation
- Identify patterns in success/failure
- Adjust goals based on actual performance
- Celebrate progress with visual charts

## Integration with Other Features

Habits integrate with:
- Calendar view for planning
- Reminders for consistency
- Statistics for reflection
- Rewards/gamification elements
