## Overview

Overtime Pre-Approval Requirements establish a gatekeeper process preventing unauthorized overtime work, controlling labor costs while maintaining compliance with overtime pay obligations.

## How It Works

### Request Process
1. Employee anticipates need for overtime
2. Submits request to supervisor
3. Includes justification and estimated hours
4. Supervisor approves or denies
5. Approval documented in system

### Enforcement
- Time tracking system checks approval
- Warns if entering unapproved overtime
- May block timesheet submission
- Flags for supervisor review

## Benefits

**Cost Control**:
- Prevents surprise overtime costs
- Enables budget forecasting
- Reduces unnecessary overtime
- Promotes efficiency

**Compliance**:
- Documents business need
- Creates audit trail
- Supports FLSA requirements
- Protects against wage claims

**Planning**:
- Advance notice of workload issues
- Opportunity to reallocate work
- Better resource management

## Exceptions

Emergency situations may require:
- Post-facto approval
- Blanket pre-approvals
- On-call policies
- Manager discretion
