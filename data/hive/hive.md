## Overview

Hive is a comprehensive platform designed for hybrid and remote teams, offering project, time, and goal management capabilities. While primarily known as a project management tool, its time tracking features are robust and should not be overlooked.

## Time Tracking Features

- Integrated time tracking within project management workflows
- Track and manage time across projects
- Ensure productivity and efficiency across teams and assignments
- Seamless integration with existing project management features

## Key Project Management Features

- Project management and collaboration tools
- Goal management and tracking
- Task assignment and progress monitoring
- User-friendly interface designed for minimal learning curve

## Community and Development

- Public roadmap for transparency
- Public forum where users can upvote and suggest features
- Community-driven product development

## Pricing

- Free plan available
- Most popular plan priced at $12 per user, per month

## Target Users

Hybrid and remote teams looking for time tracking integrated with solid project management features.
