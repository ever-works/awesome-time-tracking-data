Jibble is a comprehensive time tracking and attendance software that is FREE forever for unlimited users, making it an attractive option for businesses of all sizes. Recognized by Forbes as the Best Time Clock Software of 2025.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking & Attendance
- Track employee attendance in real time with GPS tracking, face recognition, kiosk mode & timesheets
- Works whether your team works at a desk, on the road, or across multiple locations
- Multiple clock-in methods: mobile app, web browser, desktop app, and kiosk mode

### Multiple Access Methods
- **Mobile App**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go tracking
- **Web Browser**: Access from any device with internet
- **Desktop App**: Native desktop applications
- **Kiosk Mode**: Turns a tablet into a high-speed attendance terminal

### Advanced Security Features
- **Biometric Attendance**: AI-powered facial recognition ensures the right person is clocking in
- **GPS Tracking**: Enforces location-based attendance
- **Geofencing**: Create virtual boundaries for clock-in/out locations
- **Offline Functionality**: Tracks attendance without internet connection, syncing data once back online
