## Definition

Allocating a fixed time period to each planned activity.

## How It Works

Instead of "Write report", schedule "9-11am: Write report".

### Key Elements

1. Identify task
2. Estimate time needed
3. Allocate specific slot
4. Work only during that slot
5. Stop when time expires

## Benefits

- Prevents perfectionism
- Creates urgency
- Improves time estimation
- Prevents work expansion (Parkinson's Law)
- Makes commitments visible
- Reduces multitasking

## Best Practices

- Schedule breaks between boxes
- Build in buffer time (25%)
- Group similar tasks
- Protect focus boxes
- Be realistic about durations
- Review and adjust regularly

## Tools

- Google Calendar
- Outlook Calendar
- Notion
- Sunsama
- TimeBloc

## Common Mistakes

- No buffer time
- Back-to-back boxes
- Unrealistic estimates
- Too rigid
- Not accounting for energy levels
