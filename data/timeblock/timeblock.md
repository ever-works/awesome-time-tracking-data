## Overview

TimeBlock is a Chrome browser extension that blocks distracting websites during specified hours, helping users maintain focus during designated work or study times.

## How It Works

Automatically prevents access to distracting sites like social media, streaming platforms, and games during set time ranges. Users configure which websites to block and during what hours, and the extension enforces these rules automatically.

## Key Features

- **Time-Based Blocking**: Set specific hours when certain sites are blocked
- **Custom Block Lists**: Choose which websites to restrict
- **Automatic Enforcement**: No manual activation needed during blocked hours
- **Schedule Flexibility**: Different rules for different times of day
- **Focus Protection**: Prevent impulsive browsing during work hours

## Use Cases

- Blocking social media during work hours (9am-5pm)
- Restricting entertainment sites during study time
- Preventing late-night browsing before bedtime
- Creating distraction-free morning routines
- Enforcing focused work periods

## Benefits

- Reduces temptation without requiring willpower
- Maintains productivity during scheduled work times
- Helps establish healthy digital boundaries
- Supports time blocking and deep work practices

## Platform

Available as a Chrome browser extension from the Chrome Web Store.
