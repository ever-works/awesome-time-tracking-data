## Overview

ZoomInfo Chorus is an AI-powered Conversation Intelligence platform designed to enhance customer engagement by capturing and analyzing interactions across various communication channels, including phone calls, video meetings, and emails. The platform supports sales and revenue teams in achieving their targets by providing a comprehensive understanding of customer conversations.

## Acquisition

ZoomInfo acquired Chorus.ai, a leading provider of Conversation Intelligence solutions, for $575 million, integrating its capabilities into the ZoomInfo ecosystem.
