## Overview

Notion Calendar is the successor to Cron, a popular calendar app that Notion acquired in 2022. After the acquisition, Notion renamed the app and linked it to their ecosystem while keeping the core calendar functionality free for all users.

## Features

- **Multiple Calendar Integration**: Connect Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, and Apple iCloud accounts
- **Multiple Account Support**: View calendars from different accounts in one place
- **Menu Bar Access**: Quick access from macOS menu bar
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Extensive keyboard navigation for power users
- **Time Zone Support**: Easy management across multiple time zones
- **Meeting Scheduler**: Share availability links for easy scheduling
- **Beautiful Interface**: Clean, modern design inherited from Cron
- **Free Forever**: All features available at no cost
- **Desktop & Mobile**: Apps for macOS, iOS, and Android
- **Natural Language Entry**: Create events using plain language
- **Notion Integration**: Connect with Notion databases and pages
- **Calendar Overlays**: View multiple calendars with color coding
- **Scheduling Links**: Create custom booking pages

## What Notion Calendar Does

- Provides a streamlined calendar viewing experience
- Syncs across all your devices
- Integrates with Notion for contextual information
- Offers quick event creation and editing
- Helps manage multiple calendars from one interface

## What It Doesn't Do

- **No Auto-Scheduling**: Won't automatically schedule your tasks
- **No Task Management**: Limited task/to-do functionality
- **No Capacity Planning**: Doesn't predict deadline achievement
- **No Time Blocking Automation**: Manual placement of events required

## Pricing

**Completely Free**: No paid tiers, subscriptions, or hidden costs. All features available to everyone.

## Best For

- **Notion Users**: Those already in the Notion ecosystem
- **Calendar Power Users**: People who need keyboard-first navigation
- **Multi-Account Users**: Those managing multiple calendar accounts
- **Budget-Conscious**: Users seeking premium features without cost
- **Cross-Platform Needs**: Teams working across macOS, iOS, and Android

## Comparison to Alternatives

**vs. Motion**: Motion offers AI auto-scheduling and task management, but costs $29-49/month. Notion Calendar is free but lacks automation.

**vs. Google Calendar**: Notion Calendar offers better multi-account management and a more modern interface, while Google Calendar has more integrations.

**vs. Clockwise**: Clockwise offers AI-powered scheduling optimization for teams. Notion Calendar is simpler and individual-focused.
