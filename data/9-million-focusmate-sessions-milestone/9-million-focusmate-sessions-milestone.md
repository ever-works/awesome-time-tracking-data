## Milestone Achievement

Focusmate has supported over 9 million virtual coworking sessions, representing a significant validation of body doubling as an effective productivity technique.

## What This Represents

**Global Scale:**
- Used daily by thousands in 150+ countries
- Diverse user base across industries and roles
- Multiple session types (25, 50, 75 minutes)
- Continuous growth since launch

**Validation of Concept:**
- Body doubling works at scale
- Virtual format is effective (not just in-person)
- Sustainable user engagement over time
- Crosses cultural and geographic boundaries

## Session Distribution

**User Patterns:**
- Some users do multiple daily sessions
- Others use occasionally for difficult tasks
- Students during study periods
- Professionals for deep work blocks
- Entrepreneurs for accountability

**Effectiveness Data:**
- 161% productivity increase for neurodivergent members
- Over 100 organizational clients including Fortune 200 companies
- High user retention and repeat usage rates

## Why Body Doubling Works

The 9 million sessions validate that:

**Accountability Effect:**
- Presence of another person creates gentle pressure
- Commitment to start and finish time
- Social obligation helps overcome procrastination

**Focus Enhancement:**
- Parallel working reduces distraction
- Modeling focused behavior
- Structured start and end with brief check-ins

**Community Connection:**
- Reduces isolation of remote/solo work
- Global community of productivity-focused individuals
- Shared experience of focused work

## Growth Context

**Contributing Factors:**
- Rise of remote and hybrid work
- Increased ADHD awareness and accommodation
- Recognition of attention as scarce resource
- Digital tools enabling global connections

## 2026 Significance

The 9 million session milestone demonstrates that unconventional productivity tools addressing human psychology (not just time management mechanics) can achieve mainstream adoption and proven effectiveness.
