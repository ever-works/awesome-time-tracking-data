## Overview

Brainwaves is a productivity and focus application designed to help users concentrate, sleep, and relax through the use of ambient music, binaural beats, and isochronic tones. The app claims to help improve concentration in as little as 15 minutes.

## Features

- 600+ programs across sleep, focus, and healing frequencies
- Combines ambient music, binaural beats, and isochronic tones
- Available on iOS, Android, and desktop platforms
- Personalized playlist creation
- Mix and match different sounds
- Variety of soundscapes to choose from
- Customizable experience to cater to specific needs
