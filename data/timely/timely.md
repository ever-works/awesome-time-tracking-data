## Overview

Timely is an AI time tracking software for consultancies, agencies, and SaaS businesses that automates time tracking through memory tracking technology. The platform captures work activity automatically and generates timesheets with AI assistance.

## Features

- **Automatic Time Tracking**: Memory tracking captures all work activity in the background without manual timers
- **AI Timesheet Generation**: Complete timesheets in under 2 minutes with AI-powered suggestions
- **Calendar Integration**: Syncs with calendars to automatically map time from meetings and events
- **Privacy-First Design**: All data is stored privately and only visible to the user
- **Project Budgeting**: Track project hours against budgets in real-time
- **Detailed Reporting**: Generate comprehensive reports on time allocation, profitability, and productivity
- **Team Collaboration**: Share timesheets and collaborate on project time tracking
- **Integrations**: Connects with popular project management and productivity tools

## Use Cases

- Consultancies tracking billable hours across multiple clients
- Agencies managing time for creative and marketing projects
- SaaS companies monitoring development time and resource allocation
- Professional services firms requiring accurate time capture for billing

## Benefits

- Increases timesheet completion rates through automation
- Eliminates time leakage by capturing all work automatically
- Reduces administrative overhead by 75% compared to manual tracking
- Improves billing accuracy with complete activity records
- Provides insights into productivity patterns and work habits
