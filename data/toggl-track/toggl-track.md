## Toggl Track

Toggl Track is a team-focused time tracking platform that turns tracked time into custom reports to help organizations understand productivity and revenue.

**Website:** https://toggl.com/track

---

### Features

- **Time tracking for teams**
  - Designed for team use and organization-wide time tracking.
  - Aims to support high time-tracking adoption without intrusive monitoring.

- **Custom reporting**
  - Builds reports from your teamâ€™s time data.
  - Customizable views to analyze productivity, projects, and revenue.

- **Calendar View**
  - Visual calendar-based time tracking and review.
  - Integrates with external calendars.
  - Allows copying calendar events into Toggl Track as time entries.

- **Flexible, cross-platform tracking**
  - Track time from:
    - Web app
    - Desktop apps
    - Mobile apps
    - Browser extensions
  - Enables tracking time in different contexts and devices.

- **Background / automated time tracking**
  - Automated tracking running in the background to capture activity and assist with logging time.

- **Integrations and workflow fit**
  - 100+ integrations with common online tools (e.g., Jira, Salesforce, Asana, Slack and others referenced in imagery/text).
  - Open API to connect with custom or internal systems.
  - Designed to fit into existing workflows rather than replace them.
