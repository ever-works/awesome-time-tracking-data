## Overview

Tick (formerly Tick Spot) is a time tracking application with a unique focus on project budgets rather than just hours. It's designed to help teams stay within allocated time budgets through visual budget indicators and proactive alerts, making it particularly valuable for agencies managing fixed-price projects or strict hourly budgets.

## Budget-First Approach

### Visual Budget Indicators
- Color-coded budget health (green, yellow, red)
- Real-time budget vs actual display
- Percentage of budget consumed
- Projected overrun warnings
- Time remaining calculations

### Proactive Budget Management
- Alerts when approaching budget limits
- Weekly budget burn rate tracking
- Pace indicators (on track, ahead, behind)
- Budget reallocation suggestions
- Historical budget accuracy data
