## Overview

Clio is the leading cloud-based legal practice management software, offering comprehensive time tracking, billing, case management, and collaboration tools for law firms of all sizes.

## Features

- **Time Tracking** - Track billable hours with timers, manual entry, or automatic tracking
- **Billing & Invoicing** - Create professional invoices and accept online payments
- **Case Management** - Manage cases, contacts, and calendars in one place
- **Document Management** - Store and organize documents with version control
- **Client Portal** - Secure portal for client communication and document sharing
- **Trust Accounting** - Compliant trust accounting with IOLTA management
- **Mobile Apps** - Full-featured iOS and Android apps for on-the-go access
- **Integrations** - Connect with 200+ legal tech integrations
- **Security** - Bank-level encryption and SOC 2 Type II compliance

## Use Cases

Widely used by solo practitioners, small firms, mid-sized practices, and legal departments across all practice areas.
