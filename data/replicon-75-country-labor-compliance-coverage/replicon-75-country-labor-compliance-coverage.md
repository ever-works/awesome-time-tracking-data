## Overview

Replicon (now part of Deltek) provides pre-configured labor compliance rules for 75+ countries, automatically ensuring time tracking, overtime calculation, breaks, pay, and leave management comply with local regulations globally.

## Coverage Scope

**75+ Countries**: Pre-configured compliance rules across:
- North America
- Europe
- Asia-Pacific
- Latin America
- Middle East
- Africa

**Compliance Areas**:
- Overtime calculation rules
- Mandatory break requirements
- Pay period regulations
- Leave entitlements
- Working time directives
- Record-keeping requirements

## How It Works

**Automatic Application**:
1. Configure employee location/jurisdiction
2. System applies relevant labor rules automatically
3. Time entries validated against regulations
4. Overtime calculated per local laws
5. Breaks enforced as legally required
6. Violations flagged before payroll
