## Overview

Covetrus Pulse is marketed as the industry's first cloud-based all-in-one Veterinary Operating System (vOS) designed to simplify workflows, save time, and empower next-level practice management. Formerly known as eVetPractice, the platform brings all Covetrus Platform solutions into one flexible, connected care environment.

## AI-Powered Features

### Time Savings
- Covetrus AI saves an average of 6+ hours per week per vet
- Auto-generates SOAP notes at no extra cost
- Embedded AI features throughout the platform
- Automated clinical documentation
