## Overview

Workforce.com provides time and attendance staffing and scheduling with GPS clocking, break tracking, and payroll-ready timesheets. The platform's scheduling syncs with attendance, HRIS, and payroll, with each component natively embedded together for seamless workforce management.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking & Attendance
- Entirely web-based—staff can clock in and out on any device
- Kiosk app available for tablets
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android devices
- GPS tracking captures exact location when employees clock in and out
- Does not track location throughout shift—only at clock-in/out times

### Preventing Time Theft
- Selfie clock-in with photo identification
- Optional biometric face scanning confirms identity
- GPS tracking verifies clock-in location
- Geofencing defines approved clock-in areas
- Prevents buddy punching and early/late clock-ins without approval

### Overtime Prevention
- Real-time alerts when employees approach overtime
- Managers notified before overtime thresholds are hit
- Smarter scheduling to distribute hours evenly
- Cost control through proactive monitoring

### Geofencing
- Define clock-in areas with geographic boundaries
- Verify employees are within designated range of jobsite
- Ensure staff clock in from approved locations only
- Support for multiple job sites and locations

### Scheduling Integration
- Native integration between scheduling and time tracking
- Sync with HRIS and payroll systems
- Real-time schedule updates
- Automated timesheet generation

### Break Tracking
- Track meal and rest breaks for compliance
- Ensure labor law compliance
- Automatic break reminders
- Break time reporting
