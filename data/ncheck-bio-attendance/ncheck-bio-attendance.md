## Overview

NCheck Bio Attendance is a comprehensive biometric attendance management system designed to register and manage employee attendance using advanced biometric identification technologies including face, fingerprint, and iris recognition.

## Features

- **Multi-Modal Biometric Recognition**: Support for face, fingerprint, and iris biometric identification for high accuracy and security
- **Contactless Attendance**: No-contact face and iris biometric scanning for a more hygienic experience
- **Offline Capabilities**: Records attendance in real-time with no connectivity required, syncing when connection is available
- **Hardware Flexibility**: Uses standard webcams for office environments and supports various biometric terminals
- **Mobile Access**: Log attendance from anywhere using a personal smartphone
- **Payroll Integration**: Seamless integration with QuickBooks, Tally ERP, and other payroll systems
- **Cloud and On-Premise**: Deployment options for both cloud and on-premise environments
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Immediate attendance recording and monitoring

## Use Cases

- Office environments requiring secure access control
- Retail workforce management
- Manufacturing facilities with time clock requirements
- Hazardous environments requiring precise tracking
- Multi-location businesses needing centralized attendance management
