## Overview

Virto Software provides nurse scheduling and workforce management solutions that integrate seamlessly with Microsoft 365 and SharePoint. Their tools help healthcare organizations manage complex shift schedules while leveraging existing Microsoft infrastructure.

## Features

- Microsoft 365 and SharePoint integration
- Visual shift scheduling calendar
- Drag-and-drop schedule creation
- Shift assignment and rotation management
- Time-off request handling
- Staff availability tracking
- Automatic schedule notifications
- Mobile-friendly interface
- Certification tracking
- Reporting and analytics
- Multi-location support
- Customizable to organization needs

## Pricing

Virto Software offers various licensing models for their scheduling solutions, including per-user and site licenses. Pricing varies based on the number of users and deployment options. Contact Virto for specific pricing information.
