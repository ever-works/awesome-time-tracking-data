## Overview

Microsoft Viva Insights (formerly MyAnalytics) is a personal productivity analytics tool built into Microsoft 365 that provides AI-powered insights about work patterns, meeting habits, focus time, and collaboration. It offers personalized recommendations for improving productivity and work-life balance based on analysis of email, calendar, calls, and chats.

## Personal Insights

### Work Patterns Analysis
- Total work hours per week
- After-hours work time
- Meeting time vs focus time
- Email volume and response times
- Collaboration patterns
- Time spent with specific people or groups

### Focus Time

- Fragmentation analysis
- Best times for focus
- Meeting-free time availability
- Suggestions for protecting focus
