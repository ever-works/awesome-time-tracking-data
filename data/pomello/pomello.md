## Overview

Pomello uses Trello to organize tasks and employs the popular Pomodoro Technique to supercharge productivity. It's available as a Chrome web browser extension that integrates with your existing Trello workflow.

## Key Features

- Integrates seamlessly with existing Trello workflows
- Adds a pomodoro count to card titles
- Logs events in card comments
- Tracks productivity stats
- Prompts to move completed cards to other lists
- Customizable options for each board and/or list
- Roll timer over on same card for multi-Pomodoro tasks

## How It Works

After connecting your Trello account, select a list and you're ready to use Pomello! The app helps you work in focused 25-minute intervals (Pomodoros) with breaks in between, following the traditional Pomodoro Technique principles.
