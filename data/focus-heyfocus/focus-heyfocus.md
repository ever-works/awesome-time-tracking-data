## Overview

Focus (HeyFocus) is a Mac application that blocks time-wasting websites and apps to help you work better. It provides a simple but powerful way to eliminate digital distractions during focused work sessions.

## Key Features

### Website and App Blocking
Blocks access to distracting websites and applications that you specify, preventing you from accessing them during focus sessions.

### Cross-Browser Support
Works on all popular web browsers including Safari, Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.

### Built-in Timers
Comes with built-in timers for productivity techniques including the Pomodoro Technique, allowing you to schedule focus and break periods.

### Scheduling
Set recurring schedules to automatically enable focus mode during specific times of day or days of the week.

## How It Works

1. Add websites and apps to your blocklist
2. Start a focus session with your preferred duration
3. Focus blocks access to distracting sites until the timer ends
4. Take a break when the session completes

## Benefits

- Removes the temptation to check social media or news sites during work hours
- Helps build better work habits through consistent blocking schedules
- Supports productivity methods like Pomodoro with integrated timers

## Platform Availability

Available exclusively for macOS. For Windows users, alternatives like Cold Turkey Blocker or Freedom are recommended.

## Pricing

Offers both free and paid versions, with the paid version unlocking advanced scheduling features and unlimited blocklists.
