## Overview

myNoise is a comprehensive ambient sound generator that provides hundreds of customizable soundscapes to help users concentrate better, relax, or sleep. Unlike simple noise generators, myNoise offers calibrated sound generators with advanced features.

## Sound Library

myNoise offers an extensive collection of soundscapes including:
- Nature sounds (rain, ocean, wind, forest)
- White, pink, and brown noise variations
- Musical drones and tones
- Industrial and mechanical sounds
- Ethnic and cultural instruments
- Binaural beats and isochronic tones
- ASMR-focused sounds

## Advanced Features

- Individual slider controls for each sound frequency
- Calibrated sound generation based on hearing profile
- Animate feature for evolving soundscapes
- iOS and Android apps with offline playback
- No advertisements
- Community-contributed generators
- Support for various audio formats

## Unique Capabilities

- Hearing test to calibrate sounds to your specific hearing profile
- Equalization controls for fine-tuning each soundscape
- Save and share custom sound settings
- Timer and alarm functionality

## Use Cases

- Masking tinnitus symptoms
- Improving focus and concentration
- Creating optimal study environments
- Relaxation and meditation
- Sleep improvement
- Blocking office distractions

Free to use with optional donations to support development. Mobile apps available for one-time purchase with offline access and additional features.
