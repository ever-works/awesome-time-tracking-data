## Overview

Pomotroid is a simple and configurable Pomodoro timer that aims to provide a visually-pleasing and reliable way to track productivity using the Pomodoro Technique. Built with Electron, it's a cross-platform desktop application for Linux, macOS, and Windows.

## Features

- **Customizable Themes**: Ships with 37 themes and supports fully custom themes with live hot-reload
- **Progress Tracking**: Review your day at a glance, track weekly streaks, and explore your full history with a 52-week heatmap
- **Desktop Integration**: Dynamic tray icon shows your progress arc in real time
- **Desktop Notifications**: Handle round transitions automatically
- **Configurable Timers**: Adjust work and break durations to suit your workflow
- **Minimal Interface**: Clean, distraction-free design that focuses on the timer
- **Audio Notifications**: Customizable alert sounds for break and work sessions
