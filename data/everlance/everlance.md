## Overview

Everlance is a comprehensive mileage and expense tracking solution designed for freelancers, gig workers, small business owners, and independent contractors. The app automates the tedious task of logging business miles and expenses, ensuring users capture every tax-deductible dollar while spending minimal time on administrative tasks.

## Features

- **Automatic Mileage Tracking**: GPS-based automatic detection of business drives
- **Manual Mileage Entry**: Add trips retroactively or adjust automatically logged miles
- **Receipt Scanning**: Snap photos of receipts with automatic data extraction
- **Expense Categorization**: Organize expenses by IRS categories for tax purposes
- **Trip Classification**: Swipe to classify trips as business, personal, or commute
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor mileage and expenses as they happen
- **Tax Deduction Calculator**: Estimate tax savings from tracked expenses
- **IRS-Compliant Reports**: Generate reports meeting IRS requirements
- **Multiple Vehicles**: Track mileage across multiple vehicles
- **Team Management**: Manage employee mileage and expenses (premium)
- **Integrations**: Sync with QuickBooks, TurboTax, and other platforms
- **Cloud Backup**: Automatic backup of all data

## Mileage Tracking

### Automatic Detection
- Uses smartphone GPS to detect when driving
- Automatically logs start/end points, distance, and route
- Background tracking doesn't drain battery significantly
- Smart learning adapts to your driving patterns

### Classification
- Swipe right for business trips
- Swipe left for personal trips
- Set rules for automatic classification (e.g., trips to office)
- Bulk classify similar trips

### Accuracy
- GPS tracking provides precise mileage
- Route visualization shows exact path taken
- Edit trips if needed for accuracy
- Meets IRS audit requirements

## Expense Tracking

### Receipt Capture

### Expense Categories

### Missed Deductions

- Ensures maximum tax savings
