## Overview

Pomofocus is a web-based Pomodoro timer that requires no installation and works across all devices with a browser.

## Features

- **Browser-Based**: No downloads required, works on desktop and mobile
- **Customizable Timer**: Adjust work and break durations to your preference
- **Task List**: Organize tasks alongside the timer
- **Visual Design**: Clean interface with color-coded sessions
- **Statistics**: Track completed Pomodoros and productivity
- **Notifications**: Browser notifications for session transitions
- **Templates**: Pre-configured timer settings for different work styles
- **Accessibility**: Works across all modern browsers
- **Free to Use**: Completely free online tool
