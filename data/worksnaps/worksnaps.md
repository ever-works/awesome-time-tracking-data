## Overview

Worksnaps is a time tracking service designed for remote and distributed teams that combines automatic time tracking with screenshot capture to provide visual verification of work activities.

## Features

- **10-Minute Interval Tracking**: Each work snap represents 10 minutes of work verified by a screenshot, mouse and keyboard volume, and most active applications
- **Automatic Activity Monitoring**: Tracks time based on mouse and keyboard interactions; idle periods are not counted as work time
- **Screenshot Capture**: Randomly captures screenshots within each 10-minute interval for visual work verification
- **User Control**: Users receive notifications before screenshots are sent and can review, submit, or discard them
- **Private Mode**: Users can set screenshots to be visible only to themselves for later review
- **Offline Tracking**: Captures work data even when offline, syncing once internet connection is re-established
- **Customizable Settings**: Adjust screenshot frequency and privacy filters to suit team needs
- **Activity Levels**: Monitors keyboard and mouse activity to gauge user engagement

## Integrations

Integrates with Asana, JIRA, Basecamp, Trello, Redmine, Teamwork, Huddle, Zapier, Podio, Freshbooks, Harvest, Unfuddle, PivotalTracker, Axosoft, Taiga, Worktile, Teambition, and Tower.

## Platform Compatibility

Compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## Best For

Small to medium-sized businesses with remote teams or freelancers who need straightforward, cost-effective time tracking with productivity monitoring.
