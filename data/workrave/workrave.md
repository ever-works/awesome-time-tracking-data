## Overview

Workrave is a free and open-source program that assists in the recovery and prevention of Repetitive Strain Injury (RSI). The program runs on GNU/Linux and Microsoft Windows, monitoring your keyboard and mouse usage to provide timely break reminders.

## Break System

Workrave implements a three-tier break structure designed to maintain healthy computer usage:

### 1. Microbreaks
- **Default**: Every 3 minutes
- **Duration**: About 30 seconds
- **Purpose**: Brief pause to relax hands and eyes
- **Fully Configurable**: Adjust timing and duration to your needs

### 2. Rest Breaks
- **Default**: Every 45 minutes
- **Duration**: About 10 minutes
- **Purpose**: Longer break to move around and reset
- **Fully Configurable**: Customize frequency and length

### 3. Daily Limit
- **Tracks**: Total daily computer usage
- **Purpose**: Prevents excessive daily screen time
- **Configurable**: Set your own daily usage limits
- **Prompt**: Alerts you when limit is reached

## Philosophy

Workrave is built on the principle that regular breaks are essential for long-term computer health. By automating break reminders based on actual usage, it helps users maintain healthy habits without thinking about it.
