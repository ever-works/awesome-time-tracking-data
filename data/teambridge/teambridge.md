## Overview

TeamBridge is a workforce management solution designed for healthcare organizations to streamline staff scheduling, time and attendance tracking, and labor compliance. It uses automation to reduce manual scheduling work and ensure optimal staffing.

## Features

- Automated staff scheduling based on demand
- Time and attendance tracking
- Credential and license management
- Labor compliance monitoring
- Shift marketplace for open shifts
- Mobile app for employees
- Real-time labor cost tracking
- Overtime and fatigue alerts
- Integration with payroll systems
- Advanced analytics and forecasting
- Multi-facility support
- Self-service employee portal
