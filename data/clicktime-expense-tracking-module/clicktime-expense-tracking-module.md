## Overview

The ClickTime Expense Module makes expense reporting easy for employees and accurate for managers by integrating expense tracking directly with time tracking and project management. This unified approach provides complete financial visibility for project-based work.

## Key Features

### Mobile Receipt Scanning
Employees can scan and upload images of their receipts through the mobile app, providing proof of purchase to management and eliminating paper receipt management.

### Expense Classification
Users can:
- Classify expenses by type
- Upload receipt images as documentation
- Tie expenses to specific projects
- Add notes and descriptions
- Submit expense reports directly from mobile devices

### Manager Review and Approval
Managers can review, approve, and reject expense sheets through ClickTime Mobile, streamlining the approval process and reducing administrative delays.

### Project-Based Expense Tracking
Expenses are linked directly to projects, enabling:
- Accurate project cost tracking
- Complete financial visibility per client
- Better budget management
- Comprehensive project profitability analysis

## Reporting Capabilities

### Prebuilt Expense Report Templates
ClickTime provides ready-made templates for common expense reporting needs:
- **Mileage Reports**: Track business travel by vehicle
- **Client Expense Reports**: All expenses billable to specific clients
- **Project Expense Reports**: Expenses organized by project
- **Employee Reimbursement**: Individual employee expense summaries

### Over 70 Prebuilt Reports
ClickTime offers more than 70 prebuilt report templates covering various aspects of time and expense data, making it easy to surface needed information quickly.

## Integration with Time Tracking

### Unified Financial View
Combining time and expense tracking provides:
- Complete project cost picture (labor + expenses)
- Accurate billable vs. non-billable tracking
- True profitability calculations
- Comprehensive client invoicing data

### Single Platform Benefits
- One login for all financial tracking
- Consistent data across time and expenses
- Unified reporting and analytics
- Streamlined administration

## Time Categorization

ClickTime categorizes time at entry - billable, CapEx, R&D - ensuring finance gets usable data without burdening teams. This same approach extends to expense categorization.
