## Overview

The To-Done List is an unconventional productivity technique that focuses on tracking completed work rather than pending work.

## How It Works

Keep a running list of all the tasks you have already completed. This can be maintained alongside a traditional to-do list or used independently.

## Benefits

- Provides visual evidence of progress and accomplishments
- Motivates continued productivity through positive reinforcement
- Helps combat the feeling of never getting anything done
- Offers a retrospective view of how time was actually spent
