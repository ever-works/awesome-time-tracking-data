## Overview

Lawcus Time Insights is a comprehensive analytics feature released in 2026 as part of the Lawcus legal practice management platform. It gives law firms enhanced visibility into time tracking patterns, billing efficiency, and practice profitability through data-driven insights.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking Analytics

- **Attorney Utilization**: Track billable hours vs non-billable hours per attorney
- **Matter Time Distribution**: See how time is allocated across different matters
- **Historical Trends**: Analyze time tracking patterns over weeks, months, quarters
- **Time Entry Lag**: Identify delays between work completion and time entry
- **Productivity Metrics**: Measure individual and team productivity

### Billing Intelligence

- **Realization Rate Analysis**: Track percentage of time entries that convert to revenue
- **Write-Off Patterns**: Identify common reasons for time write-downs
- **Collection Efficiency**: Monitor how quickly invoices are paid
- **Rate Optimization**: Analyze which rates and billing structures are most profitable
- **Client Profitability**: Determine which clients generate the most revenue

### Practice Management Insights

- **Matter Profitability**: Calculate profit margins by matter type or practice area
- **Resource Allocation**: See which attorneys spend time on which matter types
- **Capacity Planning**: Forecast attorney availability based on current workload
- **Bottleneck Identification**: Find workflow inefficiencies
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Project future revenue based on WIP and historical data

## 2026 Innovation

### AI-Powered Recommendations

- **Billing Suggestions**: AI recommends optimal billing practices based on firm data
- **Staffing Optimization**: Suggests attorney assignments for new matters
- **Rate Recommendations**: Proposes rate adjustments based on market data
- **Time Entry Prompts**: Reminds attorneys of likely unbilled time

### Real-Time Dashboard

- **Live Metrics**: Real-time view of firm performance
- **Customizable Views**: Create dashboards for different roles (partners, managers, billing staff)
- **Visual Analytics**: Charts, graphs, and heat maps for easy interpretation
- **Alerts**: Automated notifications for important metrics or thresholds

## Integration with Lawcus Platform

Time Insights seamlessly integrates with other Lawcus features:

- **Matter Management**: Pull time data from all matters
- **Billing Module**: Connect insights directly to invoicing
- **Document Management**: Track document-related billable time
- **Client Portal**: Share relevant analytics with clients
