## Overview

Sembly AI is an intelligent meeting assistant designed to act as an AI-powered teammate for your organization. It automatically joins calls, records and transcribes discussions, and delivers clear and actionable insights.

## Platform Support & Languages

- Works with Google Meet, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Cisco Webex
- No installations or downloads needed
- Accurately identifies different speakers
- Supports conversations in 48 languages
- Provides a full spectrum of meeting-related services
- Automates deliverables

## Team Intelligence Features
