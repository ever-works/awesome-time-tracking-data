## Overview

Time Confetti describes calendar fragmentation into small, unusable time chunks.

## The Problem

- 10-15 minute gaps between commitments
- Too short for deep work
- Too long to ignore
- Collectively significant (2-3 hours/day)
- Creates feeling of busyness without productivity

## Solutions

### Consolidation
- Batch meetings together
- Create larger uninterrupted blocks
- Use meeting-free days

### Strategic Use
- Pre-plan micro-tasks
- Use for email/admin
- Take genuine breaks
- Mindfulness practice
