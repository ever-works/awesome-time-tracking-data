## busybusy Time Tracking Features

### Overview
busybusy is a construction-focused time tracking app that combines mobile time clocks, GPS-based tracking, project and cost code tracking, and scheduling tools for field teams, supervisors, and office staff.

### Features

- **Multiple Ways to Track Time**  
  - Employees can clock themselves in/out on their devices.  
  - Supervisors can clock in/out their crews.  
  - Employers can set up a single clock-in location for all employees.

- **Clock In / Clock Out Tools**  
  - Start/stop work, log breaks, and switch between projects or cost codes.  
  - Track equipment and operator time.  
  - Track materials used.  
  - Record work accomplished during the shift.

- **Supervisor Tools**  
  - Clock in/out entire teams at once.  
  - Apply group actions such as scheduled breaks and time off.  
  - Centralize time management for crews in the field.

- **Scheduling**  
  - Create and manage work schedules for employees and crews.  
  - Send schedule notifications to employees.  
  - Attach detailed job instructions to scheduled tasks.  
  - Add a location pin so employees know where to report.

- **Photos & Notes**  
  - Automatically time-stamp, GPS-stamp, and project-stamp each photo.  
  - Search photos by date, project, or employee.  
  - Attach and share job-related content such as plans, receipts, and documents.

- **“Who’s Working?” Universal Map**  
  - Map view of each active project.  
  - See where employees are working in real time.  
  - View what employees are doing and the locations of equipment.

- **GPS Breadcrumbing**  
  - GPS timestamps that log employee location updates throughout the day.  
  - Helps verify that clock-ins and clock-outs occur at appropriate locations.

- **Required Onsite Clock-In**  
  - Enforces location-based rules so employees must be on site to clock in.  
  - Reduces off-site and early clock-ins.
