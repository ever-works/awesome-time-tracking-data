## Overview

Project Archive functionality enables organizations to maintain clean active project lists while preserving historical data for compliance, reference, and learning purposes.

## Key Features

### Archive Process
- Mark project as archived/inactive
- Remove from active selection lists
- Preserve all historical data
- Maintain searchability
- Prevent new time entries

### Data Retention
- Time entries preserved
- Budget information retained
- Reports remain accessible
- Invoices maintained
- Notes and attachments saved

### Access Control
- View-only mode
- Search/reporting access
- Export capabilities
- Audit trail maintained

## Compliance Requirements

**DCAA**: 3-6 year retention
**Legal**: Varies by jurisdiction
**Tax**: 7 years typical
**Audit**: Based on contract terms

- Archive promptly after completion
- Document archive reason
- Regular archive reviews
- Consistent naming
- Clear archive policy

## Reporting

**Include Archived**:
- Multi-year trends
- Career retrospectives
- Client relationship history
- Department performance

**Exclude Archived**:
- Current capacity
- Active budgets
- Real-time dashboards
