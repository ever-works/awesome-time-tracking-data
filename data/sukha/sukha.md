## Overview

Sukha is a flow state website designed to fight WFH distractions, protect your focus, restore your energy and finish work earlier.

## Key Features

### Focus Enhancement
- Scientifically designed music to maintain flow state
- AI assistant that notices when you drift and gently nudges you back into focus
- Distraction detection technology
- Energy restoration tools for sustainable productivity

### Work Session Management
- Structured work sessions to maintain concentration
- Break timing optimization
- Focus time tracking and analytics
- Productivity insights based on work patterns

### Remote Work Optimization
- Specifically designed for work-from-home environments
- Helps combat common remote work distractions
- Creates virtual boundaries for focused work time

## Target Users

Remote workers, freelancers, and knowledge workers seeking to maintain deep focus and productivity in home environments.
