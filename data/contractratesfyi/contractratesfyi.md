## Overview

ContractRates.fyi is a crowdsourced collection of user-submitted rate data designed to help freelancers and contractors figure out what to charge. All data is community-verified and provides realistic market benchmarks across different developer roles and specializations.

## Income Calculator

The platform offers a simple but powerful calculator showing annual earnings based on:
- Rate per hour
- Hours per week
- Months worked per year
