## Overview

ServiceTitan is an end-to-end software platform built specifically for residential and commercial service businesses in the trades (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, etc.). It combines CRM, dispatching, time tracking, invoicing, and business intelligence into a unified system designed to help service contractors manage technicians and grow profitability.

## Time Tracking Integration

### Technician Time Management
- Automatic time capture from job dispatch
- Clock-in/out tied to job arrival/completion
- Drive time tracking between jobs
- Break and lunch tracking
- Overtime monitoring and alerts
- Time approval workflows

### Job-Based Time Tracking
- Time automatically associated with customer jobs
- Labor costing by job
- Technician productivity metrics
- Billable vs non-billable time
- Time-and-materials invoicing
- Warranty vs paid work tracking
