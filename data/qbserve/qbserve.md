## Overview

Qbserve is an automatic time tracking app for Mac that tracks productivity, generates reports, and helps you understand how you spend your time on your computer.

## Features

- Automatic time tracking of all Mac activities
- Productivity scoring for applications and websites
- Detailed productivity reports
- Invoice generation from tracked time
- Privacy-focused - all data stored locally
- Distraction alerts
- Goal setting and productivity targets
- Integration with calendar apps

## Pricing

One-time purchase for Mac. Offers a free trial period.
