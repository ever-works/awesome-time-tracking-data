## Overview

Orkana is a privacy-first time tracking tool with offline data storage, automatic activity monitoring, real-time insights, goal setting, Pomodoro sessions, and app blocking.

## Privacy & Data Storage

All data stays on your machine with zero cloud dependency. You can export it anytime in Markdown or JSON format and upload it to ChatGPT, Claude, or any LLM.

## Automatic Tracking

The app provides automatic tracking in the background with no manual timers and no logging. All apps are tracked, with detailed tracking for VS Code, Cursor, Xcode, JetBrains IDEs (projects) and Safari, Chrome, Arc, Firefox, Edge, Brave (tabs).

## Productivity Features

- See deep work, context switches, and focus scores, with the ability to export to ChatGPT or Claude for AI-powered insights
- Pomodoro Focus blocks distracting apps during work sessions with built-in break cycles
- Set daily caps on distracting apps and get notified before you exceed them
- Set time goals and track streaks for motivation

## Availability

The current version is 1.0.6, and it's available for Windows (including ARM64/Snapdragon) and macOS.

## Target Users

The app is designed for developers, writers, designers, and students who spend long hours on computers and want clarity about their time usage without sacrificing privacy.
