## Overview

TimeFleX Solutions is developed by SDFE GmbH, based in Hürtgenwald, Germany. The platform provides a new approach to scheduling by covering all of an organization's scheduling coordination needs and integrating seamlessly with leading enterprise solutions such as Microsoft Exchange (Outlook) and HCL Notes.

## Core Philosophy

TimeFleX Solutions offers a comprehensive, modular platform designed to streamline and enhance business operations, providing all the tools needed for managing internal services, optimizing workspace utilization, or coordinating complex scheduling in one unified system.

## Key Modules

### Group Calendar
The TimeFleX Group Calendar transforms Outlook into professional scheduling software. Key features include:
- Saves time when creating and managing appointments
- Creates cross-department transparency in the availability and scheduling of people, rooms, and resources
- Works flawlessly with MS Outlook and MS Teams
- Integrates smoothly into existing infrastructure

### Desk Sharing
Optimizes workspace utilization with flexible desk booking options:
- Employees can reserve desks from anywhere
- Hybrid work support for flexible office presence
- Real-time desk availability visualization
- Space optimization for modern work environments

### Room Panel
Improves transparency in room management:
- Digital door signage for meeting rooms
- Large format displays to visualize meeting room bookings
- Manages MS Exchange meeting room bookings
- Real-time room availability status

### MeetingFinder
- Simplifies scheduling coordination
- Finds optimal meeting times across participants
- Integrates with existing calendar systems

### Service Requests
- Manages internal service requests
- Catering coordination
- Facility management integration

## Integration Capabilities
