## Overview

Vimcal is built for speed and efficiency in calendar management. Designed for power users who schedule many meetings, it emphasizes keyboard shortcuts, instant actions, and removing friction from scheduling workflows.

## Key Features

- Keyboard-first navigation (Vim-inspired)
- Instant meeting creation
- Multiple time zone display
- Smart booking links
- Calendar overlay for availability
- Mobile apps (iOS/Android)
- Integrations with Zoom, Google Meet, Teams
- Personal links with custom availability

## Speed Optimizations

- Create meetings in 2-3 keystrokes
- Instant time zone conversion
- Quick scheduling without leaving app
- Fast switching between calendars
- Command palette for all actions

## Use Cases

- Sales teams booking many calls
- Executives managing complex schedules
- Remote teams across time zones
- Anyone scheduling 5+ meetings daily

Free for individuals. Team plans start around $15-20/user/month.
