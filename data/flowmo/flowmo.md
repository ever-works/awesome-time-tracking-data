## Overview

Flowmo is the first app developed specifically for the Flowmodoro method, which is geared toward deep work and entering flow state. The app implements the Flowtime Technique, a flexible productivity method that focuses on work efficiency and diverges from fixed intervals like the Pomodoro Technique.

## What is Flowmodoro?

Flowmodoro is a flexible, productivity-boosting approach to time management that involves aligning your productivity with your personal rhythms and energy levels. Unlike the Pomodoro Technique which uses forced intervals, Flowmodoro allows users to work uninterrupted until they decide to take a break.

## How It Works

### Flexible Work Periods
- Work on a task without time limits
- Continue as long as you're in flow state
- Stop when concentration naturally wanes
- No forced interruptions during peak focus

### Smart Break Calculation
- Break duration based on work session length
- Typically 20% of work time
- Example: 90 minutes work = 18 minutes break
- Adaptive to individual needs

## Comparison with Pomodoro

### Pomodoro:
- Fixed 25-minute intervals
- Forced breaks every 25 minutes
- Can interrupt flow state
- Better for routine tasks

### Flowmodoro (Flowmo):
- Variable work periods
- Breaks when naturally needed
- Preserves flow state
- Better for deep work

## The Flowtime Technique Origins

The Flowtime Technique was invented by teacher and productivity expert Zoe Read-Bivens in 2016 to overcome shortcomings of the Pomodoro Technique, particularly for work requiring extended concentration.
