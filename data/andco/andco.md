## Overview

And.Co is a business management software suite designed specifically for freelancers. It consolidates time tracking, invoicing, proposals, contracts, and reporting into a single platform.

## Features

- Time tracking tools with detailed report generation
- Proposal creation
- Invoice generation (note: cloning invoices is not supported, requiring manual data entry)
- Contract management
- Prompt customer service accessible directly via their website

## Pricing

- Free plan: Limited features, 1 client only
- Pro Plan: $18 USD per month (unlimited features)
- 30-day money-back guarantee available

## Pros

- All-in-one feature set for freelancer business management
- Responsive customer service
- Prompt and helpful support via direct website messaging

## Cons

- Free plan is quite limited
- Some features require time-consuming setup
- Cannot clone invoices, resulting in extra data entry
