## Overview

Motion is an AI-powered productivity tool that combines calendar management, task management, and project planning into a single intelligent system. Its defining feature is automatic time blocking—Motion analyzes your tasks, deadlines, and calendar to automatically schedule when you should work on each item, then dynamically reschedules everything when priorities change.

## How Motion Works

### Input
You provide Motion with:
- **Tasks**: What needs to be done
- **Deadlines**: When tasks must be completed
- **Duration estimates**: How long each task takes
- **Priority levels**: Relative importance
- **Calendar availability**: Existing meetings and commitments
- **Work hours**: When you're available to work

### AI Scheduling Engine
Motion's algorithm:
1. Analyzes all tasks and their constraints
2. Calculates optimal scheduling to meet all deadlines
3. Auto-books tasks into available calendar slots
4. Respects your energy patterns (if configured)
5. Leaves buffer time between items
6. Groups similar tasks when beneficial

### Dynamic Rescheduling
When something changes (new task, meeting added, delay occurs):
- Motion instantly recalculates the entire schedule
- Automatically moves tasks to maintain feasibility
- Alerts you if deadlines become impossible
- Suggests what to delegate or drop
- Updates your calendar in real-time
