## Overview

Auto-generated timesheets for healthcare use AI to create review-ready timesheets for physicians in under 30 seconds, addressing the administrative burden that contributes to physician burnout.

## How It Works

**Automatic Data Collection**
Systems collect time data from:
- EMR/EHR systems
- Calendar appointments
- Email activity
- Hospital systems
- Call logs

**AI Validation**
Automatically validates against:
- Contract terms
- Compliance requirements
- Organizational policies
- Payment rules

**One-Click Submission**
Physicians review and submit in under 30 seconds.
