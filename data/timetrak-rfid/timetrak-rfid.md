## Overview

TimeTrak is an enterprise-grade time and attendance system offering RFID proximity card readers, fingerprint biometric terminals, and advanced job costing features for large organizations in manufacturing, construction, and industrial sectors.

## Features

- **RFID Proximity Cards**: Touchless time clock using HID proximity badges
- **Biometric Options**: Fingerprint and facial recognition terminals available
- **Job Costing**: Track time by job, task, or cost center for accurate project costing
- **Multiple Sites**: Manage time tracking across multiple locations from central dashboard
- **Offline Capability**: Terminals continue tracking even without network connection
- **Automated Calculations**: Automatic overtime, holiday pay, and shift differential calculations
- **Badge Integration**: Works with existing HID building access badges
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive reports on labor costs, attendance, and project time
- **Payroll Export**: Direct integration with major payroll providers
- **Compliance Tracking**: Monitor compliance with labor regulations and union rules
