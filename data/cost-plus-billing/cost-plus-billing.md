## Overview

Cost-Plus Billing charges clients based on actual costs incurred plus an agreed-upon margin, making accurate time tracking essential for both billing and profitability.

## Common Structures

**Cost Plus Fixed Fee (CPFF)**:
- Reimburse all costs
- Add fixed dollar amount
- Example: Costs + $50,000

**Cost Plus Percentage (CPP)**:
- Reimburse all costs
- Add percentage markup
- Example: Costs × 1.15 (15% markup)

**Cost Plus Incentive Fee (CPIF)**:
- Base fee + performance bonus
- Rewards efficiency
- Common in government contracts

## Time Tracking Requirements

### Accuracy Essential
- Daily time entry
- Proper project allocation
- Contemporaneous documentation
- Audit trail maintenance

### Cost Justification
- Fully loaded rates documented
- Overhead calculations transparent
- Expenses properly allocated
- Regular reporting to client
