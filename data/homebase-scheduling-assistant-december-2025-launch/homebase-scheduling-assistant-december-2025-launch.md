## Launch Details

In December 2025, Homebase introduced Scheduling Assistant, a new AI-powered feature that automatically optimizes shift creation, resolves scheduling conflicts, and reduces manual scheduling work while keeping teams aligned.

## Key Capabilities

**AI-Optimized Shift Creation:**
- Analyzes historical staffing patterns
- Considers employee availability and preferences
- Balances workload distribution fairly
- Accounts for labor budget constraints
- Predicts optimal staffing levels by time period

**Automated Conflict Resolution:**
- Identifies scheduling conflicts before publishing
- Suggests alternative arrangements
- Flags availability violations
- Detects potential overtime issues
- Prevents double-booking

**Intelligent Recommendations:**
- Proposes schedule improvements
- Identifies coverage gaps
- Suggests shift adjustments
- Optimizes for labor cost
- Maintains fairness in shift distribution
