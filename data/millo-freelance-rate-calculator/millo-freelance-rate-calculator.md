## Overview

Millo's Freelance Rate Calculator is a comprehensive tool that helps freelancers calculate rates across multiple pricing models: project, hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly. It provides a complete financial picture for sustainable freelance pricing.

## Multiple Rate Formats

### Hourly Rate
Target average hourly fee for hourly billing

### Day Rate
Target average daily rate for day-based contracts

### Per-Project Rate
Target average cost for each client project

### Weekly Target
Target average weekly income goals

### Monthly Target
Target average monthly income goals

## Comprehensive Factors

### Taxes (15-17%)
Accounts for freelance tax obligations that typically consume 15-17% of total annual revenue

### Work Hours
- Hours per day you want to work
- Days per week you'll be working
- Weeks per year (accounting for vacation)

### Business Expenses
- Software subscriptions
- Equipment and tools
- Marketing costs
- Professional development
- Insurance
- Office space

### Platform Fees
- Freelance platform commissions
- Credit card processing fees
- Payment gateway costs
- Other transaction fees
