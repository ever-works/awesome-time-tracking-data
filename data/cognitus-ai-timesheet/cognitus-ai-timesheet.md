## Overview

Cognitus AI Timesheet is an SAP BTP-based, mobile-ready timesheet application that offers AI-driven workforce time management with comprehensive visibility and seamless integration with SAP Cloud ERP and third-party payroll tools.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Automation**: Automates project-based time tracking with smart alerts for missing entries or exceeded hours
- **Grid-Based Interface**: Intuitive interface for efficient time entry with one-click copying from prior weeks
- **Microsoft Teams Integration**: DCAA-compliant AI-powered Timesheet agent accessible directly in Microsoft Teams
- **Automatic Validation**: AI-guided input with automated validations for hours, comments, and completeness
- **Compliance**: DCAA & DFARS compliance checks for labor charging and security requirements
- **Mobile Access**: Employees can manage timesheets anytime, anywhere with flexible mobile access
- **Proactive Alerts**: Automatic alerts for missing or inconsistent entries
- **Manager Visibility**: End-to-end visibility into hours, codes, and comments with automated alerts

## Target Users

Specifically designed for government contractors and businesses using SAP, particularly those requiring DCAA compliance and strict audit readiness.
