## Overview

symplr Workforce is an enterprise-grade workforce management solution designed specifically for healthcare organizations, helping hospitals and health systems optimize scheduling, time tracking, and labor cost management while ensuring compliance with healthcare regulations.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Scheduling**: Machine learning recommendations for optimal staff distribution and workload balancing
- **Predictive Staffing**: Forecast staffing needs up to 60 days in advance with 96% accuracy
- **Time & Attendance**: Accurate time capture with automated timesheet generation
- **Shift Differentials**: Automatically calculate complex healthcare shift premiums and pay rules
- **Float Pool Management**: Optimize utilization of float and pool staff across departments
- **Credential Tracking**: Ensure staff assignments match required certifications and credentials
- **Labor Budget Management**: Real-time tracking of labor costs against budgets
- **Self-Scheduling**: Empower staff to select shifts that match their preferences and availability
- **Overtime Control**: Proactive alerts and automation to minimize unnecessary overtime
- **Mobile Access**: Staff and managers can manage schedules from mobile devices
- **Payroll Integration**: Export verified hours with all differentials to payroll systems
- **Compliance**: Support for FMLA, ACA, union rules, and other healthcare-specific regulations

## Benefits

Helps reduce last-minute schedule changes, distribute workloads fairly, and improve staff satisfaction while controlling labor costs in complex healthcare environments.
