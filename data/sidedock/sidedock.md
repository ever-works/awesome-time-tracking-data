## Overview

SideDock is a powerful productivity Chrome extension that adds a sleek side panel to your browser, keeping essential tools accessible without tab-switching.

## Features

- **Built-in Task Manager**: Organize your day with a lightweight yet powerful task manager, add priorities, labels, and track progress without opening another app
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Boost productivity with focused work sessions and intentional breaks using the integrated Pomodoro technique
- **StayFocused Website Blocker**: Eliminate digital distractions by blocking time-wasting websites during focus sessions
- **AI Tools Access**: Quick access to ChatGPT and other AI assistants directly from the sidebar
- **Side-by-Side Workflow**: Keep YouTube videos, notes, and tasks open alongside your current work

## Use Cases

Ideal for knowledge workers, students, and anyone who wants to maintain focus while browsing, combining time management and distraction blocking in a single browser extension.

## Pricing

Free to install from the Chrome Web Store with premium features available.
