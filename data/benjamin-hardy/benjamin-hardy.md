## Overview

Benjamin Hardy is an organizational psychologist and bestselling author who writes extensively on productivity, personal development, and the psychology of achieving goals.

## Notable Works

- **Willpower Doesn't Work** - Challenges conventional wisdom about willpower, arguing that environment design is more effective than relying on self-control
- **Personality Isn't Permanent** - Explores how personality is shaped through decisions and environment rather than being fixed
- **Life Is In The Transitions** - Contributes insights on navigating life's major changes

## Key Philosophy

Hardy's approach focuses on environmental design over willpower. He advocates that sustainable productivity comes from shaping your environment to support desired behaviors rather than relying on self-control alone.
