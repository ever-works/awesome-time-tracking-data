## Overview

Deputy's AI-Powered Auto-Scheduling uses artificial intelligence to craft optimal schedules for shift-based businesses. The system analyzes multiple data points to create schedules that balance labor costs, customer demand, and employee preferences.

## How It Works

### AI Analysis
The system considers:
- Historical sales and traffic patterns
- Labor demand forecasts
- Employee availability and preferences
- Skills and certifications required
- Labor budget constraints
- Compliance rules (breaks, overtime, etc.)

### Automated Schedule Creation
Auto-Scheduling automatically:
- Creates fully costed schedules in minutes
- Assigns shifts to qualified employees
- Balances workload across the team
- Reduces unnecessary wage costs
- Ensures adequate staffing levels

### Employee Notification
Once schedules are created, Deputy notifies staff automatically when they're working via push notifications and SMS.
