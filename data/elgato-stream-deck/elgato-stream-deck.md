## Overview

The Elgato Stream Deck is a customizable control panel with physical LCD keys that can transform time tracking into a one-button operation. While originally designed for streamers, it has become popular among productivity users for automating workflows.

## Time Tracking Applications

- **Timebox & Track Plugin**: Transforms Stream Deck into a powerful productivity companion with buttons that become live countdown timers
- **Toggl Integration**: Press any Toggl button to start tracking time, with the button turning red and showing current tracking duration
- **Clockify Plugin**: Track, start and stop Clockify timers directly from Stream Deck keys
- **One-Click Tracking**: No apps to open, no forms to fill out—just press a button and focus on work

## Productivity Benefits

- **Instant Actions**: Trigger complex automations with a single button press
- **Visual Feedback**: LCD keys show current status and timer duration in real-time
- **Custom Layouts**: Create pages for different projects or clients
- **Multi-Action Buttons**: Chain multiple actions together (start timer + open app + launch document)

## Models & Pricing

Stream Deck models range from $53 to $209. The Stream Deck Neo ($99.99, 8 keys) is recommended for most productivity users as a starting point.
