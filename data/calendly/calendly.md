## Overview

Calendly automates meeting scheduling by providing booking links where invitees can see your availability and select times that work for both parties. This eliminates email ping-pong and saves 5-10 minutes per meeting scheduled.

## Key Features

- Personal booking links
- Calendar integration (Google, Outlook, iCloud)
- Customizable availability rules
- Team scheduling (round robin, collective)
- Buffer time between meetings
- Meeting types with different durations
- Payment collection
- Custom confirmation pages
- Automated reminders
- Zoom/Teams integration

## Use Cases

- Sales demos and consultations
- Client meetings
- Recruitment interviews
- Office hours
- Service provider appointments
