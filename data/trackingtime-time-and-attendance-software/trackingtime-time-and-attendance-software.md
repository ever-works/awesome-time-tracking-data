# TrackingTime – Time and Attendance Software

**Description**

TrackingTime is a time and attendance platform for hybrid and remote teams that records team members’ presence, work hours, and clock-ins in one place. It provides real-time visibility into who is working and when, helping HR and operations teams manage attendance, shifts, and workforce allocation without manual spreadsheets.

---

## Features

- **Automated attendance tracking**  
  - Captures attendance instantly with reliable clock-ins and automated logs.  
  - Records clock-ins and clock-outs in real time with minimal manual input.  
  - Centralized, automated data collection for attendance records.

- **Real-time workforce visibility**  
  - Shows who is present, late, or absent in real time.  
  - Provides up-to-the-minute insights across the organization.  
  - Helps maintain daily operational flow and staffing alignment.

- **Compliance-ready records**  
  - Maintains accurate, audit-friendly attendance histories.  
  - Supports labor regulation compliance and payroll accuracy.

- **Attendance tracking across work models**  
  - Monitors attendance for onsite, hybrid, and fully remote teams.  
  - Tracks presence, late arrivals, early departures, and attendance patterns.  
  - Offers organization-wide visibility into attendance behavior.

- **Clock-in / clock-out tools**  
  - Employees can clock in and out via desktop, mobile, web, or integrated apps.  
  - Captures precise timestamps without manual entry.  
  - Enforces shift rules and aligns logs with the correct employee and schedule.  
  - Managers can review, edit, and approve attendance entries.

- **Employee attendance histories**  
  - Stores detailed attendance records for each employee.  
  - Enables analysis of trends and recurring attendance issues.  
  - Supports performance evaluations and staffing decisions with data.  
  - Helps ensure alignment with attendance policies.

- **Shift and presence management**  
  - Manages presence, shifts, and attendance records from a single platform.  
  - Automates clock-ins and scheduling to reduce admin work.

- **No-spreadsheet attendance management**  
  - Replaces manual spreadsheets with a centralized system.  
  - Provides cleaner, more reliable attendance records.  
  - Reduces errors through automated logging and real-time updates.
