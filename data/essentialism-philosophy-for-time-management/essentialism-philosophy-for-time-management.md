## Overview

Essentialism is the disciplined pursuit of less but better through systematic identification and elimination of non-essentials.

## Core Principles

### Explore
- Discern the vital few from the trivial many
- Ask: What is essential?

### Eliminate
- Cut out everything that isn't essential
- Say no to good opportunities to say yes to great ones

### Execute
- Make execution effortless
- Remove obstacles
- Create systems

## Time Application

- 90% rule: If not 9/10 "yes", it's a "no"
- Graceful "no" repertoire
- Essential intent statements
- Routine and ritual for essentials

## Benefits

- Less but better results
- Regain control of choices
- Escape overwhelm
- Higher impact per hour
