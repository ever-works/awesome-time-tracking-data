## Overview

Atomic Habits by James Clear explores how small changes can lead to remarkable results, introducing the concept of "atomic habits" - tiny daily routines that yield significant improvements over time.

## Key Concepts

- **The 1% Rule**: Small improvements compound into remarkable results
- **Four Laws of Behavior Change**: Make it obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying
- **Habit Stacking**: Link new habits to existing routines
- **Identity-Based Habits**: Focus on who you want to become, not what you want to achieve
- **Environment Design**: Shape your environment to make good habits easier
- **The Plateau of Latent Potential**: Understanding why habits seem ineffective before breakthrough

## Time Management Relevance

The book emphasizes that effective time management isn't about dramatic overhauls but about small, consistent improvements in daily routines that compound over time.
