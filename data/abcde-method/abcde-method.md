## Overview

The ABCDE Method is a powerful priority-setting technique created by Brian Tracy that helps identify which tasks are truly important versus mere distractions.

## The Five Categories

### A - Must Do
Important tasks with serious consequences if not completed. Top priorities that should be tackled first.

### B - Should Do
Still important tasks but with less severe consequences. These come after your A tasks.

### C - Nice to Do
Tasks that have mild consequences and can be done after higher priorities.

### D - Delegate
Tasks that can be passed on to someone else more suited to complete them.

### E - Eliminate
Trivial tasks that provide little to no value and can be eliminated.

## Implementation Process

1. **List All Tasks**: Write everything you need to do for the coming day
2. **Categorize**: Assign A, B, C, D, or E to each task
3. **Prioritize Within Categories**: Number items (A-1, A-2, etc.)
4. **Start with A-1**: Begin immediately on highest priority task
5. **Complete Before Moving**: Finish A-1 before starting A-2
6. **Discipline**: Stay focused on current priority

## Key Principle

The key to making this method work is to discipline yourself to start immediately on your "A-1" task and stay at it until it is complete.

## Benefits

- Clear task prioritization
- Focus on high-impact activities
- Reduced time on low-value tasks
- Better decision-making about task importance
- Increased productivity and efficiency
