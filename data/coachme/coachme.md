## Overview

Coach.me is a habit tracking platform that stands apart from competitors by offering integrated professional coaching services alongside traditional habit tracking features.

## Features

- Habit tracking and streak monitoring
- Access to certified habit coaches
- Personalized coaching advice and motivation
- Customizable reminders and notifications
- Community support and accountability
- Progress visualization and analytics
- Integration with Google Calendar
- Integration with Slack
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Social features for group challenges
- Journal entries for habit reflections

## Coaching Integration

Coach.me's integrated coaching feature sets it apart from other habit trackers. Users can connect with certified habit coaches for personalized advice and motivation, providing human support beyond automated reminders.
