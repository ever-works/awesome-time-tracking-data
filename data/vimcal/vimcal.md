## Overview

Vimcal is the world's fastest calendar, beautifully designed for people who have too many meetings. Built around speed and efficiency, it reduces the number of steps needed for calendar tasks to half or fewer compared to traditional calendars.

## Features

- **Instant Response Times**: Sub-100ms response times for all calendar operations
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Comprehensive shortcuts for every action to maximize speed
- **Drag-to-Share Availability**: Quickly share available time slots by dragging across the calendar
- **Time Zone Management**: See overlapping work hours with anyone in any time zone
- **Smart Scheduling**: 'Find a Time' feature automatically suggests optimal meeting slots
- **Unified Calendar View**: See all Outlook and Google calendars in one view
- **AI-Powered Features**: Meeting suggestions and participant context
- **Focus Mode**: Built-in pomodoro timer and ambient sounds
- **Social Profiles**: View social profiles and company information for meeting participants

## Vimcal EA

Vimcal EA is the first calendar built specifically for Executive Assistants:
- Reduces proposing times with holds from 77 clicks to 8 clicks
- Customize time zones and preferences for every executive you support
- All holds disappear automatically when meetings are confirmed
