## Overview

GoodNotes is a digital handwriting and note-taking app designed primarily for iPad with Apple Pencil support. It allows users to create and write in digital notebooks, making it a popular platform for digital bullet journaling.

## Features

- Handwriting input using Apple Pencil with realistic pen feel
- Digital bullet journal templates for daily logs, monthly logs, habit trackers, and more
- Searchable handwritten notes across all notebooks
- Cloud-synced notes that cannot be lost
- Unlimited pages without the physical constraints of a paper notebook
- Easy reorganization of notebook content
- Supports various pen styles, highlighters, and shape tools
- PDF annotation and import capabilities
- Multiple notebook organization with folder structures

## Use Cases

- Digital bullet journaling for users who prefer the analog feel of handwriting
- Paperless planning and task management
- Meeting notes with the convenience of searchability
- Creative journaling and mood tracking
- Habit tracking and goal planning

## Pricing

GoodNotes offers a free tier with limited notebooks and a premium subscription for full access to all features, unlimited notebooks, and cross-device sync.
