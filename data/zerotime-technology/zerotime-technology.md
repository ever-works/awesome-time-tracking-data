## Overview

ZeroTime is Deltek Replicon's proprietary AI-powered time tracking technology that automatically captures work activity across multiple applications, eliminating the need for manual timers while providing accurate billable and payable hour records for enterprise organizations.

## How ZeroTime Works

### Automatic Activity Capture
- Monitors work across multiple applications automatically
- Tracks time spent in project management tools, communication platforms, and business applications
- Records activity without requiring start/stop timers
- Operates invisibly in the background

### AI-Based Classification
- Machine learning categorizes activities by project and task
- Intelligently assigns time to appropriate billing codes
- Distinguishes billable from non-billable work
- Learns from user patterns and corrections

### Timesheet Generation
- Automatically populates timesheets with captured data
- Pre-categorizes entries for user review
- Enables quick approval rather than manual entry
- Maintains compliance with labor regulations
