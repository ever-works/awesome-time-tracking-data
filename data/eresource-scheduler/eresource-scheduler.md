## Overview

eResource Scheduler is a cloud-based resource management and time tracking platform that helps organizations optimize how they allocate and schedule staff across projects. It combines visual scheduling tools with time tracking to give teams insight into capacity, utilization, and project costs.

## Features

- Visual resource scheduling with drag-and-drop interface
- Time tracking integrated with scheduled bookings
- Real-time availability views across the team
- Capacity planning and utilization reporting
- Clash detection to prevent over-allocation
- Leave and absence management
- Custom reporting and analytics
- Multi-user access with role-based permissions
