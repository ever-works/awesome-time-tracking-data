## Overview

Zoho Projects includes robust time tracking functionality designed to help teams track work hours, generate timesheets, and manage project billing. The platform combines project management with precise time tracking to provide insights into productivity and project profitability.

## Time Tracking Features

- **Built-In Timers** - Start/stop timers for tasks with one click
- **Manual Time Entry** - Add time logs for completed work
- **Timesheet Management** - Generate and approve weekly or monthly timesheets
- **Billable Hours Tracking** - Distinguish between billable and non-billable time
- **Budget Alerts** - Receive notifications when approaching time budgets
- **User Logs** - Track who worked on what and for how long
- **Custom Time Fields** - Add estimated vs actual time to tasks
- **Mobile Time Tracking** - Log hours from iOS and Android apps

## Integration & Reporting

- **Zoho Invoice Integration** - Convert tracked time directly into invoices
- **Comprehensive Reports** - Analyze time by project, task, user, or date range
- **Gantt Charts** - Visualize project timelines with actual vs estimated time
- **Productivity Analysis** - Identify bottlenecks and optimize workflows
- **Export Capabilities** - Download time data in multiple formats
