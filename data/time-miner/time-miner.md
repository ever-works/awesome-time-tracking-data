## Overview

Time Miner is retroactive timekeeping software for lawyers designed to recover billable hours that may have been missed. Unlike traditional time tracking that requires manual timers, it scans past communications in emails, calls, and texts to create billable time entries automatically.

The platform integrates with tools lawyers already use, including Outlook, Gmail, RingCentral, Dialpad, and MyCase, enabling tracking and categorization of all activities and automatic generation of detailed billable hour reports.

## Features

- Add client emails and phone numbers to track billable hours for communications with clients via email and phone
- Scan past communications (emails, calls, texts) to retroactively create billable time entries
- Generate detailed billing entries including call duration, contact details, and timestamps
- Sync billed entries with legal practice management or billing systems for accurate invoicing
- Integrate with Outlook, Gmail, RingCentral, Dialpad, and MyCase
- Track and categorize all communication activities automatically
- Create detailed billable hour reports

## Use Cases

- Solo practitioners who need a simple way to recover missed billable time
- Lawyers who work across multiple communication channels and forget to start timers
- Firms requiring retroactive billing entry generation from email and phone records

## Best For

Solo law practitioners who want a simple time tracking tool focused on capturing missed billable hours from past communications.
