## Overview

Tactiq is a Chrome extension that transforms online meetings by providing live, speaker-identified transcriptions without recording audio, ensuring privacy and ease of use. The tool is powered by GPT-4 for ultra-accurate AI meeting summaries.

## Key Features

- Live, in-meeting transcriptions with speaker identification
- Does not record or store audio from meetings - transcript happens in real-time and is saved as text
- One-click AI actions for generating summaries, action items, and project updates
- Custom AI prompts that can be saved and reused in all future meetings
- Supports over 60 languages
- Integrates seamlessly with popular meeting platforms and productivity tools

## Time-Saving Benefits

- Ask AI questions and generate meeting insights in seconds
- Spend less time reviewing messy notes - everything is perfectly organized and accessible
- Eliminate manual work of summarizing meetings and creating action items
- Save hours each day with automated meeting documentation

## Platform Support

- Google Meet

- Microsoft Teams
