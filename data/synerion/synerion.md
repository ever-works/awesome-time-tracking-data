## Overview

Synerion is workforce management software with integrated time and attendance features that simplify time tracking. It automates time tracking and timesheets using barcode badges, magnetic stripe cards, biometrics, facial recognition, and fobs, all through the cloud and mobile devices.

## Features

- Variety of modern time punch verification methods (facial recognition, fobs, barcode badges, magnetic stripe cards, biometrics)
- Automated scheduling processes
- Third-party app integrations
- Cloud and mobile device access
- Tracking of day-to-day hours, vacation time, on-call time, sick days
- Payroll integration and labor law compliance support

## Pricing

Synerion starts at $2 per user per month. Free trial available only upon special request.

## Drawbacks

- Free trial only available upon special request

## Use Cases

Ideal for businesses seeking to eliminate paper timesheets, automate attendance tracking, improve payroll accuracy, and ensure compliance with labor laws through comprehensive workforce management.
