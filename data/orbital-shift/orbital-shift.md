## Overview

Orbital Shift's staff scheduling software makes it simple to create employee schedules, track employee hours, allow shift swaps, and control labor costs.

## Features

- **Employee Scheduling** - Create and manage employee schedules with drag-and-drop simplicity
- **Time Clock** - Built-in online time clock for employees to track their work hours
- **Shift Swapping** - Allow employees to request shift swaps with manager approval workflows
- **Labor Cost Control** - Monitor and optimize labor costs in real-time
- **Mobile Access** - Access schedules and time tracking from mobile devices
- **Reporting** - Generate reports on labor costs, hours worked, and scheduling metrics

## Use Cases

Ideal for businesses with hourly workers including retail, hospitality, healthcare, and service industries.
