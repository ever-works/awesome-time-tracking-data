## Overview

SimplePractice is a comprehensive EHR platform designed specifically for behavioral health professionals that integrates billing, scheduling, time tracking, and record-keeping functionalities within a secure, HIPAA-compliant dashboard.

## Features

- Appointment scheduling and calendar management
- Client portal for secure communication
- Telehealth video sessions
- Electronic billing and invoicing
- Insurance claim filing
- Credit card processing
- Clinical note templates
- Treatment plan documentation
- Progress notes and assessments
- Client intake forms
- Automated appointment reminders
- Group session management
- Customizable documentation templates
- Client messaging
- File storage and sharing

## HIPAA Compliance

SimplePractice provides:
- Encrypted data storage and transmission
- Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
- Secure client communications
- Audit logs and access controls
- Compliant video conferencing

## Time Tracking and Billing

Enables behavioral health practitioners to:
- Track billable session time
- Generate invoices from appointments
- Process insurance claims
- Accept online payments
- Track outstanding balances
- Generate financial reports

## Ideal For

- Therapists and counselors
- Psychologists
- Social workers
- Marriage and family therapists
- Mental health counselors
- Group practice owners
- Solo practitioners

## Platform Understanding

SimplePractice understands mental health practice operations at a depth that general medical platforms simply do not match, with workflows specifically designed for behavioral health.

## Pricing

Starts at $29 per month for solo practitioners. Higher tiers available for group practices with additional features and user seats.
