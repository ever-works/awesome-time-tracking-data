## Overview

Life Note is an AI journaling app that lets you journal with history's greatest minds, getting personalized wisdom from Steve Jobs, Aristotle, Marcus Aurelius, and more. Available free on iOS, Android, and Web, it combines evidence-based journaling practices with AI mentorship.

## Core Philosophy

Life Note's philosophy combines **wisdom + reflection + agency**. It's described as a wisdom engine designed to help you live an aligned, meaningful life by connecting ancient wisdom with modern self-improvement practices.

## Key Features

**Mentor Intelligence**
Personas inspired by 1,000 great minds across 20+ disciplines provide personalized guidance based on your journal entries and goals.

**Goal Awareness**
The AI reads your goals, recognizes patterns, and helps you stay aligned with what matters most.

**Weekly Reflection Engine**
Every Sunday, receive a mentor letter summarizing your progress, insights, and areas for growth.

**End-to-End Encryption**
Utilizes AES-GCM encryption — the same standard used by governments — meaning even Life Note's own team cannot read your entries.

**Personalized Accountability**
AI journaling enhances goal achievement through:
- Asking clarifying questions
- Identifying patterns blocking progress
- Providing encouragement without judgment
- Adapting to your unique journey
