## Overview

WorkTime provides HIPAA-compliant employee monitoring and time tracking designed for healthcare organizations. It tracks staff productivity and time without accessing patient data or violating HIPAA regulations.

## Features

- HIPAA-safe monitoring (no access to PHI or EHR systems)
- Non-intrusive time and productivity tracking
- Application and website usage monitoring (healthcare apps excluded)
- Active vs idle time detection
- Attendance tracking
- Productivity analytics
- Department and shift reporting
- No keystroke logging
- No screen content capture in medical systems
- Secure data encryption
- Audit-ready compliance reports
- Privacy-focused monitoring approach
- Resource allocation insights
