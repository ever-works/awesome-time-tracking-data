## Overview

Geekland manufactures industrial-grade time clock kiosk hardware built on the Android platform. Their devices combine multiple biometric authentication methods with network connectivity and support for third-party time and attendance software from the Google Play Store.

## Features

- **Multi-Modal Authentication**: Facial recognition camera, fingerprint reader, NFC (13.56MHz), optional RFID (125KHz), and barcode scanner
- **Android Platform**: Runs Android OS with access to Play Store apps for time tracking
- **Power over Ethernet (PoE)**: Single-cable installation for both power and network connectivity
- **Industrial Grade**: IP65-rated weatherproof models available for harsh environments
- **Large Touchscreen Displays**: Available in 10.1-inch and other sizes for easy employee interaction
- **Wall Mounting**: Designed for secure wall installation in high-traffic areas
- **WiFi Support**: Wireless connectivity option alongside wired ethernet
- **SDK Availability**: Software development kit available for OEM customization
- **Multi-Language Support**: Interface available in multiple languages
- **Real-Time Sync**: Data synchronization with cloud-based time tracking platforms

## Hardware Models

- **GK-1066-TA**: 10.1-inch employee time clock kiosk with PoE and WiFi
- **GK-GD101E**: 10-inch Android time clock with PoE, barcode scanner, fingerprint reader, and RFID
- Additional models with varying screen sizes and feature combinations

## Integration

Geekland time clocks integrate with popular time and attendance software platforms through app installation from the Google Play Store or custom development using their SDK.

## Applications

Suitable for manufacturing facilities, warehouses, construction sites, healthcare facilities, and any environment requiring robust, reliable employee time tracking with multiple authentication options.
