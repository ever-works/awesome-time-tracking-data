## Overview

OpenSourceScrum is a collection of free, open-source tools and resources for teams practicing Scrum and agile methodologies, providing alternatives to commercial project management platforms without subscription fees.

## Features

- **Sprint Management**: Plan and track sprints with backlog prioritization
- **User Stories**: Create and manage user stories with acceptance criteria
- **Task Boards**: Visual boards for sprint and kanban workflows
- **Burndown Charts**: Track sprint progress and team velocity
- **Time Tracking**: Log time spent on stories and tasks
- **Backlog Grooming**: Organize and prioritize product backlog
- **Team Collaboration**: Comments, attachments, and team communication
- **Reporting**: Sprint reports, velocity charts, and team metrics
- **Customizable**: Full access to source code for customization
- **Self-Hosted**: Deploy on your own infrastructure for data control
- **No Vendor Lock-In**: Export data and migrate freely
- **Active Community**: Community support and contributions

## Open Source Benefits

- **Zero Licensing Costs**: Free to use for unlimited users and projects
- **Data Ownership**: Keep all project data on your own servers
- **Customization**: Modify and extend to meet specific needs
- **Transparency**: Review source code for security and functionality
- **Community Support**: Benefit from community knowledge and contributions

## Target Audience

Agile teams, startups, and organizations that prefer open-source solutions for philosophical, cost, or security reasons, or need extensive customization capabilities.

## Deployment

Typically self-hosted on organization's own servers or cloud infrastructure, with various open-source options available including web-based and desktop applications.

## Pricing

Completely free and open-source under various licenses (MIT, GPL, Apache). May incur infrastructure costs for self-hosting.
