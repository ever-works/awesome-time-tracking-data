## Overview

Toast Scheduling is an employee scheduling solution designed specifically for restaurants and integrated seamlessly with the Toast point-of-sale system. It uses POS data to help restaurants optimize labor scheduling based on actual sales patterns.

## Features

- **POS Integration** - Access sales data directly for informed scheduling decisions
- **Drag-and-Drop Scheduling** - Intuitive schedule builder with shift templates
- **Labor Cost Forecasting** - Predict labor costs based on scheduled shifts and sales forecasts
- **Sales-Based Scheduling** - Schedule staff based on historical sales patterns
- **Shift Templates** - Save and reuse common shift patterns
- **Mobile Access** - Employees view schedules and request time off via mobile app
- **Shift Swapping** - Employee-initiated shift trades with manager approval
- **Time-Off Requests** - Digital PTO request and approval system
- **Communication** - Send schedule updates and announcements to staff
- **Compliance Tracking** - Monitor compliance with labor laws and break requirements
- **Payroll Integration** - Export hours to payroll systems
- **Real-Time Updates** - Schedule changes sync instantly across all devices

## Benefits for Restaurants

By integrating scheduling with POS data, restaurants can match staffing levels to customer demand, reducing labor costs while maintaining service quality.
