## Overview

WorkMax TIME is a cloud-based construction time tracking platform that employs patented facial recognition technology to verify employee identity during clock-ins, preventing buddy punching and time theft on construction sites. The platform is a product of Foundation Software company.

## Key Features

### Facial Recognition Technology
- Patented facial recognition verifies identity during clock-ins, task changes, and clock-outs
- Automated photo comparison process with intelligent computing
- Alerts when images don't meet the required match percentage threshold
- Eliminates buddy punching and time fraud

### GPS & Location Verification
- Built-in GPS verification confirms worker locations during clock-in
- Virtual geofencing creates boundaries around jobsites
- Ensures workers are physically present when clocking in
- Location-verified time entries for accurate payroll

### Mobile Time Clock
- Workers clock in/out directly from mobile devices
- Spanish language support to reduce language barriers
- Simple PIN entry with 4-9 digits, no password needed
- Works on iOS and Android devices

### Integration & Automation
- Seamless integration with construction accounting, payroll, and HR solutions
- Eliminates manual data entry for payroll processing
- Real-time data sync to accounting systems
- Automated timesheet generation

## Security Features

- Advanced facial recognition prevents time theft
- GPS verification ensures on-site presence
- Geofencing technology restricts clock-ins to approved locations
- Secure cloud-based data storage

## Target Industries

Primarily designed for construction industry but also suitable for:
- Field service operations
- Mobile workforce management
- Any industry requiring verified time and attendance tracking
