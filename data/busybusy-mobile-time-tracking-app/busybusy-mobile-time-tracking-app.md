# busybusy Mobile Time Tracking App

**Category:** Mobile Time Tracking  
**Brand:** busybusy  
**Website:** https://busybusy.com/mobile-time-tracking-app/

## Overview
busybusy is a cloud-based mobile time tracking app designed for construction and field teams. Employees can clock in/out from their phones, track time by job and cost code, capture GPS locations, and sync hours to timesheets and payroll.

## Features
- **Mobile time tracking**
  - Employees clock in and out from Android or iOS phones and tablets.
  - Time tracked by job and cost code.
  - Hours synced into timesheets for payroll.

- **GPS-based tracking**
  - GPS time tracking to record locations with clock-in/clock-out events.
  - GPS monitoring to provide real-time insight into where employees are in the field.
  - Map-style view to see mobile crews’ locations (employee GPS view).

- **Progress and productivity tracking**
  - Track progress and work completed on jobs.
  - Associate work with specific employees and tasks.
  - Record how long each task takes for better analysis of productivity.

- **Crew and job oversight**
  - Accurately track mobile crews’ hours across multiple sites.
  - Gain transparency into which employees are on which job and for how long.

- **Access and deployment**
  - Cloud-based solution, accessible via mobile apps or web browser.
  - Supports Android and Apple (iOS) devices (phones and tablets).

- **Data for payroll and job costing**
  - Centralized time records that can be used for payroll processing.
  - Detailed time and task data suitable for analyzing job costs and estimating future work.
