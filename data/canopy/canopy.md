## Overview

Canopy is a cloud-based accounting practice management software with CRM, document management, a client portal app, workflow, payments, time and billing. Founded in 2014 by Kurt Avarell, a Wall Street tax attorney, the platform was designed specifically for accounting and tax firms to streamline their entire practice workflow.

## Features

- **Time & Billing**: Track billable and non-billable hours, generate invoices, and collect payments faster
- **IRS Transcript Retrieval**: Access to IRS transcripts, forms, and resolution tools directly within the platform
- **Client Portal**: Secure document sharing and client communication
- **Workflow Automation**: Automate repetitive tasks and standardize processes
- **Document Management**: Centralized storage and organization of client documents
- **CRM Functionality**: Manage client relationships and engagement
- **Tax Resolution**: Specialized tools for tax resolution work
- **Invoice Management**: Send one-time and recurring invoices, including future-scheduled invoices
- **Payment Integration**: Integrated billing and payment options
- **AI-Enhanced Features**: Automate repetitive tasks with AI capabilities
- **QuickBooks Online Integration**: Seamless connection with accounting software
- **Mobile App**: Access your practice on the go
- **Reporting**: Track billable hours, revenue, and firm performance
