## Overview

Journyx is an enterprise-grade time tracking and project management platform that offers multiple time capture methods, including biometric time clock systems. It provides comprehensive solutions for organizations needing secure, accurate time tracking combined with project and resource management capabilities.

## Biometric Time Clock Features

### Secure Authentication
- Fingerprint recognition
- Facial recognition options
- Prevents buddy punching and time theft
- Unique biometric identifiers per employee
- Fast authentication (seconds)

### Time Clock Capabilities
- Physical time clock hardware options
- Web-based time clocks
- Mobile device time capture
- Badge/card readers
- PIN-based backup authentication

## Time Tracking Features

### Flexible Time Entry
