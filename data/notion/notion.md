## Overview

Notion is a powerful AI-assisted platform that helps manage projects, knowledge bases, and teams. Its AI agents perform time-consuming tasks, freeing users to focus on productive work.

## Features

- AI-assisted platform for content generation and information organization
- Email with automatic organization
- Calendar with built-in scheduling and project timelines
- Project roadmaps with timeline views
- Document organization and searchable databases
- Customizable Kanban-style boards with tailored data views
- Tag-based task tracking for status, priority, and type
- Onboarding guide generation from workspace content

## Pricing

- Free: 1 user
- Paid Monthly: $10 - $20 per user
- Paid Annually: $12 - $24 per user per month
- Enterprise pricing available

## Platform Availability

Desktop, Web, Android, iOS
