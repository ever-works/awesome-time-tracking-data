## Overview

Flocus is your aesthetic productivity dashboard featuring a Pomodoro timer, task management, and detailed productivity analytics.

## Features

- Beautiful, customizable aesthetic interface
- Pomodoro timer with history tracking
- Task and project management
- Daily, weekly, and monthly productivity statistics
- Focus session insights
- Ambient sounds for concentration
- Progress tracking and goal setting
- Calendar integration

## Pricing

Free tier available with basic features. Premium plans offer advanced analytics and customization options.
