## Overview

Parim is staff time tracking and shift scheduling software that allows you to track every shift down to the minute in real time and get full visibility of your team's work in only one place. It's particularly suited for businesses managing shift workers, field staff, and large-scale event staffing operations.

## Features

### Time Tracking
- Monitor employee work hours and attendance in real time
- Track every shift down to the minute
- Multiple clock-in methods:
  - Mobile app
  - SMS
  - Phone calls (for field workers without internet)
- Full visibility of team work in one place

### Geofencing Clock-In
- Ensures team starts shifts at the right place
- Optional automatic shift start when employees reach the site
- Location verification for field workers
- Prevents time theft with location-based validation

### Payroll & Compliance
- Set up hourly pay rates to shifts
- Automatic salary calculation based on worked hours
- Export timesheets in seconds
- Achieve compliance and accuracy
- Efficient payroll management

### Shift Scheduling
- Organize employee work schedules
- Track staff availability
- Prevent scheduling conflicts
- Assign staff with right skills and qualifications to right shifts
- Manage thousands of employees for large events

### Team Management
- Monitor shift coverage
- Find replacements quickly
- Track attendance
- Ensure proper staffing levels
- Skills and qualifications matching
