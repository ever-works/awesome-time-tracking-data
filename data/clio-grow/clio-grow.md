## Overview

Clio Grow is a legal CRM (Customer Relationship Management) platform designed specifically for law firms to better manage client relationships, automate intake processes, and convert more leads into clients.

## Key Features

- **Lead Management**: Track and manage potential clients from first contact through conversion
- **Client Intake Automation**: Streamline onboarding with automated workflows and forms
- **Online Intake Forms**: Allow prospective clients to submit information 24/7
- **E-Signatures**: Collect signatures on engagement letters and agreements electronically
- **Pipeline Management**: Visualize where leads are in your conversion process
- **Automated Follow-ups**: Set up email campaigns and reminders to nurture leads

## Integration with Clio Ecosystem

Clio Grow integrates seamlessly with Clio Manage, allowing firms to transition clients from lead capture through matter management and billing in one unified platform.

## Benefits for Law Firms

- Convert more leads into clients with streamlined intake
- Save time on administrative tasks with automation
- Improve client experience with professional, efficient onboarding
- Track marketing ROI by measuring lead sources and conversion rates
- Never miss a follow-up with automated reminders

## Best For

- Law firms of all sizes looking to grow their practice
- Firms handling high volumes of initial consultations
- Practice areas with standardized intake processes
- Firms wanting to improve conversion rates from leads to clients
