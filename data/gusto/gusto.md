## Overview

Gusto is a modern, user-friendly payroll and HR platform for small businesses that includes time tracking and attendance management alongside comprehensive payroll and benefits administration.

## Time Tracking Features

- Simple time clock for hourly employees
- Timesheet management
- GPS-enabled mobile app
- Automatic overtime calculation
- PTO tracking and accruals
- Direct integration with payroll

## Payroll & HR

- Full-service payroll processing
- Automatic tax filing
- Benefits administration (health, 401k)
- New hire onboarding
- Compliance assistance
- Employee self-service

## Benefits

- Extremely user-friendly interface
- Excellent customer support
- Transparent pricing
- Fast implementation
- Mobile-first design

## Target Users

- Small businesses (1-100 employees)
- Startups
- Companies wanting simple, modern HR/payroll

## Pricing

Transparent per-employee monthly pricing plus base fee.
