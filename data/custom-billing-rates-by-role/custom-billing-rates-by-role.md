## Overview

Custom Billing Rates by Role enable nuanced pricing that reflects the varying value and cost of different team members' time, supporting sophisticated billing models and maximizing revenue.

## Rate Structures

### Role-Based Rates
- Junior Developer: $100/hour
- Mid-Level Developer: $150/hour
- Senior Developer: $200/hour
- Architect: $250/hour
- Project Manager: $175/hour

### Skill-Based Rates
- General Consulting: $150/hour
- Technical Specialist: $200/hour
- Subject Matter Expert: $300/hour

### Client-Specific Rates
- Standard clients: Full rate
- Preferred partners: 10% discount
- Non-profit: Reduced rate
- Government: Contract rate

## Implementation

**System Configuration**:
- Define roles/levels
- Assign billing rates
- Map employees to roles
- Set client-specific overrides

**Auto-Application**:
- Employee tracks time
- System applies their rate
- Generates invoice
- No manual rate entry

## Benefits

**Revenue Optimization**:
- Charge appropriate value
- Reflect expertise levels
- Competitive pricing
- Margin protection

**Transparency**:
- Clear rate cards
- Predictable client costs
- Justifiable pricing

**Administration**:
- Automated calculations
- Reduced errors
- Faster invoicing
- Audit trail

## Common Patterns

**Blended Rates**:
- Mix of junior/senior work
- Average rate calculated
- Simpler client billing

**Task-Based Rates**:
- Rate varies by work type
- Same person, different tasks
- More granular pricing
