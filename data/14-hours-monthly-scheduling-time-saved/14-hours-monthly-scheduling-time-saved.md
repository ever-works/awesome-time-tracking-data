## Key Metric

7shifts scheduling platform saves managers an average of 14 hours per month on scheduling tasks, allowing them to focus on higher-value activities like team development and customer service.

## What Gets Automated

**Manual Tasks Eliminated:**
- Creating weekly/monthly schedules from scratch
- Checking employee availability across shifts
- Resolving scheduling conflicts manually
- Communicating schedule changes via multiple channels
- Tracking time-off requests
- Managing shift swaps and coverage requests
- Ensuring compliance with labor laws

**Automated Features:**
- Drag-and-drop schedule creation
- Auto-fill based on availability and qualifications
- Conflict detection and resolution suggestions
- One-click schedule publishing
- Automatic notifications to team members
- Self-service shift trading
- Labor cost optimization

## Time Breakdown

**14 hours/month =**
- ~3.5 hours per week
- ~30 minutes per day
- ~168 hours per year

**Redirected to:**
- Staff training and development
- Customer experience improvements
- Strategic planning
- Team relationship building
- Problem-solving and coaching

## ROI Impact

Beyond time savings:

- **Labor Cost Reduction**: Thousands saved annually through optimized scheduling
- **Reduced Errors**: Automated compliance prevents costly mistakes
- **Employee Satisfaction**: Self-service features improve work-life balance
- **Faster Communication**: Instant notifications replace phone calls/texts
