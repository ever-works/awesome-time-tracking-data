## Overview

Timelogger is a conveniently simple and easy-to-use time tracking app to record all your time and improve your productivity with a quick tap on your iPhone, Apple Watch, iPad, and Mac.

## Key Features

### Cross-Device Sync
- iCloud integration keeps data securely updated across all devices
- Seamless syncing between iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac
- Access your time data from any Apple device

### Quick Time Tracking
- One-tap time tracking for quick task logging
- Simple interface focused on ease of use
- Minimal friction to start and stop tracking

### Apple Watch Support
- Track time directly from your wrist
- Start and stop timers without pulling out your phone
- View active timers on your watch face

## Use Cases

- Freelancing and client work
- Repetitive daily routines
- Homemaking tasks
- Personal projects
- Any activity you want to track and control

## Platform

iOS (iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Mac)
