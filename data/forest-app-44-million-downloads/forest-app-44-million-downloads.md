## Overview

Forest, the gamified focus timer and phone addiction prevention app, has achieved 44 million downloads globally. This milestone demonstrates the massive scale of demand for tools that help people stay focused and manage smartphone distractions.

## What Forest Does

Forest helps users stay focused by:
- Planting virtual trees that grow during focus sessions
- Killing the tree if users leave the app before time expires
- Building a virtual forest representing accumulated focus time
- Partnering with Trees for the Future to plant real trees
