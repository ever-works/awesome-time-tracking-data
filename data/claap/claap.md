## Overview

Claap is the first async video collaboration platform that helps hybrid teams to make faster and better decisions. Share updates, get feedback and make decisions without the need to be in the same room, at the same time. Claap combines meeting recording, screen recording, and a video wiki into a single tool.

## Key Features & Capabilities

### AI Assistants

Claap offers a suite of AI assistants designed to level up sales performance:
- Automatically records and transcribes meetings in 99 languages
- Generates personalized summaries using customizable templates
- Crafts follow-up emails
- Automates administrative tasks

### Async Video Collaboration

- Record quick videos of your screen
- Get contextual feedback
- Make decisions asynchronously
- Share updates without scheduling meetings
- Collaborate across time zones effectively
