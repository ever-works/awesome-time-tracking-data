## Overview

Miranda is a time zone converter and meeting scheduler app designed for iOS and Mac that helps you visually find the best time to schedule international meetings and calls across different time zones.

## Platform Availability

- **iOS**: Requires iOS 15.0 or later
- **Mac**: Requires macOS 12.0 or later with Apple M1 chip
- **Apple Vision**: Requires visionOS 1.0 or later
- Works offline without internet connection

## Key Features

- Visual time zone coordination
- Interactive world map
- Scroll wheel for easy time adjustment
- Add meetings directly to calendar
- Share meeting times easily
- Dark and light mode support
- Fully offline functionality
- Minimalist interface design

## Design Philosophy

Inspired by Dieter Rams' design principles, Miranda offers a clean, minimalist interface that focuses on functionality and ease of use.

## How to Use

1. Add the time zones you need to coordinate
2. Use the scroll wheel to adjust times
3. View the corresponding times across all zones
4. Check the world map for visual reference
5. Add meetings to your calendar
6. Share meeting times with participants

## Use Cases

- Scheduling international conference calls
- Coordinating with remote team members
- Planning meetings across multiple time zones
- Checking time differences while traveling
- Managing global client relationships

## Benefits

- Visual, intuitive interface
- No internet required after setup
- Native iOS and Mac experience
- Quick time zone comparisons
- Easy calendar integration
