## Overview

Timing is an automatic time tracking app exclusively for macOS that saves time by automating time tracking as much as possible. It automatically tracks every app, document and website you use, including the full file path or URL.

## Key Features (2026)

### AI Summaries
New in 2026: AI summaries instantly reveal what you worked on and highlight key patterns in your time usage.

### Automatic Tracking
Timing automatically tracks:
- Every application you use
- Every document and file you work on (with full paths)
- Every website you visit (with complete URLs)
- Idle time when you're away from your Mac

### Productivity Scoring
Built-in productivity tracking allows you to:
- Assign productivity scores to projects
- Track how productivity improves over time
- Identify productive vs. unproductive patterns

### Privacy First
Tracked times are stored locally on your Mac and never leave your device unless you explicitly opt into iCloud sync.

### Idle Time Detection
Timing automatically detects when you stop using your Mac, letting you exclude idle time from your records.

## Benefits

### No Manual Timers
Unlike Toggl or Clockify, you never need to remember to start/stop timers.

### Complete Picture
Captures everything, including unexpected interruptions and context switches.

### Accurate Records
Retrospectively create timesheets from automatically tracked data.

### Productivity Insights
Identify time-wasting patterns and optimize work habits.

## Platform & Compatibility

Exclusively for macOS, taking full advantage of system-level APIs for comprehensive tracking.

## Pricing

Offered through subscription pricing or as a one-time purchase through SetApp. Free trial available to test the automatic tracking features.

## Comparison to Alternatives

### vs. RescueTime
Timing provides more detailed tracking and better project categorization.

### vs. Toggl
Timing is automatic; Toggl requires manual timer control.

### vs. Memtime
Similar automatic tracking but Timing has longer track record on Mac.
