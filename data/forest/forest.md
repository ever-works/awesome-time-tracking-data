## Overview

Forest is a gamified productivity and time tracking app that helps users stay focused by growing virtual trees. The unique approach combines focus timers with environmental impact, as successful focus sessions can contribute to planting real trees.

## Features

- **Virtual Tree Growing** - Plant a virtual tree that grows while you focus
- **Gamification** - Earn coins and unlock new tree species
- **Focus Timer** - Pomodoro-style focus sessions with customizable durations
- **Time Tracking** - Automatic tracking of focus time across tasks and projects
- **Phone Blocking** - Tree dies if you leave the app, discouraging phone use
- **Real Tree Planting** - Partner with Trees for the Future to plant real trees
- **Statistics** - View focus time statistics and productivity trends
- **Tags** - Categorize focus sessions by project or activity
- **Forest Timeline** - Visual timeline of your productivity forest
- **Team Forest** - Collaborate with friends or coworkers on group focus sessions

## How It Works

1. Set a focus time (e.g., 25 minutes)
2. Plant a virtual tree
3. Stay focused - leaving the app kills the tree
4. Complete the session to earn coins and add tree to your forest
5. Use coins to unlock species or plant real trees

## Use Cases

Ideal for students, remote workers, and anyone struggling with phone distraction who responds well to gamification and visual progress.
