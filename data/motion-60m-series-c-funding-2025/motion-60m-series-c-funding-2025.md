## Overview

In December 2025, Motion announced a $60 million Series C funding round at a $550 million valuation, representing one of the largest investments in the AI productivity space and validating the market for AI-powered scheduling and task management.

## Deal Details

### Funding Metrics

- **Amount Raised**: $60 million Series C
- **Valuation**: $550 million post-money
- **Date**: December 2025
- **Lead Investors**: Not publicly disclosed
- **Notable Addition**: Ashu Desai (former Y Combinator Visiting Partner) joined board

### Significance

- One of largest productivity software funding rounds of 2025
- Validates AI-first approach to time management
- Signals shift from manual to automated scheduling
- Demonstrates investor confidence in "AI Employee" concept

## What This Means for the Industry
