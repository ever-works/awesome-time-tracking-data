## Overview

Moxie is a comprehensive freelance business management platform that includes intelligent time tracking. Unlike standalone time trackers, Moxie connects your hours to your entire business workflow including invoices, projects, and client communications.

## Features

- Automatic time tracking with smart categorization
- Project and task management
- Client relationship management (CRM)
- Invoice generation from tracked time
- Proposal and contract creation
- Payment processing
- Expense tracking
- Financial reporting
- Client portal
- Automated workflows
- Email integration
- Calendar sync
- Mobile access

## Pricing

Subscription-based pricing with different tiers. Free trial available. Pricing designed for solo freelancers and small agencies.
