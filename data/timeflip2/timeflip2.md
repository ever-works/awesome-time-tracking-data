## Overview

TimeFlip2 is a 12-sided physical time tracking device (dodecahedron) that transforms time tracking into a simple, tactile experience by flipping the device to different faces assigned to specific tasks.

## How It Works

### Physical Device
- 12-sided dice-like tracker
- Each face represents a different task or activity
- Flip to start tracking that activity
- Bluetooth-enabled for automatic sync
- Battery-powered with long battery life

### Task Assignment
- Assign tasks, projects, or activities to each of 12 sides
- Use stickers, drawings, or labels to identify sides
- Customize via mobile or desktop app
- Change assignments as priorities shift

### Automatic Tracking
- No manual time entry required
- Simply flip to switch tasks
- Device automatically records time per face
- Real-time sync to connected apps
