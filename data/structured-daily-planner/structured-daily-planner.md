## Key Information

**What it is:**
Structured offers a clear visual timeline that brings your calendar, to-dos, routines, and habits together. It's a visual day planner designed to help you manage your daily life with clarity, and its timeline-based design minimizes overwhelm and keeps your schedule simple and actionable.

**Platform Availability:**
The app requires iOS 17.0 or later, Mac requires macOS 14.0 or later, Apple Vision requires visionOS 1.0 or later, and Apple Watch requires watchOS 10.0 or later. It's also available on Android and web platforms.

**User Base:**
Over 1.5 million active planners use the app, and they reached nearly 9 million users in the past year alone.

**Key Features:**
- A range of icons you can choose to assign to any given task on your schedule, creating a visually pleasing timeline
- AI to draft your schedule in seconds and automatically reschedule missed tasks
- Breaking down large projects into notes and sub-tasks
- Recurring events and syncing to the iOS Calendar and Reminders apps (with Pro subscription)

**Pricing:**
The app is free, but there is an optional subscription and an in-app purchase for a lifetime subscription.
