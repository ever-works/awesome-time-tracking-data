## Overview

TimeTrakGO is a biometric time clock system specifically designed to integrate seamlessly with Gusto payroll. It features facial recognition, geofencing, and mobile time tracking capabilities for both field and office workers.

## Features

- **Gusto Integration**: Direct integration with Gusto payroll system
- **Facial Recognition**: Biometric clock-in via facial recognition
- **Geofencing**: Location-based attendance verification
- **Mobile Time Clock**: Track time from smartphones and tablets
- **Photo Verification**: Capture photos at clock-in for verification
- **Job Costing**: Track time by project, client, or cost code
- **Break Tracking**: Automated break and lunch tracking
- **Overtime Alerts**: Real-time overtime notifications
- **PTO Management**: Track paid time off and vacation
- **Scheduling**: Employee scheduling and shift management
- **Reporting**: Detailed time and attendance reports
- **Offline Mode**: Works without internet connection

## Gusto Payroll Benefits

- Automatic time sync to Gusto
- Eliminates manual timesheet entry
- Reduces payroll processing time
- Ensures accurate wage calculations
- Simplifies tax filing and compliance

## Target Market

Small to medium-sized businesses using Gusto for payroll who need integrated time tracking with biometric features.
