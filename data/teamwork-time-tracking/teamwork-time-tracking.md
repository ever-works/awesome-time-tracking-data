## Overview

Teamwork.com provides integrated time tracking as part of its project management platform, allowing teams to manage tasks, track time, collaborate, and generate profitability reports all in one place.

## Time Tracking Features

### Time Formats
- Tracks time in hours and minutes by default
- Two viewable formats: Time format (2h, 4h 30m, 13m) or Hours format (2.00, 4.5, 0.217)
- Time tracking available on all plans
- Both timer-based and manual entry options

### Billable & Profitability
- Billable and non-billable hour designation
- Real-time profitability impact visibility
- Make adjustments to ensure spending the right time on the right tasks
- Billing and payment automation with robust time tracking mechanisms

### Reporting
- Time Tracking Report provides clear view of all logged time across projects
- Shows billable and non-billable hours breakdown
- Custom and out-of-the-box reports for tracking progress
- Skills-based resourcing and profitability reports (new in 2026)
