## Overview

WorkHours is a thoughtfully designed app for tracking time and calculating earnings across all your projects, both professional and personal, with seamless integration for iPhone and Apple Watch.

## Key Features

### Apple Watch Integration
- Control timers directly on your wrist without reaching for your phone
- Custom complications to see running timers and open the app right from your watch face
- Start and stop tracking from your Apple Watch

### Quick Time Tracking
- Clock in and select a task with a single swipe
- Create a Job Card for each project or task you want to track
- Use timers to log your work automatically
- Interactive Live Activities - clock in/out from your Lock Screen or Dynamic Island
- Lock Screen Widgets for quick status checks

### Earnings Tracking
- Track earnings with automatic overtime calculations
- Support for multiple pay rates
- Calculate tips and additional compensation

### Project Management
- Manage time across every facet of life - day jobs, side jobs, and personal tasks
- Create separate job cards for different projects
- Organize and categorize work efficiently

### User Experience
- Clean, intuitive interface
- Quick timer functionality
- Real-time sync across devices

## Use Cases

- Freelancers tracking multiple clients
- Hourly workers monitoring overtime
- Personal project time management
- Side hustle earnings calculation
