## Overview

Client Portal Time Visibility gives clients direct access to time tracking data for their projects, creating transparency, building trust, and reducing billing friction through self-service information access.

## Features

### Time Entry Viewing
- Real-time or daily updates
- Detailed task descriptions
- Team member identification
- Date and duration
- Billable vs. non-billable

### Reporting
- Hours by date range
- Hours by team member
- Hours by task/phase
- Budget consumption
- Burn rate trends

### Budget Tracking
- Total hours vs. budget
- Remaining capacity
- Overage warnings
- Forecast completion

## Access Control

**Permissions**:
- View-only (no editing)
- Project-specific access
- Date range restrictions
- Role-based visibility

**Security**:
- Secure login
- Encrypted transmission
- Audit logs
- Session timeouts

## Benefits

**For Clients**:
- Project visibility
- Real-time updates
- Budget monitoring
- Self-service access
- Reduced surprises

**For Service Providers**:
- Reduced status inquiries
- Fewer billing disputes
- Faster invoice approval
- Trust building
- Competitive differentiator

## Implementation Considerations

**Data Filtering**:
- Exclude internal notes
- Hide cost/rate data
- Show billable only
- Clean descriptions

**Branding**:
- White-label interface
- Custom domain
- Company logo
- Color scheme

## Common Concerns

**Too Much Detail**:
- Solution: Aggregate by day/phase
- Summarize minor tasks
- Focus on outcomes

**Junior Staff Visibility**:
- Solution: Show role, not name
- Emphasize quality control
- Explain team structure
