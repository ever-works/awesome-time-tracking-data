## Overview

The Tim Ferriss Show is one of the most popular business podcasts worldwide, hosted by author and angel investor Tim Ferriss. Each episode features in-depth interviews with exceptional people from different fields — athletes, investors, artists, CEOs — deconstructing their routines, habits, and productivity systems.

## Format

- Long-form interviews (1-3 hours) with guests
- Focus on actionable tactics, tools, and routines
- Regular segments on time management and life optimization
- Available on all major podcast platforms
