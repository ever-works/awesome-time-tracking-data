## Overview

Thomas Frank is a YouTuber, author, and the creator and main writer at College Info Geek, a platform dedicated to helping students build a remarkable college career and life beyond.

## College Info Geek

College Info Geek (collegeinfogeek.com) is a comprehensive resource focused on:
- Study techniques and strategies
- Time management for students
- Productivity systems and tools
- Career development
- Personal development and habit building
