## Overview

Accelo excels at quote-to-cash management for agencies focused on sales and delivery integration. Accelo positions itself as the complete client work management platform, handling everything from prospect identification through final payment collection.

## Features

### Time Tracking

- **Web Timer**: Track time in browser with project and task selection, notes, and descriptions
- **Automatic Time Tracking**: Background tracking of computer activities
- **Mobile Apps**: Track time on iOS and Android devices
- **Manual Entry**: Drag-and-drop interface for retrospective time logging
- **Bulk Entry**: Enter multiple time entries efficiently
- **Multiple Views**: Daily, weekly, and monthly timesheet views or spreadsheet format

### Project Management

- Sales and delivery integration
- Quote-to-cash workflow automation
- Project templates and milestones
- Resource allocation and scheduling
- Budget tracking and forecasting

### Reporting and Analytics
