## Overview

Workfrom is a platform that helps remote workers discover the best cafes and workspaces for productive remote work in any city. The platform features 16,913 verified locations with comprehensive details about each workspace.

## Key Features

- **16,900+ Verified Locations**: Extensive database of cafes and workspaces worldwide
- **Detailed Information**: WiFi speeds, noise levels, power outlet availability, and amenities
- **Search Filters**: Find locations by reliable WiFi, quiet ambiance, natural light, and other criteria
- **Body Doubling Sessions**: Members can be paired to keep each other accountable while working
- **Community Features**: Track involvement through a points system earned by checking in at locations
- **Location Reviews**: Real feedback from remote workers about workspace quality

## Productivity Benefits

87% of body doubling session participants report improved focus and engagement. The platform helps remote workers avoid common cafe pitfalls like unreliable WiFi, time limits, uncomfortable seating, or lack of power outlets.

## Use Cases

Ideal for:
- Digital nomads seeking reliable work locations while traveling
- Remote workers looking for alternatives to home offices
- Freelancers wanting community and accountability
- Anyone seeking productive cafe environments with verified amenities

## Community Engagement

Beyond location listings, Workfrom has built community features including accountability sessions and a gamified check-in system to encourage exploration of new workspaces.
