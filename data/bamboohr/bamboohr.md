## Overview

BambooHR is a leading HRIS (Human Resource Information System) platform that includes time tracking and attendance management as part of a complete employee management solution.

## Time Tracking Features

- Time clock for hourly employees
- Timesheet management
- PTO request and approval
- Attendance tracking
- Integration with payroll
- Mobile app access

## HRIS Features

- Applicant tracking
- Onboarding workflows
- Employee database
- Performance management
- Benefits administration
- Reporting and analytics

## Benefits

- Single system for all HR needs
- User-friendly interface
- Strong reporting capabilities
- Employee self-service portal

## Target Users

- Small to medium businesses
- Companies with 10-1000 employees
- Organizations wanting integrated HR platform

Custom pricing based on company size and features needed.
