## Overview

Adit is an all-in-one AI-powered dental practice management software designed to centralize and automate dental office operations. The platform brings calls, texts, payments, reviews, and scheduling together into one powerful dashboard.

## Key Features

### Centralized Communications
- Unified dashboard for all patient communications
- Integrated phone system
- Two-way text messaging
- Email communications
- Review management

### AI-Powered Automation
- Automated appointment reminders
- Smart patient communication workflows
- AI-driven scheduling optimization
- Intelligent review collection
- Automated payment processing

### Practice Management Tools
- Online appointment scheduling
- Patient portal access
- Payment processing and billing
- Review and reputation management
- Multi-location support

## Benefits for Dental Practices

- Reduced no-shows through automated reminders
- Improved online reputation management
- Streamlined patient communications
- Enhanced practice efficiency
- Better patient engagement
- Consolidated technology stack
