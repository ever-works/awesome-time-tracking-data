## Overview

WorkflowMAX is a job and profitability management software solution designed to assist service firms in optimizing their operations. The platform provides comprehensive control over time management, team collaboration, and profit tracking in a single interface.

**Integrated Calendar:**
- Syncs directly with Microsoft Outlook and Google Calendar
- Meetings, appointments, and events appear automatically
- Convert to timesheet entries with a few clicks
- Eliminates double-entry between calendar and timesheets

**Timesheet Approvals & Reminders:**
- Automated reminder workflows
- Approval chains for timesheet review

- Escalating reminders
- Reduces administrative burden

- Capture every billable second

**Timesheet Management:**

- Accurate time reporting for all team members
- Integrated with job costing
- Real-time profitability insights
