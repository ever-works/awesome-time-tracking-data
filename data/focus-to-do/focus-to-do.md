## Overview

Focus To-Do combines the Pomodoro Technique with comprehensive task management to help users stay focused and accomplish their goals.

## Features

- **Pomodoro Timer**: Traditional 25-minute work sessions with breaks
- **Task Management**: Integrated to-do list and task organization
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, Chrome extension, and web
- **Cloud Sync**: Synchronize tasks and timers across all devices
- **Statistics**: Track productivity patterns and completed Pomodoros
- **White Noise**: Built-in ambient sounds for focus
- **Project Organization**: Group tasks by projects
- **Reminders**: Notifications for tasks and Pomodoro sessions
- **Reporting**: Visual reports on time spent and tasks completed
