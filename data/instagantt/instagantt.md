## Key Information

**What is Instagantt?**
Instagantt is a web app for creating Gantt charts which help you visually plan project schedules with your team.

**Integration with Asana:**
Instagantt connects to Asana in seconds and keeps tasks and due dates in two-way sync. Instagantt is purpose-built for Asana with real-time two-way sync, dependencies, baselines, shareable timelines, and CSV/PDF export.

**Main Features:**
- A powerful timeline for Asana, tasks, and subtasks in one place
- Draw your tasks on your chart, and change them with Drag & Drop
- Team workload, change tracking, estimated hours, image and PDF exports, and critical path
- Instagantt's Day View and Week View allow you to adapt your timeline for detailed daily planning or broader weekly overviews

**History with Asana:**
Gantt charts were previously only possible via a 3rd-party tool called Instagantt. Asana later released its own Timeline view feature, though as a replacement for Instagantt (i.e. a tool to generate proper Gantt charts) it's a poor substitute according to some users.

**Pricing:**
Start free with Instagantt; build online timelines and export. Upgrade for baselines, workload, and advanced sharing.
