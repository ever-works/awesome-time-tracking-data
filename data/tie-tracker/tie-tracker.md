## Tie Tracker

### Description
Tie Tracker is a free, open-source time tracking application for recording and managing working hours across clients and projects. It focuses on a clean, minimal interface suitable for personal or lightweight professional use, including basic reporting and billing support.

### Features
- **Time tracking**
  - Simple work hours tracking
  - Track time across multiple projects
  - Assign time entries to specific clients and projects
  - Track productivity and billable vs. non-billable time

- **Billing & invoicing**
  - Mark individual time entries as billed or unbilled
  - Export open (unbilled) invoices to XLSX timesheets (compatible with Excel, LibreOffice, Numbers, etc.)
  - Export timesheets and invoices to PDF

- **Views & summaries**
  - Weekly work summary
  - Daily list of activities and time entries
  - Simple, minimal reports for tracked time

- **General**
  - Free and open-source
  - Clean, minimal UX optimized for straightforward time logging

### Pricing
- **Free**
  - Entire application is free to use
  - Open-source project with no paid plans listed
