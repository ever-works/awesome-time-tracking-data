## Overview

Ravetree is a comprehensive work management platform built specifically for digital agencies and professional services firms. It combines project management, time tracking, resource planning, and CRM functionality in one integrated solution.

## Features

- Project planning and task management
- Time tracking with timers and manual entry
- Resource capacity planning and scheduling
- Budget tracking and project profitability
- Client relationship management (CRM)
- File sharing and collaboration
- Customizable workflows and automation
- Team communication and messaging
- Expense tracking
- Invoice and estimate creation
- Detailed reporting and analytics
- Integration with accounting software
- Mobile app for time tracking

## Pricing

Ravetree offers custom pricing based on team size and requirements. Plans typically include:
- Starter plans for small teams
- Professional plans for growing agencies
- Enterprise plans with advanced features and support
