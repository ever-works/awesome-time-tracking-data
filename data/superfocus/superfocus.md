## Overview

Superfocus is a versatile focus timer application that supports multiple time management techniques in one app. It allows users to switch between Flowtime, Pomodoro, and custom timing methods based on their current needs.

## Supported Techniques

- **Flowtime Timer**: Work until focus fades, then take proportional breaks
- **Pomodoro**: Traditional 25-minute work / 5-minute break cycles
- **Custom Timers**: Set your own work and break durations
- **52/17 Method**: 52 minutes work, 17 minute break
- **90-Minute Blocks**: Ultradian rhythm-aligned focus sessions

## Features

- **Flexible Timing**: Switch between techniques as needed
- **Session History**: Track all focus sessions over time
- **Statistics**: Analyze your focus patterns and productivity
- **Break Reminders**: Notifications for break time
- **Ambient Sounds**: Optional background sounds for focus
- **Cross-Platform**: Web, desktop, and mobile versions
- **No Account Required**: Use immediately without signup
- **Privacy-First**: All data stored locally

## Why Flexible Timers Matter

Different types of work require different focus approaches. Creative work might benefit from Flowtime, while administrative tasks work well with Pomodoro. Superfocus adapts to your needs.
