## Overview

Activity logs represent a comprehensive approach to time tracking where you chronicle every activity and the time spent on it throughout your day.

## How to Maintain an Activity Log

### Recording Format
- Note the start and end time of each activity
- Write a brief description of what you did
- Optionally categorize activities (work, personal, meetings, etc.)
- Add any relevant notes or context

### Example Entry
```
9:00-9:45am: Email triage and responses (45 min)
9:45-11:30am: Client presentation prep (1h 45min)
11:30-12:00pm: Team standup meeting (30 min)
```

## Benefits

- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify how time is actually spent vs. planned
- **Accountability**: Increases awareness of time usage
- **Analysis**: Provides data for improving workflow efficiency
- **Billing**: Useful for freelancers tracking billable hours

## Best Practices

- Record activities in real-time or shortly after completing them
- Be specific enough to be useful but not overly detailed
- Review logs weekly to identify patterns
- Use categories to group similar activities for analysis
