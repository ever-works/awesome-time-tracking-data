## Overview

focusedOS transforms your Mac & iPhone for ultimate productivity by reducing common distractions with powerful blocking features and visual management that automatically dims apps you're not using.

## Key Features

### Distraction Dimming
Automatically dims applications that you're not currently using, or keeps just a couple on-screen at a time, helping you maintain focus on your primary task.

### Powerful Blocking
Blocks distracting websites and apps during focus sessions, preventing interruptions from social media, news, and other time-wasting activities.

### Cross-Device Support
Available for both Mac and iPhone, allowing you to maintain focus across all your Apple devices.

### Visual Focus Management
Uses visual cues like dimming and highlighting to guide your attention to the task at hand, reducing cognitive load.

## Use Cases

- Deep work sessions requiring undivided attention
- Writing and creative work
- Coding and development
- Study sessions
- Any task requiring extended concentration

## How It Differs

Unlike simple website blockers, focusedOS takes a holistic approach to focus management by combining blocking with visual workspace management, creating an environment optimized for concentration.

## Platform Availability

Available for macOS and iOS, making it ideal for users in the Apple ecosystem who want consistent focus tools across devices.
