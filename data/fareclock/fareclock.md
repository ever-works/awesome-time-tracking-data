## Overview

Fareclock is a comprehensive time clock and workforce management app designed specifically for businesses with mobile and field-based employees. The platform combines time tracking with GPS geofencing, geomapping, and facial recognition to ensure accurate attendance records and prevent time theft.

## Key Features

### Geofencing & Location Tracking

- **Customizable Geofences**: Set virtual boundaries for job sites or specific work areas with fully customizable radius settings
- **Real-Time Notifications**: Receive immediate alerts when employees clock in or out of geofenced locations
- **Unauthorized Clock-In Prevention**: Employees can only clock in when physically present within designated geofenced areas
- **GPS Tracking**: Track workforce location across multiple job sites with precision

### Time & Attendance

- **Mobile Time Clock**: Employees clock in and out using their iOS or Android devices
- **Facial Recognition**: Photo verification ensures the right person is clocking in
- **Buddy Punch Prevention**: Biometric and location features prevent employees from clocking in for each other
- **Break Tracking**: Accurately record break times and ensure compliance

### Workforce Management

- **Smart Scheduling**: Create and manage employee schedules efficiently
- **Job Costing**: Track labor, materials, and equipment expenses with precision
- **Custom Report Fields**: Generate detailed reports tailored to business needs
- **Payroll Integration**: Powerful payroll calculation tools streamline the payment process

### Additional Capabilities

- **Jobs and Cost Codes**: Easily create and assign jobs and cost codes for project tracking
- **Multi-Location Support**: Manage teams across multiple job sites from a single platform
- **Overtime Calculation**: Automatic overtime tracking based on configurable rules
- **Mobile and Desktop Access**: Access from anywhere via mobile app or web dashboard
