## Overview

ManyRequests is a comprehensive client portal and project management platform designed specifically for creative professionals, designers, and agencies. It includes integrated time tracking capabilities that help track billable hours while managing client requests and projects.

## Features

- **Time Tracking for Designers**: Track time spent on design projects and client requests
- **Client Portal**: Centralized portal for client communication and request management
- **Request Management**: Organize and prioritize client requests and revisions
- **Billable Hours Tracking**: Accurately track time for client billing
- **Project Organization**: Manage multiple client projects simultaneously
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked time and project work
- **Team Collaboration**: Coordinate work across design team members
- **Client Feedback**: Streamline revision requests and approval processes

## Use Cases

- Freelance designers tracking client project hours
- Design agencies managing multiple client accounts
- Creative studios handling ongoing retainer work
- Web design teams tracking billable hours
- Graphic designers managing project profitability

## Benefits for Creative Professionals

- Simplifies tracking time without disrupting creative flow
- Provides clear visibility into project profitability
- Streamlines client communication and request management
- Reduces administrative overhead
- Improves accuracy of client billing

## Workflow Integration

ManyRequests integrates time tracking directly into the creative workflow, allowing designers to track time while working on client requests without switching between multiple tools.

## Pricing

ManyRequests offers subscription-based pricing with different tiers based on the number of clients and team members. Free trial available.
