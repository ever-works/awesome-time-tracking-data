## Overview

Timing is an automatic time tracking app for Mac that watches what you do on your computer and tracks how much time you spend on different activities. Once installed, it runs in the background automatically tracking your time.

## Key Features

### Automatic Tracking
- Automatically records all activities on your Mac
- Tracks applications used, documents opened, and websites visited
- No need to manually start and stop timers
- Captures time that would otherwise fall through the cracks

### Smart Assignment
- Set up rules to automatically assign time to projects
- The more rules you create, the more automatic your tracking becomes
- Manual timers available when needed
- Intelligent categorization of activities

### Advanced Capabilities
- **Call Tracking**: Track time spent in meetings and calls
- **Mobile Integration**: Import iPhone & iPad usage data
- **Calendar Events**: Record time for calendar events automatically
- **Productivity Insights**: Understand how you spend your time

### Reporting
- Generate professional reports suitable for clients
- Detailed time breakdowns by project, activity, and date
- Export options for billing and invoicing
- Visual analytics and charts
