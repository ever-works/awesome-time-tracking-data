## Overview

Infor Workforce Scheduling is enterprise software that makes it easy to create shift-driven or demand-driven schedules that comply with complex labor rules and regulations.

## Features

- **Shift-Driven Scheduling** - Create schedules based on fixed shift patterns
- **Demand-Driven Scheduling** - Generate schedules based on forecasted demand
- **Complex Labor Rules** - Ensure compliance with union rules, labor laws, and contracts
- **Optimization Algorithms** - Automatically optimize schedules for efficiency and cost
- **Employee Preferences** - Consider employee availability and preferences
- **Multi-Location Support** - Manage scheduling across multiple locations
- **Real-Time Adjustments** - Make schedule changes in real-time as needs change
- **Integration** - Connect with Infor HCM and other enterprise systems

## Use Cases

Designed for large enterprises, healthcare systems, manufacturing facilities, and organizations with complex scheduling needs and labor compliance requirements.
