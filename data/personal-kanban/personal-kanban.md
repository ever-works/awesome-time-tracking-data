## Overview

Personal Kanban is an adaptation of Kanban for individual productivity. It uses visual workflow management with just two essential rules to help you manage work effectively.

## Two Essential Rules

**Rule 1: Visualize Your Work**
- Make all work visible on a board
- See everything in one place
- Understand total workload
- Create clarity and transparency

**Rule 2: Limit Work-in-Progress (WIP)**
- Restrict number of active tasks
- Finish before starting new work
- Reduce context switching
- Maintain focus

## Basic Board Setup

**Three Columns:**
- **Ready**: Tasks to do
- **Doing**: Work in progress (WIP limited)
- **Done**: Completed work

**Optional Columns:**
- Backlog
- Waiting
- This Week
- Today

## Benefits

- Reduced stress from visible work
- Improved focus through WIP limits
- Sense of accomplishment seeing Done
- Better understanding of capacity
- Flexibility to adapt

## Implementation

- Physical board (whiteboard, wall)
- Digital tool (Trello, Notion, etc.)
- Simple is better
- Start minimal, adjust as needed
