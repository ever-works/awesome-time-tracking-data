## Overview

SyntheBrain World Clock Meeting Planner is a free online tool designed to help global teams schedule meetings across different time zones. The platform simplifies the complex task of finding suitable meeting times for participants in multiple locations.

## Key Features

**Multi-Location Comparison:**
- Compare up to 5 locations simultaneously
- Support for 200+ cities worldwide
- Visual representation of time zones

**Working Hours Visualization:**
- See working hours across different time zones at a glance
- Color-coded displays for easy interpretation
- Identify overlapping business hours instantly

**Drag-and-Drop Interface:**
- Intuitive drag-and-drop functionality for selecting times
- Adjust meeting times and see instant updates across all zones
- Easy-to-use interface requires no training

**Meeting Scheduling:**
- Find optimal times that work across all selected time zones
- Avoid scheduling meetings outside business hours
- Account for time zone differences automatically

## Use Cases

- Remote teams coordinating across continents
- International client meetings
- Global company all-hands meetings
- Virtual events with worldwide attendance
- Distributed team collaboration

## Accessibility

As a web-based tool, SyntheBrain World Clock Meeting Planner works on any device with a browser - no downloads or installations required. The tool is completely free to use.

## Competitive Advantage

While many time zone converters exist, SyntheBrain differentiates itself through its drag-and-drop interface and visual working hours display, making it particularly user-friendly for teams that frequently schedule cross-timezone meetings.
