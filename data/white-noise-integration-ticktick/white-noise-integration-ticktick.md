## Why White Noise Helps Focus

### Masking Distractions
- Covers inconsistent background noises
- Creates predictable audio environment
- Reduces startle response to sudden sounds
- Minimizes attention pulls

### Psychological Anchoring
- Associates sound with focus mode
- Creates mental trigger for concentration
- Establishes work ritual
- Signals brain to enter flow state

### Optimal Stimulation
- Provides enough sensory input to prevent boredom
- Not so much as to be distracting
- Maintains consistent arousal level
- Supports sustained attention
