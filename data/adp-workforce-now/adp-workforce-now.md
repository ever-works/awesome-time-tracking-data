## Overview

ADP Workforce Now is an all-in-one HR solution designed for mid-sized businesses with 50-999 employees. It combines comprehensive time and attendance tracking with payroll, HR management, benefits administration, and talent management in a unified cloud-based platform.

## Features

- **Time & Attendance**: Multi-method time tracking including web, mobile, kiosk, and biometric clocks
- **Payroll Processing**: Full-service payroll with tax filing and compliance management
- **HR Management**: Complete employee lifecycle management from hire to retire
- **Benefits Administration**: Benefits enrollment and management
- **Talent Management**: Recruiting, onboarding, performance, and learning management
- **Compliance Management**: Automated compliance with federal, state, and local regulations
- **Scheduling**: Employee scheduling with shift management
- **Leave Management**: PTO, FMLA, and other leave tracking
- **Mobile Access**: Mobile app for employees and managers
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Pre-built and custom reports with dashboards
- **Integrations**: Connect with 401(k) providers, insurance carriers, and other systems
- **Self-Service Portal**: Employee and manager self-service capabilities

## ADP Product Family

- **RUN Powered by ADP**: For small businesses (1-49 employees)
- **ADP Workforce Now**: For mid-sized businesses (50-999 employees)
- **ADP Vantage HCM**: For large enterprises (1,000+ employees)
