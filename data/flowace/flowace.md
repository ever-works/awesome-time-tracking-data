## Overview

Flowace is an AI-powered time tracking and productivity monitoring platform that helps teams boost efficiency through intelligent insights and automated tracking. The software provides exact, constant information on how team members spend their energy and time.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Time Tracking**: Tracks time based on real activity, apps used, websites visited, calendar entries, meeting hours, and break durations without requiring constant user input
- **Attendance Management**: Auto clock-in/out with detailed attendance reports, tracking 'In' and 'Out' times, shift hours, and idle time automatically
- **Productivity Monitoring**: Monitors both productive and unproductive apps and websites, issues missing hour alerts, identifies potential burnout, and provides insights into overall company productivity
- **Silent Mode**: Operates unobtrusively in the background, enabling seamless tracking with minimal impact on device performance
- **Smart Screenshots**: Work/privacy mode that only starts when employees are in the work zone, with masked screenshots to keep confidential data safe
- **Project Management**: Helps manage projects and track time with integrated timesheets and project budgeting with accurate real-time productivity analytics
- **Integrations**: Seamlessly integrates with Salesforce, Asana, Jira, and Azure

## Platform Compatibility

- Web tracker (no software download needed)
- Mobile tracker for measuring work calls or outdoor meeting hours
- Desktop applications for Windows and macOS
