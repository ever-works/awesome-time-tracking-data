## Overview

Freckle (formerly Let's Freckle) is a time tracking tool built around the philosophy that time tracking should be quick, easy, and non-intrusive. Its tagline is "Time tracking that doesn't suck" and it's designed specifically for teams who bill by the hour.

## Features

- **Quick Entry**: Type time entries in natural language (e.g., "2h meeting with client #projectname")
- **Timer Mode**: Start/stop timers for tasks with one click
- **Tagging System**: Organize time with unlimited custom tags
- **Team Dashboard**: See what everyone's working on in real-time
- **Detailed Reports**: Filter by project, person, tag, or date range
- **Budgets and Estimates**: Track project budgets and get alerts when approaching limits
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices directly from tracked time
- **Multiple Integrations**: Connects with QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Basecamp, and more
- **Mobile Apps**: Full-featured iOS and Android applications
- **API Access**: Build custom integrations with RESTful API
- **Team Permissions**: Control what team members can see and edit
- **Unlimited Projects**: No limits on projects or clients

## Philosophy

 Freckle prioritizes simplicity and speed over feature bloat. The interface is intentionally minimal, making it ideal for teams who want straightforward time tracking without learning curves or overwhelming options.

## Pricing

Freckle offers straightforward per-user pricing:
- **Freelancer**: $5/month for 1 user
- **Small Team**: $12/month for up to 3 users
- **Team**: $49/month for unlimited users
- **Agency**: $199/month for unlimited users plus white-labeling

All plans include unlimited projects, clients, and time entries with a 30-day free trial.
