## Overview

Micro-breaks are very short rest periods (30-60 seconds) taken every 30-60 minutes during work to prevent mental and physical fatigue while maintaining sustained productivity.

## How It Works

- Work for 30-60 minutes
- Take 30-60 second break
- Stand, stretch, or look away
- Return refreshed
- Repeat throughout day

## Benefits

- Prevents eye strain
- Reduces physical tension
- Maintains mental freshness
- Prevents decision fatigue
- Sustains focus longer
- Improves overall productivity

## Micro-Break Activities

- Stand and stretch
- Look out window (20-20-20 rule: every 20 min, look 20 feet away for 20 sec)
- Deep breathing
- Quick walk
- Drink water
- Simple stretches

## vs. Traditional Breaks

- More frequent
- Much shorter
- Less disruptive
- Easier to implement
- Better sustained focus

## Implementation

- Set timer or use app reminders
- Build into pomodoro cycles
- Make it automatic habit
- Track adherence and impact
