## Overview

Calamari is an HR software solution that streamlines HR tasks with comprehensive leave management and clock in/out modules, simplifying PTO calculation and attendance tracking for teams of all sizes.

## Features

- **Leave Management**: Track vacation days, sick leave, and all types of time off
- **Clock In/Clock Out**: Digital time clock for attendance tracking
- **PTO Calculation**: Automated calculation of paid time off balances
- **Absence Planning**: Calendar view of team absences for better planning
- **Approval Workflows**: Customizable approval processes for leave requests
- **Integration Hub**: Connect with Slack, Jira, Google Apps, Asana, and more
- **Mobile Apps**: Clock in/out and request time off from mobile devices
- **Reporting**: Generate reports on attendance, leave usage, and time tracking
- **Multiple Leave Types**: Support for various leave categories (vacation, sick, personal, etc.)
- **Team Calendar**: Visualize team availability and absences
- **Notifications**: Automated alerts for pending requests and approvals
- **Employee Portal**: Self-service portal for viewing balances and requesting time off

## Pricing

- **Calamari Leave**: Starting at $2 per employee per month (monthly billing) or $1.70 per employee per month (annual billing)
- **Clock In/Clock Out Module**: Starting at $2.50 per employee per month (monthly billing) or $2.00 per employee per month (annual billing)
- **Combined Plans**: Discounted pricing available when using both modules

## Best For

Small to medium-sized businesses looking for affordable HR software focused on leave management and attendance tracking, especially teams already using tools like Slack or Google Workspace.
