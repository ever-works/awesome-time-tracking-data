## Overview

Construction time tracking apps in 2026 focus heavily on mobile-first solutions with GPS verification, offline capabilities, and construction-specific features like job costing and certified payroll integration.

## Essential Features for Construction

### GPS Verification
- GPS tracking captures exact clock-in/out locations
- Geofencing verifies workers are within designated job sites
- Prevents time theft from remote clock-ins
- Route mapping for mobile crews

### Offline Capability
- **Critical Requirement**: Construction sites often have terrible cell coverage
- App must work offline in basements and remote locations
- Everything syncs automatically when back in range
- No lost data from connectivity issues

### Facial Recognition
- Prevents buddy punching by requiring selfie match
- Stops employees from clocking in for absent coworkers
- Verifies actual presence on jobsite
- Photographic proof for disputed time entries

### Certified Payroll
- Generate certified payroll reports for government contracts
- Include Workers' Comp codes in payroll processing
- Compliance with Davis-Bacon and prevailing wage requirements
- Audit-ready documentation

## Top Solutions for 2026

- **Workyard**: Best for GPS tracking and certified payroll
- **Timeero**: Excels at offline tracking with location data
- **ExakTime**: Multilingual support with physical time clock option
- **ClockShark**: Real-time field updates with clock-out questions
- **WorkMax**: GPS stamps with facial recognition and PIN codes

## Industry Challenges Addressed

- Variable job site locations
- Poor connectivity in buildings under construction
- Time theft through remote clock-ins
- Prevailing wage compliance requirements
- Accurate job costing across multiple sites
