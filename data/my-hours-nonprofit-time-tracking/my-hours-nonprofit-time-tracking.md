## Overview

My Hours provides a free volunteer hours tracker for nonprofits, simplifying grant management by organizing and customizing tracking according to each contract's terms.

## Features

- **Free for Nonprofits**: No-cost solution for volunteer organizations
- **Grant Management**: Organize time tracking by grant requirements
- **Custom Reports**: Create report templates for different grants
- **Scheduled Delivery**: Automatically deliver reports to stakeholders
- **Instant Invoicing**: Generate invoices when needed
- **Project Tracking**: Track time across multiple projects and grants
- **Volunteer Management**: Manage multiple volunteers and staff
- **Timesheet Approval**: Review and approve volunteer hours

## Grant Compliance

Helps ensure compliance with grant requirements by providing accurate records of how time is spent on specific activities or projects funded by grants.

## Benefits

- Simplified grant reporting
- Customizable to each grant's requirements
- Professional reports for funders
- Organized tracking across multiple grants

## Platform

Web-based with mobile apps.

## Pricing

Free tier available for small teams.
