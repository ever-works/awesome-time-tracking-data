## Overview

ScreenshotMonitor is an employee monitoring and time tracking solution that captures periodic screenshots of employee computer screens. The platform is designed for businesses managing remote teams who want to track productivity and ensure work is being completed.

## Features

- **Screenshot Capture**: Automatic or on-demand screenshots at configurable intervals
- **Time Tracking**: Track hours worked with automatic idle time detection
- **Activity Monitoring**: Track keyboard and mouse activity levels
- **Application Tracking**: See which applications employees are using
- **Website Tracking**: Monitor which websites are visited during work hours
- **Productivity Reports**: Generate reports on employee productivity levels
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: View employee screens in real-time (optional)
- **Privacy Settings**: Employees can blur or delete screenshots

## Use Cases

- Companies managing remote or distributed teams
- Businesses paying freelancers by the hour
- Agencies tracking contractor productivity
- Organizations with compliance or security requirements

## Privacy Considerations

- Employees should be informed about monitoring practices
- Some jurisdictions require employee consent
- Platform includes features to blur sensitive information
- Employees may have the ability to delete certain screenshots
