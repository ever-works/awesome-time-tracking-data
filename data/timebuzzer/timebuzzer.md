## Overview

timeBuzzer is a time tracking application that combines software with an optional physical hardware buzzer device for effortless time tracking. The platform helps individuals and teams track time across projects in real-time with just one click, making it particularly appealing for consultants and agencies looking to increase billable hours.

## Features

- **Physical Hardware Buzzer**: Optional Bluetooth-enabled device for one-click time tracking without opening apps
- **Multi-Platform Apps**: Desktop apps (Windows, macOS), mobile apps (iOS, Android), and web interface
- **Project Organization**: Organize time entries by projects and clients
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Track time as you work with live timers
- **Manual Entry**: Add time entries manually when needed
- **Time Analysis**: Visualize where you spend time with reports and analytics
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync with calendar apps for comprehensive time overview
- **Team Features**: Share projects and track team time
- **Invoicing Support**: Export time data for billing and invoicing
- **Flexible Tracking**: Switch between tasks by tapping different sides of the hardware buzzer
- **Automatic Sync**: All devices sync automatically in real-time

## Benefits

- Save hours per person every week on administrative time tracking tasks
- Increase billable hours by up to 20% through accurate time capture
- Reduce time tracking friction with hardware device
- Get instant overview of time spent across projects

## Hardware

The timeBuzzer device is an octagonal physical buzzer that sits on your desk. Each side can be assigned to different projects or tasks. Simply flip the buzzer to start tracking time for that activity.

## Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Basic time tracking features
- **Pro Plan**: Starting at approximately $8 per user per month
- **Hardware Buzzer**: One-time purchase, pricing available on website

The software can be used without the hardware buzzer, but the physical device is the signature feature that differentiates timeBuzzer from other time tracking tools.
