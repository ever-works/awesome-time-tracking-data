## Overview

Practice by Numbers is an all-in-one software that streamlines everything from appointments to marketing for dental practices. Founded by dentists and built for dentists, the platform consolidates, streamlines, and improves day-to-day operations with analytics, patient communication, reputation management, online scheduling, and more—all in a single platform.

## Key Features for 2026

### Business Analytics
- Purpose-built dental analytics software
- Turns raw PMS data into real-time, actionable insights
- Integrates with Dentrix, Open Dental, and Eaglesoft
- Track production, payments, and practice performance in one dashboard
- Make faster, data-driven decisions

### Communication Tools
- Intelligent, integrated, cloud-powered VoIP phone system
- Built specifically for dental practices
- Patient communication automation
- Two-way messaging capabilities

### Digital Forms
- Secure online forms
- Direct synchronization with practice management systems
- Streamlined patient intake process

### AI Integration
- All-in-one AI engine for dental operations
- Automated workflow enhancements
- Intelligent practice insights
