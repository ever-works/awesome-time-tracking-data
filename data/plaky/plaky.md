## Overview

Plaky is a visual project management tool that combines intuitive Kanban-style boards with time tracking capabilities. It's designed for creative agencies, marketing teams, and project-based businesses that need simple yet powerful project organization.

## Features

- Visual Kanban boards and list views
- Custom fields and task attributes
- Time tracking integration
- Task dependencies and subtasks
- File attachments and collaboration
- Activity feed and notifications
- Multiple board templates
- Team member assignments
- Due dates and deadline tracking
- Filters and search functionality
- Mobile app access
- Integrations with time tracking tools

## Pricing

Plaky offers a freemium model:
- Free plan with unlimited tasks and boards for small teams
- Pro plan with advanced features and integrations
- Enterprise plan with premium support and customization

Pricing is competitive and designed to scale with team growth.
