## Overview

mJob provides construction-specific time tracking with GPS verification, job costing, scheduling, and field management tools designed for contractors and construction crews.

## Features

- GPS time clock with location verification
- Real-time job costing and labor allocation
- Crew scheduling and dispatch
- Equipment tracking
- Photo documentation
- Certified payroll reports
- Integration with QuickBooks and other accounting platforms

## Construction-Specific Capabilities

- Multiple job site support
- Cost code tracking by phase
- Prevailing wage compliance
- Union vs non-union differentiation
- Davis-Bacon reporting

## Platform

iOS, Android, Web

## Target Users

General contractors, subcontractors, specialty trades, construction companies
