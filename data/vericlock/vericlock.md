## Overview

VeriClock is a cloud-based time clock software designed for employee timekeeping, scheduling, and payroll management. It processes over 23 million timesheets and serves more than 25,000 users across various industries.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Eliminate manual tracking and billing for employee time with automated clock-in/clock-out
- **Employee Scheduling**: Set up schedules and engage the team with easy scheduling tools
- **Location Management**: GPS-based clock in and out with continuous tracking and geofencing
- **PTO Tracking**: Track time off and manage approvals for vacation and sick days
- **Accounting and Payroll**: Calculate payroll including overtime and connect with payroll providers (Sage 50, QuickBooks)
- **White Glove Service**: Active support and customization assistance
- **In-App Signature Capture**: Capture digital signatures within the mobile app
- **Multiple Overtime Rules**: Configurable overtime calculation rules
- **Customizable Job and Service Codes**: Define custom codes for jobs and services
- **Exportable Reports**: Customizable, exportable reports in CSV, XLSX, and PDF formats
- **Group Clock-In**: Support for group clock-in capability
- **TimeGuard**: Auto clock-out employees who forget to clock out
- **Configurable E-Mail Alerts**: Set up email alerts on a per-company or per-user level
- **Per-Company or Per-User Settings**: Granular configuration options
- **Mobile Apps**: Available for both iOS and Android platforms
- **Employee Onboarding Assistance**: Support for onboarding new employees
