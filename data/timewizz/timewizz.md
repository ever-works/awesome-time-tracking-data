## Overview

TimeWizz is designed to make complex work environments simple, positioning itself as a modern solution for time tracking. TimeWizz is a comprehensive time management software designed specifically for agencies and SMBs.

## Features

- **Quick Start/Stop Tracking**: Start and stop tracking just by hitting the space bar for instant time recording
- **Automatic Calculations**: Automatically calculates project time, free time, sick time and more
- **Simple Setup**: No account or credit card information required for setting up trial access
- **All-in-One Platform**: Comprehensive solution that handles various time management needs in one place
- **Agency-Focused**: Specifically designed to meet the needs of agencies and small-to-medium businesses

## Target Users

TimeWizz is ideal for agencies, SMBs, and teams that need a comprehensive time management solution without the complexity of enterprise-level software.

## Pricing

There is a free trial version for the first 30 days with no account or credit card information required for setting up trial access.
