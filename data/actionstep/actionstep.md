## Overview

Actionstep is comprehensive legal practice management software that combines time tracking, billing, and matter management in one integrated platform. The system simplifies invoicing, tracks billable hours in real time, and ensures compliance with legal billing standards.

## Time Tracking Features

### Real-Time Hour Tracking
- Live billable hour capture
- Integrated timer functionality
- Mobile time tracking via apps
- Automatic time entry from activities
- Time tracking across multiple matters

### Billing and Invoicing
- Automated invoice generation from tracked time
- Customizable invoice templates
- Multi-currency billing support
- Trust accounting integration
- Payment processing capabilities

## Practice Management Capabilities

- Matter and case management
- Document automation and management
- Client relationship management (CRM)
- Task and workflow automation
- Calendar and deadline tracking
- Conflict of interest checking

## Compliance and Standards

- Legal billing standards compliance
- Jurisdictional rule adherence
- Ethics compliance tools

- Audit trail and reporting
