## Overview

Croogloo is a software solution specifically designed for film and TV productions, helping production offices manage script sides, time reports, and distribution all in one platform.

## Industry Focus

Croogloo is not designed for general freelancers or businesses - it's specifically built for the **film and television production industry**, making it a highly specialized time tracking and management solution.

## Time Reports Features

Croogloo's Time Reports capabilities cover everything from collecting daily time reports in the production office to crew members submitting their hours:

### Daily Time Reports
- Crew members can submit their daily hours through the platform
- Production office can collect and manage all time reports centrally
- Digital submission eliminates paper-based time tracking

### Weekly Timesheets
- Comprehensive weekly timesheet compilation
- Automated aggregation of daily reports
- Easy review and approval workflows

### Accounting Integration
- The Accounting email account set to ADMIN on the production receives all submitted Daily Time Reports and Weekly Timesheets
- An email is sent every time a report or timesheet is submitted
- Seamless handoff from production to accounting departments
