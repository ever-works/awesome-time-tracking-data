## Overview

Timetrack mini makes time tracking simple and beautiful, focusing on the only day that counts — today. The app embodies "pure time in a timeless design."

## Key Features

### Intuitive Time Tracking
- Easy start-stop functionality for daily tasks
- Up to six tracker buttons with customizable icons and colors
- Homescreen widget for even quicker time tracking
- Works in background and completely offline

### Statistics & Insights
- Daily and past summaries
- Beautiful interactive diagrams
- Track patterns over time
- Visual representation of time usage

### Design & Experience
- Beautiful dark mode support
- Minimalist, clean interface
- No distractions or unnecessary features
- Focus on essential time tracking

### Privacy & Pricing
- No ads or subscriptions
- Developer does not collect any data from this app
- All essential features free to use
- One-time PRO purchase available for additional features

## Philosophy

The app emphasizes simplicity and focuses only on what matters - tracking your time today without complexity or distraction.
