## Overview

DATABASICS provides enterprise-grade time and expense management solutions with comprehensive year-end planning resources. Their platform helps organizations manage timesheets, leave balances, mileage, per diem rates, and expense compliance.

## Year-End Time & Expense Management

### Year-End Checklist
- Review and close timesheet periods
- Verify leave balance accuracy
- Update mileage rates for new year
- Reconcile expense categories
- Prepare for tax reporting
- Audit compliance records

### 2026 Preparation
- Monitor IRS mileage rate announcements (2026: 72.5¢/mile)
- Update per diem rate tables
- Review and update expense policies
- Configure new fiscal year settings
- Plan system updates and training
