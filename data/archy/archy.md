## Overview

Archy is a comprehensive practice management platform designed exclusively for dental practices, offering time tracking, appointment scheduling, patient records, and revenue cycle management in a modern, cloud-based solution.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track staff hours and productivity with integrated time clock
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Visual calendar with automated reminders and confirmations
- **Patient Management**: Complete patient records with treatment history and notes
- **Billing & Insurance**: Submit insurance claims and process payments electronically
- **Digital Forms**: Paperless patient intake with customizable digital forms
- **Clinical Charting**: Comprehensive charting with odontograms and treatment planning
- **Team Communication**: In-app messaging and task assignment for dental teams
- **Analytics**: Practice performance dashboards with revenue and productivity metrics
- **Mobile Access**: Access patient records and schedules from any device
- **Integration**: Connects with imaging systems, payment processors, and accounting software
