## Overview

Mileage and expense tracking integrates with time tracking to provide complete project cost accounting. The system captures travel distances, applies standard mileage rates, and records expenses alongside labor hours for accurate billing and reimbursement.

## Key Features

- GPS-based automatic mileage calculation
- IRS standard mileage rate application
- Receipt photo capture and attachment
- Expense categorization
- Client/project expense allocation
- Reimbursement approval workflows

## Benefits

- Complete project cost picture
- Accurate client billing
- Simplified employee reimbursement
- Tax compliance documentation
- Reduced manual expense reports

## Common Use Cases

- Field service technicians traveling between jobs
- Sales representatives visiting clients
- Consultants with client site visits
- Construction workers moving between projects
