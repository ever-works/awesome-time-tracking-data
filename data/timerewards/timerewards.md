## Overview

TimeRewards is time tracking and management software built specifically with nonprofit organizations in mind. It addresses the unique needs of nonprofits including volunteer hour tracking, grant compliance, and in-kind contribution reporting.

## Features

- **Volunteer Hour Tracking**: Comprehensive tracking of volunteer time for grant applications and reporting
- **Grant Budget Management**: Tools to manage and track grant budgets effectively
- **Compliance Reporting**: Generate reports required by funders and government agencies
- **In-Kind Contribution Tracking**: Track and value volunteer hours as in-kind donations
- **Matching Fund Documentation**: Document volunteer time for grant matching requirements
- **Professional Services Time Tracking**: Includes billable hours tracking for nonprofit consulting services

## Use Cases

- Tracking volunteer hours for grant applications
- Documenting in-kind contributions for matching funds
- Managing multiple grant budgets and compliance requirements
- Generating funder reports
- Professional services billing for nonprofit consulting
