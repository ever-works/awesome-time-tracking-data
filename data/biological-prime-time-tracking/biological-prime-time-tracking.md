## Concept

Your Biological Prime Time (BPT) is when you have the most energy and focus. Everyone's BPT is different.

## Finding Your BPT

Track for 2-3 weeks, rating hourly:
- Energy level (1-10)
- Focus ability (1-10)
- Motivation (1-10)

## Common Patterns

- Morning Larks: Peak 6am-11am
- Night Owls: Peak 2pm-midnight
- Afternoon People: Peak 11am-4pm

## Using Your BPT

- Schedule high-value work during peaks
- Protect BPT from meetings
- Match energy to task difficulty
- Work with biology, not against it

## Benefits

- Higher quality work
- Less wasted effort
- Better energy management
- Improved work-life balance
