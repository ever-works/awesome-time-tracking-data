## Overview

Coast is a free employee scheduling and shift swapping app designed to simplify workforce management for shift-based businesses. The platform emphasizes ease of use and mobile accessibility.

## Key Features

- **Free Shift Swap App**: Completely free for all businesses regardless of size
- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for smartphones to give employees schedule access on the go
- **Real-Time Schedule Updates**: Changes sync instantly across all team members' devices
- **Shift Trading**: Employees can offer shifts and pick up available shifts from coworkers
- **Team Communication**: Built-in messaging for coordinating shift coverage
- **Schedule Publishing**: Create and share work schedules with the entire team
- **Availability Management**: Employees can set their availability preferences

## Shift Swapping Process

1. Employee marks a shift as available for trade
2. Other team members are notified about the open shift
3. Qualified employees can claim the shift
4. Manager receives notification and approves the swap
5. Schedule automatically updates for both employees

## Benefits for Businesses

- Zero cost for scheduling and shift management

- Increases employee satisfaction through schedule flexibility
- Minimizes no-shows and last-minute callouts

## Benefits for Employees
