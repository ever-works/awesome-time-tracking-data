## Overview

Actiwatch is a medical-grade wrist-worn device that tracks activity, sleep, and light exposure to understand circadian rhythms. While primarily used in clinical and research settings, understanding circadian biology helps optimize work schedules and time management.

## Features

- **Activity Tracking**: Movement patterns throughout day and night
- **Sleep Monitoring**: Sleep duration, quality, and patterns
- **Light Exposure**: Ambient light levels affecting circadian system
- **Circadian Phase**: Identify your biological optimal times
- **Long-Term Tracking**: Weeks to months of continuous data
- **Research-Grade**: Validated for scientific studies
- **Clinical Use**: Sleep disorder diagnosis and monitoring

## Time Management Applications

### Identify Peak Performance Windows
- Determine when you're naturally most alert
- Schedule deep work during biological peak times
- Recognize natural energy dips
- Plan breaks aligned with ultradian rhythms

### Optimize Sleep Schedule
- Find your natural sleep-wake timing
- Identify sleep debt patterns
- Optimize sleep for next-day performance
- Track impact of schedule changes

### Workplace Scheduling
- Align work hours with chronotype
- Optimize shift work schedules
- Reduce circadian disruption
- Improve overall productivity

## Note

Actiwatch devices are typically prescribed by healthcare providers or used in research settings. Consumer alternatives include fitness trackers with sleep tracking (Fitbit, Oura Ring, Apple Watch) though with less scientific accuracy.
