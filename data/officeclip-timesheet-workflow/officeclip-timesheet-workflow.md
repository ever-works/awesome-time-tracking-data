## Overview

OfficeClip Timesheet Workflow is a business management solution that automates the entire timesheet submission, review, and approval process. The platform emphasizes reducing administrative burden while maintaining control and accuracy through intelligent routing and multi-level approval capabilities.

## Features

- **Automated Routing**: Timesheets automatically routed to designated managers based on organizational hierarchy
- **Three-Level Approvals**: Support for up to three sequential approval stages
- **Manager Notifications**: Automatic alerts when timesheets require review
- **Submission Validation**: Ensure timesheets meet completeness and accuracy requirements before routing
- **Approval Workflows**: Configurable paths for different departments, projects, or employee types
- **Rejection Handling**: Return timesheets with comments for correction and resubmission
- **Status Tracking**: Real-time visibility into where timesheets are in the approval chain
- **Deadline Management**: Set submission and approval deadlines with automatic reminders

## Approval Hierarchy Configuration

Organizations can establish:
- Direct manager approval (Level 1)
- Department head approval (Level 2)
- Finance or executive approval (Level 3)

Each level can have custom rules for auto-approval based on hours, overtime, or other criteria.

## Benefits

- Eliminate manual timesheet distribution and follow-up
- Ensure proper approval authority and segregation of duties
- Reduce approval cycle time from days to hours
- Prevent timesheets from being overlooked or lost
- Maintain consistent approval processes across the organization
- Create audit trails for compliance and payroll verification

## Integration

Part of the broader OfficeClip suite, integrating with project management, expense tracking, and payroll modules for comprehensive business management.

## Use Cases

- Professional services firms with billable hours
- Consulting companies with project-based billing
- Organizations with union or regulatory reporting requirements
- Businesses requiring segregation of duties in payroll processing
