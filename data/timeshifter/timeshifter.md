## Overview

Timeshifter offers apps and services to reduce jet lag, manage shift work disruption, and prepare for peak performance by controlling human circadian rhythms through scientifically-backed interventions.

## How It Works

Combines the only elements shown to reset the circadian clock:
- Light exposure (timing is critical)
- Dark exposure
- Melatonin supplements (when appropriate)
- Caffeine (to alleviate symptoms)
- Strategic napping

## Features

### Jet Lag App
- Import travel schedule
- Enter sleep pattern and chronotype
- Receive personalized day-by-day advice
- Light exposure recommendations
- Optimal timing for sleep, melatonin, caffeine

### Shift Work App
- Import work schedule
- Get advice to tackle circadian and sleep disruption
- Personalized recommendations based on your patterns

## Scientific Foundation

Developed using circadian neuroscience research. The right timing of light exposure is critical for quickly adjusting your circadian clock to new time zones and staying alert.
