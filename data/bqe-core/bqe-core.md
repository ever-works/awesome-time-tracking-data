## Overview

BQE CORE is a comprehensive professional services automation (PSA) solution designed specifically for architecture, engineering, and professional services firms. It provides end-to-end business management from time tracking through invoicing and accounting.

## Time Tracking & Timesheets

- Multiple time entry methods (timer, manual, mobile)
- Quick time entry from project dashboard
- Bulk time entry for multiple days
- Time allocation by project phase and task
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Offline time tracking with sync
- Timesheet approval workflows
- Integration with project budgets
