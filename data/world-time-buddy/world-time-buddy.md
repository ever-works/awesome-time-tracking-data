## Overview

World Time Buddy is a popular visual time zone converter and world clock that simplifies scheduling across multiple time zones. It helps users find convenient meeting times for conference calls, webinars, online meetings, and phone calls with participants in different locations.

## Features

- Visual time zone comparison with color-coded hours (work hours, sleep hours, off-hours)
- Support for multiple cities simultaneously
- Automatic daylight saving time adjustments
- Easy drag-and-drop interface to find optimal meeting times
- Works on both desktop and mobile devices
- No signup required for basic features

## Use Cases

- Scheduling international conference calls and meetings
- Planning travel and tracking flight arrival times
- Coordinating with remote team members across time zones
- Finding the best time to contact colleagues or clients globally

## Pricing

Free to use with optional premium features.
