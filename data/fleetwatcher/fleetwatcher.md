# FleetWatcher

## Overview
FleetWatcher is a construction telematics and equipment/trucking management platform focused on paving and materials operations. It provides real-time visibility into equipment, trucks, and material movements so contractors can optimize load cycles, reduce idle time, and improve jobsite productivity and cost control. It can also complement workforce time tracking by supplying accurate equipment-time data for job costing and payroll allocation.

## Features

### Telematics & Materials Management
- Real-time tracking of trucks and equipment using wireless telematics
- Load Cycle Analysis to monitor and analyze haul cycles and material movements
- Insight into truck behavior (delays, detours, downtime, routing issues)
- Visibility into field operations to reduce delays and bottlenecks
- Support for both company-owned trucks and third‑party haulers
- Monitoring of machine usage time, engine hours, and idling
- Tracking fuel usage and idle time on paving equipment ("yellow iron")
- Equipment diagnostics monitoring to help prevent failures and unplanned downtime

### Paving & Trucking Operations
- Materials Management Solution (MMS) tailored to paving and materials delivery
- Tools to optimize materials delivery performance in real time
- Identification and reduction of inefficient routes, unauthorized stops, and truck “pack” behavior
- Help balancing truck counts to avoid under‑trucking or over‑trucking

### Workforce, Time & Payroll
- Timecard capture and approval workflows for field employees
- Fast timecard approval to accelerate payroll processing
- Paperless ticketing for loads/material deliveries
- Support for managing and streamlining payments to third‑party haulers
- Use of accurate equipment-time data for project allocation and payroll/job costing

### Applications & Communication
- Mobile app for drivers and supervisors, usable on smartphones or tablets
- Dispatch capabilities for assigning and updating loads and routes
- Two-way communication between dispatch/supervisors and drivers for real-time updates and messaging

### Data & Analytics
- Usage monitoring for equipment and fleets
- Analytics around haul cycles, idle time, and equipment utilization to support operational and cost decisions
