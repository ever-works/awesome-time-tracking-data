## Overview

Klokki is an automatic time tracking application for Mac that creates rules to trigger timers based on the work you do, designed specifically for freelancers who want to reduce manual timer management.

## How It Works

### Rule-Based Automation

Klokki automatically tracks time by creating rules that will trigger the timer based on the work you do:

- **Application-Based Rules**: Start timer when specific apps open
- **Window Title Rules**: Track based on document or project names
- **URL Rules**: Trigger timers for specific websites
- **Custom Conditions**: Combine multiple criteria

### Automatic Tracking Benefits

- Reduces need to remember manual timer start/stop
- Captures time even when you forget
- Learns from your work patterns
- Ensures more accurate time records
