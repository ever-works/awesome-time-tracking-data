# Breeze

**Website:** https://www.breeze.pm/

**Category:** Time tracking & project management

**Summary**  
Breeze is a simple project management tool for teams, combining task and project organization with built‑in time tracking so teams can log hours, monitor progress, and report on billable and non‑billable work.

## Features

- **Project management** – Organize work into projects to keep initiatives structured and trackable.
- **Task management** – Create, assign, and manage tasks for individuals and teams.
- **Kanban boards** – Visualize workflow and task progress using board-style views.
- **Time tracking**  
  - Track time spent on tasks and projects.  
  - Support for **billable and non‑billable hours**.
- **Project reporting** – Generate project reports based on tasks, time tracked, and progress data.
- **Project calendar** – View tasks and key dates on a calendar to plan schedules and deadlines.
- **Project timeline** – Timeline views for understanding project duration, phases, and dependencies at a high level.
- **Team workload management** – See team workload to balance assignments and avoid overloading people.
- **Project automation** – Automate repetitive project and task workflows.
- **Project roadmap** – Plan and visualize long‑term project goals and milestones.
- **Breeze Chat** – Built‑in chat for team communication around projects and tasks.
- **Breeze Forms** – Capture structured inputs (e.g., requests or briefs) directly into Breeze as tasks or items.
- **AI features** – AI-assisted capabilities (e.g., for organizing, summarizing, or planning within projects).
- **Project “puzzle” / modular tools** – Flexible components to tailor project workflows (as hinted by project_puzzle).
- **Mobile access** – Mobile-friendly access/apps for working on the go.
- **Integrations** – Connect Breeze with other tools via integrations.
- **Developer & API options** – Developer resources and APIs for custom integrations or extensions.
- **Apps** – Dedicated apps (e.g., desktop or mobile) for using Breeze outside the browser.
