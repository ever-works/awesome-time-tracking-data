## Overview

Timesheets.com offers employee timesheet software with direct payroll integration to QuickBooks and other major payroll systems, operating since 2004.

## Payroll Integration

Hours-to-payroll sync with:
- Intuit QuickBooks (Desktop & Online)
- ADP
- Microsoft Dynamics
- SurePayroll
- Other payroll services via export

## Key Features

### Time Tracking
- Real-time clock punches or manual entry
- GPS location tracking
- IP Lock for location verification
- Electronic signatures
- Mobile time clock

### Compliance
- FLSA timesheet guidelines
- State labor timesheeting standards
- Customizable templates
- Audit trails
- Approval processes

### Expense Tracking
- Mileage reporting
- Expense management
- Bonuses and per diems
- Cash advances

## Support

Live support by phone, chat, or email included.

- Small businesses with hourly employees
- Companies using QuickBooks for accounting
- Organizations requiring labor compliance
