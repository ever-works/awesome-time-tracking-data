## Overview

MyCase is a legal practice management solution that includes robust time tracking and billing capabilities. The platform is notable for its Smart Time Finder feature that helps attorneys recover lost billable hours.

## Smart Time Finder

MyCase's proprietary tool that analyzes activities and suggests time entries attorneys may have forgotten to record. In 2023:
- Customers captured additional 579,665 hours
- Average value per lawyer: approximately $22,425
- Automated recovery of billable work

## Key Features

### Time Tracking
- One-click timers from case screens
- Mobile time capture
- Batch time entry
- Smart suggestions for missed time
- Calendar integration

### Billing Integration
- Time entries sync directly to invoices
- Customizable rate tables
- Multiple billing methods (hourly, flat fee, contingency)
- Online payment processing
- Client payment portals

### Practice Management Integration
- Calendaring
- Document management
- Client communication
- Task management
- Case tracking
- Lead management

## Target Audience

Law firms of all sizes seeking comprehensive practice management with strong time tracking capabilities.

## Unique Advantage

Smart Time Finder helps recover billable hours that would otherwise be lost, directly impacting firm revenue.
