## Overview

Rewatch is the only solution that combines an AI-powered notetaker, screen recorder, and collaborative video hub — so you can eliminate useless meetings, reduce costs, and stay focused on your work.

## Acquisition by Atlassian

Now acquired by Atlassian, Rewatch will integrate with Loom to provide AI meeting assistant capabilities, expanding Atlassian's video collaboration ecosystem.
