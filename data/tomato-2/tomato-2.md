## Overview

Tomato 2 is a simple application for increasing personal productivity using the Pomodoro Technique. Work for 25 minutes, take a 5 minute break, then repeat.

## Features

- **Silent Mode**: Go to silent mode and don't be interrupted when your session ends
- **5 More Minutes**: Option for when you're in the middle of something important
- **Always-Visible Break Timer**: Never lose track of your break time
- **Session Goals**: Ability to set goals for your current session
- **Customizable Durations**: Configurable focus and break durations
- **Short and Long Breaks**: Support for both types of breaks
- **Weekly Chart**: Track your productivity over time
- **Website Blocking**: Optional in-app purchase to block distracting websites

## Design

Tomato 2 is carefully designed to look great in both light and dark mode, following your system settings automatically.

## Pricing

Free to download from the Mac App Store with an optional in-app purchase for website blocking.
