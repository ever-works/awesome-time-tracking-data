## Overview

hh2 Remote Payroll is built specifically for deep integration with construction ERPs, such as Sage 300 CRE, Sage 100, Sage Intacct, Foundation, and Acumatica. Instead of sending flat files back and forth, it connects directly to the existing accounting system.

For accounting teams, this matters. hh2 pulls project files, cost codes, general ledger accounts, union locals, and certified payroll classifications directly into the field app. When a foreman codes time on a job site, they use the same accounting framework as the finance team.

That alignment reduces payroll processing time and lowers the risk of mapping errors. Instead of correcting timecards after export, labor costs are entered directly into the ERP, with the correct structure already applied.
