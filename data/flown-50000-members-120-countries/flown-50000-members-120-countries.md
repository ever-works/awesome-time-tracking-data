## Global Community Scale

Since its 2020 founding in London, Flown has grown to serve over 50,000 members across more than 120 countries worldwide.

## Growth Trajectory

### Post-Pandemic Acceleration
- Founded in 2020 during COVID-19 pandemic
- Addressed surge in remote work challenges
- Rapid adoption as remote work became permanent
- Sustained growth post-pandemic

### Geographic Diversity
- Members on every continent
- 24/7 session availability across time zones
- Cultural diversity in community
- Global perspective on productivity
