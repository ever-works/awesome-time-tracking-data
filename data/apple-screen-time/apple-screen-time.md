## Overview

Apple Screen Time is a built-in feature providing comprehensive tracking and controls for device usage across iPhone, iPad, and Mac, helping users develop healthier digital habits.

## Features

- **Daily & Weekly Reports**: Detailed breakdowns of device and app usage
- **App Limits**: Set time limits for specific apps or app categories
- **Downtime**: Schedule time away from screens
- **Always Allowed**: Whitelist essential apps during downtime
- **Communication Limits**: Control who can be reached and when
- **Content & Privacy**: Restrict inappropriate content and purchases
- **Cross-Device Tracking**: See usage across all Apple devices
- **Family Sharing**: Monitor and control children's device usage
- **App Usage Trends**: Identify usage patterns over time

## Time Awareness Benefits

Provides visibility into how time is actually spent on devices, enabling more conscious decisions about digital time allocation and reducing time-wasting behaviors.

## Platform

iOS 12+, iPadOS, and macOS Catalina or later.

## Pricing

Free, included with all compatible Apple devices.

## Setup

Settings > Screen Time on iOS/iPadOS, or System Preferences > Screen Time on Mac.
