## Overview

Insightful provides healthcare-specific time tracking and workforce management software with built-in HIPAA compliance. It helps healthcare organizations track staff productivity while maintaining patient data security.

## Features

- HIPAA-compliant infrastructure
- Dual data encryption (in transit and at rest)
- Twice-hourly automated data backups
- Automatic time tracking
- Productivity monitoring without PHI access
- Shift and schedule management
- Remote and on-site staff tracking
- Department-level analytics
- Attendance tracking
- Integration with healthcare HR systems
- Real-time dashboards
- Compliance reporting
- Privacy-focused monitoring
- Activity categorization

## Pricing

Subscription-based pricing per user. Healthcare packages with HIPAA compliance features. Free trial and demo available.
