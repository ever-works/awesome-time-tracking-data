## Overview

Vitamin-R 4 is the latest version of the time boxing productivity tool for macOS. Since its first release in 2010, Vitamin-R has helped tens of thousands of creative professionals, software developers, and ADHD sufferers find focus, overcome procrastination, and maintain motivation.

## Key Features

- **Time Slicing**: Structures work around alternating sets of highly focused "time slices" of 15-25 minutes
- **Customizable Interface**: Offers unrivaled customizability from minimalist single-pane to full-featured window
- **Task Management Integration**: Works with OmniFocus, Things, and other task managers
- **Built-in Noise Machine**: Ambient sounds to help maintain focus
- **Distraction Blocking**: Blocks distracting websites and applications
- **Log Book & Analytics**: Sophisticated tracking and analysis of productivity patterns
- **Breadcrumbing**: Helps you pick up where you left off when switching tasks
- **Now and Later Board**: Quick dump for tasks and ideas without losing focus

## Requirements

macOS 13.5 or later, with native support for Intel and Apple Silicon Macs.
