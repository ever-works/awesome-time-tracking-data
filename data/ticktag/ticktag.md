## Overview

TickTag takes a unique approach to time tracking by using hashtags as the primary organization method. Every hashtag you create becomes a smart category that can be customized with colors, descriptions, and billing rates.

## Features

- **Hashtag-Based Tracking**: Use hashtags to organize and categorize your time entries
- **Smart Categories**: Each hashtag becomes an intelligent category you can customize
- **Color Coding**: Assign colors to different hashtags for visual organization
- **Hourly Rates**: Attach billing rates to specific hashtags for freelance work
- **Descriptions**: Add context and notes to each hashtag category
- **macOS Native**: Built specifically for macOS with native design and performance

## Use Cases

TickTag is ideal for freelancers, consultants, and professionals who need flexible, tag-based time tracking that adapts to their workflow rather than forcing rigid project structures.

## Launch

TickTag was launched on Product Hunt in October 2025.

## Platform

macOS only.
