## Overview

Rippling is an integrated workforce management platform that unifies HR, IT, and finance tools to streamline operations. The time and attendance module connects directly with payroll, benefits, and compliance systems, creating a seamless employee lifecycle management experience.

## Time & Attendance Features

- **Automated Time Tracking** - Clock in/out via desktop, mobile, kiosk, or badge readers
- **Geofencing** - Location-based attendance verification for field and office workers
- **Automated Timesheet Approval** - Workflow-based approvals with manager notifications
- **Overtime Calculation** - Automatic overtime tracking compliant with labor laws
- **PTO Integration** - Time-off requests automatically adjust schedules and timesheets
- **Compliance Monitoring** - Built-in checks for labor law compliance across jurisdictions
- **Break Tracking** - Ensure meal and rest break compliance
- **Shift Scheduling** - Create and manage employee schedules

## Integration Advantages

Rippling's value comes from deep integration across workforce systems:

**Payroll Integration** - Time data flows directly into payroll processing, eliminating manual entry and reducing errors. Overtime, PTO, and regular hours automatically calculate gross pay.

**HR Automation** - New hires are automatically set up in time tracking when onboarded. Terminations immediately revoke time tracking access.

**Benefits Administration** - Hours worked determine benefits eligibility and 401(k) contributions.

**Expense Management** - Combine time tracking with expense reports for complete project costing.

## Compliance & Labor Laws

- **Multi-State Compliance** - Automatically applies correct labor laws based on work location
- **Audit Trails** - Complete record of all time entries and modifications
- **FLSA Compliance** - Proper exempt/non-exempt classification and overtime rules
- **Break Law Compliance** - Enforces meal and rest break requirements by jurisdiction

- Multi-state employers requiring compliance automation
- Organizations consolidating fragmented HR technology
- Growing businesses wanting scalable workforce infrastructure
