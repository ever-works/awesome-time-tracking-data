## Overview

FreeAgent is a UK-focused accounting tool with smart invoicing and tax features built specifically for freelancers, contractors, and small businesses. It combines comprehensive time tracking with full accounting functionality.

## Features

- Time tracking and project management
- Invoicing with customizable templates
- Expense tracking with receipt capture
- Bank feeds for automatic transaction import
- Self Assessment and MTD-compliant tax preparation
- Payroll management
- Project profitability tracking
- Mileage tracking
- CIS (Construction Industry Scheme) support
- Multi-currency support
- Integration with major UK banks

## Time Tracking Capabilities

Projects, time tracking, and expense claims are fully integrated within FreeAgent. You can create timesheets for yourself and employees, track billable and non-billable hours, and automatically convert tracked time into invoices.

## Pricing

Pricing starts at approximately £24/month with different tiers based on business needs. Free trials are available to test the platform.
