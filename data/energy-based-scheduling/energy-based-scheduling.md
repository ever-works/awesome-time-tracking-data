## Overview

Energy-Based Scheduling is a time management approach that prioritizes managing your energy levels over managing time. Instead of following a rigid schedule, you align your tasks with your natural energy fluctuations throughout the day.

## Core Principles

- Identify your personal energy patterns (morning person vs. night owl)
- Schedule high-cognitive tasks during peak energy periods
- Reserve low-energy times for routine, administrative work
- Take strategic breaks to maintain energy levels
- Recognize that energy, not time, is your most valuable resource
- Monitor and track energy levels to optimize scheduling

## Implementation

1. Track your energy levels throughout the day for 1-2 weeks
2. Identify patterns in your energy peaks and troughs
3. Categorize tasks by energy requirement (high, medium, low)
4. Schedule demanding work during high-energy periods
5. Use low-energy times for emails, meetings, and admin tasks
6. Include recovery activities and breaks in your schedule

## Benefits

- Improved work quality and creativity
- Reduced burnout and fatigue
- Better work-life balance
- Increased productivity without working longer hours
- More sustainable work habits
