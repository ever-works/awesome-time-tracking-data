## Overview

Julie Morgenstern is an organizing and time management expert, New York Times best-selling author, consultant, and speaker. She specializes in productivity, work-life balance, time management, change management, and organizing systems.

## Notable Works

- **Organizing from the Inside Out** - A revolutionary approach to organizing that works with individual psychology and habits
- **Time Management from the Inside Out** - A personalized approach to time management
- **Dare to Disappoint: Living a Brave and Authentic Life**
- **Never Check Email in the Morning** - Strategies for prioritizing and managing daily work
