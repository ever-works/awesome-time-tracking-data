## Overview

Focus Keeper provides a straightforward Pomodoro timer experience, staying true to Francesco Cirillo's original time management technique.

## Features

- **Classic Pomodoro Timer**: 25-minute work sessions with 5-minute breaks
- **Session Tracking**: Count completed Pomodoros
- **Simple Interface**: Clean, minimalist design focused on functionality
- **Work/Break Management**: Automatic transitions between work and break periods
- **Long Break Support**: Extended breaks after multiple Pomodoros
- **Customizable Intervals**: Adjust work and break durations
- **Statistics**: Track productivity patterns over time
- **Notifications**: Alerts for session transitions

## Pricing

Free version available with premium features through in-app purchase.
