## Overview

WaspTime RFID is a contactless time and attendance tracking system that uses RFID proximity badges to automatically record employee clock-ins and clock-outs, ideal for manufacturing, warehouse, and industrial environments.

## Features

- **RFID Time Clocks**: Touchless proximity badge readers for quick clock in/out
- **Proximity Badges**: RFID badges or key fobs for each employee
- **Automatic Tracking**: Eliminates manual time entry and buddy punching
- **Multiple Locations**: Support for multiple time clock terminals across facilities
- **Real-Time Data**: Instant sync of time data to central system
- **Overtime Alerts**: Automatic notifications when employees approach overtime
- **Payroll Integration**: Direct export to ADP, Paychex, QuickBooks, and other payroll systems
- **Job Costing**: Track time by job or department for accurate cost allocation
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive reports on attendance, hours worked, and labor costs
- **Compliance**: Helps ensure compliance with labor laws and union regulations

## Pricing

Standard Edition starts at approximately $695 for software plus hardware costs for RFID readers ($300-500 each). Enterprise Edition with advanced features available for larger organizations.
