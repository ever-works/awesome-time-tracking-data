## Overview

Productive is an all-in-one agency management platform that combines time tracking, project management, resource planning, and financial management. It's designed specifically for agencies and professional services firms.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Track billable and non-billable hours with timer or manual entry
- **Project Management**: Manage projects with tasks, budgets, and deadlines
- **Resource Planning**: Schedule team members and balance workload across projects
- **Budgeting**: Set project budgets and track spending in real-time
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked time and project milestones
- **Financial Reporting**: P&L reports, utilization rates, and profitability analysis per project
- **Sales Pipeline**: Manage deals and track conversion from lead to project
- **Forecasting**: Predict revenue and resource needs based on pipeline

## Pricing

Productive offers tiered pricing starting at $11 per user per month for essential features. Professional and Ultimate tiers provide advanced financial management, custom fields, and API access. Contact their team for detailed pricing.
