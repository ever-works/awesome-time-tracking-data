## Overview

Nifty is a project management platform that combines task management, collaboration tools, and integrated time tracking to help teams manage projects from start to finish. Time-tracking and reporting tools are available in the Starter plan and higher tiers.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracking for tasks and projects
- **Project Management**: Tasks, milestones, and project timelines
- **Time Reporting**: Detailed reports on time spent across projects and team members
- **Task Management**: Create, assign, and track tasks with dependencies
- **Team Collaboration**: Discussions, file sharing, and real-time updates
- **Gantt Charts**: Visual project timelines and dependencies
- **Calendar View**: See all tasks and deadlines in calendar format
- **Workload Management**: Monitor team capacity and distribution
- **Document Management**: Create and share project documentation
- **Integrations**: Connect with Slack, Google Drive, Zoom, and more
- **Automated Workflows**: Automate repetitive tasks and notifications
- **Client Portal**: Share project progress with clients
- **Custom Fields**: Tailor projects to your specific workflow needs

## Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Basic features for small teams (up to 2 projects)
- **Starter**: Time tracking included, suitable for growing teams
- **Pro**: Advanced features with enhanced time tracking and reporting
- **Business**: Enterprise features with custom solutions

Exact pricing available on the Nifty website.

## Best For

Teams seeking an all-in-one project management solution with integrated time tracking, without needing to rely on third-party time tracking tools. Ideal for agencies, creative teams, and professional services.

## Key Advantage

Unlike some competitors (like Basecamp), Nifty includes native time tracking features, eliminating the need for third-party integrations and providing a more seamless workflow.
