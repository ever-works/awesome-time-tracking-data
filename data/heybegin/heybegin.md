## Overview

Heybegin is a time tracking platform specifically built for the hospitality and service industries, including restaurants, cafés, and similar businesses. It combines time tracking with workforce management features in a unified platform.

## Features

- Smart scheduling for shift-based teams
- Real-time time tracking
- Automated timesheets
- Payroll integration
- Multi-location management
- Compliance checks for labor regulations
- Claims 10% reduction in labor costs

## Pricing

- Free plan available
- Paid plans starting at €49 per month
- Advanced features reserved for higher tiers

## Best For

Hospitality and service industry businesses with shift-based teams, multi-location operations, and a need for integrated scheduling and time tracking.
