## Overview

Freelance Pricing Calculator provides data-backed hourly, daily, and project rates for your industry. It considers your entire financial picture—including business expenses, tax obligations, non-billable hours, and desired lifestyle—to reverse-engineer the exact rates you need to charge.

## Comprehensive Calculation Approach

### Complete Financial Picture
- Business expenses
- Tax obligations (25-35% typically)
- Non-billable hours
- Desired lifestyle costs
- Savings goals
- Emergency fund

### Reverse Engineering
Starts with your end goal and works backwards to determine necessary rates.

## Key Considerations

### Tax Planning
- 25-35% depending on location
- 30% safe buffer if unsure
- Deduction considerations
- Tax strategy optimization

### Platform Fees
- Fiverr: typically 20%
- Upwork: 10-20%
- Other platforms: 10-20% range
- Direct clients: 0%

### Billable Hours Reality
- 1000-1200 billable hours/year for full-time freelancing
- 15-30 billable hours/week average
- Non-billable: admin, marketing, client communication

## Calculation Examples

### $100k Annual Goal
- Need to charge: $75-$90/hour
- Accounts for: taxes, expenses, non-billable time
- Assumes: 1100 billable hours/year

### Platform Freelancing
- Quote rate must include platform fees
- Example: $100 project = $120 quote (20% fee)
- Always calculate net after fees
