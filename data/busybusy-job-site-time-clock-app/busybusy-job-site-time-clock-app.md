### busybusy Job Site Time Clock App

**Category:** Attendance & Payroll Management  
**Best for:** Construction and mobile trade companies managing field crews across multiple job sites.

#### Overview
busybusy Job Site Time Clock App is a job site time tracking system for construction and other mobile trade crews. It turns smartphones or tablets (Android/iOS) into on-site time clocks so workers can clock in and out on the job site, while supervisors capture accurate, project- and location-specific time data.

#### Features
- **Mobile time clock on job sites**  
  - Uses Android or iOS phones and tablets as job site time clocks.  
  - Supports fixed kiosks or shared devices on-site for crew clock-in/clock-out.

- **GPS-based time tracking**  
  - Captures GPS-based time data to show where time is spent in the field.  
  - Records location-specific time entries for greater accuracy on mobile job sites.

- **Project and task-level tracking**  
  - Allows supervisors to pinpoint how much time workers spend on specific tasks.  
  - Associates time entries with particular projects or job sites for better job costing.

- **Supervisor visibility**  
  - Gives field supervisors insight into crew time usage across changing job sites.  
  - Helps monitor whether workers are on-site and how their time is allocated.

- **Improved job costing and cost control**  
  - Time data can be used to better calculate job site costs and overhead.  
  - Supports analysis of labor utilization and productivity on projects.

- **Replaces paper timesheets**  
  - Digitizes time collection for crews who move between job sites.  
  - Reduces manual timesheet handling and associated errors.

- **Bilingual interface**  
  - Available in both Spanish and English for workers and employers.
