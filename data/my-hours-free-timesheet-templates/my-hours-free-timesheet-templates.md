# My Hours Free Timesheet Templates

Collection of ready-made timesheet files for tracking work hours without dedicated time-tracking software.

- **Provider:** My Hours  
- **Category:** Time tracking utilities & calculators  
- **Formats available:** Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word, PDF, Google Sheets

## Description
My Hours Free Timesheet Templates are downloadable timesheet files designed to help individuals and teams record work hours, overtime, time off, and breaks. They can support basic time tracking, payroll calculations, and client billing using common office tools instead of specialized software.

## Features
- **Multiple file formats**  
  - Templates usable in **Excel**, **Word**, **PDF**, and **Google Sheets**.

- **Pre-made timekeeping structure**  
  - Ready-made tables for entering employee work hours.  
  - Suitable for companies or individuals that need a simple, document-based way to track time.

- **Work-hour tracking**  
  - Record **daily work hours** for employees.  
  - Track **overtime hours**.  
  - Capture **vacation/leave** entries.  
  - Record **lunch breaks**.

- **Payroll & billing support**  
  - Fields for **hourly-based “Total pay”** calculation.  
  - Use "Total pay" fields for **payroll calculations**.  
  - Use recorded hours and pay fields to support **client billing**.

- **Guided usage**  
  - Each template includes an **instructions panel** explaining how to use the sheet.  
  - Includes an **example filled template** to demonstrate correct usage.  
  - Includes a **blank template** ready for entering actual work hours.

## Weekly Timesheet Template
A specific template type focused on weekly tracking.

- **Purpose**  
  - Shows a **single employee’s work hours** over a **single workweek**.  
  - Creates an overview of an employee’s **tasks and other details** for that week.

- **Use cases**  
  - Weekly **payroll calculation** for individual employees.  
  - Tracking **overtime** on a weekly basis.  
  - Preparing **hourly-based client billing**, using "Total pay" to see billable hours and amounts.

- **Formats**  
  - Available in **Excel**, **Google Sheets**, and **Word** (based on provided download icons).
