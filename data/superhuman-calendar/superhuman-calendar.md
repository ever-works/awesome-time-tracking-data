## Overview

Superhuman launched advanced AI-powered calendar features integrated with its email client, offering intelligent scheduling, availability sharing, and automated meeting management. The company is expanding its calendar offering and considering a standalone calendar app.

## Features

- **Instant Event**: AI suggestions to help schedule meetings based on email content
- **Availability Sharing**: Share your calendar availability directly from email conversations
- **Automatic Scheduling**: AI-powered feature that automatically finds optimal meeting times
- **Meeting Briefing Dossier**: Automated preparation materials for upcoming meetings
- **Email-Calendar Integration**: Seamless connection between inbox and calendar events
- **Smart Suggestions**: Context-aware scheduling recommendations based on email threads

## AI Capabilities

Superhuman's AI analyzes email content to suggest meeting times, identify scheduling opportunities, and prepare relevant information for upcoming calls. The system learns from your patterns to optimize calendar management.

## Benefits

- Reduces time spent on scheduling coordination
- Keeps calendar and email in sync automatically
- Provides context for meetings through AI-generated briefings
- Streamlines the entire scheduling workflow
