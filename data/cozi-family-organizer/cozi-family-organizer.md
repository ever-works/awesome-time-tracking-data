## Overview

Cozi Family Organizer is the surprisingly simple way to manage everyday family life. A 3-time Mom's Choice Award Winner and The TODAY Show "must-have app" for a better life.

## Key Time Management Features

### Shared Calendar

- View shared schedules in a color-coded calendar from any computer or mobile device
- Add up to 12 members, each with their own name and color
- Events appear in assigned colors in the calendar
- Filter the view to show all family members or just one individual

### Notifications & Reminders

- Others in the family stay up to date with automatic notifications and agenda emails
- Set reminders and send automated daily or weekly agenda emails to family members

### Accessibility

- Accessible from any device
- Lets the whole family collaborate in one secure place
- Includes "Cozi Today" — a morning snapshot of daily plans, to-dos, and shopping lists

## Additional Features

- Coordinate and communicate schedules and activities
- Track grocery lists
- Manage to-do-lists
- Plan ahead for dinner
- Keep the whole family on the same page

## Pricing

Free to use, with an optional premium subscription (Cozi Gold) that adds features like extended calendar views and additional reminders.
