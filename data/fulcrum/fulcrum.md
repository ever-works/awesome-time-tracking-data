## Overview

Fulcrum is a manufacturing execution system (MES) designed to give manufacturers real-time visibility and control over shop floor operations, with comprehensive job tracking and labor time capture capabilities.

## Features

- **Job Tracking**: Track jobs through all production stages in real-time
- **Labor Time Capture**: Automatic recording of setup time, run time, and labor hours
- **Machine Monitoring**: Track machine time, downtime, and utilization
- **Production Tracking**: Monitor quantities produced, scrap, and work-in-process
- **Barcode Scanning**: Use barcodes to clock in/out of jobs and operations
- **Digital Work Instructions**: Display instructions, drawings, and specs at workstations
- **Real-Time Dashboards**: Live visibility into shop floor status and bottlenecks
- **Inventory Tracking**: Monitor raw materials, WIP, and finished goods
- **Quality Tracking**: Record in-process inspections and quality data
- **Scheduling**: Visual production schedule with drag-and-drop adjustments
- **Reporting**: Detailed reports on job costs, labor efficiency, and production metrics
- **Integration**: Connect with ERP, accounting, and CAD systems

## Benefits

Eliminates manual time cards and job travelers, provides accurate job costing with actual labor and machine hours, identifies production bottlenecks in real-time, and improves on-time delivery.

## Target Audience

Made-to-order and engineer-to-order manufacturers, job shops, and contract manufacturers who need detailed tracking of custom jobs.
