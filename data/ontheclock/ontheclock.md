## Overview

OnTheClock is a simple, easy-to-use employee time clock and scheduling software designed as the best budget-friendly choice for small teams that need reliable digital time tracking.

## Features

### Time Clock
- **Web-Based Access**: Clock in from any device with internet connection
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for remote workers
- **Multiple Clock-In Methods**: Web portal, phone app, SMS, or phone call
- **GPS Tracking**: Automatic location capture at clock-in/out
- **Photo Verification**: Optional photo capture to prevent buddy punching

### Scheduling
- **Employee Scheduling**: Create and publish work schedules
- **Shift Reminders**: Automatic notifications for upcoming shifts
- **Schedule Templates**: Save and reuse common scheduling patterns

### Payroll & Reporting
- **Payroll Integration**: Export time data to various payroll systems
- **Overtime Tracking**: Automatic overtime calculation based on configurable rules
- **PTO Management**: Track vacation, sick days, and other time off
- **Detailed Reports**: Generate reports on hours worked, labor costs, and attendance

### Administration
- **Timesheet Approval**: Managers can review and approve timesheets
- **Department Tracking**: Organize employees by department or location
- **Job Code Tracking**: Track time spent on different job codes or projects

## Benefits
