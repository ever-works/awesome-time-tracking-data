## Overview

Structured is a beautifully designed iOS app for visual day planning and time blocking. It presents your day as an interactive timeline that combines calendar events and tasks, making it easy to visualize and optimize your daily schedule.

## Key Features

- **Visual Timeline**: See your entire day at a glance
- **Task & Calendar Integration**: Unified view of events and tasks
- **Drag-and-Drop**: Easily reschedule items
- **Focus Timer**: Built-in timer for time-boxed work
- **Recurring Tasks**: Set up daily routines
- **Widgets**: Home screen and lock screen widgets
- **Time Estimates**: Add duration to tasks
- **Subtasks**: Break down complex tasks
- **Calendar Sync**: Two-way sync with Apple Calendar
- **Dark Mode**: Beautiful dark theme
- **Shortcuts**: iOS Shortcuts integration
- **Notifications**: Reminders for upcoming items
