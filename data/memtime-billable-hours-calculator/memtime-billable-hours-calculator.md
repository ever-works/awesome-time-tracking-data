## Overview

The Memtime Billable Hours Calculator is a sophisticated free online tool that helps consultants, freelancers, and professional services firms calculate billable revenue, analyze utilization rates, and project profitability based on tracked hours and billing parameters.

## Key Calculations

### Total Billable Revenue
Computes money that can be invoiced for all billable hours logged:
- Billable hours × hourly rate
- Minus discounts
- Plus expenses and disbursements
- Equals total invoiceable amount

### Billable Efficiency (Utilization Rate)
Measures how much time was spent on billable work against total time logged:
- Formula: (Billable Hours / Total Hours Logged) × 100
- Industry benchmark: 60-80% for consultants
- Helps identify revenue leakage

### Revenue Projections
Forecasts income based on:
- Current billable efficiency rate
- Available working hours
- Target hourly rates
- Expected client mix

## Inputs Required

- Billable hours worked
