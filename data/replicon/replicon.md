## Overview

Replicon is a leading cloud-based project time tracking platform (now part of Deltek) that helps mid-market and enterprise organizations manage time, projects, and workforce operations with accuracy and confidence. Built on the Replicon Time Intelligence™ Platform, it provides a single source of truth for all time data.

## Features

### AI-Powered Time Tracking
- Intelligent, AI-enabled timesheets
- Reduces manual effort
- Eliminates missed billable or payroll hours
- Automatically captures employee time for projects, clients, and internal work
- Accurate time capture for precise billing and payroll

### Workforce Management
- Attendance and scheduling management
- Labor compliance for shift-based teams
- Automatically calculates gross pay
- Real-time visibility into time tracking
- Reduces timesheet errors
- Simplifies workforce management for any team

### Compliance & Pay Rules
- Pre-configured rules in 75+ countries
- Automated overtime, breaks, pay, and leave calculations
- Calculates pay accurately
- Reduces compliance risk
- Industry-specific compliance features

### Mobile & Field Capabilities
- Mobile time capture for distributed teams
- Cloud clock for field workers
- Geo-location tracking
- Offline entry with automatic sync
- Multilingual and multi-currency support
- Global scalability

### Integration
- Connects with payroll, billing, and ERP systems
- Integration with ADP Workforce Now
- Seamless data flow across business systems
