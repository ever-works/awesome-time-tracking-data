## busybusy Landscaping Time Tracking Software

### Overview
busybusy Landscaping Time Tracking Software is a cloud-based, mobile time tracking solution designed for landscaping businesses. It helps track crew hours by property and task, replace paper time cards with digital timesheets, and provide accurate labor data for payroll and job costing.

### Features
- **Landscaping-focused time tracking**  
  - Track employee and crew hours for landscaping jobs.  
  - Associate time with specific properties, jobs, and tasks.  
  - Replace paper time cards with digital time tracking.

- **Mobile time clock & timesheets**  
  - Employees clock in/out from the field using mobile devices.  
  - Timesheets are stored digitally in the cloud.  
  - Real-time visibility into who is working and where.

- **Job costing & reporting**  
  - Reporting tools to view labor statistics from past jobs.  
  - Analyze which projects come in under budget.  
  - Use labor data to understand job profitability.  
  - Time data can feed into payroll and job costing processes.

- **GPS-related capabilities**  
  - GPS support (tagged feature) to add location context to time entries and crews in the field.

- **Field communication & documentation**  
  - Company messaging (internal communication feature indicated by "Company Messaging").  
  - Capture and store project photos associated with jobs ("Project Photos").

- **Platforms & access**  
  - Native iOS app (available via Apple App Store).  
  - Native Android app (available via Google Play).  
  - Cloud-based storage for time and job data.
