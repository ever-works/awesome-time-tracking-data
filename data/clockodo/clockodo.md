## Overview

Clockodo is a cloud-based time tracking software solution designed for small to medium-sized businesses. It provides simple, efficient time tracking combined with project management, reporting, and team collaboration features.

## Features

- **Easy Time Tracking**: Simple timer and manual time entry options
- **Project Management**: Organize time entries by projects and clients
- **Team Collaboration**: Multiple users can track time and collaborate on projects
- **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports on time spent, project progress, and team productivity
- **Invoicing Support**: Export time data for billing and invoicing purposes
- **Absence Management**: Track vacation days, sick leave, and other absences
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for tracking time on the go
- **Integrations**: Connect with other business tools and accounting software
- **Customer Management**: Organize clients and projects hierarchically
- **Flexible Views**: Multiple views for analyzing time data
- **Export Options**: Export data in various formats for further analysis
- **User Permissions**: Control access levels for different team members

## Best For

Small to medium-sized businesses, freelancers, and agencies looking for straightforward time tracking without overwhelming complexity.
