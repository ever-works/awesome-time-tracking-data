## Overview

Chronos Timesheet is a lightweight time tracking solution designed for small businesses, consultants, and freelancers who need simple, effective timesheet management without enterprise-level complexity.

## Features

- Time tracking with start/stop timers
- Manual time entry
- Project and task organization
- Expense tracking
- Timesheet approvals
- Invoicing and billing
- Customizable reports
- Client management
- Multiple billing rates
- Overtime calculation
- Export to Excel and PDF
- Multi-user support
