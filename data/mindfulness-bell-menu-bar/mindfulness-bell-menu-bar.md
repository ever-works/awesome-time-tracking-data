## Overview

Mindfulness Bell Menu Bar is a macOS application that lives in your menu bar and effortlessly integrates mindfulness into your daily routine with timely reminders to pause and breathe, helping reduce workday stress and anxiety.

## Features

- **Menu Bar Integration**: Sits unobtrusively in your macOS menu bar
- **Customizable Bell Intervals**: Set fixed or random intervals for mindfulness reminders
- **Pleasant Bell Sounds**: Choose from various calming bell sounds
- **Non-Intrusive Notifications**: Gentle reminders that don't disrupt your workflow
- **Background Operation**: Works silently in the background with minimal resource usage
- **Mindfulness Practice Support**: Based on practices from Buddhist monasteries

## How It Works

When the bell rings, the practice is to pause everything and return to your breath, helping you stay present and mindful throughout your workday.

## Platform

macOS only

## Use Cases

- Reducing workplace stress and anxiety
- Building a consistent mindfulness practice
- Taking regular mental breaks during focused work
- Preventing burnout in demanding work environments
