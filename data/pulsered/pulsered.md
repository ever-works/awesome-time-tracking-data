## Overview

Pulse.red is a powerful time tracker designed for those who appreciate simplicity. Created after years of working with overloaded time tracking solutions, Pulse focuses on what matters most without bells and whistles.

## Key Features

- **Simple Time Entry**: Track time manually or use a timer, with friendly email reminders if you forget to turn off the timer
- **Team Collaboration**: Colleagues can see live who is working on what
- **Minimal Reports**: Natural simple reports of hours spent by each team member on every project, without pointless infographics
- **Multi-Device Support**: Team members can track time spent on tasks from any device
- **Intuitive Design**: No manuals or tooltips needed - using Pulse is very intuitive

## Design Philosophy

Pulse was created in response to time tracking solutions that were overloaded with integrations, billing modules and other features that distracted from core time tracking functionality. The focus is on staying focused on the main things that matter.
