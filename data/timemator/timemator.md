## Overview

Timemator is an automatic time tracking app designed specifically for the Apple ecosystem that lets you automatically record your working time by tracking your project files, applications, or websites. Forget about the timer forever with intelligent automation.

## Features

### Automatic Tracking
- Automatically tracks project files, applications, and websites
- Rules-based automation for hands-free time tracking
- No manual timer starting/stopping required
- Intelligent activity detection

### Apple Ecosystem Integration
- Native apps for macOS, iOS, and watchOS
- iCloud sync seamlessly syncs data across all devices
- Leverages native Apple capabilities for optimal performance
- Apple Watch support for quick time tracking on the go

### Timesheet Creation
- Easily create timesheets from tracked time
- Export capabilities for billing and reporting
- Project and task organization
- Detailed time reports

## Platform Support

- macOS (native app)
- iOS (iPhone and iPad)
- watchOS (Apple Watch)
- iCloud sync across all devices

## Benefits

- **Truly Automatic**: Set up rules once and let Timemator track automatically
- **Ecosystem Integration**: Designed specifically for Apple devices with deep system integration
- **Privacy**: All data synced via iCloud, keeping your information private
- **No Manual Input**: Forget about starting and stopping timers

## Ideal For

Mac and iOS users who want automatic time tracking that integrates seamlessly with their Apple devices and leverages iCloud for secure, private data synchronization.
