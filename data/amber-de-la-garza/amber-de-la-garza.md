## Overview

Amber De La Garza is a productivity consultant, coach, trainer, speaker, and writer who specializes in helping small business owners improve their time management and elevate their productivity.

## Leverage Lab

Creator of Leverage Lab, a program designed to help small business owners maximize profits, reduce stress, and make time for what matters most through strategic productivity improvements.

## Productivity Straight Talk Podcast

Host of the Productivity Straight Talk Podcast, where she shares practical time management strategies and productivity tips tailored to entrepreneurs and small business owners.

## Key Focus Areas

- Time management for small business owners
- Creating sustainable productivity systems
- Reducing stress through better task management
- Leveraging tools and processes for maximum efficiency
- Work-life integration for entrepreneurs
