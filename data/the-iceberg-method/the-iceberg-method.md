## Overview

The Iceberg Method is a knowledge management and information organization technique that helps you convert raw information into actionable insights.

## How It Works

1. **Capture** - Save all emails, notes, articles, and other information you encounter in a digital system
2. **Categorize** - Organize information using folders and tags for easy retrieval
3. **Monthly Review** - Review everything you've stored each month
4. **Brainstorm Application** - Consider how captured information can be applied to your current work and projects

This method ensures that valuable information doesn't get lost and gets actively used rather than passively collected.
