## Overview

Sorted³ is the world's first "Hyper-Scheduler" that seamlessly integrates your calendar events and tasks into one unified timeline so you can manage your entire day from a single place.

## Hyper-Scheduling Concept

Hyper-scheduling is a foundational approach to add more focus to your day and minimize stress by combining tasks, calendar events, and notes in one continuous timeline. Unlike traditional planners, Sorted³ is built with flexibility in mind to adjust to your changing schedule.

## Key Features

- **Unified Timeline** - Tasks, calendar events, and notes in one continuous timeline view
- **Time Blocking** - Assign fixed time blocks to tasks and visualize them clearly on your timeline
- **Auto-Schedule** - Automatically schedules tasks based on priorities and deadlines (PRO feature)
- **Magic Select** - Select and move multiple tasks at once for quick schedule adjustments
- **Dynamic Flexibility** - Seamlessly adjust your plan as meetings run over or tasks take longer
- **Time Ruler** - Signature feature to help shift things around with just a few taps
- **iCloud Sync** - Synchronization between iOS and macOS devices (PRO feature)
- **Notes Integration** - Attach notes and context to your tasks and events
- **Apple Watch Support** - View and manage your schedule from your wrist
