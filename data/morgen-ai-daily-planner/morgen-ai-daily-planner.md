## Overview

Morgen is an AI-powered daily planning app that works across all your calendars and task managers, designed for daily users who need to consolidate multiple calendars and task sources into a single planning interface.

## Key Features

### Calendar Integration
Morgen consolidates calendars from Google, Outlook, Apple, Fastmail and more in one app, providing a unified view of all your appointments and events.

### Task Management Integration
Filter and schedule tasks from across project management, to-do, and PKM tools including Notion, Todoist, ClickUp, and Obsidian.

### AI Planning
The AI Planner provides smart suggestions for task scheduling. All suggested placements pulsate in your calendar view so you can review and adjust them before approval - it doesn't automatically schedule without your confirmation.

### Frames Feature
Create recurring time blocks called "Frames" for specific tasks, such as "Deep Work" daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM, helping establish consistent routines.

### Scheduling Links
Share personalized booking links to allow others to schedule time with you based on your real availability across all connected calendars.
