## Overview

FingerTec is a leading manufacturer of biometric time and attendance systems, providing a comprehensive range of hardware devices with advanced biometric authentication technologies and cloud-based management software.

## Features

- **Multiple Biometric Technologies**: Fingerprint, face recognition, palm vein, and iris scanning
- **WiFi & Cloud Connectivity**: Wireless time clocks that sync data to cloud automatically
- **Large Capacity**: Store thousands of employees and millions of transactions
- **Touchscreen Interface**: Modern, user-friendly touchscreen displays
- **Access Control Integration**: Combine time attendance with door access control
- **Battery Backup**: Uninterrupted operation during power outages
- **Multi-Language Support**: Interface available in multiple languages
- **Web-Based Software**: TimeTec cloud platform for data management and reporting
- **Mobile Apps**: Employee self-service and manager access via mobile
- **Shift Management**: Configure complex shift patterns and schedules
- **Leave Management**: Track vacation, sick leave, and other absence types
- **Payroll Reports**: Generate reports compatible with payroll systems
- **API Access**: Integration capabilities for custom applications

## Product Range

Offers various models from basic fingerprint readers to advanced multi-biometric terminals, including outdoor-rated and access control integrated devices.

## Pricing

Hardware pricing varies by model and features. Cloud software subscription available with different tiers based on number of employees and features needed.
