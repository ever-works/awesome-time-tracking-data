## Overview

AbacusLaw is a comprehensive legal practice management software founded in 1983, designed for law firms of any size across every industry. It provides a full-service solution with built-in accounting capabilities and seamless integration between case management and billing.

## Key Features

### Calendaring
- **Rules-Based Calendaring**: Automatically schedules critical deadlines using Federal, State, and Local court rules
- **Organized Calendar**: Creates a comprehensive calendar system for managing court dates and deadlines
- **Conflict Checking**: Built-in conflict of interest checking

### Time Tracking & Billing
- **Contextual Time Capture**: Capture time as you enter information like clients, matters, calendar events, notes, email, or documents
- **Automatic Integration**: Information entered in case management automatically integrates with billing and accounting
- **Comprehensive Billing**: All-in-one functionality for time and billing management
- **Trust Accounting**: Built-in trust accounting capabilities

### Additional Features
- **Document/Form Automation**: Automated document generation
- **Matter Management**: Complete case and matter tracking
- **Contact Management**: Client and contact database
- **Document and Email Management**: Integrated document storage and email management
- **Accounting**: Full accounting and balance management

## Integration Benefits

AbacusLaw eliminates duplicate data entry by automatically integrating client and case information with billing and accounting systems, saving time and reducing errors.
