## Savings Calculator (Savings by Switching)

A web-based payroll savings calculator that estimates how much money and time a business could save by switching from manual paper timecards to a more efficient time tracking and payroll system (such as busybusy).

---

### Features

- **Payroll-focused savings estimation**  
  Estimates potential cost savings related to time tracking and payroll processes.

- **Comparison of manual vs. digital time tracking**  
  Compares typical costs of paper timecards and manual entry against an automated system.

- **Use of industry benchmarks**  
  Incorporates American Payroll Association data indicating employees round an average of 42 minutes per day on their timecards.

- **Dollar-based savings output**  
  Presents estimated costs and savings in dollar amounts (e.g., estimated current monthly cost vs. estimated cost with a digital tool, and the difference between them).

- **Web-based tool**  
  Accessible via browser; no installation required.

- **Designed for time tracking and payroll optimization**  
  Aimed at helping businesses quantify potential savings before changing their time tracking or payroll process.
