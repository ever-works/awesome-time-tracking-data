## Overview

Qbserve keeps track of what you do on your Mac and provides constant feedback on your productivity. The app automatically tracks Mac application usage and categorizes activity as productive, neutral, or distracting.

## Automatic Categorization

### Pre-Categorized Database
Qbserve recognizes more than 5,500 different applications, games, and websites (over 8,100 total sites/apps), organizing them into 3 main categories:
- **Productive**: Work-related tools and applications
- **Neutral**: General purpose tools that vary by context
- **Disruptive**: Social media, games, entertainment

### AI Learning
The AI learns individual work patterns over time, improving categorization accuracy and adapting to your specific workflow.

## Detailed Tracking

Qbserve tracks:
- Apps and their windows
- Websites and specific pages
- YouTube videos watched
- Subreddits visited
- Chats in Skype
- Teams in Slack
- Document titles and names

## Project Tracking

Automatic project tracking based on:
- Opened web pages
- Documents being worked on
- Window titles
- Application context

This allows for invoicing and project time reporting without manual categorization.

## Privacy & Data Storage

Qbserve emphasizes privacy:
- All data stored locally on your Mac
- No cloud synchronization required
- No subscription needed
- Data remains confidential and secure
- User maintains complete control

## Real-Time Feedback

Instant productivity indicators:
- Visual feedback on current productivity level
- Color-coded activity categorization
- Real-time awareness of work habits
- Alerts for excessive distraction time

## Invoicing Support

Automatically tracks work hours and can generate invoices based on collected data, making it suitable for freelancers and consultants.

## Platform

Mac-only (macOS exclusive), optimized for Apple's operating system.

## Pricing

One-time purchase (no subscription), with all data kept locally on device.

## Limitations

Only available for Mac users, limiting accessibility for Windows or Linux-based businesses.

## Key Advantage

Combines automatic tracking with strong privacy protection, appealing to users who want productivity insights without cloud data storage or subscription fees.
