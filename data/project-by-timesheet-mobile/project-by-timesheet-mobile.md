## Overview

Project+ is a workforce management tool that extends Timesheet Mobile's time tracking capabilities with powerful team collaboration features. It enables mobile teams to communicate, coordinate, and complete work more effectively in the field.

## Key Features

- **Multilingual Communication**: Instant team messaging in four languages for diverse workforces
- **Job Checklists**: Create and complete structured checklists for tasks and job requirements
- **Image Sharing**: Share photos among team members for documentation and coordination
- **Real-Time Collaboration**: Connect teams instantly regardless of location
- **Mobile-First Design**: Built specifically for workers using smartphones in the field
- **Task Completion Tracking**: Monitor which checklist items have been completed
- **Integration with Time Tracking**: Works seamlessly with Timesheet Mobile's GPS and time tracking features

## Use Cases

Project+ is particularly valuable for:
- Construction crews coordinating across multiple job sites
- Field service teams needing to share photos and updates
- Healthcare workers following standardized procedures
- Security teams reporting from various locations
- Any mobile workforce requiring structured collaboration

## Industries

Commonly used in:
- Construction
- Field Services
- Healthcare
- Security
- Transportation
- Event Management

## Benefits

By combining communication, checklists, and visual documentation in one mobile app, Project+ helps ensure work is completed correctly and teams stay coordinated without needing to return to an office or desktop computer.
