## Concept

Meeting cost calculators reveal the true financial expense of meetings by calculating: Number of attendees × Average hourly rate × Meeting duration

## Example Calculation

**Scenario**: 1-hour meeting with 8 people
- Average rate: $75/hour
- Cost: 8 × $75 × 1 = $600
- Weekly (if recurring): $2,400/month
- Annual: $28,800

## Impact of Awareness

When people see the cost:
- Question meeting necessity
- Reduce attendee count
- Shorten duration
- Improve preparation
- Stay on agenda

## Tools

- Browser extensions showing meeting cost
- Dedicated calculator websites
- Calendar plugins
- Custom spreadsheets

## Best Practices

- Display cost in meeting invites
- Review high-cost meetings quarterly
- Set cost thresholds requiring approval
- Track total meeting spend
