## Overview

SingularityApp is a time management and productivity application designed around goal-oriented planning rather than time-based scheduling. The app encourages users to complete tasks efficiently and rewards them with free time rather than piling on additional work.

## Features

- **Strict time limits** — Set a maximum time budget for any task to prevent work from expanding to fill available time
- **Task start time and deadline recording** — Log when tasks begin and set firm deadlines
- **Milestone scheduling** — Break large projects into smaller milestones with individual deadlines to ensure even effort distribution
- **Reminders** — Automated notifications for upcoming deadlines and milestones
- **Animated praise bars** — Gamified encouragement when tasks are completed ahead of schedule
- **Result-based goal setting** — Plan tasks based on desired outcomes (e.g., "write 500 words") rather than time spent (e.g., "work for 2 hours")
- **Rest planning** — Schedule rest periods as habitual tasks to avoid overworking
- **Time budget enforcement** — Set a strict cut-off time after which all work stops

## Pricing

The app offers a free tier with core functionality available at no cost.
