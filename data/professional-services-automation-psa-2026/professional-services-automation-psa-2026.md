## Overview

Professional Services Automation (PSA) software represents the evolution of project management tools specifically designed for consulting, agencies, and professional services firms, integrating time tracking, resource management, financial tracking, and client management in unified platforms.

## Core Components

### Time & Expense Tracking
- Built-in timers and timesheets
- Expense capture and categorization
- Billable vs. non-billable designation
- Mobile time entry
- Automatic time suggestions

### Resource Management
- Capacity planning
- Skills matching
- Availability scheduling
- Utilization tracking
- Forecasting and allocation

### Financial Management
- Real-time profitability tracking
- Budget management
- Invoicing and billing
- Revenue recognition
- Cost tracking

### Project Management
- Task and milestone tracking
- Gantt charts and timelines
- Collaboration tools
- File management
- Client portals
