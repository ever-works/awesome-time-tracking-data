## Overview

Time tracking spreadsheet templates are free, downloadable timesheet formats available for Excel, Google Sheets, Word, and PDF. These templates provide a simple, accessible way to track time without specialized software, with auto-calculation features built in.

## Available Template Types

- **Daily Timesheets**: Track hours for a single day
- **Weekly Timesheets**: Most common format for tracking work hours
- **Biweekly Timesheets**: Two-week tracking for biweekly pay periods
- **Monthly Timesheets**: Full month time tracking
- **Project Timesheets**: Track time by project or client
- **Employee Timesheets**: Templates for multiple employees
- **Attorney/Legal Timesheets**: Specialized for legal billing

## Key Features

Timesheet spreadsheets typically include:
- Auto-calculation of hours and wages
- Columns for project/task names
- Editable formulas and formatting
- Date ranges and pay periods
- Overtime calculations
- Customizable fields

## Popular Sources

- **MyHours**: Offers free Excel timesheet templates plus Google Sheets, Word & PDF versions
- **TrackingTime**: Provides free templates for teams, freelancers, and project-based work
- **Toggl Track**: All Google Sheets templates are free to copy, share, and print
- **Quidlo**: Specialized Google Sheets templates with customization options
- **Clockify**: Spreadsheet time tracking with template downloads

## Benefits

- **Free**: No software purchase required
- **Familiar Interface**: Most people know Excel or Google Sheets
- **Customizable**: Easy to modify for specific needs
- **No Learning Curve**: Intuitive for most users
- **Universal Access**: Works on any device with spreadsheet software

## Limitations

- Manual data entry required
- No automatic tracking
- Limited reporting compared to dedicated software
- No integrations with other tools
- Prone to human error

## Use Cases

- Small businesses with simple time tracking needs
- Freelancers tracking project hours
- Organizations with limited budgets
- Teams wanting full control over their tracking format
- Temporary or seasonal time tracking needs
