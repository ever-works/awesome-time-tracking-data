## Overview

HR for Health is a comprehensive HR management solution specifically designed for medical and dental practices, offering integrated time tracking, payroll, benefits administration, and compliance tools tailored to the unique needs of healthcare providers.

## Features

### Time & Attendance
- **Integrated Time Clock**: Built-in timekeeping system designed for healthcare settings
- **Precise Rounding**: Rounds to the hundredth of an hour to prevent overtime disputes and ensure accurate payroll
- **Compliance-Focused**: Ensures adherence to healthcare industry labor regulations
- **Multi-Location Support**: Track time across multiple practice locations

### Payroll Management
- **Integrated Payroll Processing**: Seamless connection between time tracking and payroll
- **Healthcare-Specific Calculations**: Handles complex pay structures common in medical/dental practices
- **Tax Compliance**: Automated tax calculations and filings
- **Direct Deposit**: Electronic payment processing for staff

### HR Compliance
- **Healthcare Regulations**: Tools for HIPAA, OSHA, and other healthcare compliance requirements
- **Document Management**: Secure storage for employee records and compliance documentation
- **Training Tracking**: Monitor required certifications and continuing education
- **Policy Management**: Maintain and distribute practice policies

### Employee Management
- **Onboarding**: Streamlined new hire processes for clinical and administrative staff
- **Performance Management**: Track employee evaluations and goals
- **Benefits Administration**: Manage healthcare, retirement, and other employee benefits
- **PTO Tracking**: Monitor vacation, sick leave, and other time off

### Additional Features
- **401(k) Integration**: Retirement plan management tools
- **Employee Self-Service**: Portal for staff to access pay stubs, request time off, and update information
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive reports for practice management and decision-making
- **Advisory Services**: Access to HR experts familiar with healthcare industry

## Target Users

- **Medical Practices**: Private practices, group practices, specialty clinics
- **Dental Practices**: General dentistry, specialty dental practices, dental groups
- **Healthcare Facilities**: Outpatient centers, urgent care facilities
- **Multi-Location Practices**: Practices with multiple offices or locations

## Industry Focus

Designed as a one-stop-shop specifically for medical and dental practices, addressing the unique HR challenges in healthcare including:
- Complex staffing requirements
- Credential and license tracking
- Healthcare-specific compliance needs
- Variable shift schedules
- Clinical versus administrative staff management

## Integration

HR for Health integrates with practice management software and other healthcare technology systems to create a comprehensive operational ecosystem.

## Support

Access to healthcare HR specialists who understand industry-specific challenges and regulations.
