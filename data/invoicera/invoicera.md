## Overview

Invoicera is trusted time billing software for professionals that allows users to track every billable minute, generate precise invoices, and streamline payments. It's designed to turn tracked hours into invoices with smart time billing software in just a few clicks.

## Time Tracking & Billing Features

- **Multiple Tracking Methods**: Use timers for real-time tracking, train team members to log time accurately, and pick appropriate billing increments (e.g., 6- or 15-minute blocks)
- **Automatic Invoice Generation**: Convert billable hours into detailed invoices with a few clicks
- **Comprehensive Management**: Includes invoicing and payments, expense management, recurring/subscription billing, client/vendor panels
- **Time-Based Billing**: Dedicated features for professionals who bill based on hours worked
- **Staff Permissions**: Control access and permissions for team members

## Additional Capabilities

- Invoice approval processes
- Multilingual and multi-currency support
- Financial reporting and analysis
- Purchase order management
- 24/7 access for time tracking, billing, and reporting
- Real-time invoice editing within templates

## Platform Support

Cloud-based software with support for Android and iOS platforms, accessible from anywhere with internet connection.

## Pricing

Free/Forever plan available with different pricing tiers. Offers a 7-day free trial with no credit card required to explore time-based billing and other features. Starting price is free with paid plans available for additional features.
