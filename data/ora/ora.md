## Overview

Ora is a project management and collaboration platform that includes integrated time tracking capabilities. The platform offers an effortless timesheet solution designed for both freelancers and agency teams.

## Features

- **Effortless Timesheets**: Input, review, and report task hours with just a few clicks per week
- **Project Management**: Full project planning and task management capabilities
- **Time Tracking Integration**: Seamlessly track time against tasks and projects
- **Daily and Weekly Planning**: Organize work across different time horizons
- **Reporting and Analytics**: Generate insights from tracked time data
- **Team Collaboration**: Share progress and coordinate with team members
- **Task Organization**: Structured approach to managing deliverables

## Use Cases

- Freelancers managing multiple client projects
- Agencies tracking team time across projects
- Teams needing combined project management and time tracking
- Organizations requiring simple timesheet workflows

## Strengths

- Minimal clicks required for time entry
- Combines project management with time tracking
- Suitable for both individual freelancers and teams
- Streamlined reporting capabilities
