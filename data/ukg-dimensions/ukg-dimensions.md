## Overview

UKG Dimensions (formerly Kronos) is a cloud-based HCM solution that helps businesses simplify payroll computations, accelerate talent acquisition, manage time and attendance, and support proactive, strategic talent management on a global level.

## Features

### Time and Attendance

- Advanced tracking and reporting capabilities
- Accurate calculation of hours worked
- Multiple clock-in methods (biometric, badge, mobile)
- Real-time attendance monitoring
- Automated overtime calculations

### Scheduling and Labor Management

- Intelligent scheduling based on labor forecasting
- Skills-based scheduling
- Shift bidding and swapping
- Schedule optimization
- Mobile schedule access

### Compliance Management

- Compliance-focused features for labor law adherence
- Audit trails and reporting
- Automated alerts for compliance violations
- Multi-jurisdiction support

### Security

Enterprise-level security features including:
- Data encryption
- Role-based access controls
- Audit logging
- Compliance certifications

## Hardware Integration

Kronos stands out for its reliable time clock hardware options:
- Biometric time clocks
- Badge readers
- Touch-screen terminals
- Mobile clock-in solutions
