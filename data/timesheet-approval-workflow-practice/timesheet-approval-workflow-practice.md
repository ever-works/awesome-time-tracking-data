## Overview

Implementing a structured timesheet approval workflow with rapid review cycles is essential for maintaining data quality and preventing billing errors. Best practice recommends 24-hour review turnaround.

## Key Principles

- Project managers review timesheets within 24 hours of submission
- Catch miscategorized entries before invoicing
- Identify budget anomalies early
- Provide immediate feedback to employees
- Use approval hierarchy based on organizational structure
- Automate approval routing and notifications
- Track approval status in real-time

## Benefits

- Prevents billing errors from reaching clients
- Catches project budget overruns early
- Improves time categorization accuracy
- Enables rapid correction of mistakes
- Provides timely feedback to improve future entries
- Ensures compliant and accurate invoicing
- Reduces end-of-month reconciliation issues

## Implementation

Effective approval workflows require clear ownership, automated notifications, simple approval interfaces, escalation procedures for delays, and reporting on approval cycle times.
