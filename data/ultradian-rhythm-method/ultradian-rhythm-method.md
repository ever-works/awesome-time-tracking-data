## Overview

Ultradian rhythms are natural 90-120 minute cycles of alertness that occur throughout the day. Working with these biological rhythms optimizes productivity and prevents burnout.

## The Science

- Body operates in 90-120 minute cycles
- Peak alertness followed by recovery need
- Discovered by sleep researcher Nathan Kleitman
- Present during waking and sleeping hours

## How to Apply

1. Work intensely for 90 minutes
2. Take 20-minute break
3. Repeat cycle 4-5 times per day
4. Honor recovery periods

## Benefits

- Aligns with natural biology
- Prevents mental fatigue
- Sustainable long-term
- Higher quality focus
- Reduces burnout risk

## vs. Pomodoro

- Longer sessions (90 vs 25 minutes)
- Based on biology not arbitrary time
- Longer breaks (20 vs 5 minutes)
- More individual variation allowed
