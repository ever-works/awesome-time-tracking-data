## Overview

Laurel represents the next generation of time tracking software, leveraging advanced AI and machine learning to eliminate manual time entry. As of January 2026, it's the highest-funded company in the time tracking sector with over $153M raised, signaling major market confidence in AI-driven time management.

## AI-Powered Features

### Automatic Time Capture
- AI agents monitor all work activity across apps, meetings, and communications
- Automatically categorizes activities by project and task type
- Learns from user corrections to improve accuracy over time

### Predictive Duration Modeling
- Machine learning analyzes historical data to predict how long tasks will take
- Provides realistic project estimates based on team performance patterns
- Warns when projects are likely to exceed budgets or deadlines

### Smart Timesheet Generation
- Automatically generates complete timesheets without manual input
- Suggests missing time entries based on calendar and communication patterns
- Handles complex scenarios like multi-project work and context switching

### Intelligent Categorization
- Natural language processing understands context from emails, tickets, and messages
- Automatically assigns time to correct projects and clients
- Detects billable vs non-billable time patterns

## Enterprise Features

- Advanced compliance and audit trails

- Custom approval workflows

## Technology Stack

Laurel uses proprietary large language models trained specifically on workplace activity data. The platform processes signals from:
- Calendar appointments
- Email and Slack communications
- Code commits and pull requests
- Project management tool activity
- Document editing and collaboration

## Target Market

Laurel primarily targets:
- Professional services firms (consulting, legal, accounting)
- Enterprise software companies
- Agencies managing multiple clients
- Organizations with complex project tracking needs

## Competitive Advantages

The significant funding enables Laurel to:
- Develop more sophisticated AI models than competitors
- Offer enterprise-grade security and compliance
- Provide hands-on implementation and training
- Continuously improve prediction accuracy with larger datasets

## Pricing

Laurel's pricing is enterprise-focused, typically requiring custom quotes based on organization size and needs. Pricing models include per-user licensing and value-based pricing tied to time capture accuracy improvements.

## Platform

Web application with desktop agents for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Mobile apps for iOS and Android provide time review and approval capabilities.
