## Overview

Voice time entry enables workers to create time entries, start/stop timers, and add descriptions using voice commands and speech-to-text conversion. This hands-free approach is essential for workers who can't easily access keyboards or screens during work activities.

## Key Capabilities

- **Timer Control**: "Start timer on Project X" or "Stop current timer"
- **Manual Entry**: "Log 2 hours to client meeting yesterday"
- **Description**: "Add note: reviewed contract terms"
- **Project Selection**: "Switch timer to development project"
- **Query Status**: "How much time today?" or "Show this week's hours"

## Ideal Use Cases

### Field Workers
Technicians with hands full of tools or equipment

### Drivers
Safely log time while driving between job sites

### Medical Professionals
Healthcare workers maintaining sterile hands

### Manufacturing
Workers operating machinery or on assembly lines

### Accessibility
Employees with mobility or vision impairments

## Technology Requirements

- Voice assistant integration (Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa)
- Speech-to-text engine
- Natural language processing
- Mobile app with voice command support
- Microphone-enabled device

## Benefits

- Truly hands-free operation
- Safer in certain work environments
- Faster than typing on mobile
- Accessibility for all users
- Natural interaction method
- Multitasking capability

## Limitations

- Requires quiet environment for accuracy
- Privacy concerns in shared spaces
- Language and accent limitations
- Potential transcription errors
- Limited complex command support
