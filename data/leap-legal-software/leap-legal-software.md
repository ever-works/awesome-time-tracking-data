## Overview

LEAP is a cloud-based legal practice management platform that integrates time tracking, billing, document automation, and matter management for law firms. The software is designed to streamline legal workflows and capture every billable minute.

## Features

- Automatic time tracking with timers
- Time entry from any activity (emails, calls, documents)
- Matter-centric time tracking
- Customizable billing rates by attorney or matter type
- Pre-billing and invoice generation
- Trust accounting and IOLTA compliance
- Document automation and assembly
- Calendar and deadline management
- Client portal for secure communication
- Mobile app for time tracking on the go
- Integration with legal research platforms
- Detailed productivity and profitability reports

## Pricing

LEAP offers subscription-based pricing with different tiers:
- Essential plan for solo practitioners
- Professional plan for growing firms
- Enterprise plan for large firms

Pricing typically ranges from $100-$200+ per user per month depending on features and firm size.
