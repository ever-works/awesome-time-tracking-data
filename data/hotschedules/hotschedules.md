## Overview

HotSchedules is a comprehensive workforce management solution designed specifically for the unique challenges of restaurant and hospitality operations, helping managers optimize staffing, control labor costs, and improve team communication.

## Scheduling Features

- **Drag-and-Drop Scheduling**: Intuitive interface for creating and modifying schedules
- **Auto-Scheduling**: AI-powered schedule generation based on sales forecasts and labor rules
- **Shift Templates**: Save and reuse common scheduling patterns
- **Availability Management**: Collect and manage employee availability preferences
- **Shift Trading**: Allow employees to swap shifts with manager approval
- **Time-Off Requests**: Digital request and approval workflow
- **Multi-Location**: Manage schedules across multiple restaurant locations
- **Conflict Detection**: Automatic alerts for double-bookings and rule violations

## Time Tracking

- **Mobile Time Clock**: Employees clock in/out from smartphones
- **POS Integration**: Clock-in directly from point-of-sale systems
- **Break Tracking**: Monitor meal and rest breaks for compliance
- **Overtime Alerts**: Real-time notifications when approaching overtime
- **Tip Tracking**: Record and manage gratuity distribution
- **GPS Verification**: Location-based clock-in for field staff
- **Timesheet Approval**: Manager review and approval workflows

## Labor Cost Management

- **Sales Forecasting**: Predict sales based on historical data and trends
- **Labor Forecasting**: AI-powered staffing recommendations based on demand
- **Real-Time Labor Tracking**: Monitor labor costs against sales throughout shifts
- **Budget Management**: Set and track labor budget targets
- **What-If Analysis**: Model different scheduling scenarios
- **Labor Percentage**: Calculate and optimize labor as percentage of sales

## Team Communication

- **Team Messaging**: Built-in chat for shift communication
- **Shift Notifications**: Automatic alerts for schedule changes
- **Announcement Board**: Post updates and important information
- **Document Sharing**: Distribute training materials and policies
- **Mobile App**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go access

## Compliance Features

- **Break Enforcement**: Ensure required breaks are taken
- **Overtime Management**: Monitor and control overtime hours
- **Minor Labor Laws**: Track restrictions for underage workers
- **Certification Tracking**: Monitor required certifications (food handler, alcohol service)
- **Audit Trails**: Complete history of schedule and time changes

## Reporting & Analytics

- Labor cost reports by location, department, or position
- Schedule vs. actual hours comparison
- Overtime and compliance reports
- Employee hours and attendance tracking
- Productivity metrics
- Custom report builder
