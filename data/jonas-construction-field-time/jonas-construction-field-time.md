## Overview

Jonas Construction Field Time is a comprehensive field time entry solution that integrates directly with Jonas Construction ERP software, providing real-time visibility into labor, equipment, and production data from the field.

## Features

- Mobile field time entry
- Labor hour tracking by cost code
- Equipment time recording
- Production quantity tracking
- Real-time data sync to ERP
- Job costing integration
- Supervisor approval workflows
- GPS location verification
- Offline mode for remote sites
- Daily field reports
- Material usage logging
- Safety documentation
- Time card approval process

## Pricing

Part of Jonas Construction ERP suite. Pricing available through Jonas Software based on company size and modules needed.
