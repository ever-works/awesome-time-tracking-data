## Overview

Timing is an automatic time tracking application designed specifically for macOS. It runs in the background, recording all activities without requiring manual timers, making it ideal for freelancers and professionals who want comprehensive productivity insights.

## Features

- Fully automatic tracking of all Mac applications and documents
- No manual timers required
- Privacy-focused with local data storage
- Detailed productivity reports and analytics
- Integration with popular Mac applications
- Offline functionality

## Use Cases

- Mac users tracking personal productivity
- Freelancers billing by the hour
- Knowledge workers analyzing time allocation
- Anyone seeking automatic, passive time tracking
