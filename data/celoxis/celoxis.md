## Overview

Celoxis is a comprehensive online project management software that combines advanced features with cutting-edge AI capabilities to optimize project planning, execution, and delivery. It is designed to help organizations efficiently plan, track, and manage projects across various industries.

## Time Tracking Capabilities

### Timesheet Features
- Integrated time sheets and expense module
- Track time, costs, and billing
- Weekly timesheet screen for quick time logging
- Update task progress directly from timesheets
- Consultants log hours directly into Celoxis
- Every billable hour is accounted for

### Time Tracking Integration
- All time entries feed directly into project plans
- Automatic updates to resource calendars
- Managers notified if projects exceed allocated hours
- Real-time tracking of project time
- Time tracking keeps teams accountable and transparent
