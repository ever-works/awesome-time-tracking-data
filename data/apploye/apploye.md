## Overview

Apploye is a comprehensive employee time tracking and productivity monitoring software designed for remote and hybrid teams. It provides detailed insights into how time is spent while respecting employee privacy.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Manual and automatic time tracking with timers
- **Screenshots**: Optional screenshot monitoring at configurable intervals
- **Activity Tracking**: Monitor app and website usage
- **Productivity Scoring**: Automatic productivity categorization
- **Timesheets**: Automated timesheet generation
- **Project & Task Tracking**: Organize time by projects and tasks
- **Payroll**: Built-in payroll management
- **Invoicing**: Generate client invoices from tracked time
- **GPS Tracking**: Optional location tracking for field teams
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive time and productivity reports

## Privacy Features

- Employees can blur sensitive screenshots
- Configurable monitoring settings
- Transparent tracking that employees can see
- Private time option for personal breaks
