## Overview

The Medium Method combines the best aspects of paper-based and digital productivity tools, leveraging the strengths of each medium.

## Core Components

- **Main notebook** - Capture ideas, tasks, quotes, notes, and meeting minutes
- **Travel notebook** - Same purpose as the main notebook, designed for use while traveling
- **Daily Post-it note** - Lists all appointments and the 3 most important tasks for that day
- **Pen or pencil** - For analog capture and quick notation
- **Task management app** - For digital task organization and reminders
- **Online calendar app** - For scheduling and time blocking
- **Note-taking app** - For searchable digital notes and references
