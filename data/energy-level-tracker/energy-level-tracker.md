## Overview

The Energy Level Tracker app helps users quantify and track their energy levels at any time of day to determine when they're at their peak performance state. This insight enables better scheduling decisions and improved work-life balance.

## Key Features

- Track energy levels multiple times throughout the day
- Simple entry system for quick logging
- Visual analytics showing energy patterns over time
- Identify personal peak performance windows
- Correlate energy levels with activities and habits
- Historical tracking to spot trends and patterns

## Benefits

### Productivity Optimization

- Schedule high-priority tasks during energy peaks
- Plan deep work sessions when energy is highest
- Identify low-energy periods suitable for routine tasks
- Avoid scheduling demanding work during predictable dips

### Well-Being Management

- Gain insight into daily habits affecting energy
- Manage moods more effectively
- Take mental breathers at appropriate times
- Achieve more stability and peace of mind
- Make data-driven decisions about rest and recovery

## Connection to Biological Prime Time

The app supports the Biological Prime Time methodology by providing concrete data about personal energy patterns rather than relying on assumptions or general guidelines.

## Use Cases

- Knowledge workers optimizing deep work schedules
- Shift workers understanding energy patterns
- People with chronic fatigue managing energy expenditure
- Anyone seeking to align work with natural rhythms
- Productivity enthusiasts tracking multiple variables

## Best Practices

1. Track consistently at the same times each day for accurate patterns
2. Note activities and contexts alongside energy levels
3. Review patterns weekly to identify trends
4. Adjust work schedules based on discovered patterns
5. Combine with time tracking to optimize productivity

## Platform Availability

Primarily web-based application accessible from any device with a browser.
