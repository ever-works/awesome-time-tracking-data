## Overview

Optimized is a lifelogging and quantified self application that helps users track their everyday activities, places visited, people met, mood, and many other aspects of life to identify patterns and improve overall well-being.

## Features

- **Activity Logging**: Record all daily activities with timestamps and durations
- **Location Tracking**: Automatically track places visited with GPS and manual entry
- **People Tracking**: Log interactions and meetings with specific people
- **Mood Tracking**: Track emotional state throughout the day with customizable mood scales
- **Time Analysis**: Detailed analytics on how time is spent across different activities
- **Photo Journal**: Attach photos to logged activities and events
- **Custom Categories**: Create unlimited custom categories for activities
- **Search & Filter**: Powerful search to find past activities and patterns
- **Reports**: Generate reports on time spent by category, person, or location
- **Data Visualization**: Charts and graphs showing trends and patterns over time
- **Privacy**: All data stored locally on device with optional cloud backup
