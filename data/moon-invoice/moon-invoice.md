## Overview

Moon Invoice is a comprehensive invoicing and time tracking solution optimized for mobile devices, allowing freelancers and small businesses to manage their billing and time tracking on-the-go.

## Features

- **Mobile Time Tracking**: Track time directly from your smartphone or tablet
- **Timer Function**: Start/stop timer for active projects
- **Manual Time Entry**: Add time entries retroactively
- **Project Management**: Organize time by client and project
- **Invoice Generation**: Convert tracked time into professional invoices
- **Expense Tracking**: Record and categorize business expenses
- **Multiple Currencies**: Support for international clients
- **Payment Integration**: Accept payments through integrated payment gateways
- **Recurring Invoices**: Set up automatic recurring billing
- **Estimates and Quotes**: Create and send project estimates
- **Client Portal**: Allow clients to view invoices and make payments
- **Reporting**: Financial reports and time tracking analytics
- **Cloud Sync**: Sync data across multiple devices
- **Offline Mode**: Continue working without internet connection

## Pricing

Moon Invoice offers several pricing options:
- Free plan with limited features
- Professional: Approximately $7.99/month
- Business: Approximately $12.99/month with additional features
- Custom enterprise pricing available

Available on iOS and Android with desktop web access.
