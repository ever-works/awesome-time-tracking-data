## Overview

The Productivity Show is a podcast by Asian Efficiency that explores productivity tools, techniques, and habits. Hosted by Mike Vardy and previously Thanh Pham and Dave Lukas, the show covers practical strategies for managing time, setting goals, and optimizing workflows.

## Topics Covered

- Time management methodologies and techniques
- Productivity app and tool reviews
- Goal setting and achievement strategies
- Habit building and workflow optimization
- Focus and concentration practices
- Work-life balance and energy management

## Format

- Regular episodes with interviews and solo segments
- Practical, actionable productivity advice
- Available on all major podcast platforms
