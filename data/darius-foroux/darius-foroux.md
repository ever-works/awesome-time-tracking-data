## Overview

Darius Foroux is a prolific author and founder of The Sounding Board, a private community for people who love to learn and create. He has written seven books covering productivity, habits, decision making, and wealth building.

## Published Works

His books cover topics including:
- Productivity and time management
- Habit formation and maintenance
- Decision-making frameworks
- Wealth building principles

## The Sounding Board

Foroux founded The Sounding Board, a private community designed for people who are committed to learning, creating, and continuous self-improvement.
