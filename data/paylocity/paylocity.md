## Overview

Paylocity is a comprehensive cloud-based payroll and human capital management (HCM) platform that helps businesses streamline their HR, payroll, talent management, and workforce operations.

## Time & Attendance Features

Paylocity's workforce management capabilities include multi-device time and attendance tracking, enabling employees to clock in through mobile apps, web browsers, or physical kiosks. Managers can oversee attendance, approve timesheets, and coordinate schedules.

**Key Features:**
- Time tracking via mobile, web, or kiosk
- Geofencing capabilities to control labor costs and maintain compliance
- Automated accruals for time off
- AI-driven scheduling forecasts and budget monitoring
- Labor allocation analytics and predictive insights
- Employee self-service for viewing schedules and requesting time off
- Automated meal-attestation compliance workflows

## Platform Enhancements (2026)

In 2026, Paylocity enhanced its platform with improved user experience and compliance features, including upgraded Employee Voice tools with guided setup, expanded mobile capabilities, role-based training modules, interview self-scheduling, vendor integrations for pre-hire assessments, and automated compliance workflows.

## Integration

Time tracking data seamlessly connects with payroll, saving approximately 2 hours on every payroll run by automating the flow of approved hours into paycheck calculations.
