## Overview

Billables AI is an automated legal time tracking solution that operates passively in the background, using artificial intelligence to accurately capture and categorize billable time for attorneys and law firms.

## Features

- Automatic time capture without manual timers
- AI-powered activity matching to client matters
- Multi-tasking activity harmonization
- Automatic narrative description generation
- Integration with legal practice management software
- Billable hours optimization
- Privacy-focused design
- Desktop activity monitoring
- Email and document tracking
- Meeting time capture

## Benefits

- Eliminates manual time entry and timer management
- Recovers lost billable hours through automatic tracking
- Reduces administrative burden on attorneys
- Improves billing accuracy and completeness
- Generates detailed time entries with AI-written descriptions

## Target Users

- Solo practitioners and small law firms
- Large legal departments
- Attorneys billing by the hour
- Legal professionals seeking to maximize billable hours
