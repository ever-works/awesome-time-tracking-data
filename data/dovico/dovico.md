## Overview

Dovico provides time tracking software specifically designed for engineers and architects, helping firms track project time, manage costs, and bill accurately for professional services.

## Key Features for Engineers & Architects

### Project Time Tracking

- Track time by project and phase
- Monitor design, development, and construction administration phases
- Capture time across multiple projects simultaneously
- Allocate hours to specific deliverables

### Billable Hours Management

By meticulously tracking time, architects and engineers can:

- Capture every billable minute spent on a project
- Ensure accurate and fair charging
- Differentiate between billable and non-billable work
- Generate detailed invoices with time breakdowns

### Project Phase Tracking

Monitor time across design phases:

- Schematic design
- Design development
- Construction documents
- Construction administration
- Project closeout

## Integration Capabilities

- Connects with project management tools

## Reporting & Analytics
