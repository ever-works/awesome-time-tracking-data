## Overview

Focus Mode by Geekbot is a Pomodoro-inspired tool designed specifically for Slack. If Slack is your biggest source of distraction, this tool works as a Pomodoro timer that tells your coworkers what you're busy with. It sets a customized away status, stops notifications from interrupting you, replies to DMs notifying people that you are in Focus Mode, and then notifies your Slack contacts when Focus Mode is over.

## How It Works

1. Activate Focus Mode from Slack
2. Set duration (default aligns with Pomodoro intervals)
3. Geekbot updates your Slack status to show you're focused
4. Incoming DMs receive an automatic reply with your focus status
5. Notifications are suppressed during the session
6. When the timer ends, your contacts are notified that you're available again

## Key Features

- **Custom Status Display**: Shows coworkers you're in a focus session
- **Automatic DM Responses**: Replies to direct messages with your focus status
- **Notification Muting**: Suppresses Slack notifications during focus time
- **Session Completion Alerts**: Notifies contacts when focus mode ends
- **Customizable Duration**: Align with Pomodoro 25-minute sessions or set custom intervals
- **Slack-Native**: Works entirely within Slack without switching apps

## Use Cases

- **Remote Teams**: Communicate focus status without interrupting team flow
- **Developers**: Protect deep work sessions from Slack distractions
- **Anyone Distracted by Slack**: Turn Slack from a distraction into a focus ally

## Benefits

- Reduces context switching from Slack interruptions
- Communicates availability to team members without manual updates
- No additional app or browser extension needed
- Encourages team-wide respect for focus time
- Simple to activate and manage from within Slack
