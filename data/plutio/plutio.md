## Overview

Plutio is an all-in-one business management platform designed for freelancers and small agencies, combining time tracking with project management, client management, invoicing, and proposals.

## Features

- Time tracking with timers and manual entry
- Project and task management
- CRM and client management
- Proposal creation and e-signatures
- Invoicing and payment processing
- Contract management
- Team collaboration
- White-label client portal

## Time Tracking

- Project and task-based time tracking
- Billable hour management
- Time reports and analytics
- Invoice generation from tracked time
- Budget tracking against time
- Team time overview

## Freelancer-Focused Benefits

- Client portal for transparency
- Proposal to project workflow
- Integrated payment processing
- Contract and agreement management
- Expense tracking
- Financial reporting

## Pricing

~$19/month for freelancers
Higher tiers for agencies and teams

## Target Users

Freelancers, consultants, small agencies, solopreneurs
