## Overview

The Now Habit, developed by Neil Fiore, introduces the concept of 'Unscheduling' — a reverse approach to calendar planning designed to combat procrastination and reduce anxiety about work.

## The Unscheduling Method

In your calendar, first schedule the following in this order:

1. **Fixed Commitments** - Meals, commute, sleep, mandatory meetings
2. **Self-care Activities** - Meditation, exercise, personal health time
3. **Guilt-free Play** - Socializing, hobbies, leisure activities
4. **Work** - Only then, schedule work into remaining time blocks

## Core Philosophy

- You can only manage your time when you acknowledge all its uses
- Scheduling play first removes the guilt that often accompanies leisure
- Knowing your free time is protected makes work sessions more focused
- This approach reduces the anxiety that often leads to procrastination
