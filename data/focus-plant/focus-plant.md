## Overview

Focus Plant is a Pomodoro forest productivity app available on Google Play that was updated on February 10, 2026. It combines time tracking with gamification by allowing users to grow virtual plants during focus sessions, similar to the popular Forest app but with its own unique features.

## Key Features

### Timer Modes
- Set custom session lengths for focused work
- Built-in Pomodoro timer with planned breaks
- Flexible break intervals
- Session history tracking

### Focus Enhancement
- **Enhanced App Block**: Prevent distractions by blocking specified apps during focus sessions
- **Healing Sounds**: Background soundscapes to improve concentration
- **Breathing Exercises**: Built-in breathing techniques for stress reduction
- **Core Focus Timer**: Simple, effective timing mechanism

### Gamification Elements
- Grow virtual plants during focus sessions
- Build a forest of productivity
- Visual representation of accumulated focus time
- Motivational feedback system

## Productivity Benefits

Focus Plant helps users:
- Build consistent focus habits through visual rewards
- Reduce phone usage and digital distractions
- Track productive time spent on important tasks
- Create psychological barriers to multitasking
- Maintain work-life balance through structured breaks

## 2026 Updates

The February 2026 update brought enhanced app blocking features, improved healing sounds library, and refined breathing exercise integration, showing active development and commitment to user experience.

## Target Users

Students, remote workers, and anyone seeking to improve focus and reduce digital distractions while tracking productive time through an engaging, game-like interface.
