## Overview

Eye Monitor is an iOS application designed to prevent health problems associated with extended screen time, including repetitive strain injury (RSI), backache, and dry eyes. The app functions as a fully automated fatigue monitor and break reminder system.

## Key Features

**Automated Break Reminders:**
- Tracks screen usage automatically
- Sends timely notifications for break times
- Customizable break intervals
- Timed breaks to ensure adequate rest

**Health Focus:**
- Prevents digital eye strain
- Reduces risk of RSI and repetitive injuries
- Addresses sedentary health concerns
- Promotes better posture through regular movement breaks

**Fatigue Monitoring:**
- Monitors cumulative screen exposure
- Tracks patterns of device usage
- Provides insights into screen time habits
- Helps identify when fatigue may be building

## Health Benefits

**Eye Strain Prevention:**
Regular breaks help reduce digital eye strain, dry eyes, and visual fatigue caused by prolonged focus on screens.

**Physical Health:**
Prompts users to stand, stretch, and move, reducing the risk of back pain and repetitive strain injuries.

**Mental Wellness:**
Breaks improve focus and productivity by preventing mental fatigue and allowing the brain to rest.

## Platform

Available exclusively for iOS devices through the Apple App Store. The app runs in the background and integrates with iOS system features to track screen time automatically.

## Target Users

- Knowledge workers spending long hours on devices
- Students engaged in extended study sessions
- Anyone experiencing eye strain or fatigue from screen use
- Remote workers without natural break triggers
- iOS users seeking automated health reminders
