## Overview

QSR Automations by Crunchtime is a comprehensive restaurant technology platform specializing in kitchen display systems, guest management, and workflow automation. The platform integrates with over 80 point-of-sale partners to streamline quick service restaurant (QSR) and casual dining operations.

## Features

- **Kitchen Display Systems**: Digital order management for kitchen workflow
- **Guest Management**: Waitlist and reservation management
- **Workflow Automation**: Automate repetitive restaurant tasks
- **POS Integration**: Connects with 80+ point-of-sale systems
- **Labor Management**: Employee scheduling and time tracking
- **Real-Time Metrics**: Speed-of-service and performance analytics
- **Order Accuracy**: Reduce errors with digital order displays
- **Multi-Location Support**: Manage multiple restaurant locations
- **Mobile Integration**: Mobile ordering and device support
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive operational reports

## 2026 AI Integration

AI is now embedded across payments, reservations, workforce management, customer-facing platforms, and back-of-house operations, bringing together speed-of-service metrics, staffing data, inventory usage, audit results, and sales to create a full operational picture.

## Target Market

Quick service restaurants (QSR), fast casual dining, casual dining, and multi-unit restaurant operators.

## Integration Ecosystem

Crunchtime products easily integrate with over 80 point-of-sale partners, enabling comprehensive data flow between POS systems and management functions including inventory control, staff scheduling, and order management.
