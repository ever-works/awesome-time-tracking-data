## Overview

Reclaim is an AI-powered app that creates 40% more time for teams by automatically scheduling tasks, habits, meetings, and breaks. The platform is free to use on Google Calendar and Outlook Calendar.

## Features

### AI Time Blocking
- Continuously analyzes your availability
- Uses intelligent time blocking for important tasks
- Defends habits, meetings, and Focus Time
- Flexible holds that adapt to changes
- Priority-based scheduling automation

### Automatic Scheduling
- AI time blocking syncs and plans tasks directly to calendar
- Schedules by priority before due dates
- Takes stress out of planning
- Keeps schedule up-to-date with latest task list
- Automatically reshuffles schedule when week changes
- Tasks auto-schedule based on hundreds of thousands of datapoints

### Flexible Calendar Management
- Initially schedules smart events as 'Free'
- Automatically flips to 'Busy' as time books up
- Locks events when start time draws near
- Maintains calendar flexibility
- Adapts to last-minute changes

### Priority-Based Scheduling
- Set your priorities
- Reclaim builds schedule around what matters most
- Automatic rescheduling when conflicts arise
- Handles PTO, important meetings, urgent tasks
- Events reschedule to next best time automatically

### Key Features
- Focus Time blocking
- Habits scheduling
- Tasks management
- Smart Meetings
- Scheduling Links
- Time tracking analytics
- Buffer time management
