## Overview

Timebox & Track transforms your Elgato Stream Deck into a powerful productivity companion, eliminating the friction from time tracking. No apps to open, no forms to fill out—just press a button and focus on your work.

## Key Features

### Live Countdown Timer
Your Stream Deck key becomes a live countdown timer, showing exactly how much time remains in your session.

### Automatic Logging
When you're done, your session is automatically logged—no manual entry required.

### Project Tagging
Tag your activities by project, task, or category and switch tags mid-session if your focus shifts.

### One-Button Operation
Start and stop time tracking with a single button press, keeping you in your flow state.

## Related Plugins

Other time tracking plugins available for Stream Deck include:

- **Toggl Plugin**: Integrate with Toggl Track service and assign different keys to different projects
- **Clockify Plugin**: Track, start and stop Clockify timers on your Elgato Stream Deck
- **Tomato Timer**: Pomodoro-style timers to help balance work and break time

## Benefits

The physical button interface makes time tracking habitual and effortless, reducing the cognitive load of switching to a time tracking app. Users report better adherence to time tracking because the tactile act of pressing a button is more satisfying and memorable than clicking in software.

## Requirements

Requires an Elgato Stream Deck device (any model) and the Stream Deck software installed on your computer.
