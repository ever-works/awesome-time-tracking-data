## Overview

Coefficient Billable Hours Calculator is a streamlined, free online tool specifically designed for finance and operations teams who need to quickly calculate billable hours and revenue. It provides transparency into the formulas used and includes example rates to help teams standardize billing calculations.

## Features

- **Instant Calculation**: Calculate billable hours and revenue in real-time
- **Formula Transparency**: See the exact formulas used for calculations
- **Example Rates**: Pre-populated example rates for common scenarios
- **Multiple Entry Support**: Add various time entries and rates
- **Revenue Totals**: Automatic summation of all billable amounts
- **Time Format Flexibility**: Support for hours, minutes, or decimal time
- **Rate Variations**: Different hourly rates for different tasks or people
- **Copy/Paste Friendly**: Easy to transfer data to/from spreadsheets
- **No Registration**: Use immediately without signup
- **Team-Oriented**: Designed for multi-person billing scenarios

## Calculation Methodology

### Basic Formula
**Billable Amount = Hours Worked × Hourly Rate**

### Advanced Calculations
- **Total Revenue**: Sum of all individual billable amounts
- **Average Rate**: Total revenue ÷ total hours
- **Blended Rates**: Weighted average across different hourly rates
- **Time Conversion**: Automatic conversion between time formats

## Comparison to Alternatives

### vs. Time Tracking Software
- Simpler and faster for quick calculations
- No software installation or configuration
- Better for ad-hoc calculations
- Less comprehensive for long-term tracking

### vs. Spreadsheets
- Pre-built formulas save time
- No formula errors
- Easier for non-technical users
- Less flexible for custom calculations

### vs. Accounting Software
- Faster for preliminary calculations
- No system access required
- Useful for estimates and proposals
- Not a replacement for formal invoicing

## Limitations
