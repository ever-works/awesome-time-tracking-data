## Overview

Trello is a visual collaboration tool that uses boards, lists, and cards to organize projects and tasks. While Trello doesn't have native time tracking built-in, it offers extensive time tracking capabilities through Power-Ups (Trello's plugin system) and integrations with popular time tracking services.

## Time Tracking Power-Ups and Integrations

- Everhour for Trello - Native time tracking directly on cards
- Toggl Track integration - Start timers from Trello cards
- Harvest integration - Track time and manage invoices
- Clockify for Trello - Free time tracking on cards
- Timely integration - Automatic time tracking for Trello tasks
- Plus for Trello - Time tracking with reporting
- Pomello - Pomodoro timer for Trello cards
- Time tracking custom fields for manual entry

## Features

- Drag-and-drop card interface
- Lists for workflow stages
- Labels, due dates, and checklists
- File attachments and comments
- Card templates
- Butler automation for workflows
- Calendar and timeline views
- Multiple board views
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Team collaboration features
- 200+ Power-Ups for extended functionality

## Pricing

Free plan available with unlimited cards and up to 10 boards. Standard plan at $5/user/month, Premium at $10/user/month, and Enterprise for larger organizations. Time tracking Power-Ups may have additional costs.
