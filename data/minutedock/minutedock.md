## Overview

MinuteDock is a straightforward time tracking and invoicing solution designed for small businesses and freelancers who want simplicity without sacrificing essential features.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking
- **Quick entry**: Fast time logging with minimal clicks
- **Timer or manual**: Choose your preferred tracking method
- **Project-based**: Organize time by projects and tasks
- **Team tracking**: Support for multi-user time tracking
- **Mobile apps**: iOS and Android for on-the-go tracking

### Billing & Invoicing
- **Billable hours**: Mark time as billable or non-billable
- **Multiple rates**: Different billing rates per project or task
- **Invoice generation**: Create invoices directly from tracked time
- **Expense tracking**: Add expenses to invoices
- **Currency support**: Multi-currency billing

### Integrations

#### FreshBooks Integration
Seamless integration allowing you to:
- Import FreshBooks projects
- Track time against FreshBooks clients
- Push time entries to FreshBooks
- Sync billing data automatically

#### Xero Integration
Connect with Xero for:
- Automated invoice sync
- Contact synchronization
- Streamlined accounting workflow
- Real-time financial data

### Reporting
- **Time reports**: Detailed breakdowns by project, client, or team member
- **Export capabilities**: CSV exports for further analysis
- **Summary dashboards**: Quick overview of time allocation
- **Billable vs non-billable**: Clear separation for profitability insights
