## Overview

Google Calendar is a ubiquitous scheduling tool integrated into Google Workspace, providing reliable calendar management, team resource booking, and smart scheduling suggestions for organizations of all sizes.

## Key Features

- Workspace integration: seamlessly connects with Gmail, Meet, and Drive for unified collaboration
- Shared calendars: enables team visibility and resource booking across departments
- Smart scheduling: suggests meeting times based on availability and automatically adds video conferencing links
- Multiple calendar support and color coding for organization
- Goal and event scheduling with notification reminders
- Appointment scheduling for office hours and availability slots

## Pricing

- Personal: free with Google account
- Business Starter: $6/user/month (annual); includes custom email and 30GB storage
- Business Standard: $12/user/month (annual); adds enhanced security and 2TB storage
- Business Plus: $18/user/month (annual); includes advanced security and compliance features
- Enterprise: custom pricing with enhanced controls

## Considerations

- No native time tracking; requires third-party integrations for detailed time logging and analysis
- Focused on scheduling rather than task dependencies or workflow automation
- Effectiveness depends on team adoption and consistent calendar usage
