## Overview

monday Vibe is a development platform introduced in 2025 that enables teams to build custom time tracking solutions directly within monday.com without relying solely on third-party integrations.

## Features

- **Custom Views**: Build bespoke time tracking views tailored to your workflow
- **Dashboard Widgets**: Create custom widgets to display time data
- **Full App Development**: Develop complete time tracking applications within monday.com
- **No-Code/Low-Code**: Build solutions without extensive programming knowledge
- **Native Integration**: Leverages monday.com's existing data structures
- **Customization**: Tailor time tracking to specific business requirements

## Benefits

- Build exactly what your team needs without waiting for third-party solutions
- Keep all data within the monday.com ecosystem
- Customize workflows to match your unique processes
- Eliminate dependency on external integrations
- Leverage monday.com's infrastructure and security

## Use Cases

- Teams with highly specific time tracking requirements
- Organizations requiring custom approval workflows
- Companies needing industry-specific time categorization
- Businesses wanting full control over their time tracking solution

## Availability

Available since 2025 as part of monday.com's platform evolution toward greater customization and flexibility.

## Pricing

Pricing depends on monday.com plan level; custom development capabilities may require higher-tier plans.
