## Overview

"Speedy meetings" is a Google Calendar setting that automatically reduces default meeting lengths, creating buffer time between meetings. When enabled, 30-minute meetings become 25 minutes and 60-minute meetings become 50 minutes.

## How to Enable

1. Open Google Calendar settings
2. Go to "Event settings"
3. Check "Speedy meetings"
4. Save

**Result**: All new meetings default to shortened times

## Impact

### For Individuals
- 5-10 minute breaks between meetings
- Time for bio breaks
- Moment to process and take notes
- Preparation time for next meeting
- Reduced stress

### For Organizations
- Cultural shift toward respecting time
- More meetings starting on time
- Less cascading lateness
- Better meeting effectiveness

## The Research Behind It

Microsoft's 2021 research showed that even 10-minute breaks between meetings allow stress levels to reset, while back-to-back meetings cause cumulative stress buildup.

## Adoption

- Enable organization-wide as admin
- Recommend to all team members
- Explain rationale (health, productivity)
- Lead by example
