## Overview

Tofu is designed specifically for freelancers and creative professionals who need simple yet powerful tools to manage their finances, track time, and get paid faster.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracker with timer and manual entry options
- **Invoicing**: Create and send professional invoices with customizable templates
- **Recurring Invoices**: Set up automatic recurring billing for retainer clients
- **Expense Tracking**: Record and categorize business expenses
- **Payment Processing**: Accept online payments through integrated gateways
- **Client Management**: Organize client information and project history
- **Proposals and Estimates**: Create and send professional project proposals
- **Financial Reporting**: Track income, expenses, and profitability
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Work with international clients
- **Tax Preparation**: Generate reports for tax filing
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go management
- **Bank Connection**: Connect bank accounts for automatic transaction import
- **Receipt Capture**: Take photos of receipts for expense tracking

## Pricing

Tofu offers straightforward pricing:
- Free plan for basic invoicing
- Pro plan: Starting at $15/month with full features
- Premium plan: $30/month with advanced reporting and unlimited clients

30-day free trial available for paid plans.
