## Overview

Tivazo is a time tracking and employee monitoring solution designed for remote teams, freelancers, and distributed workforces. It offers automatic time tracking combined with optional productivity monitoring features.

## Features

- Automatic time tracking with desktop app
- Optional screenshot capture
- Application and website tracking
- Activity levels monitoring
- Project and task time allocation
- Idle time detection
- Detailed productivity reports
- Team timesheets and approval
- Client access to reports
- Integration with project management tools
- Mobile time tracking
- Payroll-ready exports

## Pricing

Tivazo offers affordable pricing plans:
- Basic plan with core time tracking
- Professional plan with monitoring features
- Business plan with advanced analytics

Pricing is per user per month with discounts for annual subscriptions.
