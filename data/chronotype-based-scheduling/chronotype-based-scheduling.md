## Overview

Chronotype-Based Scheduling aligns work activities with an individual's natural chronotype.

## Three Chronotypes

- Morning Types (Larks): Peak performance early morning
- Evening Types (Owls): Peak performance late afternoon/evening  
- Intermediate Types: Performance peaks 4-6 hours after waking

## Benefits

20-30% productivity improvements and significant stress reductions when work aligns with chronotype.
