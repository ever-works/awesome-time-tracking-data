## TrackingTime Project Management

**Description**  
TrackingTime Project Management is a project tracking and planning tool with built-in time tracking. It helps teams organize work, visualize project progress across multiple views, understand time and cost data, and keep projects on schedule and within budget.

---

## Features

### Project Views & Planning
- **Task List view** – Organize tasks by priority, track progress in real time, and maintain clarity across all project work.
- **Board (Kanban) view** – Visualize workflows on a Kanban board to manage stages, spot bottlenecks, and keep teams aligned.
- **Timeline view** – Plan projects on a visual timeline to map task dependencies, schedule work, and manage deadlines.

### Reporting & Insights
- **Project Report** – Real-time project reports showing progress, time, costs, and team performance.
- **Live project tracking** – Monitor each project in real time to see where time and effort are being spent.
- **Forecasting progress** – Use live insights to forecast project progress and assess whether you are on track.

### Milestones & Progress Tracking
- **Milestone tracking** – Define and track project milestones as they happen along the timeline.
- **Dependency visualization** – Visualize task dependencies to understand how tasks impact each other.
- **Real-time status visibility** – See exactly where the team stands at any moment and identify potential roadblocks early.

### Workload & Resource Management
- **Workload visibility** – See who is working on what to understand team capacity.
- **Resource allocation** – Allocate tasks and resources efficiently based on workload.
- **Burnout prevention** – Balance assignments to avoid overload and maintain sustainable performance.

### Time Tracking & Goal Management
- **Built-in time tracking** – Connect time tracking directly to project plans and tasks.
- **Estimated vs. actual time** – Compare planned time against actual tracked time for tasks and projects.
- **Goal and deadline management** – Track deliverables and deadlines to keep goals realistic and achievable.
- **Automated progress tracking** – Use automated updates to follow project progress without manual status checks.
- **Deadline alerts** – Receive alerts to stay ahead of deadlines and avoid schedule slippage.
