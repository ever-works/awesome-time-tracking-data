## Overview

Tide is a productivity and mindfulness app that merges a Pomodoro timer with immersive nature soundscapes to help users focus, relax, and sleep better. It creates an optimal auditory environment for concentration and stress reduction.

## Key Features

- Pomodoro-style focus timer
- High-quality nature sound library
- Meditation and breathing exercises
- Sleep timer with fade-out
- Daily focus statistics
- Focus history and insights
- Beautiful, minimalist design
- Cross-platform (iOS, Android, macOS, web)
- Offline playback
- No account required

## Sound Library

Tide offers a curated collection of nature sounds:
- Gentle rain and thunderstorms
- Crashing ocean waves
- Rustling forest and birdsong
- Crackling campfire
- Mountain stream
- White noise variations

## Focus Modes

- **Focus**: Pomodoro-based work sessions with customizable intervals
- **Breathing**: Guided breathing exercises for stress relief
- **Sleep**: Extended soundscapes with sleep timer
- **Meditation**: Timed meditation sessions
- **Nap**: Short rest periods with gentle wake-up

## Customization

- Adjustable focus session lengths
- Customizable break durations
- Sound mixing capabilities
- Volume controls
- Notification preferences
- Daily goal setting

## Statistics & Insights

- Total focus time tracking
- Daily and weekly trends
- Focus session history
- Streak tracking
- Achievement badges

## Use Cases

- Deep work sessions requiring intense focus
- Studying and exam preparation
- Creative work and writing
- Stress reduction and relaxation
- Meditation practice
- Improving sleep quality
- Managing anxiety

## Unique Advantages

- No subscription required for core features
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- Works offline without internet
- Privacy-focused (no account needed)
- Combines productivity and wellness

## Pricing

Free version includes core features and several soundscapes. Premium subscription unlocks additional sounds, unlimited focus sessions, and advanced statistics.
