## Overview

Apple Notes (also called Notes or iCloud Notes) is the built-in note-taking application for all macOS and iOS devices. It has evolved from a basic notes app into a competent, feature-rich tool for capturing and organizing information.

## Features

- Markdown support for formatting notes
- Sharing and collaboration on notes
- Tag-based organization with powerful search function
- Audio recording directly within notes
- Document scanning built in
- Drawing and handwriting support
- Accessible via web browser at icloud.com/notes (works on PC and Chromebook)
- Seamless synchronization across Apple devices

## Pricing

Free with 5GB of iCloud storage included across all iCloud services. Additional storage starts at $0.99/month for 50GB.

## Limitations

- Not available on Android devices
- Cross-platform usage requires the web interface
