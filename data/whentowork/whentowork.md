## Overview

WhenToWork (W2W) is a scheduling tool that stands out for its practical shift management capabilities. It provides the complete set of must-have capabilities for robust and flexible employee scheduling suited for hospitality, healthcare, retail, and other shift-based businesses with hourly employees.

## Features

### Shift Scheduling & Management
- Drag-and-drop manual scheduling
- Choose from employee lists when adding or editing shifts
- Add employees, positions (jobs), and shifts easily
- AutoFill feature gets you the best schedule in just one click
- Use employee preferences, availability, and skills to build schedules
- Fair, flexible schedules that are easier to fill

### Employee Self-Service
- Staff can set availability
- Trade shifts with manager oversight
- Request time off
- Real-time notifications
- Tradeboard for picking up open shifts
- Manager approval options or "at will" shift picking

### Monitoring & Controls
- Real-time schedule statistics
- Monitor shift gaps
- Avoid overtime before it becomes a problem
- Track schedule coverage
- Identify scheduling conflicts early

### Communication
- Built-in communication tools
- Notifications for schedule changes
- Real-time updates to employees
- Manager-to-employee messaging

## What Makes WhenToWork Different

WhenToWork is designed for teams needing straightforward scheduling without complex features. It focuses on core scheduling capabilities that shift-based workplaces actually need, making it particularly suitable for small to medium-sized businesses that don't need enterprise-level complexity.
