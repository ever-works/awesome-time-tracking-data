## Music Science

### Research Findings
- Instrumental music improves focus vs. lyrics
- Consistent tempo aids concentration
- Familiar music less distracting than new
- Personal preference matters significantly
- Volume sweet spot: 50-70 decibels

### Optimal Characteristics
- 60-80 beats per minute
- Minimal dynamic range
- Instrumental or minimal vocals
- Familiar but not attention-grabbing
- Moderate complexity
