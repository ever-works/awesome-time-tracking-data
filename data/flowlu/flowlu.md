## Overview

Flowlu is a comprehensive business management platform that integrates time tracking with project management, CRM, financial management, and collaboration tools, designed for small to medium-sized businesses and agencies.

## Features

- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracker with timer and manual entry options
- **Project Management**: Kanban boards, Gantt charts, and task management
- **CRM**: Customer relationship management with sales pipeline tracking
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked time and project expenses
- **Financial Management**: Track income, expenses, and profitability
- **Team Collaboration**: File sharing, discussions, and knowledge base
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive reports on time, projects, and finances
- **Integrations**: Connect with popular tools and services
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go time tracking

## Pricing

Flowlu offers multiple pricing tiers starting with a free plan for up to 2 users. Paid plans start at approximately $29/month for teams, with additional features and users available at higher tiers.
