## Overview

Forecast is an AI-driven project and resource management software that helps professional services teams of 25-200+ efficiently plan, execute, and monitor projects on a single platform. Forecast helps small businesses manage projects, tasks, and budgets with AI-assisted planning and time tracking.

## Key Features

### Time Tracking

- **Automatic and Manual Options**: Automatic and manual time tracking options, tie entries to projects/tasks, enforce approvals
- **Smart Time Suggestions**: Uses recent activity to recommend time entries, reducing manual input and improving tracking accuracy
- **Project-Based Tracking**: Track time directly against specific projects and tasks
- **Approval Workflows**: Enforce time entry approvals for accuracy

### Resource Planning & Utilization

- **Role-Based Scheduling**: Ensures the right people are staffed on the right projects
- **Resource Forecasting**: Helps teams forecast resource needs and balance workloads by analyzing historical data and upcoming project demands
- **Utilization Tracking**: Improves resource planning and utilization
- **Capacity Management**: Analyze team capacity and allocate resources effectively

### Financial Oversight

- **Budget Management**: Monitor project budgets in real-time
- **Margin Tracking**: Track project margins and profitability
- **Integration of Timesheets and Financials**: Reconcile forecasts to actuals to steer budgets and margins
- **Resource Visibility**: Provides clear visibility into resource costs

### AI-Assisted Features

- **AI Planning**: Artificial intelligence assists with project planning
- **Smart Recommendations**: AI suggests optimal resource allocation
- **Activity-Based Suggestions**: System learns from recent activity patterns
- **Predictive Analytics**: Forecast future resource needs based on data
