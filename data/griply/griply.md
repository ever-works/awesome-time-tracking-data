## Overview

Griply is a relatively new productivity platform that emerged in late 2025, quickly gaining recognition on Product Hunt for its holistic approach to personal productivity. Unlike tools that focus on a single aspect (tasks, habits, or goals), Griply connects all three into an integrated system.

## Core Features

### Goal Tracking
- Set and track long-term goals with clear milestones
- Break down large goals into actionable steps
- Visual progress indicators for each goal
- Regular goal review prompts and retrospectives

### Habit Building
- Daily habit tracking with streak counters
- Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, custom intervals)
- Habit stacking suggestions to build routines
- Visual habit calendars showing consistency patterns

### Task Management
- Traditional to-do lists with priorities and due dates
- Task categorization by goal or project
- Smart task suggestions based on your goals
- Integration with calendar for time-blocked tasks

## Unified Philosophy

Griply's key insight is that tasks, habits, and goals shouldn't be managed in separate tools. Every task can be linked to a larger goal, every habit contributes to progress metrics, and goals automatically generate suggested tasks.

For example:
- **Goal**: Run a marathon by December
- **Habits**: Run 3x per week, stretch daily
- **Tasks**: Buy running shoes, sign up for race, complete training plan week 1

Griply shows how all three work together toward the same outcome.

## Key Differentiators

- **Connected System**: Tasks automatically link to goals, habits support goal metrics
- **Smart Suggestions**: AI recommends habits and tasks based on your goals
- **Unified Dashboard**: See tasks, habits, and goal progress in one view
- **Reflection Prompts**: Regular check-ins to review what's working
- **Minimal UI**: Despite comprehensive features, maintains clean interface

## Target Users

- Individuals working on long-term personal development goals
- People who previously juggled multiple productivity apps
- Goal-oriented professionals wanting to connect daily actions to bigger picture
- Anyone seeking a unified system for life management

## Platform

Web application with mobile apps for iOS and Android (launched early 2026).

## Pricing

Griply offers a free tier with core features. Premium plans start around $5-8/month with unlimited goals, advanced analytics, and priority support.

## Reception

Featured as a Top Product on Product Hunt in January 2026, with particularly positive reception from users frustrated by app-hopping between separate goal, habit, and task management tools.
