## Overview

Clients & Profits is comprehensive agency management software designed specifically for advertising, marketing, and creative agencies. It includes a robust time tracking toolset integrated with project management, job costing, and billing functions.

## Time Tracking Features

- **Smart Timers**: Intelligent timer functionality for tracking active work
- **Daily and Weekly Time Cards**: Flexible time entry options to suit different workflows
- **Mobile Time Cards**: Track time on-the-go via mobile devices
- **Timesheets**: Comprehensive timesheet management with approval workflows
- **Missing Time Worksheets**: Identify and prompt for missing time entries
- **Staff Utilization Reports**: Analyze how team members spend their time
- **Productivity Reports**: Measure and optimize team productivity

## Additional Features

- **Job Costing**: Track costs against specific projects and clients
- **Billing & Invoicing**: Generate invoices based on tracked time
- **Project Management**: Manage creative projects from concept to completion
- **Resource Planning**: Allocate creative talent across multiple projects
- **Traffic Management**: Coordinate workflow across the agency

## Use Cases

- Advertising agencies managing multiple client campaigns
- Creative agencies tracking billable hours
- Marketing agencies optimizing team utilization
- Production companies managing project costs
- Design studios tracking project profitability

## Benefits

- Complete visibility into agency operations
- Accurate job costing and project profitability
- Improved resource allocation and utilization
- Streamlined billing and invoicing processes
- Enhanced productivity tracking and optimization
