## Overview

My Hours is a comprehensive time tracking solution that emphasizes powerful reporting capabilities and affordability.

## Features

- **Activity Reports**: Detailed breakdowns of time spent on activities
- **Economy Reports**: Financial analysis of tracked time
- **Pivot Tables**: Advanced data analysis and visualization
- **Project Tracking**: Organize time by projects and tasks
- **Team Collaboration**: Track team time and productivity
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices from tracked hours
- **Budget Management**: Track project budgets and time allocation
- **Free Forever Plan**: Generous free plan with core features

## Pricing

- Free Forever Plan: Available with essential features
- Paid Plans: Low-cost upgrades for additional features and users
