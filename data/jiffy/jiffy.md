## Overview

Jiffy is a time tracker designed for streamlined work time tracking, allowing users to spend a minimum amount of time while generating accurate time reports. Jiffy's beauty is in its simplicity—it's like having a stopwatch on your phone or tablet for all the jobs that you need to track.

## Key Features

- **Simple Interface**: Start, stop, and switch between projects/tasks with a simple click
- **Calendar View**: Edit and add times, insert breaks, and get an overview of the full workweek in a familiar calendar interface
- **Offline Use**: Stores your data on your device with export functionality
- **No Account Required**: Just download it and start right away
- **Data Export**: Export your tracked time data for reporting and invoicing
- **Minimal Effort**: Focuses on making time tracking as effortless as possible

## Platform

Available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).

## Pricing Model

Jiffy uses a freemium "try before you buy" model. All features in the app are available to test out, with the only limitation being that the history is limited to 4 weeks on the free tier.

## Use Cases

Ideal for freelancers, consultants, and professionals who need simple, straightforward time tracking without complex features or steep learning curves.
