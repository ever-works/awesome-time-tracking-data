## Overview

Micro-Commitment reduces resistance to starting by committing to tiny time increments.

## How It Works

1. Commit to just 2-5 minutes
2. Start timer
3. Work for committed period
4. Stop if desired, but usually continue
5. Momentum carries you forward

## Psychological Basis

- Zeigarnik effect: Started tasks create completion pressure
- Reduces perceived effort
- Bypasses resistance to "big" commitments
- Activates working memory systems
