## Overview

The Timeular Tracker is a unique 8-sided Bluetooth device that makes time tracking physical and automatic. Each side represents a different activity - flip the tracker to switch between them, and the software automatically records your time.

## Physical Device Features

- **8 Activity Sides**: Assign activities to each side
- **Bluetooth Connected**: Wireless connection to computer/phone
- **LED Indicator**: Shows connection status
- **Automatic Tracking**: Start/stop just by flipping
- **Battery Powered**: USB rechargeable
- **Desk Accessory**: Sits on your desk
- **Tactile Experience**: Physical interaction with time tracking

## Software Features

- **Activity Assignment**: Customize each tracker side
- **Automatic Time Capture**: Records time as you flip
- **Manual Entries**: Add time without tracker
- **Reports**: Detailed time analysis
- **Integrations**: Connect with calendar and project tools
- **Team Features**: Track team time
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android support

## How It Works

1. Assign activities to each of the 8 sides
2. Place tracker on your desk
3. Flip to corresponding side when changing tasks
4. Time is automatically tracked
5. Review and export data in software
