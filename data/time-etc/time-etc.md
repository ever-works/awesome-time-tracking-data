## Overview

Time Etc is a virtual assistant service that helps busy professionals and entrepreneurs reclaim time by delegating administrative and routine tasks to experienced assistants. Rather than tracking time spent on low-value work, Time Etc helps eliminate it from your schedule entirely.

## Services Offered

- Administrative support
- Email and calendar management
- Research and data entry
- Travel planning and booking
- Social media management
- Customer service support
- Bookkeeping and invoicing
- Personal tasks and errands
- Meeting scheduling and coordination
- Document preparation and formatting

## How It Works

1. Subscribe to a monthly plan with dedicated assistant hours
2. Get matched with an experienced assistant
3. Delegate tasks via email, phone, or task management platform
4. Assistant completes work within agreed timeframes
5. Track time usage through online dashboard

## Pricing

Time Etc offers subscription plans:
- 10 hours/month: Starting around $360
- 20 hours/month: ~$700
- 40 hours/month: ~$1,360
- 60 hours/month: ~$1,980

All assistants are US-based with experience. Unused hours roll over for up to 3 months.
