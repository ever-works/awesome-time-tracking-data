## Overview

Focus@Will is a music service based on human neuroscience that helps you focus, reduce distractions, maintain your productivity and retain information when working, studying, writing and reading.

## How It Works

Many of the channels in Focus@Will use Brainwave Entrainment, which means they are specially designed to adapt your brainwaves to the activity you are doing. The system manages the ratio between your endogenous attention (the task you are focusing on) and your exogenous attention (your reptile brain looking for potentially dangerous fight or flight external stimuli).

Focus@will claims it boosts productivity by playing music that's been rigorously engineered to soothe a part of your brain known as the limbic system, which is believed to be responsible for emotion and motivation.

## Key Features

- Individualized music-for-work solution based on connection to the biggest brain database in the world
- Every audio mix is different for every user
- Music exclusive to the platform
- Combines the science behind how music interacts with the brain with a Pomodoro-style timer

## Effectiveness

According to Focus@will's research, the most active users have a 200-400 percent increase in focus time.
