## Overview

symplr Workforce is a comprehensive workforce management solution built specifically for healthcare organizations. It addresses the unique challenges of healthcare staffing, including shift-based scheduling, credential management, and strict regulatory compliance requirements.

## Features

- Time and attendance tracking
- Shift-based scheduling and staffing
- Credential and license management
- Compliance tracking and reporting
- Labor analytics and forecasting
- Self-scheduling and shift bidding
- Mobile app for clinicians and staff
- Integration with payroll and HR systems
- Patient census-based staffing
- Fatigue management
