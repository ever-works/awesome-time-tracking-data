## Overview

A time audit is a systematic practice of tracking how you spend time over 1-2 weeks to identify time wasters and optimize workflows.

## Benefits

- Identify time wasters and productivity leaks
- Understand actual vs. perceived time allocation
- Optimize schedule based on energy levels
- Improve time estimates
- Increase billable hours

## How to Conduct

1. Choose tracking method (manual or automated)
2. Define activity categories
3. Track everything for 5-7 days
4. Analyze patterns and findings
5. Implement improvements

## Tools

- RescueTime (automatic)
- Toggl Track (manual)
- ActivityWatch (open-source)
- Spreadsheets

## Frequency

- Initial: 1-2 weeks comprehensive
- Follow-up: Quarterly
- Ongoing: Light automatic tracking
