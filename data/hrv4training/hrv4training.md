## Overview

HRV4Training is a heart rate variability tracking application designed for athletes, coaches, and anyone interested in optimizing their training and recovery through daily HRV measurements and lifestyle tracking.

## Features

- **Camera-Based HRV**: Measure HRV using your smartphone camera without additional hardware
- **Daily Assessments**: Quick morning assessments to evaluate recovery status
- **Training Advice**: Personalized recommendations on whether to train hard or take it easy
- **Contextual Data**: Track sleep, travel, stress, and other factors affecting recovery
- **Wearable Support**: Compatible with chest straps and wearables for enhanced accuracy
- **Trend Analysis**: Long-term trend analysis to understand your baseline and variations
- **Recovery Score**: Daily recovery score based on HRV and other metrics
- **Training Load**: Track training load and correlate with recovery status
- **Export Data**: Export data for analysis in other platforms
- **Research-Backed**: Based on peer-reviewed scientific research on HRV
