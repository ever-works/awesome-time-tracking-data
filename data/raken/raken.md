## Overview

Raken is a construction timesheet app and software platform designed specifically for construction teams working on jobsites. The platform enables field teams to track time, manage daily reports, and integrate with broader project management workflows.

## Key Features

- **Construction-Specific Workflows**: Built specifically for construction industry needs and processes
- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for field workers to clock in and out from jobsites
- **Daily Reporting Integration**: Timesheets integrate with daily construction reports
- **Project Tracking**: Link time entries to specific construction projects and tasks
- **Field-Optimized Interface**: Designed for ease of use on construction sites

## Target Users

Construction companies, general contractors, subcontractors, and construction field teams who need specialized time tracking that understands construction workflows and terminology.

## Benefits

- Streamlines timesheet collection from field teams
- Reduces administrative burden of tracking construction labor hours
- Improves accuracy of job costing and labor allocation
- Integrates time data with daily construction reports for comprehensive project tracking
- Mobile accessibility ensures workers can track time from any jobsite
