## Overview

Tiimo is an award-winning visual daily planner designed to address the core challenges of executive functioning. Built by and for neurodivergent people, Tiimo provides structure and clarity for ADHD and autistic individuals.

## Recognition

- iPhone App of the Year 2025
- Apple Design Awards Finalist 2024
- Best Assistive Technology 2022 (Neurodiversity Awards)
- 3+ Million downloads worldwide
- 1+ Million active users

## Key Features

- **Visual Schedule Planner**: Color-coded daily overview with easy drag-and-drop tasks
- **AI Co-Planner**: Personal planning assistant that helps create, prioritize, and plan tasks that work for your brain
- **Focus Timer**: Visual timer with widgets and timelines to make time visible and track progress
- **Customization**: Choose from 3,000+ colors and custom icons to create a visual system that works for your brain
- **Transition Management**: Tools to manage transitions between tasks and activities
- **Sensory-Friendly Design**: Interface designed with sensory needs in mind
- **Task Widgets**: Home screen widgets for quick access to your schedule

## Design Philosophy

Co-designed with ADHD and autism experts, and developed by a Copenhagen team committed to neurodivergent empowerment. Many team members are neurodivergent themselves, ensuring genuine understanding of challenges rather than theoretical accessibility.

## Target Users

While built with ADHD and Autism in mind, the tools support executive functioning for every brain type.

## Platform Availability

Available on iOS and Android.
