## Overview

amGtime is a provider of advanced facial recognition time clock terminals that use AI-powered biometric technology for contactless employee authentication. Their systems are designed to prevent time theft and streamline attendance management.

## Features

- AI-powered facial recognition (recognition in under 1 second)
- Touchless biometric authentication
- Temperature screening capability
- Mask detection
- Multi-factor authentication options
- Cloud-based attendance management
- Real-time reporting and analytics
- Mobile app integration
- Payroll system integration
- Works in various lighting conditions
- Large employee capacity (up to 6,000 faces)
- HIPAA and data privacy compliant

## Pricing

amGtime offers various terminal models at different price points, typically ranging from mid to premium pricing. Software subscriptions are available for cloud-based workforce management features. Contact their sales team for specific pricing.
