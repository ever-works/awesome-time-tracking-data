## Overview

eBillity is a time tracking and billing platform designed for professional services, with particular focus on healthcare, legal, and consulting industries. It helps professionals accurately track billable time and streamline the invoicing process.

## Features

- Time tracking with timers and manual entry
- Expense tracking and receipt capture
- Project and matter management
- Customizable invoicing and billing
- Client portal for invoice payment
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Integration with QuickBooks and other accounting software
- Mileage tracking and reimbursement
- Team time tracking and approval workflows
- Detailed reporting and analytics
- Multiple billing rates and methods
- Recurring invoices and retainers
