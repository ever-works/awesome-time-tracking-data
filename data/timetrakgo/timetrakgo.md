## Overview

TimeTrakGO is a comprehensive time tracking solution designed specifically for small businesses, combining cloud-based software with optional biometric hardware. The platform allows employees to clock in from their mobile device, computer, or a wall-mounted time clock, while managers track attendance in real time.

## Features

### Biometric Authentication
- Facial recognition technology that identifies employees in less than one second
- Fingerprint scanning with support for multiple fingerprints per user
- Physical GOtime5 Smart Clock with bright touchscreen display
- Offline mode for reliable clock-ins even without internet

### Flexible Clock-In Methods
- Web-based time tracking
- Mobile app with GPS geolocation
- Tablet app with facial recognition
- Physical fingerprint time clock hardware

### Management Features
- Real-time attendance dashboards
- Employee scheduling capabilities
- Time off requests and approval workflows
- PTO tracking and custom pay codes
- Compliance-ready reporting

### Integrations
- ADP
- Paychex
- QuickBooks Desktop
- Gusto
- Other leading payroll services
