## Overview

Grain is an AI notetaker that automatically generates meeting recordings and transcripts with precise AI-powered notes. Grain's standout feature is creating shareable video clips from transcripts or notes, allowing teams to easily organize and share meeting highlights.

## Key Features

### Highlight Reels and Video Clips

- Create shareable video clips directly from transcripts or notes
- Search words within the transcription and highlight specific sections to make smaller video snippets
- Organize meeting highlights into playlists and stories
- Perfect for training libraries, customer testimonials, and team collaboration

### Recording Capabilities

- Three flexible meeting recording options
- Automatically join and record meetings when enabled
- Support for Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Webex
- High-quality video and audio capture

### AI-Powered Transcription

- Precise, accurate transcripts generated automatically
- Searchable transcript text
- Time-stamped entries for easy navigation
- Ability to create clips from any part of the transcript

## Content Organization
