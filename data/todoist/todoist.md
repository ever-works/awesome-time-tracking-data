## Overview

Todoist is one of the world's most popular task management applications, known for its clean design, powerful features, and cross-platform availability. It helps individuals and teams organize tasks, set priorities, track projects, and build productive habits through its intuitive interface and smart features.

## Core Features

### Task Management
- Quick task capture
- Natural language input ("tomorrow at 3pm")

- Task notes and attachments

## Comparison to Alternatives

### vs Things 3
- Todoist: Cross-platform, cheaper, collaboration
- Things: Mac/iOS only, more polished, no collaboration

### vs Microsoft To Do
- Todoist: More powerful, better features
- To Do: Free, Microsoft ecosystem integration

### vs Notion
- Todoist: Focused on tasks, faster, simpler
- Notion: More flexible, database-driven, heavier

## Best Practices

### Inbox Zero
- Capture everything
- Process inbox regularly
- Organize into projects
- Clear to zero daily

### Priority Management
- Reserve P1 for truly urgent
- Most tasks should be P4
- Use sparingly for impact

### Project Organization
- Life areas as top-level projects
- Specific projects within areas
- Use sections for phases
- Archive completed projects
