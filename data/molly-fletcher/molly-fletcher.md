## Overview

Molly Fletcher is a sports agent turned entrepreneur, author, and motivational speaker who helps leaders, teams, and organizations unleash their potential.

## Notable Works

- **The Energy Clock** - Explores energy management as the foundation of sustainable productivity and performance

## Background

Fletcher spent 20+ years as a sports agent representing coaches, athletes, and media personalities before transitioning into executive coaching and speaking. Her unique background in high-stakes sports negotiation informs her approach to productivity and leadership.
