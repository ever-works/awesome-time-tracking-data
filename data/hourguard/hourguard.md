## Overview

HourGuard is the ultimate time-tracking app designed to streamline your workday and maximize efficiency. Whether you're managing a team or tracking your own hours, HourGuard provides powerful features to keep you on top of your tasks.

## Key Features

- **Easy Time Tracking**: Start and stop timers with a single tap to track work hours accurately
- **Team Management**: Managers can oversee multiple team members' timesheets in one place
- **Timesheet Generation**: Automatically generate detailed timesheets for payroll processing
- **Task Categorization**: Organize time entries by project, client, or task type
- **Reporting**: Generate reports to analyze time spent across different activities

## Use Cases

- Freelancers tracking billable hours for multiple clients
- Small business owners managing employee work hours
- Project managers monitoring time allocation across team members
- Employees tracking personal productivity and work-life balance
