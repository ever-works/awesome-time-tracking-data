# Toggl Track – Google Calendar Integration

**Category:** Time Tracking APIs & Integrations  
**Tags:** integration, automation, calendar  
**Vendor:** Zapier / Toggl Track

A Zapier-based integration that connects Toggl Track with Google Calendar. New time entries created in Toggl Track are automatically turned into detailed events in Google Calendar, allowing you to visualize tracked time (such as meetings or focused work blocks) directly in your calendar without manual copy-paste.

---

## Features

### Automated Time → Calendar Sync (via Zapier)
- Creates a new Google Calendar event for each new Toggl Track time entry.  
- Uses the time entry details (e.g., description, start/end time, duration) to populate event fields in Google Calendar.  
- Eliminates manual copying of time entries into calendar events.

### Calendar-Based Time Management in Toggl Track
- **Calendar View in Toggl Track:** View your workday or workweek in a grid-style calendar layout inside Toggl Track.
- **Google Calendar Event Import:** Link your Google Calendar to your Toggl Track workspace to see Google Calendar events inside Toggl Track’s Calendar view for a fuller picture of your schedule.
- **Quick Time Entry from Calendar:** Use calendar events shown in Toggl Track to quickly create or adjust related time entries.
- **Edit Time Visually:** Duplicate, delete, and move time entries directly in the Calendar view by dragging or adjusting entries.
- **Gap Detection:** Easily spot free or untracked gaps in your day in the grid view and fill them with appropriate time entries.

### Multi-Calendar Ecosystem
- Works alongside other calendar integrations provided by Toggl Track:
  - Outlook Calendar integration (separate, free integration).
  - iCal integration to subscribe to your Toggl Track time entries and display them in other calendar tools that support iCal.

### General Integration Characteristics
- Designed to keep your tracked time and your calendar view in sync, enabling better planning, review, and accountability.
- Can be incorporated into broader Zapier workflows for additional automation (e.g., connecting Toggl Track + Google Calendar with other apps, if configured by the user).
