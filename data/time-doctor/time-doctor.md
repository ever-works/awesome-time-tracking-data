## Overview

Time Doctor is a time tracking and productivity monitoring platform designed for remote and distributed teams. It provides detailed insights into how time is spent while offering various privacy controls and monitoring options.

## Time Tracking Features

### Automatic Time Tracking
- Tracks time automatically as you work
- Manual time entry also available
- Idle time detection (keyboard and mouse activity)
- Automatic pause when computer is idle
- Mobile time tracking for field workers

### Activity Monitoring (Optional)
- Screenshot capture at customizable intervals
- Application and website usage tracking
- Active window titles
- Productivity categorization of apps and sites
- All monitoring features can be disabled for privacy

## Productivity Analytics

### Individual Insights
- Time spent on productive vs unproductive sites
- Most used applications
- Productivity percentage calculations
- Distraction alerts
- Focus time tracking

### Team Analytics
- Team productivity dashboards
- Time spent by project and client
- Billable vs non-billable time
- Department productivity comparisons
- Attendance tracking

## Project & Client Tracking

- Track time by project, client, and task

## Privacy & Transparency

### Employee Privacy Options
- **Blurred Screenshots Mode**: Screenshots blurred by default
- **Monitoring Toggle**: Employees can pause tracking
- **Transparent Monitoring**: Employees see when they're being tracked
- **Data Access**: Employees can view their own data
- **Delete Options**: Ability to delete specific time entries

### Compliance
- GDPR compliant
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- Data encryption in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits

## Alternative Use: Privacy-Focused Mode

Time Doctor can be configured without screenshots or activity monitoring, functioning as a simple time tracker for teams that value privacy while still needing time tracking for billing or project management.
