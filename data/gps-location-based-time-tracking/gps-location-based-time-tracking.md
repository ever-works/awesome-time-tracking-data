## Overview

GPS Location-Based Time Tracking uses smartphone GPS to capture employee location data during clock events, providing verification, automation, and insights for mobile workforces.

## Key Features

### Location Verification
- Timestamp with GPS coordinates
- Confirms on-site presence
- Prevents buddy punching
- Creates accountability

### Geofencing
- Define virtual boundaries around job sites
- Auto clock-in when entering area
- Alerts when clocking from wrong location
- Restrict time tracking to approved locations

### Route Tracking
- Breadcrumb trail of movement
- Time spent at each location
- Travel time documentation
- Mileage calculation

## Use Cases

**Construction**: Verify workers at correct job sites
**Field Service**: Track technician locations and arrival times
**Delivery**: Confirm delivery completion locations
**Healthcare**: Home health visit verification
**Sales**: Track time spent with clients

## Privacy Considerations

- Track only during work hours
- Transparent policies
- Employee consent
- Data retention limits
- Comply with privacy laws
