## Overview

Timelines Time Tracking offers a unique approach to tracking time by presenting your activities on a fully interactive timeline interface. This visual representation makes it easy to see how your day unfolds and where your time goes.

## Features

- **Interactive Timeline**: Track and visualize your time on a fully interactive timeline interface
- **Subcategory Management**: Easily manage and organize subcategories within your time tracking
- **Quick Access Pinning**: Pin frequently used categories for instant 1-tap access
- **Shared Goals**: Set goals for category groups and track progress collaboratively
- **Visual Interface**: See your time allocation at a glance through timeline visualization
- **Category Groups**: Organize related activities into logical groups

## Use Cases

Timelines is perfect for individuals and teams who prefer visual representations of their time and want to quickly start tracking without navigating through complex menus.
