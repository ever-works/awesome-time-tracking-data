## Overview

HAQQ provides a free billable hours calculator designed specifically for lawyers and law firms to calculate billable hours, revenue targets, and utilization rates—no signup required.

## Features

- **Billable Hours Calculation**: Calculate total billable hours for attorneys
- **Revenue Targets**: Determine revenue targets based on billable rate
- **Utilization Rate**: Calculate attorney utilization and efficiency
- **No Signup Required**: Use immediately without registration
- **Legal-Specific**: Designed for law firm billing practices
- **6-Minute Increments**: Supports standard legal billing increments
- **Multiple Attorneys**: Calculate for individual or multiple attorneys

## Use Cases

- Law firms tracking attorney productivity
- Solo practitioners calculating revenue
- Legal departments monitoring billable efficiency
- Law firm administrators planning resources

## Platform

Web-based tool accessible from any browser.

## Pricing

Completely free to use.
