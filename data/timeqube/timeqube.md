## Overview

Timeqube is a unique hand-made timer for business meetings, presentations, and public speaking. It's the first distraction-free timer that lets you focus on the meeting itself.

## How It Works

Instead of tick-tock sounds and noisy alarms, the Timeqube uses colors: GREEN, ORANGE, and RED, keeping meeting participants aware of time without the stress of regular timers. Timeqube provides gentle visual stimuli directly to the brain without diverting attention from the task at hand.

## Time Presets

Universal preset times include: 2, 5, 15, 30, 45, 60 minutes. There are three different models available with varying light colors and time presets.

## Use Cases

- Clinical psychologists and mental health professionals
- Visual learners and ADHD individuals who benefit from seeing time move
- Meeting participants who can intuitively cross-check time during meetings
- Presentations and public speaking engagements

## Benefits

It improves subtle time-awareness throughout meetings to naturally end meetings faster, and uses a passive, stress-free visual approach rather than audible alarms.
